About Me

Hello,

Hello, my name is Posighdun and I'm an Administrator, Map Developer, Referee, Head Translator & Social Media Administrator here on the Avicus Network. I am also the founder and leader of Cronus, a competitive Avicus tournament team. I first joined Avicus back when it was MCZONE, around April 2013. I was just a try hard back then, I didn't even know the community existed. In November 2014 I registered on the Forums and since then I have been an active member of this community. In January I applied for Staff, in which i was granted a Junior Moderator trial but soon ended unsuccessfully.

I then reapplied in the April application period and was promoted to Junior Moderator on 15th June, 5 days later i was promoted to Moderator on the 20th of June. After giving my all in moderating I was promoted to Senior Moderator, on 2nd of October. I was promoted to Head Translator and Social media admin in January 2016. On 30th April 2016 i was promoted to Referee. I finally got promoted to Administrator on 8th of May 2016. I've had some up and down days, but Avicus is honestly my home. I have so many friends, past and current, and so many supporters. Thank you to everyone who has helped me get to where I am today, you know who you are :D

Currently, I am 19 and i live in Western Australia, Perth. I am studying Website Development in University. I am very active on all Avicus platforms and I am VERY approachable, so if you ever have a complaint, question or just want to chat then don't be afraid to do so.
