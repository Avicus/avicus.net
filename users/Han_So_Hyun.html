My Life and the Things I am Learned

Know Me!

Welcome to my introduction page! I am Athena Su Dimou and I am 13 years old. I am a proud South Korean-Greek girl. I am a very social, friendly, and kind person who loves to make friends with new people, whether they hate me or like me. I am currently living a tall apartment in Helsinki. My favorite hobbies are recording on Avicus (minecraft), learning how to code, and playing minecraft. Before I knew about minecraft, I loved to paint, draw, and read, but not anymore. I have made lots of friends ever since I knew Avicus. If you ever bump into me on Avicus, don’t hesitate to say hi!

When I was young, I traveled to several countries in the world. I have been to China, South Korea, Greece, America, Fiji, Tonga, New Zealand, Thailand, and now in Finland! Because of my nationality, I can speak more than 2 languages. I can speak Korean, English, a bit of Finnish and Greek. The two languages I want to learn the most is Arabic, and Chinese.

A Lesson

During my first month of Avicus, I have caused some trouble. I appealed many times, until I realized an important fact. That fact was that actually, the staff were handing me a key to open a new door so that I can build a new reputation for myself. I never forgot this. Ever since then, I tried my best to build myself a new reputation for myself, and gained more friends than before. I also learned that this was a huge life-lesson. To those who helped me to realize this, thank you so much.

So, you learned something today! Try applying it to your life and you will see that you are better off than before!

I hope to see you guys in game! Bye!
