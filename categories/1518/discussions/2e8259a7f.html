[9/8/17] Voting Released by ALM September 8, 2017 at 4:09 PM UTC

Hello all, it's time for the weekly Friday update!

Today we are bringing back voting! In the past, the server had voting which helped us engage new players and retain some older ones as well.

With the release of voting, we have also added a new crate. This crate, ingeniously entitled the "Vote Crate", will be placed in your backpack when you vote. When this crate is opened, you have the chance to win all kinds of rewards, some of which you can only earn by voting. These rewards are weighted, but the more and more ofter you vote will increase the weight of some of the more rare items. A list of rewards can be found at avicus.net/vote.

We support 5 voting sites With each set of 5 votes, you will receive a vote crate. The more votes you cast, the higher chances you have at receiving  some of the rarer items.

The person with the most votes after the end of the month will receive a special prize which will last for the next month.

Thanks, and enjoy casting
ALM
