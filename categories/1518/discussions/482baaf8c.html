[9/29/2017] Warn Titles by ALM September 29, 2017 at 5:09 PM UTC

Hey all, It’s Friday!

We are announcing today the return of a feature that was removed in the Atlas update. We are re-adding titles on screen when you are wanted in game. Now, you make think this is only useful for staff, but would you rather not see a warning and keep doing something wrong, or realize that you are breaking a rule and stop before receiving a more harsh infraction.

When you are warned a sound will play, and message will be shown on screen, and you will be given a temporary potion effect.

See you next week,
ALM (and the lovely Junior Developers)
