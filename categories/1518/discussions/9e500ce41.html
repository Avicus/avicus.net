Nostalgiaween Rotations


Halloween is right around the corner, so the map developers have chosen some of Avicus' spookiest nostalgic maps to haunt the rotations for the upcoming week. There are also some newer maps in the mix, for everyone's enjoyment.

The maps that were added are:

Abandoned Castle CTF by  KojaCola
Abandoned Dam by  Pavlo
Darkness by  Clijmart &  Zintenka
Deathlands by  Pavlo
Dredmor by  Ogrey  Mango_ &  KooDaLord
Haunted Hallows by  MachoMantis &  Soyuiz
Haunted Hallows CTW MachoMantis &  Soyuiz
Rigor Mortis by  Bumblebeetle13
Shadow Forest by  Pavlo

We hope you enjoy the special maps. Have a scary Halloween!

Regularly scheduled Biweekly Rotation Updates will resume on October 30th.
