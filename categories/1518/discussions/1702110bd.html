October Week 2

Biweekly Rotation Update #2


Hello everyone,

Welcome to the second biweekly rotation update. Some exciting new maps have been added, and some have been removed (based on community feedback and gameplay.)

The rotations will receive updates every two weeks, at a minimum. This is not a static restriction; there may be a case where we will release an update on a week after a previous update.

In these updates, maps needing removal shall be replaced with new maps from the testing list, or with older maps that have been out of rotations recently. The updates are not meant to be complete overhauls of the rotations; they can instead be considered general maintenance.
