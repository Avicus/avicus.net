[9/20/2017] New Morphs by ALM September 20, 2017 at 5:09 PM UTC

Hey all, It’s Wednesday!

Today we are announcing 3 new morphs! It has been a while since we touched up our collection of morphs, so I thought it was about time we added some new ones!

We are announcing the TNT, Boat, and Minecart morphs! The TNT morph can only be purchased by Emerald and Diamond users, but, the other two can be purchased by everyone! These morphs can also be earned from the voting rewards crate, and are possible in the Beta/Gamma crates. 

See you Friday,
ALM (and the lovely Junior Developers)
