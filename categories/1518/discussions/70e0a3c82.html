[9/6/17] Achievements Released by ALM September 6, 2017 at 5:09 PM UTC

Versión en Español | 

Hello everyone!

Today I am proud to announce a big update to allow you all to play our network in a new way. This update adds achievements. The update adds a new tab to profiles where a list of all of your earned achievements is displayed. When you earn a new achievement, it is also displayed to you (and others if they have the setting enabled) when you earn an achievement.

There are a lot of achievements, which I will not disclose in the interest of letting you all find them out for yourselves. Just a note that some achievements which involve legacy data require you to log in after the update for those to be updated. Some other achievements that cannot be back-loaded started being tracked a month ago.

Thanks, and hope you enjoy the update,
ALM

ALM September 6, 2017 at 10:09 PM UTC

Here's a list of achievements!

n = 10, 20, 50, 100, 200, 500, 1000, 1500, 2000, 2500, 3000, 5000

n Kills - Get n kills.
Goin' Old School (n) - Get n kills with only a fist.
Hill Capper (n) - Capture n hills in KOTH.
Cloth Grabber (n) - Touch 10 wools in CTW.
Wool Capper (n) - Capture n wools in CTW.
Just a Touch! (n) - Touch n destroyables in DTM.
Cave Destroyer (n) - Destroy n destroyables in DTM.
Touch A Leakable (n) - Touch n leakables in DTC.
Gotta Leak em' All (n) - Leak n leakables in DTC.
Flag Capper (n) - Capture n flags in CTF.
Level Up! (n) - Earn n credits.

n = 5, 10, 15, 20, 30, 40, 50, 75, 100, 150 200, 250

Match MVP (n) - Get MVP in a match at least n times.
Ballot Box Stuffer (n) - Vote on n maps.
Map Critic (n) - Rate n maps.
Winner! (n) - Win n matches.

n = 50, 60, 80, 100, 125, 180, 200, 220, 250

Cliff Diver (n blocks) - Fall from a height of n blocks.
Cliff Pusher (n blocks) - Kill someone by hitting them off from a height of n blocks.
Firing Blind (n blocks) - Hit someone with a bow from n blocks away.
Long-Ranged Sniper (n blocks) - Kill someone with a bow from n blocks away.

n = 1, 5, 10, 15, 20, 50, 100

Jouster (n) - Kill someone while on a horse at least n times.
Water Warrior (n) - Kill someone while riding in a boat at least n times.
Void Killer (n) - Kill someone when they are below y=0 at least n times.
Chop Master (n) - Kill someone while on a pig at least n times.
Railway Warrior (n) - Kill someone while riding in a minecart at least n times.
The 4th Horseman (n) - Kill someone who is on a horse at least n times.
Pirate Hunter (n) - Kill someone who is riding in a boat at least n times.
Void Victim (n) - Be killed by someone when they are below y=0 at least n times.
The Chopper (n) - Kill someone who is on a pig at least n times.
Cart Tipper (n) - Kill someone who is riding in a minecart at least n times.
Knight Battle (n) - Kill someone who is on a horse while also on a horse at least n times.
Battle at Sea (n) - Kill someone who is riding in a boat while also riding in a boat at least n times.
Void PVP (n) - Kill someone when they are below y=0 while you are also below y=0 at least n times.
The Ultimate Chop (n) - Kill someone who is on a pig while also riding on a big at least n times.
Battle on the Tracks (n) - Kill someone who is riding in a minecart while also riding in a minecart at least n times.
