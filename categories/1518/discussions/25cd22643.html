September Week 3

Biweekly Rotation Update #1


Hello everyone,

This is the first biweekly rotation update. From now on, the rotations will receive updates every two weeks, at a minimum. This is not a static restriction; there may be a case where we will release an update on a week after a previous update.

In these updates, maps needing removal shall be replaced with new maps from the testing list, or with older maps that have been out of rotations recently. The updates are not meant to be complete overhauls of the rotations; they can instead be considered general maintenance.


» Destroy Update «
Railway was removed.
Oasis (by KojaCola) was added.
Victory (by  KomodoLord1  Alpp and  Riilo) was added.


» Capture Update «
Green's Farms was removed.
Sunset Town was removed.
Antaran (by _ArmyFrog and LeapingSpider) was added.


» Conquest Update «
Rapture II was removed.
Ruins of the User (by Jiloker Flaan and Passively) was added.


» Walls Update «
Oz was removed.
Ruins Ascension (by Goodnighht and GetUrOwnWasabi) was added.


In addition to the update, we will also always provide a small sneak peek of the next one. A plus means that a map will be added, while a minus means that it will be removed. Please keep in mind that the update may be modified after the sneak peeks are posted. Here are the sneak peeks for the next update:
