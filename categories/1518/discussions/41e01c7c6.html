[9/22/17] Credit Blaster Gadget by ALM September 22, 2017 at 5:09 PM UTC

Hey all, it’s Fridayyyyyyy!

Today we are announcing a new gadget called simply the “Credit Blaster.“ It does what the name implies, blasts credit items onto the ground and allows others to pick them up. This can only be used in lobbies at the moment, but we are looking into letting spectators use them to shoot credits at players in matches. There are a few tiers of the blaster, which can easily be purchased in the shop. When equipped, simply right click and items will shoot out that when picked up bu a player, will give them credits.

To let you in on some interesting news, the junior dev are currently working on what could be one of our largest updates to date, so watch out for that in the next few weeks! Stay tuned to our social medias for previews of whats to come.

Thanks,
The Development Team
