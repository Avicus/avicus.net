Hey all, It’s Wednesday!

Today we are announcing 3 new morphs!

We are announcing the Squid, Rabbit, and Magma Cube morphs! The Magma Cube morph can only be purchased by Emerald and Diamond users, but, the other two can be purchased by everyone! These morphs can also be earned from the voting rewards crate, and are possible in the Beta/Gamma crates. 

See you Friday,
