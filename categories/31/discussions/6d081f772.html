^*Player of the Week*^ by RefuseToSink September 17, 2014 at 12:09 AM UTC


So today in Biology my teacher was droning on & on about taxonomy, when I came up with this idea. Player of the Week. 
    I'm sure you've all seen the staff spotlights, which is kind of what this is, but it's for Players. Every two weeks one person will be known as "Player of the Week" and get an interview.
        Here's how it works:
Every other week I will post a topic regarding the "Nomination" step. In that thread you will be able to nominate up to 3 different players, and yes, you may nominate yourself. You may nominate staff but it defeats the purpose of the topic. You may also ask people to nominate you. 
The three players who have the most nominations will then be put into a voting system where you can vote for your favorite. Keep in mind, You may only vote once.
After the voting period is over, I will contact the player via skype or teamspeak. 
*Note: The player must be active on the forums, or you must provide me with their skype in order for me to contact them.
Special Thanks to- RCMP_Mountie & Sulorak for offering to help me manage this topic.

The first "Player of the Week" will be announced on Wednesday, September 24th.

You may start nominating below, have fun :D
*Edit* Nominations Close after 48 hours. Be sure to check back for the final voting round and vote for who you think deserves "Player of the Week"! ^.^
