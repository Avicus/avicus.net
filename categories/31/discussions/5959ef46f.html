Hey guys, I recently thought of a great idea where very week on a Saturday I will put a question in a post that you answer (Don't use to much personal info, and remember you don't HAVE to answer) .  So this weeks question is:

What has been the scariest moment of your life so far?



My answer:

I was staying in Turkey with my brother and sister, in the same room in the summer. Me and my brother are lying in bed when all of a sudden my sister comes in and turns on the light. I lay for a bit, looking round the room. My brother was staring at me. He suddenly has fear in his eyes, and starts to scream. My sister screams to. They run out of the room. I leap out of my bed and over my brothers bed as fast as I could, to save my life. I have never been so scared. Turns out my brother thought I had been possessed by the devil and I had demon eyes. (kden)

Tl;Dr
 Brother and sister scared of my eyes, I get scared to, scariest moment of my life.
