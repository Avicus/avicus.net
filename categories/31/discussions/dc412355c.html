Teamspeak Ideas, and Briefing. by Iron January 25, 2016 at 8:01 PM UTC

Hello everyone!

Recently, I was promoted to Senior Mod.. Consequently I have deployed myself into the "Department of Team-Speak 3." I am quite happy to be working alongside Newcleus as we both have a very large amount of Teamspeak experience. I have been using TS3, for around 2 and some years and have grown experienced and accustomed to the various ranks etc. Michael has basically studied the rules, loopholes and such. Together we hope to bring forward a (hopefully) uplifting atmosphere, and improve the quality of Teamspeak. Furthermore, we hope to improve everyones experience! :)

                       As of yesterday, I have created and executed a few idea's with Newcleus. With the addition of this survey, I hope to improve the efficiency and environment of TS on a day to day basis. I have compiled the data and have learnt some patterns which I had already observed, but also some interesting bits of information. Overall, I thank all of those who participated as this feedback really appreciated! I plan on asking and informing certain moderators about their performance, and although unlikely giving out staff warnings to moderators who are not up to par. In fact, we have already removed the Moderator Ranks of certain moderators who were not up with Michael and I's standards.

                        Secondly, Michael and myself are really keen on holding some more community events/TS events. Overall, I would really like to model these events after things YOU guys want. Not what the staff see fit. As previous events have (in my opinion) failed, I would ask that you guys inform me about what you think went wrong. In addition, we could possibly host some "Music" competitions, or even some competitive type events.... (ie, staff vs regulars, mega scrims and such)

                        Thirdly, and Lastly. I have already implemented a new rank, but I really am interested in seeing what you guys want on TS. Are there any ranks and such (Note, these are aesthetic) that would please you? As of January 25th, I have created the "Birthday Boy!" rank. The rank does not provide any permissions, it is just a fun little rank to recognize that it is your special day! I do not plan on removing this, possibly making a TS Cake day type thing for everyone????!! Anyways, what ideas do you have? Questions, Comments? Ask me below. Again, I look forward towards being a Senior Moderator, and I will do my best not only to improve your experience, but to improve transparency between EVERYONE.


Thank you for your attention!
          -Iron and Michael (Newcleus)
