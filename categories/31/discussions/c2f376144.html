SKYWARS!

Yes you heard it. I have made my own version of Skywars using Walls YML

Currently: You can't use the maps when bored (ex: scrim servers) until I change the map's files to 1.7 (currently 1.8 and can only be played on 1.8 servers)

thanks for your time! o/

-FIyHacks/ProfessorUtonium/Zerkk/LeafeonMessiah/NinjaBridge/SacredFlameLava/Sethskye



explanation on what my version is: 

4 teams spawned in all 4 corners with 2-3 players

Center Holds enchants (lapis + bottles included)
You can either make traps and defend your island (you can get TNT) or you can gather up resources to make pushes to reach the enemies territory.

islands between corner islands with poet 1 bow + more blocks

You can get Snowballs (snow blocks with shovel) or you can get eggs (chickens) for Projectiles to throw!

Currently: There is no grace period from attacking RIGHT when the game starts, but soon, it will have a 1 minutegrace period
