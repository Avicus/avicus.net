Missing Your Rank? by keenanjt June 26, 2014 at 9:06 PM UTC

Due to the changes in enforcement of Mojang's EULA, as an operating Minecraft server we must provide purchase history for each user. In order to do this I wrote a script that runs through all the purchases and logs information regarding each one. If you had purchased a rank on MC Zone and upgraded on Avicus to a higher rank your rank may be missing as the script does not know of any purchases prior to Avicus. Please contact support@avicus.net with evidence of your purchases if you are missing your rank. There are other situations where you may be missing a rank as well, so don't hesitate to contact support if you are unsure.

The purchase history page will be added in the next website update.
