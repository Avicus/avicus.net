Small Rules/Guideline Change 06/02/17 by PieZ February 6, 2017 at 11:02 PM UTC

Hello Avicus.

Just a little heads up about punishment guidelines since they do affect the community. The senior staff looked into a few rules that came into the question in the last month and has decided to change the how the moderators will be punishing those who break these certain rules.

Racism and Homphobic slurs will be counted as the same type of punishment. This means racism will have less of a harsh punishment for first timers and homophobic slurs will have a harsher punishment. 
Old:
Racism-14d temp ban--> perm
New:
Racism/Homophobic Slurs- 5d tempban --> 7d tempban --> perm

The term "Nigga" has left the classification of a racial term and is now classified as a swear word. The same rules apply for swear words and obvious use of the term to try to get around the racism rule will result in a racism punishment. 

Have a good day.

Rules take place starting in a week.
