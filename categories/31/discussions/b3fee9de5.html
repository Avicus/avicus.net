[Evolve] UHC Match #30 by Cyclops August 5, 2015 at 2:08 PM UTC

Welcome to the details/info page on Match #30 (CutClean/Moles).

Match will be held on August 5th, 1:30pm Eastern Standard Time.

Whitelist will be turned off 30 minutes prior to start.

Cut Clean: There are no furnaces needed in this game. When you mine iron, an iron ingot drops, same applies for gold. Plus, the flint rates are turned up to 75%! Pigs, cows, and chickens drop 3 of their cooked meat (chickens also drop 2 feathers 100% of the time as cows drop 1 leather 100% of the time).

Moles: Teams will be randomised, but on the first night one person on each team will become the Mole. Their mission from that point on is to neutralize their own team, through whatever means they see fit. There will be six kits for them to choose from upon becoming the Mole to help them with this. Moles on different teams can see who each mole is, as well as communicating with them and broadcasting their location to all moles. The moles win if there are no non-mole players left alive. 

Server Info: https://avicus.net/forums/6aaf332fa
