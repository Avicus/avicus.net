lawrencethai July 16, 2014 at 8:07 AM UTC

Okay, 
First in Nebula, some maps have useful things like gravel for flint and also cobwebs for string. Use these to make flint and steel or fishing rods.
Strafing is your best friend and bows.

Try using your bow more often and use it when you have a good distance apart so around about 6 blocks. When you shoot with bows don't bow spam.. Just do double-charge, it will do a good amount of damage and also good amount of speed. When shooting at long range, always fully charge your bow to get maximum aim and power. 

Next is strafing. There is mainly 2 types of it: circle strafing or zig zag. Circle strafing is what most people do but zig zagging it more effective where you move left and right to dodge the enemy's attacks. 

Tips to help: Change your FOV (Field of Vision) to Quake Pro may help because you can see further around you.
Change your mouse sensitivity to about when you move your mouse left to right, it will do a 90-180 degrees turn. So it will be much easier to aim. 

Thanks for for reading this! Hope it helps 
Lawrence
