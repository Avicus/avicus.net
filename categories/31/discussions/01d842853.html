kycrafft July 2, 2015 at 3:07 PM UTC

Here are my ideas if we ever host a tournament for some of the smaller gamemodes. These are just rough outlines of what I think would work. 

SG: 

  • FFA
  • Matches of 12 players ("group" stages)
  • Points system: 1 point for a kill, 3 for a win, 1 point for top 5
  • The 12 players play 3 consecutive (or not) games (the highest scoring players ex: 4-6 move on to the next stages)

Walls:

  • 2 Teams in each match
  • 8-10 players on each team
  • each team will be split up between two quads
  • Friendly fire between different quad colors is on
  • Certain kits are banned (Not saying these kits should be banned, but for example: Fisherman, Alchemist, Snowman)

CTH:
  • 4 teams in each match 
  • 5-7 players on each team
  • Max score: 750
  • The same 4 teams will play 3 consecutive matches
  • The teams with the highest total points win (ties go to the team with the highest wins or a tiebreaker match with a score limit of 250
  • Certain kits are banned (not saying these kits should be banned, but for example: Demo, heavy, sniper, Yeti)
  • Top 2 teams move on (this is for the group stages)
