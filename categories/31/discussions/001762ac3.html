Welcome to the details/info page on Match #25 & #26 (#25: Mystery Teams/Potential Hearts) (#26: Big Teams of 10/Moles).

Match #25 will be held tomorrow, July 23nd, 12pm Eastern Standard Time.
Match #26 will be held right after match #25

Whitelist will be turned off 30 minutes prior to match #25 start.

Mystery Teams: Everyone is given a random coloured wool, the symbol of your team. If you run into someone, you can show the wool in your hand, or place it. If it is not the same colour wool, it means you are not in a team with that person. You will not be teleported to your teammates. If you run into someone that does not have the same colour wool as you, you may attack, or run. You also have a choice of showing your wool, or just killing the person straight on out, with the risk of the person you kill being your teammate. You may not use wool from sheep, temples, or crafted from string to dye, and trick people into thinking you are on the team that you are not in, however, you can use the wool you get from kills to trick people. If you die, you must say what your wool colour was, so that the people that were in your team know that they lost a teammate. This way people can also keep track on how many of each team is left over, although not knowing who.

Potential Hearts: All players will start with the normal amount of hearts (10), but be able to exceed this by healing up, usually to a maximum of 15 or 20 hearts.

Moles: On the first night one person on each team will become the Mole. Their mission from that point on is to neutralize their own team, through whatever means they see fit. There will be six kits for them to choose from upon becoming the Mole to help them with this. Moles on different teams can see who each mole is, as well as communicating with them and broadcasting their location to all moles. The moles win if there are no non-mole players left alive.
