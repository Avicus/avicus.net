omg, this is really hard to believe, but one of the freakiest, creepiest moments of my life was when i was in the sixth grade, and it was summer. 2 of my friends came to sleep over at my house, and we decided to sleep outside on the trampoline, to make the sleepover funner and unique.
Anyway, being like 12 or something, we wanted to stay up all night and have a great time, etc. So we tried. We horsed around for a while, we had a charged up laptop, lollies, phones, etc. and it was all good until about 2AM-ish. By then, we were beginning to get real tired, and it was fairly cold for a summer night, because air was getting through the little tiny holes in the trampoline pad thingy, so we were just chatting and telling stories, etc. But we were literally in the middle of talking when a seriously loud, weird noise filled the air. Looking back, it was probably not that loud, but it seemed it because everything was silent at 2AM until then. So anyway, it was this loud building up whooshing noise, as is someone was saying "shhhh" but it was getting increasingly louder, and after about 3 seconds of hearing it, it made this weird electronically noise, as if something was blasting off, but it had this sort of laser-like tone to it. its hard to explain. So all three of us were DEAD silent and still and we watched toward the noise and saw a triangular aircraft hovering about as high as a plane above us. It was a lot wider then a plane and has three blue lights one each corner. I remember the whole scenario. The scary part was that it continued this weird hovering noise RIGHT ABOVE US. It could have been miles away, but it was moving along the sky directly above us. it rotated and moved forward at the same time too, so it was spinning and going forward away from us. We were staring, stiller then ice and we just watched it fly off. It was so weird. technically we witnessed a UFO in the middle of the night, and god knows what the hell was operating it. And if it somehow saw us move, then that would have been frightening as hell. Thats probably the freakiest thing ever to happen in my life. Yes its long, but oh well
