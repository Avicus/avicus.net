Monthly update #1 by badgg January 30, 2015 at 6:01 AM UTC

Hello community, it's Fluenza here!
Over the past few weeks, Jake and I have been discussing a LOT of topics in which could potentially move Avicus forward into a more enjoyable, safe and friendly community!
One of our topics were monthly updates, informing you guys what is planned, currently in the process of being enforced and confirming information!

AuraFusion
====================================
These last few weeks have been great. I stayed up almost all night of every night discussing with Jozz about how we could improve, things we should implement, community ideas, etc. And everything seems to really be coming together. I'm feeling a lot more comfortable with the role as an Administrator now, it almost feels natural for me to do what I do.
List of things I've accomplished personally as an Administrator over these last few weeks:
1. I am now used to being an Administrator.
2. Able to attend most interview sessions/individual interviews, meetings, etc.
3. Activity on TeamSpeak has skyrocketed since my promotion took place
4. Community Party was a success (Lot of positive feedback)
5. Definitely planning on hosting more Community Events in the future (Tournaments, UHC's, etc.)
6. I've come up with a lot of ideas aside from all of this, that I will bring up in the next Admin meeting.
7. The amount of hard word Joel and I have put in to our multiple-hour-at-a-time discussions has really paid off, we've come up with more ideas I ever would have thought imaginable.


In the future, (whether that be in the near future, or a ways from now) I'm planning on hosting some sort of tourney, or PvP related competition so our Community can duke it out in a real deal situation. The winners will be awarded (not sure what yet) But that's just scratching the surface.. I still have a lot on my mind.


Fluenza_
====================================
Staff recruitment system As you know, applications are closed and we're finalising interviews and Jr Mods. I'm not here to talk about that though,as I am going to announce that hand picked staff system will take effect in mid-march. We're only trialing it, so don't worry. If this system doesn't work we'll revert back to applications.What essentially is going to happen, is we'll open applications every 4 months and whilst they're closed, hand picked staff will be enforced. This ensures constant flow of staff however I will make a seperate thread detailing this topic, as it'll need to be focused around community assistance. (answering questions and what-not)

Changes to the senior staff team So right now, we're lacking active sr mods. We're currently struggling on making every interview work and we do apologise for that. To cater for this, it is planned that there will NO longer be a Staff Administrator role, but instead the Senior Moderators would take on that role. Us Administrators would be focused on community and community only.It puts a LOT of stress on me since I have to deal with complaints 24/7 and everyone relies on one person. This way, it relies on the whole sr staff team.This is yet to be confirmed, and is awaiting discussion in the upcoming senior staff meeting. 
That being said, we're already scouting for possible candidates for sr mod. We plan to do things differently, but that has merely been discussed. Expect some Mod > Sr mod promotions within the next month or so.

Community suggestions It has come to my attention that there have been a LOT of community suggestions on the Forums which have been ignored by the development team & administration team. This is why I have decided to list all of the suggestion threads onto a spreadsheet so Ivy can read through it all. Whether this will be public viewing is still up for debate. This is not formal, so don't expect a comment on the thread stated the suggestions status. A thread might only have 4 views, but it could potentially be on the spreadsheet.

Comment from the Staff Admin No one is perfect, and I have to admit that I haven't been performing the best over the past few weeks. Over the course of Christmas in 2014, I enforced stricter rules  for staff because quite frankly I have had enough of people pushing my buttons. I have been telling the staff (since I became Admin) calmly as possible and they didn't seem to listen to me... That was the last time I will warn them calmly. I don't hesitate to demote, as long as there is proof. Therefore I sign off my message with a reminder; please gather proof whenever you see a staff member doing something wrong!



This will be a monthly thread... updating you guys on what's been happening behind the scenes and what has been done with particular issues.
