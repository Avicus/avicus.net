Hey,
So, recently Zeus had been having lots of thoughts regarding the server. School will be starting up soon and this is an important year for him. In addition, he's spent pretty much 2-3 years on here, and has been staff for almost 2 of them. Through thick and thin, he's tried to stay close to the server. However, recently he has felt no motivation to fix anything on the server, and his time is now up. Additionally, he has some things he needs to get done other than school in his life and not having the burden of being Administrator will be a start. No, he is not leaving. He'll still be pretty active, just not as staff. It’s really been a pleasure working with everyone for such a long time.
