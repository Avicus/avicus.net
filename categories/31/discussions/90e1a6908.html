Questions about making a map! by Rivzz July 12, 2014 at 2:07 AM UTC

Hello! Recently I have been playing on Avicus and love the community and all the great maps! However I would like to contribute a map and have a few questions. I have looked around already on the forums and tried to find the answers to these but I was unsuccessful.

Question #1: Are you aloud to use Terraforming?

Question #2: What themes seem to be the most popular?

Question #3: How big should the map generally be? (So it isn't too big or too small)

Question #4: Should it be created on a superflat map, or in a void setting map?

Question #5: Could you have a test server with the map for staff to check it before? That way you guys don't have to download it and then say, "Wow this is terrible." Thanks so much guys! Bye Bye for now!

ViceTechnicolour July 12, 2014 at 2:07 AM UTC

Question #1: Are you aloud to use Terraforming? Yes.

Question #2: What themes seem to be the most popular? You be creative and choose what themes you want in your maps. There is no theme that I'd really say is the most popular, however there seem to be a lot of gray maps.

Question #3: How big should the map generally be? (So it isn't too big or too small) Generally don't have maps as big as The Division 3's old edition, however don't have it the size of Senex.

Question #4: Should it be created on a superflat map, or in a void setting map? Void setting map.

Question #5: Could you have a test server with the map for staff to check it before? That way you guys don't have to download it and then say, "Wow this is terrible." We don't say that! Every map has a chance and if you want a build server, contact me on skype and i'll arrange stuff. We have 2 dev servers to test stuff on.
