DTO - New Gamemode? by Lakitue July 16, 2015 at 9:07 PM UTC

Hello people of avicus!

Me & Javi (The new developer) have recently come up with a new idea for a gamemode. It is called Destroy the Orb!
Basically instead of having monuments, there is orbs. Orbs are pretty smart. When you destroy them, you will receive points (Different orbs gives different amount of points). After a certain amount of time, the orbs will respawn and the team will be able to destroy them again. When the team have come up to a certain amount of points, they win.
So it's pretty simple, but I think it will be good. But me and Javi couldn't agree on one thing.

Should it be "Attackers VS defenders"? Where the attackers need to get a certain amount of points before the time runs out!
or
Should it be "Team VS Team"? Where both teams have orbs, and the first team to reach the amount of points win!

Remember, this gamemode is not confirmed. It is just a small little thing that Javi might be working on and in the end, it will hopefully be released :D
