Mango_ August 8, 2014 at 10:08 AM UTC

Anyone can make a map, you don't need permission or anything. Just generate a void world and build your map! Many people like to do this on a personal server, but you can do it in single player too.

Once you've completed your map you'll need to upload the world file to a file sharing website such as Dropbox or Media Fire. Then you can create a new thread in the 'Map Releases' sub-forum with the name of your map, a link to the world download, a YAML file (refer to this page) and preferably some images too.

Good luck with your idea! :)
