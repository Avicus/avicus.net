My opinion on the "low" amount of players. by SnowSX3 October 24, 2014 at 4:10 AM UTC

People worry too much about the amount of players online these days.

We're constantly coming up with new ideas on how to get more people to play on Avicus but will Avicus truly be better once 1500+ players are constantly on our servers?

If Avicus reaches over 1000 regulars with 200 people always on the forums will Avicus truly be a better place?

If we have 300+ moderators will Avicus truly be a better place?

If every server on Avicus is full will Avicus be a better place?

If Avicus becomes the most popular Minecraft server ever will Avicus be a better place?

I understand the benefits of having more players join Avicus, I really do.

But what's the point of having hundreds and hundreds of people on if the community is watered down and saturated?

It's about Quality over Quantity. 
It's about the Community, not the Numbers.

Some people seem to forget that.

Edit: I'm not saying that low numbers are beneficial to the server, I'm saying that people are too fussed over the number of players and not the existing community.
We need to focus on the community, not the amount in our community.
