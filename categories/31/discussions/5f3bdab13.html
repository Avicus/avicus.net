Rearrangements in the Mapdev Team by ViceTechnicolour February 19, 2015 at 11:02 PM UTC

Hello,

Today marks a day where a few things have happened, namely 3; it comes with promotions and a retirement. As early as today, I got a message on steam by Gray (though most of you know him as Astronis) about his position as a moderator and as a map developer. He is taking leave from both moderator and head map developer ranks. This means that he will remain as a mapdev, but the position as head map developer would transfer. He has entrusted me (Tanpopo/Para) to run the map development team, so as so I will try to steer maps in my own way. It's unfortunate that he's going from those, but he is still going to be around; maybe a bit less, however. I want to say that he is a very passionate person and one of the best people that I've ever connected myself with, and this "leave" is for a very good reason. Ily gray. <3

As well as this (though a thread may be posted soon by him himself), MonsieurStar/FlufflesSub has decided to retire the team. He will no longer be a map developer, but he is part of the family when he got in. As I said in steam, he's in the family but retiring is seen as moving away. He's still with us and I hope that all of us continue to respect him and treat him with the best wishes for what he does in the future.

So, as a recap; Astronis has retired as a moderator and head mapdev, I am now the charge of head map dev, and Star has decided to leave. 

Post any further questions or grievance below.
