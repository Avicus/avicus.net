Behind the name "Avicus" by aagrant99 January 24, 2017 at 11:01 PM UTC

A non-common first name.  People with this first name are associated with having these traits: having creative ability, imagination along practical lines,  and very observant over little details.  These people are attracted to technical, scientific, and mechanical fields but often do not pursue this interest as it takes time that they do not wish to use.  You have a pure heart, good humor, charismatic, and make friends with fun people.  You are charitable, but also are not afraid to spend lavishly on yourself.  You often can suffer health problems due to eating unhealthily with the lavish lifestyle.  You are often very energetic.  Source: https://www.kabalarians.com/Male/avicus.htm
