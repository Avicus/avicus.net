keenanjt September 29, 2016 at 3:09 AM UTC

There are many threads where you can provide constructive feedback if you would like! I'll paraphrase what admins and developers have stated already in various places:

We tested in alpha publicly - you or others in this thread may have been there - and we encountered a host of bugs which were fixed promptly. Once Atlas was in public beta and live on our main IP, we faced issues which could not have been reliably predicted. The biggest issue, our tablist, only became an issue during large, active matches. That was fixed after a day and the server was free of any major, game-changing issues. Though, the bugs we had were definitely enough to turn many players away, and I get that. We were indeed pressured to release quickly by the community and by ourselves - it had been in production for months and we had no reported major issues.

Feature-wise, we are mostly back to where Avicus was previously, with a few missing things here and there, though with many additions to the server as well.
