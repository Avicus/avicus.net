Congratulations to the new admins! by Mango_ October 31, 2015 at 10:10 PM UTC

This will be my last post as an administrator, and I would take this opportunity to announce the newly promoted administrators. Most of you would know by now that Jozz and I are retiring as of today, and we have chosen two senior mods to take on the role of admin.

These two people have shown that can meet and exceed the responsibilities of a senior mod, and both have expressed their dedication towards helping Avicus grow. So after a fair bit of discussion between Jozz, Kylo and I, we have made a decision...

Congratulations to Pelpelajax and MidnightBeats!

My ranks will be revoked and the new admins will receive their ranks. From now on all admin related questions should be directed to either Pelpelajax or MidnightBeats. Please congratulate them on their promotions! Once again, thanks for this opportunity, and good luck to everyone here.
