Thank You by MidnightBeats February 14, 2016 at 6:02 AM UTC

I've decided to retire from the Administration team and the Staff Team as a whole. There's a couple of reasons as to why, but the main ones were that I had become too busy and that I didn't feel as though I deserved the rank any longer. I had been slacking quite a bit and hadn't been doing much administrative work, and my obligations outside of Avicus began to grow. Recently, my computer broke down, which further limited my ability to do Administrative work, and at this point I decided to hang up the coat. 

I've had an amazing journey with lots of great people here on this server, and many of the experiences I've had has really changed my perception on certain things in life. I'm truly glad that I was able to experience Avicus, and in appropriate fashion, I would like to thank all those who made my experience here memorable.
