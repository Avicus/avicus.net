Recent Results of the Sr. Staff Meeting! by hpux March 22, 2015 at 6:03 AM UTC

Hello everyone,

Recently, the Senior Staff have completed an important meeting regarding upcoming events on the server. It has come to our attention for a long time now that we urgently need to involve the community in decisions made, as well as notify them when we finalize anything. We discussed ways of picking staff, community events, as well as new commands and broadcasts. I will list the verdicts on each of the topics listed above, as well as a few other points. Feel free to post your opinion below!

Topics:
Staff picking: We have come to the conclusion that it is best to hold off on handpicked staff for a little while because we had many rebuttals as to why we should not use it for now. This included the lack of an efficient way to remove bias from the system of handpicking. However, we have decided to change the format, system, and leniency in the application system. We have decided to open staff applications in approximately 4 weeks. If you really like the idea of handpicked staff, don't worry! It is quite likely we will have it implemented in the future. We have also scheduled a staff meeting for next weekend, which will contain more details on this information, so stay tuned on the forums all week!

Community Events: Thought the UHC was cancelled? Nope! It's still happening! It should occur shortly after the update (now called the "Omega" update) takes place. By popular demand, we have decided to have the first one be slave market. If all goes well, we plan to have a monthly Official Avicus UHC, possibly even bimonthly. It will be run and hosted by the Administration team, the Event Committee, as well as Cyclops and Straightened. In addition the the UHC, we are also planning to have a Plug DJ giveaway, along with many other small events leading up to the update!

The Omega Update - Beta Testing: You can probably see where I'm going with this before I even begin. This has been on my mind for many weeks now. We will host another small beta testing session for the staff tomorrow night, or the night after. If all goes well there, we will host a public beta testing session for the update! As this gets closer, I will release more information on it. At the moment, stay patient! :)

Notes:

There are one or two more topics we have discussed which I will not release until after the staff meeting next week. This is not for confidentiality from the community, but rather the efficiency of the event. You will probably see another one of these posts by next weekend, or maybe sooner. Have a great day/night!

That's all I have to say! What do you have to say? (:

Best regards,

- Peachy. c:
