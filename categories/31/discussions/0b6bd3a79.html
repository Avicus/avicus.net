Alrighty, so I'll make this clean and simple then. 

Goodbye and thankyou. 

Working at the avicus network has been one hell of an experience in itself and has taught me many lessons about leading a team of people, managing a community of people from around the globe, but more importantly has taught me more about myself then most girls or guys would ever experience, at my age anyways. 

I've decided not to give shout outs to people who particularly have influenced and changed me as a person as for one, there will likely be way too many, second, many will be left out, and third, as of late many of the relationships established since I worked as director of staff have since diminished.

Feel free to ask any questions below if you're into that sort of thing, I will be both very truthful and unfeigned about each of my answers, something which definitely hasn't been shared among most of the current staff team. I also reach out to those new staff members who look for guidance or advice, I am more than welcome to accommodate. 

So, er, yeah, its been great, but as likely said by some old guy hundreds of years ago, 'Good things always come to an end.'
