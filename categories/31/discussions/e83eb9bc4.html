keenanjt April 18, 2015 at 12:04 AM UTC

Went with this method for a number of reasons:
  • Fair and balanced method to get access to a private server. If you're in a tournament or not, you can still rent them out.
  • It's centralized. I have a feeling teams will be able to organize because all the info they need is on one page and every player is notified through alerts.
  • Maximum efficiency. Teams will only utilize the server for the time that they need. We will be using less server resources by having them occupied by teams when they are needed.
