Prestige Information by Numberz_ August 15, 2014 at 4:08 AM UTC

In each of Avicus's gamemodes you can gain prestige!
What is prestige? Prestige is a star you get next to your name you can get up to 15 different stars!
How do I get prestige? To get prestige you need to reach level 50 in any gamemode then type /prestige
Why should I prestige? Other than it being a goal it gives you a lot of swag. :3

Here is the amount of kills, wins or monuments needed to prestige.

Nebula - Depending on the maps some of the xp gained can vary team death matches (TDM) are 1 Xp per kill normally (Non Donator). In regular Nebula matches destroy the monument each kill is worth 2 Xp (Non Donator).
Prestige in Nebula only killing (Non Donator) [Each kill is 2 Xp] 6100 kills
Prestige in Nebula only killing(Gold Rank 1.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 3 Xp] 4066 kills
Prestige in Nebula only killing (Emerald Rank 2x Xp boost) [Each kill is 4 Xp] 3050 kills
Prestige in Nebula only killing (Diamond Rank 2.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 5 Xp] 2440 kills

Control The Hill - CTH is one of the hardest gamemodes to prestige in because you only get 1 Xp per kills and 2 Xp per controling the hill (Non Donator)
Prestige in CTH only killing (Non Donator) [Each kill is 1 Xp] 12199 kills
Prestige in CTH only killing (Gold Rank 1.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 2 Xp] 6100 kills
Prestige in CTH only killing (Emerald Rank 2x Xp boost) [Each kill is 2 Xp] 6100 kills
Prestige in CTH only killing (Diamond Rank 2.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 3 Xp] 4880 kills

Xp from hill controls are: 2Xp

Walls - In Walls kills are worth a lot more because you don't get a lot of kills per game.
Prestige in Walls only killing (Non Donator) [Each kill is 4 Xp] 3050 kills
Prestige in Walls only killing (Gold Rank 1.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 6 Xp] 2033 kills
Prestige in Walls only killing (Emerald Rank 2x Xp boost) [Each kill is 8 Xp] 1525 kills
Prestige in Walls Only killing (Diamond Rank 2.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 10 Xp] 1220 kills

Walls wins are: 5Xp
Elimination - Similar to Walls you with the amount of Xp per kills but this game can have more players than Walls.
Prestige in Elimination only killing (Non Donator) [Each kill is 4 Xp] 3050 kills
Prestige in Elimination only killing (Gold Rank 1.5x Xp boost)[Each kill is 6 Xp] 2033 kills
Prestige in Elimination only killing (Emerald Rank 2x Xp boost) [Each kill is 8 Xp] 1525 kills
Prestige in Elimination Only killing (Diamond Rank 2.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 10 Xp]1220 kills

Elim wins are: 5Xp
Nexus - In Nexus you can gain prestige easier by getting kills streaks and winning!
Prestige in Nexus only killing (Non Donator) [Each kill is 2 Xp] 6100 kills
Prestige in Nexus only killing (Gold Rank 1.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 3 Xp] 4066 kills
Prestige in Nexus only killing (Emerald Rank 2x Xp boost) [Each kill is 4 Xp] 3050 kills
Prestige in Nexus only killing (Diamond Rank 2.5x Xp boost) [Each kill is 5 Xp] 2440 kills

Wins are: 5Xp
Killstreaks: 5Xp every ten kills
Level 50 in every gamemode is 12199 Xp
