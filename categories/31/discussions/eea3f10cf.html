Avicus Moderator Survey by Joao_Victor July 8, 2014 at 6:07 AM UTC

Hello Avicus.

With the new Junior Moderators coming in, I am hosting a survey for the community to post their honest, anonymous feedback on our staff team.

We did a survey like this a while ago, and it worked out quite well.

Feedback helps us improve very much, so I would love to hear your opinions on our current moderation team.

General Rules:
  • Don't submit troll/joke feedback, be serious.
  • While this is anonymous, I encourage constructive criticism, try not to be excessively rude/disrespectful in your feedback.
  • This survey is meant for the moderation team only, which is Junior Mod to Senior Mod, only moderator feedback.
I will most likely bring this feedback up in a staff meeting, or to the mods mentioned, however, troll feedback and excessively rude feedback WILL be removed.

I look forward to hearing your feedback, and seeing the way our team can improve.

This survey is completely anonymous, your IP address or username are not collected in any way.
Survey Link: https://avicoresurvey.typeform.com/to/nvfxTT
