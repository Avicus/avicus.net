Avicus Staff Spotlight - Jiveh by gobernment December 17, 2015 at 5:12 PM UTC

Stealing the spotlight this time is Jiveh, one of our moderators (and officials AND mapdevs AND silver medalists!) here on the server. Feel free to post questions and discuss answers here! You're more than welcome to also show Avicus Pride and give him some support :)


What is your username in Minecraft and what is your favorite game on Avicus?

My username is Jiveh and my favourite gamemode on Avicus is CTW.

Introduce yourself in 3-4 sentences.

Hello everyone! My name is Adam (you can call me Jive). I'm 13 years old and I live in Sweden. I've been playing Avicus/McZone for about 2 years now. I usually play football and floorball in my spare time. My favourite football team is Barcelona and my favourite player is Messi

What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?

Since I really like football, I'm excited for tournaments coming up in the summer. I'm also going to France this summer and maybe Barcelona to watch them play. Also, I can't wait for christmas; I'm so excited, haha.

What interests you the most in life?

As I said before, I love football and being with friends, and of course food, sleep and music.

What music/art/media do you like?

I really like  hip-hop and punk. But I prefer to talk with people instead of listening to music, atleast when I'm playing minecraft. When it comes to TV shows, I really enjoy drama, comedy and sci-fi.

Where are you from?

I currently live in Ängelholm, Sweden, in the same city as Robin and he goes to the same school as me.

What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating?

"How do I use crates?" "Can you look at my report?"

Anything else you want to mention?

I really enjoy talking to people on teamspeak and moderating. I really like this community and that's problably why I'm still here. If you have any questions, feel free to ask me ingame or add me on skype; adamjive1. Have a nice day! :P
