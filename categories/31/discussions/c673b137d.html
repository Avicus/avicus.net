║Map Testing Event║» Friday 18th by Ogrey December 12, 2015 at 4:12 PM UTC

Hello Avicus.

The Map Development team will be holding a Map Testing event this upcoming Friday 18th at 6PM EST.
We will be testing old maps that were previously removed due to their lack of gameplay. However, there will be a pretty neat and absolutely beautiful new system that will aid us in determining whether the maps that rotate in our servers are good or not, according to the community, without having to spam the forums with reassessment posts or community removals. No I will not talk about the system.

The map pool isn't confirmed and is subject to changes. Some maps were edited and others weren't, so don't expect all of them to be the same thing they used to be before they were removed.

We hope you can attend and have as much fun as you can afford to have.
