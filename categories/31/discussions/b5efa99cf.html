Map Development | Rotations (Update) by Ogrey May 18, 2015 at 4:05 AM UTC

Hey guys.
As you know, I'm the Head Map Developer of the Maps area, so I've switched some things around to what concerns rotations.
Survival Games, Nebula (DTM/TDM), Nexus, Control the Hill and The Walls have all suffered changes on their rotation files (specially Survival Games!).

You may or may not have noticed that the player count is quite low in Survival Games, being only active a few times every one or two days. Well, to hopefully motivate players to play more on the SG servers, we've expanded the amount of maps you can play on.
