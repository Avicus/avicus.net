Greetings & Gibberish
Well this is my first game, it's probably not original but idk anyone else who did this (if you do please say so and i'll credit them).  Feedback and improvements are appreciated. Thanks for participation!

How to Play
  • The 1st poster begins a chain with some lyrics to a song. These can range from 1-5 lines (you can take these lyrics from any point in the song not just the beginning). 
  • The 2nd poster continues the lyrics or writes "stumped" if they dont know the song or the lyrics that come afterwards
  • After 2 people are "stumped" a third poster can start a new chain if he/she is also "stumped". 
  • A chain also ends when the lyrics finish, please indicate that the song is finished at the end of your post if you do finish the song off.
  • Try and keep to generally mainstream songs that people will know.
