Emmaye February 13, 2015 at 1:02 PM UTC

But the rules will still be the same?
Handpicking is recommending players for staff, coming from Moderators and above. Staff applications have more of a "rules" section. So in March, you really just follow the server rules and anything else the staff team notices that you are doing good with, like behavior and attitude. Overall, the eligibility for players are the same as in applications, except the process is different. So the rules would technically be the same, for eligibility, just the end result would be a little different (doesn't affect the requirements that much).
