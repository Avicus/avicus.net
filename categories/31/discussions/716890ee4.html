Update regarding inappropriate language by Mango_ April 24, 2015 at 10:04 AM UTC

As you may or may not be aware, the rules have recently been slightly altered. The reason I'm making this thread is to clear up any confusion about the use of inappropriate and offensive language in-game.

The first rule on https://avicus.net/rules under 'General Etiquette and Behavior' now states:

  1. Maintain a casual and positive atmosphere at all times. Inappropriate or aggressive language, regardless if it is directed at someone or not, will not be tolerated. Be respectful of all players. Harassment, bullying, rudeness and excessive swearing is strictly not allowed.

This primarily speaks for itself. In the past, players have gotten away with swearing non-stop simply because they weren't directing their remarks at any particular player. The updated rule explains that this is no longer a factor, and players who continuously swear will be warned or punished. Avicus has a community that vastly varies in age, and we want to make it a positive experience for everyone. Staff will still be somewhat lenient however don't expect to get away with it if you're being too inappropriate.

If you have any questions about this topic feel free to post below.
