Just a little tip for those of you applying...
If anyone wants to become a moderator, do not do it for the sake of kicking and banning players. Do it for the good of the community. Make your role have an importance, and make sure that you're not applying for the wrong reasons. Also, do not try to become a moderator if it affects anything in your life outside Minecraft. Also, do not let it get between you and your social life.
If you are spending way too much time on Minecraft / Moderating on Avicus, make sure that you have enough time to spend with your family and friends.
Just make sure that you know how everything will work out in your real life before applying for this position.
