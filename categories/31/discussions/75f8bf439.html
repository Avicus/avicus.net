Today's / Tomorrows challenge is called:  Ninja..

This is a basic challenge, Rules:


  • You have to drop ALL blocks in your inventory, and you CANNOT mine any block, Basically blockless.
  • You have to break the monument OUT IN THE OPEN!, Meaning the other team must have a decent defense
  • You may NOT cover yourself while breaking the monument! 
  • You HAVE to use a Iron pickaxe, or if you dont have iron use Stone / Wood!
  • [Hardest one] You may not get attacked while getting to the Monument! However there is an excuse whilst mining the monument you MAY get attacked!
  • For proof: Record it, And upload it! (If you do not have a youtube channel, post the video, on a SAFE website, so we can view that you have completed the challenge!) (Or lie and brag that you did :P)
Enjoy!
