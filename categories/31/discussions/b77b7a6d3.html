The new Voting System! by legosean10 June 4, 2014 at 8:06 AM UTC

I just thought I'd publicise it on the forums as a lot of people don't know about this.

Recently, Avicus just released it's new voting system! You simply vote for the server each 24 hours, and each time you vote you can redeem 28XP levels for the minigame of your choice. To redeem it, simply hop onto the server after voting (it can take some time for your vote to register, so be patient!) and you should have an emerald in your hand. Right click it, and a submenu will come up, just click on the item that represents the minigame of your choice and you will automatically recieve the 28XP levels!

Here is the link to vote: http://avicus.net/vote
Hope this helped a bit for some people!
