Gladiator:  

Players spawn in pits full of mobs and other players. They can earn points for 2 minutes.  Killing a mob is 1 point, players are two points. 

After 2 minutes the player with the least points is eliminated. If there is a tie all the bottom players are eliminated. 

This repeats. It goes on untill there are 4 players left. They will be set in a death match in the pit with no mobs. If there is a tie getting to the 4 player death match , any lowest players are eliminated bringing it down to 3,2,1 players in the death match.

OPTOBAT November 21, 2014 at 9:11 AM UTC

Quite a long time ago i actually thought of a mini game that i thought sounded basic, yet fun only i never really got to explain it, so i might as well now:

I think it would be cool if there was a game where you are on a rotation of mini maps, almost like large room-sized maps and you had a full inventory of Bottle O' Enchanting bottles, and when the game begins you have to run around and throw the bottle o's at each other.

Aspects of the game:

. Once you reach level 10 or something (just an example) you are eliminated
. Your own bottles can NOT effect you
. basically you have to throw exp at each other and fill up their levels until they get to the required level to be eliminated.
.Last man standing wins.

I thought this idea could work so please tell me what you think :)
