The Problem with CTW atm. by Tazz September 27, 2015 at 2:09 PM UTC

The next tournament is confirmed to be CTW, however the gamemode has two major bugs which need to be fixed. Those two problems are safety's and touches coming up in global chat.

  • First of all, the fact that safeties are useless is stupid. Safeties should be a big part of the meta because it is literally impossible to have one person get back with the wool when he can not drop/ place a wool to his teammates. If he dies, it's all over and all they have is a touch.
  • Secondly, why can I see when the opposing team get in my teams wool room? Say I sneak into to the wool room, there's literally no chance of me getting back sneakily because they know I'm there or they're blind. It should display that a wool has been touched to the team offending that wool room and the observers. It should also come up in chat every time someone touches a wool and not just the first time that wool is touched.
These changes would help the gamemode be more enjoyable and more possible to win. At this current time and in this current meta, it is really difficult to return a wool - in the all star game yesterday I had to hide in a 1x2 hole with at least 5 people hunting me to wait for my team to distract the hunters and PVP them while Danikas made an underbridge for me to return on. However, that team was randomised. It was not a team that knew eachother very well or that were experienced together. In experienced teams, something like that would not be possible. These two things should not be difficult to implement and it would help the meta.

PLEASE implement these.
