Fouled January 20, 2015 at 1:01 AM UTC

1. No, monument stealing is not allowed. This is due to the fact as it's known as "team trolling" which in result would be considering as a punishment. Report the person if they ever monument steal you, or anybody else!

2. Team spleef is not allowed, as it's a rule. This also in result would be a punishment (such as a kick, tempban, etc). 

3. This is known as "accusing others of hacking". Calling somebody a hacker in chat, is useless and causes spam. If the person was really hacking, they would just turn it off immediately, so they wouldn't get banned.
This is not allowed and is a warn, kick, tempban, etc. 

If you see anybody doing this in-game, I advise you to report them (immediately).
