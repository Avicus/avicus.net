A Thank You to Former Staff by Crazy_ January 13, 2018 at 8:01 PM UTC

As the server is winding down and will be closing in a little less than 2 weeks, all of us in the staff team want to thank every single person who has contributed to the server in the past 5 and a half years. Avicus would not be the way it is today if it wasn’t for the incredible staff team its had (and currently has!). As a token of our gratitude, and as a way to look back, we’ve listed the retired group below.



Unfortunately due to certain policies or events, not every single person who was formerly staff is on that list. However, we don’t want their contributions to go unmentioned. Whoever you are, retired, demoted, or maybe you simply never got the rank, thank you for your time, and thank you for your efforts.



- The Avicus Global Team
