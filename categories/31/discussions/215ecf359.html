Introducing our new Spring Lobby! by Passively March 21, 2017 at 4:03 AM UTC

Hello everyone! After a few months with the Christmas Lobby, the Map Development team made an updated Lobby and we're proud to announce it!. The theme is Spring (wuuu!), some of you may saw some 'spoilers' about the new lobby on our public Discord. If not, we have added a lot of new things; one of them we added a new room with all the Staff inside of it.

New things that we added:
  • Better design
  • Staff Room + Staff Events winners room
  • Easter Eggs
  • Improved Parkour + removed the ugly and locked chest
  • Big Logo (avicus logo)



Easter Egg:
We have added 2 easter eggs to make this funny and see how you guys try to find the Easter Egg. The easter egg is pretty easy to find you need to pay attention to the little details inside the lobby. Including trees, parkour, etc...
I will be giving something special to the first one that find the easter and answering us the next question: "How did you find the Easter Egg?". If you find it, don't tell anyone or you will lose the special prize.

Good Luck finding the easter egg and we hope you like the new Lobby :)

~ regards, the Map Development team.
