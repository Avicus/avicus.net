Claustrophobia and Castle Adventure v2 by ViceTechnicolour May 28, 2014 at 12:05 AM UTC

(They were removed)

Claustrophobia is my map and I hope you respect that, and it was a favorite map of many because of it's unique style and people saying we needed more maps like that. Well, unfortunately, I have had to remove that due to several inconsistencies in the gameplay and aesthetics. The clay patterns were uneven, the entire map was off to the side by 1 block, there was a bunch of stone junk everywhere, the tunnels were too narrow to PvP in, and the map was either you love it or you hate it. I am making a v2, so expect to see that within the next week.

Castle Adventure 2, while I observed, was imho the worst playing map. There was a lot of kit mixing due to the chests, getting out of spawn was too much of a challenge, the map was too laggy because of the constant leaf decay and lighting updates, and half of the map was never used making it seem small + the gameplay was not up to par with Avicus Network quality. Just thought I'd inform you about it. Please don't complain about either map.
