A few Map Developer updates. by Atheyna April 6, 2015 at 6:04 PM UTC

Two Head Map Developers.
Recently, the Map Development team has been doing a lot of thinking, and we have decided upon having 2 different head map developers for the team. One will focus on the staff section of the team, and the other will focus on the map part and making sure things get done. After much voting, the team has decided to promote me to the 2nd head map developer position. Along with this, Evoclaws has decided to come back to his head map developer position, and Tanpiku will be stepping down.

Revisions Page
With the new Omega Update out, Kylo has added in a revisions page. This page will contain all the revisions made by map developers and developers to the website, rotations, and maps. This page is located here -- https://avicus.net/revisions/

Thanks, and have a wonderful day!
