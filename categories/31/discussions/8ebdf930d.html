The Avicus Cup (Tournament sug) by ClubberNugget April 20, 2015 at 12:04 AM UTC

I have seen this suggested in the forums before but i wanted to suggest my own version now that a tournament is being planned.

This is a suggestion for another tournament at a later date.

The Avicus Cup

A Competition of Avicus players trying to prove they can compete in any gamemode!

So basically it is a rotation of each gamemode, Nebula, CTW, SG, the walls, Nexus, CTH, HG.

When you are eliminated, you are allowed to spectate the rest of the tourney.

Does not require a team, or sign up, you just show up at the time it starts.

The rotations:

Destroy the Monument:
Teams selected randomly.
Losing team is eliminated.

Team Deathmatch:
Same as DTM

CTW:
Same as DTM and TDM

Nexus:
Lowest scoring team is eliminated*.
Winning team gets a bye, meaning they do not participate in the next rotation, but they pass.

The Walls:
Same as Nexus

Control the Hill:
Same as Nexus and Walls.

Hardcore Games:
Eliminate enough players in order to leave 24 players left** (Counting the bye team).
The Winner(s) gets a free set of leather armor for the

Survival Games:
Last man standing is the winner of the Tournament.

And there you have it, maps are randomly chosen for each rotation.  The winner is declared the Avicus Champ.

how do you guys feel about this?

*=If a tie for lowest score, both teams are not eliminated.
**=If more than 24 players are still alive by the time the HG is next, multiple games will be held, then have the last 24 survivors overall progress.  (Example: 32 players are left, two games holding 17 people each, the last 12 from each round pass, the first 5 to lose would be eliminated. The winner from both rounds earns the prize).  If less then 24 players by the time HG is next, then the half of the players pass, rounded down (Example: 15 players are left, the last 7 will pass.)
