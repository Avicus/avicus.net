From my experience as a multi-server map maker I have seen that not only gameplay and aesthetics are needed, but also some of the following things.

  • Size
  • Lag (as in stained glass and stuff like that)
  • Height
  • Land, void, and height area
  • Mobility (not that many decoration fences)
  • Theme relation (No random or weird appealing maps)
