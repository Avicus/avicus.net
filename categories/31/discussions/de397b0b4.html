like [name]plays or [name]YT or [name]MC cuz you never know that you may end up
doing other games or like maybe a Vlog Series of some sort, etc.)

[ Icon/Profile Pic, Banner, Thumbnail(s), Watermark (optional), Intro, Outro ]

I personally use Obs as a recording and live streaming software as it is free and small file sizes.

For editing windows movie maker is fine but if you want to more advanced edits buy Sony Vegas or Adobe premier pro.

intros are unessecary and can be a turn off to the person who wants to watch the video.(ie annoying loud and loud dubset, to long.)


outros are are good as it gives the user a call to action whether it's to watch another video or subscribe or like the video.

last but not least produce the highest quality videos every time you make one. If you upload daily but all your videos aren't great then there is no point in uploading daily. I use Fraps, but if you're just starting out you can try to use Camtasia Studio 8 (or 9, depending on which the latest version is), since it has a free 30 day trial and you can just renew your trial once it has expired.

Fraps is a paid recording software so I wouldn't suggest you getting it unless you become bigger on YouTube and know that you'll be making videos for a while.

If you really want fraps but do not want to pay for it (although it is pretty cheap), you can just try to find a cracked version or something online, but I suggest you don't do that as it may cause problems when you're trying to record.

Hope I helped!
