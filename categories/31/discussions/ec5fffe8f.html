POOL A- Wales, England (host nation), Australia, Fiji, Uruguay

POOL B- Scotland, South Africa, Samoa, Japan, U.S.A

POOL C- New Zealand, Tonga, Argentina, Georgia, Namibia

POOL D- Ireland, France, Italy, Canada, Romania
