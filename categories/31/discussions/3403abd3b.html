Ω Community Update Ω

By  Posighdun  

Recent Promotions / Demotions

There have been quite a bit of promotions and demotions / retirements  since our last official community update.
- Ciaan, PokerFace, GoodBlokeOscar, AbusiveUser, Acceptancq, _Distraction_ and TheTNTPotato has been promoted to Junior Moderator.
- Pelpelajax, Iron & Joltz has been promoted to Moderator..
- Sho, Nathy, Iron and _Crazy all retired from Moderator.
- Zeusy retired from Senior Moderator
- Rinn was promoted to Senior Moderator

Atlas News

As I'm sure most of you are aware, Atlas was released last week. At the start there were a bunch of bugs but our Developers got down to work and managed to squash most of them. Remember to keep reporting them at the issue tracker, here. I've been working close with Funkystudios and the other developers to improve the combat mechanics on the server. Hopefully most of you will see the difference in the hit detection, thank Funkystudios for that. The next few weeks will be all about optimising Atlas, then making scrim servers and then ranked servers :D I encourage all of you to give the developers a massive thank you for their dedication throughout this last week.

Posighdun's Address
Not much to say. The admin team has been hard at work doing various things here and there. Expect a few surprises within the next few weeks. With most of the bugs now squashed we're going to be focusing on making pvp as good as possible, so please bear with us as we test things. Apart from that, one more thing, is that our advertising campaign has kicked off. We're going to be doing all sorts, from Youtubers to ads, to try and advertise this wonderful network. I am very excited for these next few months, lots is planned and lots will happen, so stay tuned on our Social Media for updates, hints and whatnot.

One quick thing, for all those who say Atlas is worse than Avicore, and all that stuff, can we please stop and give our developers and Atlas a chance to prove you wrong? Every update since mczone, Avicus, Omega, Atlas, every single update we get people who say "It's a shit update, lets revert!". Now, expressing your opinion is fine, but please give Atlas a chance. Once we get it to 100% working order, it will be amazing. Already, for me at least, the pvp aspect is so much better and when someone says lets revert, I can't understand why people would prefer blocklag over decent hit detection and better kb :(. What I'm trying to say is, give Atlas a chance, we'll prove you wrong :P


Thank you to the developers for your hard work <3
