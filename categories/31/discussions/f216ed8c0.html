In Case You Don't Know - Helpful Tip by Myv July 26, 2015 at 5:07 PM UTC

Hi all, _Za here.

As you know, many people, including me, have chosen to change their IGN, sometimes multiple times.

Because of this, many people do not know the true names of Mods and other staff, and even their friends.

To solve this, just do /namehistory <player> 

It will throw up their entire name history, as well as when they changed it.

It will say for example:

iBiZa_MiNeCrAfT (original name)
FaZe_ (changed so and so)
_Za (changed so and so)

This should stop confusion.

_Za
