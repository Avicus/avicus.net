But just a few inquiries. How do clans work?
In the case that a tournament will appear, these clans, or teams, will be the ones who compete against each other, most likely for a prize.
Of course, a tournament hasn't appeared since McZone, but we're all awaiting anxiously. Just check to see if a team is looking for applicants, and apply for a team. They'll most likely add you to a skype group to communicate.
Clans practice against each other through scrimmages, or friendly matches against each other.

Btw, the tournament will most likely be Nebula because it's our most played gamemode. Therefore, all scrimmages will be a similar gamemode to Nebula.
