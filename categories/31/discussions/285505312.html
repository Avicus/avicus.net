« Plug.DJ Romantic Event » by Atheyna February 9, 2015 at 5:02 AM UTC

Hello! I, along with the rest of the Plug.DJ team will be organizing a love song or romantic Valentines event. Make sure to bring your dates along! There will be the regular Friday event, and as this event is quite late for all you guys and girls in the GMT timezone, I will be having an event on Saturday at 6:00PM GMT.

When is it?
The event will be held this Friday, at 6:00 PM EST, 3:00 PM PST or 10:00AM AEDT 
There is no specific end time! The second GMT event will take place at 6:00PM GMT.

What do you do?
If this is your first time ever using plug.dj, you will need to sign up. Go to their page here:

https://plug.dj/dashboard

Follow the instructions to create a profile.
Keep in mind that once you create a username, you cannot change it in the future, so do not impersonate others.

I already have a profile, where do I go?
We have an official plug.dj hangout. If you're already logged into your profile, go to this link right here!

https://plug.dj/avicus


Once you "plug" into the hangout, create a playlist of songs by searching up your favourites on YouTube, and then join the wait list! Once it's your turn to DJ, everyone in the hangout can dance, listen, and have a good time to your song!

What Types of Music is allowed?
For this event, we will encourage users to play love and romantic music. 
But please nothing inappropriate, violent, gory, or racist.
Get creative with what you share! 

Who is hosting this event?
Me, along with any of the plug.dj team members that can make it .
