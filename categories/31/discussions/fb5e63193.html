SnowSX3's Retirement. by SnowSX3 September 28, 2015 at 3:09 AM UTC

Hey guys.
Just want to clear something up before I start.
I'm not leaving Avicus, I'm just retiring from my Moderator position.

I always knew right when I got promoted to Junior Moderator in January that I'd be retiring around December-February.
Going into this I knew that I was going to be Moderating while going through my last year of high school and I knew that at the end of the academic year (December ish) I'd most probably retire around that time because I'd no longer be at school anymore and I'd have to start thinking about what I was going to do with myself in the big wide world.

Unfortunately for me, I have to retire a little earlier than expected.

Here's the thing.
I recently got some of my grades back from school today and it wasn't pretty.
I have end of year exams in 6 weeks and I just can't keep spending 90% of my free time moderating anymore.
I have to focus on my studies now more than ever and I can't keep prioritising Avicus over School.

Trust me, this is as big of a surprise to me as it is to you.
I didn't plan on retiring today, it just kind of happened that way.

I really don't want to retire eh? Like, I really don't want to xD
But I just can't keep doing this.
I have to start thinking about my future. 

Having said that, it really hurts to walk away from the staff team. From the guys who've grown with me over the months. But I know they'll still be bosses without me xD

I guess the one thing I regret is never being good enough to get into the Senior Staff team.
Despite how hard I worked, I was never up to par with the standards that was required.
My goal was to become a Senior Moderator before I retired and not reaching that goal kinda stings but hey, that's life. 
Just to clarify, I never did anything with the intention of getting into the Senior Staff team, it was just something that I always aimed for. 

This community is so cool, I've learnt a lot from you guys.
The combination of praise and criticism has been so beneficial for me. I've appreciated every word of it.

I was a staff member for just over 8 months (245 days)
I was a Junior Moderator for 20 days 
I was a Moderator for 225 days. 

I'm so proud of the staff team and how far it's come since I started.
I've seen a lot of people come and go during my time as staff and I can say that the improvements that are being made and that will be made will truly change the staff team for the better.

I couldn't be more proud at how far this team has come.

I'm not going to thank anyone specific, I'll save that for my leaving thread whenever that comes up (not soon :P)
But you know who you are ;) Thank you. For everything.

There have been a lot of ups and downs during my nearly 8 month stint but I can confidently say that this has truly been the most coolest experience I've ever had.

This experience has taught me a lot of things and has definitely given me a confidence boost because believe it or not, I'm a very shy person IRL xD

So thank you for the most fun I've ever had on Minecraft.

I'll still be around, I just won't have that sexy red tag next to my name hahaha.

<3
