All CTW maps now have chest protection by ViceTechnicolour May 2, 2015 at 12:05 AM UTC

Hello,

An issue was recently reported to the map developers about chest exploits. By the chests not having a radius to prevent building around them, you could use multiple exploits in order to make the game impossible to win (with many Minecraft mechanics) and the ability to place a block above a chest to block it. There were also other things, such as covering it anyway, that made it hard to find for new players. Now, as of a recent commit, all this is fixed.

It should work once the server restarts, and in specific a special thank you to Michael for pointing it out.
