Hey everyone!

I am currently hosting a survey for you guys to tell us how the staff team has been doing over the last 4 months.

The staff team has changed a bunch since our last one in June, and I thought now would be a good time to get your thoughts.

Guidelines:
  • Don't submit joke feedback, seriously, don't waste my time.
  • Be constructive, don't be vague, if you think somebody isn't doing their job well, go in-depth about how they could improve.
  • Don't submit feedback about development issues, when submtting something, ask yourself "Can this be only be fixed by Ivy, or by the staff team" if only Ivy can fix it, then don't post it, this includes server lag and lack of updates.
  • Be careful about inactivity, as some of us are on vacation/leave, only post something about inactivity if the person has been consistently inactive over a long period of time.
