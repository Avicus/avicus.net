In Light of Recent Events. by Iron February 28, 2016 at 5:02 PM UTC

Hello,

In the past day or so, a lot has happened. The banning and demoting of UK_P1NG has been a highlight of the hour. This decision was quite a strange one and one that the seniors had not seen for a long time. The thought of a moderator hacking was trivial, and frankly highly unlikely. However, this is besides the point. The decision to ban Spookz was rushed and terribly executed. Those involved admit and regret the actions which they have committed, and are remorseful now that the community has voiced their concerns. After a long and arduous day, I decided to call upon a re-vote for Spookz's ban. All seniors were involved in this decision and it was a majority vote (4-1, Evocal and Sulorak have abstained/Been unavailable) that is [now] leading to the unban of Spookz.  

I on behalf of the Senior Moderator Team would like to apologize for our recent actions. We all were put under the spotlight and experienced a strange situation we had never faced before. With the voices of 3 incredibly loud and demanding individuals barking orders left and right, many of us were left somewhat aghast and confused. This is no specific individuals fault (Except for Michael he didn't vote), this is the culmination of a lack of sight and idea from the Senior Staff. I hope that situations like this never happen again, this lack of transparency and validity has influenced this decision. Furthermore, I can only offer my apologies. 

Cheers,
Iron & The Senior Staff.
