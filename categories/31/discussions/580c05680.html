Staff Spotlight #9 - KojaUp by BoldAndBrash September 9, 2017 at 3:09 PM UTC

Introduce yourself (4 or more sentences) Hello, my name is Koja. My name is Kochan irl and I live in Manhattan, NYC. I have been in the staff team for about 6 months. I am half Japanese, half American so I travel to Japan a couple times a year.
What are your hobbies? My hobbies include photography and playing music. I play piano and drums, and I play jazz in the school band. I also do art (making prints as well as graphic design).
What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?  I'm excited to make new maps and to hopefully make the map dev team active in fixing bugs and adding new maps to the rotations. What interests you the most in life? I'm also looking forward to the school year (I guess) even though I have terrible classes the first quarter >.>
What music/art/media do you like? I like listening to all kinda of music. I listen to a lot of R&B, Jazz and pop. As for art, I like old photography, especially of NYC.
Where are you from? I am born and raised in NYC. My dad is from Long Island and my mom is from Japan
What’s your favourite thing about moderating? My favourite thing is helping people, whether that means showing them how to submit a map, or getting rid of hackers.
What’s your most memorable experience as a moderator? Damn there are so many. I guess one would be when a chat spamming hacker came on, where there were about 30 accounts just spamming the chat all at once. It was just PieZ and I so we just had to keep banning and banning lol.
P.S. I'll put a link to some of my photography and a video of me playing drums if you are interested
