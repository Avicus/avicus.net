Staff Spotlight #2 - Jahaj by Arigenn March 8, 2016 at 1:03 AM UTC

Expect more of these soon, please welcome Jahaj to the stage!

What is your username in Minecraft and  what is your favourite game on Avicus?*​
My username is jahaj12 (Throwback to old times), and my favorite game has to be DTM because it is one of the most exiting gamemodes, and has produced some of the greatest and funniest moments in my experience on Avicus.
​*Introduce yourself in 4-5 sentences.*​
My name is Jay, better known as Jahaj, and I live in Austin, Texas. I am a more introverted person, but I love interacting with everyone on Avicus. I think I am a friendly guy who loves to have fun, but also still do my job efficiently. I play guitar and piano for probably an hour every day, and I also play Basketball and Tennis 2-3 times a week. I am a freshman in high school, and have a lot of homework, but I still spend most of my free time on Avicus. I love helping people out and making their days better, and playing and moderating on the server is one of the things that I look forward to doing on most days. :smile:
​*What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?*​
Right now I am really overly obsessed, probably too much so, in Madden Mobile and Avicus in general, whether  that be on the server, Teamspeak, or forums.
​*What interests you the most in life?*​
Well at the moment I feel like I want to be an Aeronautical Engineer when I grow up because I like both airplanes and engineering.
​*What music/art/media do you like?*​
I really enjoy most popular music, and listen to it all the time. Also I am obsessed with the NFL, specifically the Packers.
​*Where are you from?*​
Well I was born in Austin, Texas, and we moved to New Delhi, India when I was 5 years old. We moved back to Austin when I was 8 and I have lived there ever since.
​*What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating?*​
Probably things like “What are crates?”, “How do I become a moderator?” and “What are credits?”
​*Anything else you want to mention?*​
If you ever see me, make sure to say hi, I love interacting with everyone on the server!
