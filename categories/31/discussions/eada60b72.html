Avicus Staff Spotlight - FrostedCupcakez by gobernment November 27, 2015 at 3:11 AM UTC

Stealing the spotlight this time is FrostedCupcakez, one of our moderators here on the server. Feel free to post questions and discuss answers here! You're more than welcome to also show Avicus Pride and give her some support :)

What is your username in Minecraft and what is your favorite game on Avicus?
My username is currently FrostedCupcakez. I would have to say that my favourite game at the moment on Avicus is DTM. (Even though I’m really bad at rushing…)


Introduce yourself in 3-4 sentences.
Well, hey everyone! My name is Jessica and I’ve been playing Avicus for around 2 years now. (Not including MC Zone…) I have a passion for music, singing, dancing, and basically every TV show that’s on Netflix. I ride horses every Sunday, and I absolutely love my horse Abbey! (To be honest, she’s not technically mine. But we’ll just say she is.)


What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?
At the moment, I have to say that I’m really excited for high school for some reason. Although I won’t be starting until September next year, I look forward to making new friends and starting again at another school. I’m planning on attending the same high school as Heat, so hopefully everything works out with transferring and I’ll get to see him again. (Bet Heat’s packing his bags and buying a plane ticket right now.)


What interests you the most in life?
The fact that no matter how shaky life may seem, things get better with time and faith, and then it carries on once again. 
Oh, and of course food, music, sleep. Yep, basically sums it all up…


What music/art/media do you like?
I basically love every music genre; I’m a big fan of punk rock and EDM especially. When it comes to TV shows, I really enjoy crime, drama, and sci-fi and fantasy. Some of these shows include: BONES, Scandal, Supernatural, The Vampire Diaries, Grey’s Anatomy and so many more.

Where are you from?
I currently live in Alberta, Canada and in the same city as Heat and Dawning!
(Psst… Ethan. You need to move to the North and be with me and Heat.)


What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating?
I haven’t really thought about that until now, but probably “when are staff applications opening?” and “what do credits do?”


Anything else you want to mention?
I am currently 14 years old and I love being with friends. I also enjoy talking to people on Avicus, and moderating on Avicus. This is one of the nicest Minecraft communities that I have ever been apart of, and I really hope that never changes. If anyone ever needs to talk, feel free to contact me on Skype. Thank you for reading this and I hope you have a lovely day/night/afternoon/evening. (Wherever you are.)
