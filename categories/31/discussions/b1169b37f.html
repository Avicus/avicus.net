October - Monthly Update by Administrafer October 17, 2015 at 11:10 PM UTC

This October has been hectic, for a multitude of reasons. However it’s a great opportunity to shed some light with the community on what the administrators and senior moderators have been doing behind the scenes! Despite the recent negativity on the forums, we’re hoping to soon improve greatly. That being said, we would love for any hateful comments to remain off this thread. Thanks!


Teamspeak and 18+ channels:

When deleting the 18+ channels, it did state there was a possibility it was a temporary change; however it was obviously dismissed. The senior staff team all copped heavy hate, which we expected. While we haven’t particularly done anything to Teamspeak since, we’re hosting a moderator+ meeting on the 25th, where we will make our final decision whether or not it will be added back.


Other than that, we’re currently working on creating policies that affect the Teamspeak server globally, and not just the channel descriptions. This will avoid confusion for staff and users, which should hopefully decrease the “invalid” infractions.


Advertisement:

Administrafer is currently in the process of scouting for Youtubers, and has already contacted many! We may end up having multiple Youtubers with a solid fanbase, so it’s cheap for us but we still feel the flow of new players. We’re currently attempting to contact more popular users… but I can’t promise anything! While it can be expensive depending on the Youtuber, it’s certainly effective.


Staff training:

Internally, we have been working hard to ensure that each staff member is educated before dealing a punishment, and to have them perform better overall, in hopes to prevent or lessen the amount of false punishments distributed. We also aim to provide more of a confident environment so the moderator in question knows what and what not to do in a situation. We have held these on Teamspeak, and the training ranges from detecting hacks to experiencing them on a first-hand basis.


Weekly discussions

We’re reviving this! Don’t know what it is?! Essentially, Howsie22 used to do this, and it would revolve around a topic set by the administrators and seniors that address issues amongst the community. This thread would then be dedicated to that specific topic! At the end of the week, one of the admins will make an official statement that will update everyone what we’ve done about that topic.



Administrator roles:

Within the last week, Administrafer and I have discussed bringing back designated admin roles. This system has proven to be quite effective in the past (with a few setbacks here and there) however with the current state of the staff team, we have decided it’s best to reassign them. The newly designated roles are listed below:


Head of Staff and Administration - Mango_

Head of Community Relations and Support - Administrafer


The titles and specific roles relating to each role are subject to change, although for now we will be focusing on our own sectors and working on a smooth transition into our new roles. We will still be working together on a large portion of the administration tasks, and these titles do not restrict us to the respective sectors. It is simply for us to designate work in a more efficient manner and inform the community on what we will be individually managing.


Newsletters

We've been doing this for staff, and it's been a huge success - everyone loves it! Currently it's a possibility to alter the way this monthly update is presented, into a newsletter format. It would then allow community writers to add their own columns addressing particular issues (or anything for that matter). Essentially, we're aiming for interaction!


That's all folks! More headings to be edited into the OP (maybe)!

Regards,

Avicus Administrators.
