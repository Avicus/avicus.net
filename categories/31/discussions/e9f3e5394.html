Okay, I am not sure how many of these posts have been around lately, but I would like to make an official one for this matter.

Before you read any further and view my ideas about what I am here to talk about, please click on and look at this photo below.

http://imgur.com/mHlvqof

I promise you 2 things: that photo is legit/ unedited, and 2: this figure (and similar) were alive around and even less than a year ago.

Avicus, as literally everyone - ESPECIALLY our veterans - know has taken a player decline, pretty much ever since McZone turned to Avicus, but most dramatically/steeply the last several months.

More than likely on the behalf of other people, I would like to spend this post trying to make a solid effort to motivate you all to help me revive the Avicus player count, and if YOU - every single person reading this just does the few things I suggest on this post, we legitimately have a chance of doubling and further (down the road) multiplying our population!

Now let me clear up a few things. From my experience on MC and servers, when you VOTE for a server on whatever website, it slightly pushes the server's recognition up the popularity chain, thus more people seeing it. On the two sites Avicus has to vote on, only ONE of our 25 - 80 players has voted every day this month. ONE, guys...

If we all voted once every day or two, that is already a guaranteed 20-30 more players right there.

So, with so much opportunity and chance and life left in getting Avicus back on it's feet, please continue to read as I list some suggestions that I think are VITAL for us to gain players...


VOTE: As I said, we have hardly anybody voting these days and please, please believe me when I say it helps. It gains recognition and outside curiosity/popularity towards joining. Voting is so, so easy and yet so important at the same time. VOTE. Please!

STAFF STUFF: Look, I am not here to judge our staff, as they are quite literally the backbone and reason this server even exists anymore, BUT, whilst saying that, there are many in game problems that draw the fine line between a visiting/one time player and a permanent Avicadian.

Many of us complain about block lag, lag, hackers, etc. and I know for a fact there was once a time when this was more under control then it is now. Staff: set up meetings, hire more developers/game coders, get more staff, go online just a little more, just put in a little more effort into fixing these little bugs that drive away players, especially players who are used to perfect, polished off servers like Mineplex and Hive and all of that. Make changes! 
The rewards to these extra efforts will come and speak for them selves. 

REVIEWS: Now for you, the one reading this, if you are sick of coming online to just 20-30 people throughout the week, then don't just complain and accept it, you can make just as much difference as the staff! Voting for starters, but also go on popular Minecraft forums and server sites and make a comment! Make a review! Get our name out there.
You don't have to spam the crap out of every site ever hollering for people to get their ass online, just give a quick word about our games, our community, and go to another site some other time and do the same thing!

With these sites, THOUSANDS of people will at least glance at your comment and/or review, which is so much opportunity for new players to come online. 

Honestly in the generous time you have spent reading this, you could have: Voted, left a comment, wrote a 50-100 word review, and upgraded our name in another way.
It is so easy to do so and I, myself will (be continuing to) do it right after this post. Do it!

YOUTUBE: It is self explanatory! Anyone here who owns a YouTube channel can make videos and spread the server's reputation around the web. If all of us who have a gaming channel did this, then SOMEONE has to notice a video right? and your subscribers! Get them to come on line! Be a YouTuber! communicate and create ways (comments, suggestions, videos, etc.) to get people to check us out. I spent over a year on youtube trying to do this and personally got 15+ more people to try out our server. And i didn't even upload that regularly xD

COMMUNITY: Now if there is one thing i KNOW we are notorious for, it is our lively, friendly and vibrant community. When someone new comes online, involve them in the game and make them feel part of the server. Answer their questions and be happy to help them get used to Avicus.
Also swearing and abusing each other in chat isn't exactly "permanent server" attracting. Try to justify what you need to say without the full on explicit crap.

I'm sure we can all think of many other ways to get the server back on it's feet, THINK about how we can do this and act on what I have already suggested. I PROMISE that you and all of us doing these things will increase the players, how do you think that 350+ figure came to be.

The fact is, most of us have given up or gotten lazy, I say it's time for some new energy! I say we find some motivation and get some more players flowing back into Avicus like the good old days. MAKE A DIFFERENCE.

Oh and to remind you just how much potential this server has going for it (with your help), take one more look at this player count from just LAST YEAR:

http://imgur.com/mHlvqof (and trust me, I've seen it get higher)

Thank you for reading and please help be a part of the revival.

I am going to leave a section below here for any of you who have any extra suggestions for increasing the player count in the comments. I will add your suggestion to the section below.
