Do you want just copy and paste the icons into 1 texture pack?

If yes there is how:
STEP 1: Download WinRaR and Paint.Net
STEP 2: Download all texture packs
STEP 3: Open them with WinRaR
STEP 4: Select texture pack, and go to textures>gui>icons> (There should be image that you want to edit)
STEP 5: Save all textures into 1 folder, if you don't know how to correct export them, go to my profile and contact me with skype. I'm glad to help you
STEP 6: Change folder into .rar, move to .minecraft>textures and you are done!
