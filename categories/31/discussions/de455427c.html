About


So recently Arigenn took over one of ViceWatercolours main projects, which was a translation project. After this to spread out the work load, he created a department for this particular project to be included in, and has assigned me towards this project. That project was to translate the rules from English to different languages. After discussing it Arigenn, I have since got permission to turn this project into a team. Basically the translators will do various tasks from translating important documents to helping out on Teamspeak with people who speak their assigned language. Arigenn and myself will oversee this project. Currently we are not looking for any more translators but in time we will require more.
