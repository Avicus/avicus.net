_Stormz December 13, 2014 at 1:12 PM UTC

In-game, you cannot report an offline player. 

In order to see who is banned, you can go to avicus.net/infractions to check who has been recently banned.

For player-specific concerns, you can go to avicus.net/<playername> in order to see the person's profile. Go to the infractions tab and then see if that person has been banned or not. 

I hope this cleared out some questions. :D
