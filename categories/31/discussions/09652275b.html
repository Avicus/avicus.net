The AVPost No Longer Needs Editors! by Iron April 5, 2015 at 6:04 PM UTC

Hey Guys!

    Today I'm proud to announce I am reopening the AVPost! As some of you may know, it was an unofficial newsletter created by Awesome_Apricot and I (Iron/Ironsoldier2000 at the time) . Recently, in light of recent events I decided that starting this newsletter up would be a fun way for some of Avicus to write interesting stories, or share opinions! The original post is located here: https://avicus.net/forums/144889a25.
       
     So without further ado, would the community like me to restart this? If so how would you like me to get editors? Suggest any idea's and miscellaneous thoughts about the newsletter down below! However, I would really appreciate it if you guys looked at the original to get a good idea of how it looks and what it contains.

Thanks!
     -Iron

Well since you guys want it, its back! :D I am officially opening applications for the AVPost! I currently plan on having 4 or 5 editors (Not bound in stone yet).  If you would like to edit or provide articles (journalist technically) etc, just contact me on skype!
