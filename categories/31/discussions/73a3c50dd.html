Community Event - All Star Game by Spoookeh August 13, 2017 at 2:08 AM UTC

Hello

An idea originally created by Tazz and BmB 2 years ago, the 'All-Star Game' puts some of the server's best players against each other in various gamemodes and matches to see who will come out on top. 

When?
Saturday 19th August, 7pm BST / 2pm EST

What maps?
CTW - Sea Port
DTM - Rendezvous Champions
KoTH - Draxel

Who will be playing?
Seen as this is a community event, you can decide! Fill out this google form and players with the most votes in specific positions will be chosen to play if they can make it - https://goo.gl/forms/Ci4oBF8bTyOqBevt1  Each team will have 9 chosen players plus 1 pre-chosen captain allowing for people to sub in and out where necessary. 

Will there be a stream and referees?
I'm looking for 1 streamer (good quality) and 2 referees, please contact be on discord if you are interested - Spookeh#7166

See you next weekend!
