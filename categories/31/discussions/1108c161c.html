Avicus Challenges (Day 15) [Nebula] by kobetexas August 31, 2014 at 1:08 PM UTC

Challenge Name: Partners In Crime
Challenge Rating: HARD
Players: 2-4
Require: Break the 2,3,or 4 Monuments at the same time! [In TS, Find someone who is willing to do it, or use skype to contact your friend!]
Rules:
  • You may do WHATEVER it takes to get to the Monuments [Recommended 2 monument map for 2 players to do the job instead of 3 or 4]
  • Use a Diamond Pickaxe! [For a harder Challenge, use an Iron one]
  • You NEED to mine AND break the Monument at the same time with your Partner on Skype or TS.
  • When Both of you (Or 3, 4 etc.) Countdown from 3, And start mining the Monument. 
Even more challenging:
  • Use an Iron Pick to Mine the Obsidian Monument, For an EVER HARDER Challenge Use stone, However Monuments NEED to be broken at the same time!
  • NO ARMOR!
  • Use a WOOD sword!
  • NO Bow!
  • And NO Rushing Materials! (Blocks etc.)
Good luck!
