So... Avicus is shutting down.

I've been on and off, shit on the server but have also praised it.

I joined the Avicus Network February 14th 2014. I've made friends, I've lost friends, but Avicus was a key factor in my life.

If you guys don't know, I am turning 18 on December 28th 2017, in 3 days (Merry Christmas BTW) and have been wanting to get a tattoo.
I've spoken as a joke about getting an Avicus tattoo for the memes, but honestly this server itself has been such a big influence in my life, and I have invested heavily into it that I honestly feel it deserves to be imprinted on me permanently.

I am here to call out you graphic designers to design a tattoo that would be going on the upper inside of my left arm, and hopefully if I like any of the designs, I will take it. If not, I will just use the logo.

I won't be getting it on my birthday because I'm broke atm and am getting paid the next day, but I hopefully will get the tattoo before the 30th of January to be able to share it with you guys.

It's been a really great 4 years with you all and it's been amazing to see this server grow as a whole.
I am going to miss every single one of you, whether we have had our personal differences, or we were of best of friends, I wish that you all an amazing last month on Avicus. It will definitely be missed and be one of my favourite minecraft servers of all times. I will definitely never forget this.
~Vlad
