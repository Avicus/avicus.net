[Undecided] Nebula gamemode suggestion by MerlinMaster October 27, 2014 at 1:10 AM UTC

This is a gamemode where you chose where you spawn. You place the emerald block to set your spawn. Wherever the emerald block is placed, that is where you spawn.
Here are a couple of images i scrounged up.
There is where it will be in the inventory.
1. Place block
2. Die
3. Spawn where you place it
                                When you place it up high like this V
If you place it higher than the ground, you will spawn where the redstone block is.
If you place the emerald block and then someone breaks it before you die, you spawn at your teams original spawn.
Keep in mind this is a gamemode, not a suggestion to every nebula inventory.
Pros:
1. Easy rushing
2. Good for pvping/ kill farming
Cons:
1. Little harder to defend
What do you think?
