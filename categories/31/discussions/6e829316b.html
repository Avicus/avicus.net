Calling all PvPer's! by vladthegreat100 February 23, 2016 at 5:02 AM UTC


Hey There Everyone!

I've decided to make this post to call all of you pvper's out there who have the ability to record! after thinking through for a bit and getting back into the groove of making montages I've decided to make this post.

What is the idea?

The idea is that you, the community, will record your PvP clips and send in your best clips, it will work a bit like the top 5 series where you "send in" your information, the only difference is that every clip will be put in (1 per person unless not enough players are entered) unless not up to critera (Check Below for Criteria).


The Criteria?

  • Video must be rendered to 720p minimum
  • No major edits were made in the clip (I'm planning to make some edits to them possibly colour wise, etc.)
  • Must be Avicus only. No other servers please :)
  • The playe you are fighting must be fighting back. Attacking a player from behind by surprise or killing people who are afk will not be placed in the montage. ; Rule that will nullify the "Attacking a player from behind" part is if you had an impressive way of getting there.
  • Video being submitted must be less than 30 seconds long, pvp can last in that time frame.

Song used?

There is no song chosen at the moment, it will be chosen during editing time when all clips have been submitted, there will be a theme with the montage coming from the chosen song.
Song choices are allowed to be given on this post :)



How to Enter?

Registrations will be open from the making of this post to the 27th of February 2016 at 11:59pm. The montage will be complete the 29th of February 2016.
To enter fill out the form below :):

http://goo.gl/forms/6lqsuYzh9k

Good luck and have fun PvPing! :)
