Community Q&A + Feedback Survey by MidnightBeats November 9, 2015 at 1:11 AM UTC

As part of my promises as an Administrator, tntpotato and I have finalized and finished the "Community Q&A and Feedback Survey." This is an event that will (hopefully) increase some transparency between the community and staff, as well as allow the community to openly provide feedback to improve the Staff team and Map Developer team.

How will this work?

Q&A: Instructions
There will be two components to this event. The first is the Community Q&A, which will take place on this thread. The idea is that the community will ask any questions they have for the Staff team and Map Developer team. For example, you can ask questions such as "When are the next staff applications," "Is there going to be a staff reassessment?" and "Where can I post my map?"

All responses will be provided by the staff team, including the Administrators, and Map Developers will provide answers to Map Development related questions. All other responses are unofficial. 

You may also provide constructive criticism on the Staff and Map Development team, not aggressive criticism. 

Survey: Instructions

The survey is the second component to this event. You will be provided a link to a survey that TNT and I have prepared, where you will answer a series of questions that pertain to Avicus (staff team, map development, server). 

The responses are anonymous; you are free to provide as much criticism as you like, but remember, please keep the responses respectful.

Do not send troll responses.

Survey Link

All troll responses on this thread will be archived. Failure to comply with these instructions may result in a forum punishment.

Well, ask away!
-TNT and Midnite
