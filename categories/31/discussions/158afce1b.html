Avicus Map Rating/Feedback by ProfessorUtonium September 2, 2015 at 2:09 AM UTC


Not many players know about this, but there is a way to rate maps for Avicus! 

Copy this and put it into your URL:


http://avicus.net/maps/


Then, Add the map you want to rate and comment on


for example:


http://avicus.net/maps/Overworld

http://avicus.net/maps/Curabitur

You can also do maps that aren't in rotations:

https://avicus.net/maps/Illuminati


Have fun!
