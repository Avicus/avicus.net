Retirement. by SnowSX3 September 4, 2017 at 9:09 AM UTC

Hey everyone,

I am stepping down from my 4th tenure as a Moderator for this network.


During my leave of absence, a time where I should be relaxing and taking it easy, I have become incredibly stressed with everything Avicus related. 


I remember talking with  ViceTechnicolour and he told me not to let Avicus stress me out. So I'm sticking to that advice.


There is no single reason as to why I'm retiring, rather, there are multiple reasons that have added up increasingly quickly. 

(And no it's not all Podcast related.. I've been feeling disconnected for a wee while now)


I genuinely hold no grudge with anyone and wish everyone on the staff team and community the best of luck moving forward.


If I have offended you or come across as rude I seriously apologise. 

I always have this server's interest at heart and love you all.


I wish nothing but the best for every member of the current staff team. During this time of unsettlement, I know you guys have the ability to pull through.


The community are a bunch of quick judging bastards, but For fuck's sake are they loyal. The community will stick with this server through anything.. and I've always admired that.


I'm sorry that I wasn't able to do enough for this server. 

I tried my best, and at the end of the day, that's all I can do.


I wish I could have done more for this network. 

But for all of the shortcomings and mistakes there have been accomplishments and achievements as well.


And an uncomfortable amount of laughter.


Thanks guys. I'll see you round.
