Hey Guys.

Before you read: As part of the Senior Department of Community Relations, my goal is to work with the community to create a bond between them and the staff team. I'm going to be creating many other events other than this so please expect them in the future :^)

For my event, I have decided to create a Set Next Event/Party where players can request any map they want. It can be from any gamemode and it doesn't have to be in rotations. A couple of rules I will be doing are that there will be no repeats and every map has a maximum of 30 minutes to be played. No SG/Avicade/Throwback maps either. To see a list of maps, go to http://maps.avicus.net.
Times:
5/26/16 - 5pm EDT (10pm BST) (7am 5/27/16 AEDT)
5/27/16 1pm EDT (6pm BST) (3am 5/28/16 AEDT)
5/28/16 - 12am EDT (Midnight) (5am BST) (2pm 5/29/16 AEDT)

Server: the one with the most players at the time

They will last 1-3 hours (or if I have to go).


Please be there!
