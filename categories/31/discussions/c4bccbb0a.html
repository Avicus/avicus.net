Event #2: Mu Cephei This is the second official event on the Avicus Network, known as "Mu Cephei" (which is the name of a star, to fit alongside the theme.) You are given 2 weeks to construct a map in Nebula, and it can be either TDM or DTM, or even your own unique concept. However, this one has a twist unlike the other; you have to use a cut of terrain. This is for your own creativity and to see what you can do to make it really good compared to any other mapmakers on the network. There are a few rules however, but it should be fun for everyone!

Who are the judges?
Just me again. I may scout for judges in future events, however the event is not sponsored by the network but rather independently by me and with what I can do within my power.

What are the rules?
You have to follow these rules or your map will be rejected.
- You may not change the map's terrain too drastically.
- You may not create large craters into the map.
- You cannot build outside of the cut other than making obs spawn.
- You may not implement lava into the map.
- You may not hide overpowered items.
- You may not have more than 3 authors.
- You CAN build anything you want above the terrain.
- You CAN make rivers, mines, and underground accesses editing terrain.
- You CAN change the material of the grass and dirt, or remove the tall grass.

What are the guidelines?
Maps are judged in the following aspects;
- Gameplay (How do you think the map will play on the servers?)
- Aesthetics (How pleasing to the eye does it look?)
- Creativity (Is it unique and really stands out?)

YAML will be done by a map developer if you wish, and it will not count against you. I made the mistake of taking off points for this before; we're all here to help!

What gamemodes can I do?
You can do anything possible within YAML in Nebula, however it is reccomended to do DTM (Destroy the Monument) or TDM (Team deathmatch). OiTN is also possible, but that is a submode.

How do I submit my map?
Put a [CONTEST] tag in the title. By doing so, you are entered into the contest and put into a spreadsheet. It contains the map name, authors, thread link, jurisdiction of approval/denial, and it's gamemode. Only Nebula maps are eligible to be in this contest. You have the permission to post your thread link in the post below so I can see it easier.

1. Do not give inconstructive feedback by knocking others down to build yourself up.
2. Do not steal maps; all maps are your property. Violations of this are a perma ban.
3. Do not ghost produce; don't have other users make a map for you and you take credit.
4. Do not spam judges; patience is a large key. Wait for a response on your map.
5. Do not ghost view; don't tally a ton of thread views in your thread for attention.
6. Do not make joke maps; all maps must be true submissions to the server.
7. Do not submit maps made prior to this; all maps must be "newly produced".
8. Always give credit. No matter what the case, credit everyone who authored.

How can I create YAML?
Use our documentation resource at docs.avicus.net.

What is the prize?
To be announced. Most likely an emerald rank, and if said user has a rank already they may receive pets. 

What about Canes Venatici?
Details regarding Canes; one, it's a constellation, not a star in which I messed up while writing. Second of all, testings for Canes are hopefully coming however it is not a promise; if it happens, it happens. If it doesn't, it doesn't.
