SnowSX3 May 18, 2015 at 11:05 AM UTC

The new batch system is making the whole process a lot slower than last time (which is intentional).

There're 16 people per batch. 
These 16 people will get a response and their interview will be done and dusted before the next 16 people get a reply on their application.

Normally, we'd give eveyone a response and shoot through the interviews fairly quickly but we realised that it got too crowded with the sudden increase of Jr.Moderators we had.

That's why a lot of people this time around haven't got a response yet. Instead of doing eveyone at the same time, we're only getting through 16 people at a time.

Yes, it does make the whole process a lot slower but it's for the best. It makes it easier for both the Jr.Moderators and the Moderators / Sr.Staff team.

Good things come to those who wait.

Patience is key my friends.
