The Dawn & The Dusk: 2 sessions, 2 different ways to play.

This is a new rotation update to have the MCZone rots split into 2 different sections of time rather than opening a new server: There's Dawn, and there's Dusk. What do these two terms mean when it comes to playing on the server?

Dawn: Plays maps strictly from the MCZone era.
Dusk: Plays maps strictly from early avicus, early avicore, and an extra Quintus + Clay Wars.

I didn't want to separate everyone into different servers since it has the prime purpose of catering to nostalgia and the fact of the matter is that there's already too many nebula servers unvacant, and with this session update I hope that there's some more action packed onto what some of you requested. The maps that may be added are CherrylandMC, Haze, Broken World, & Abandoned Park I. This is a very early stage development, so if there could be any further changes to the nostalgic rotations you can post feedback below.
