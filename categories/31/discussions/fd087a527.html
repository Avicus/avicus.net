First off, I would like to congratulate the PowerPuff Boys on winning Race to Victory! Prizes will be distributed soon.

Pel's Retirement; A Thank You:

Sadly, yesterday was hindered with Pelpelajax's retirement. He was a great official. Please thank him if you see him.

Things We Can Improve On:

Please reply down below if you have another things that are not listed here.

  • Fix Servers. Way too laggy.
  • Fix maps. Way too skybridged based, and stalemated. 
  • Improve on detecting hackers during a tournament match. We are very sorry EC. 
Updates/Announcements:
First off, I would like to say that there will not be another "big" tournament for a while. Don't worry! Instead, we will be having small, 1 day mini-tournaments with varying gamemodes. 
New Officials? With Pelpelajax's retirement, there will be new officials. Yes, plural. Expect that very soon. 
I can promise that lots of good things are coming!

Avicus Leagues? What did you just say?
Stay tuned for more imformation. Again, coming very soon. 

Our Goals:

Basically, we want to improve Avicus' player count with tournaments. More players = better tournaments. We also want to provide good, fun tournaments.

Get hyped!
Regards, 
The Officials :)
