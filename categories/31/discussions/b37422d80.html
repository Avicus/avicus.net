Question about "Spawn Killing" by Joltz_ August 13, 2014 at 7:08 AM UTC

Does Spawn Killing count as going in the actual spawn. or Does the area around it, Like a bridge and the exit count, Because I was kicked for Killing someone on a bridge out of spawn, but I see people killing in Quintus which is called "Spawn Camping" I belive, Are they the same and both punishable? Just a question :P

SnowSX3 August 13, 2014 at 8:08 AM UTC

Spawn killing or targeting is when you attack the enemy in or around their base.
It is not exclusive to just attacking them in their base, the area around counts too.
So to answer your question, yes, they are the same thing and are both punishable.
Hope that helps.
