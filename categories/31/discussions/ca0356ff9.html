YAML Wishlist by Zintenka August 14, 2014 at 5:08 PM UTC

Cuboid, Cylinder, and etc. selection region for spawning: Will reduce cluttering and attempted spawn blocking.

Mob disguises: Oooooh yeeeaaaaah~

mob disguise:
    who: red (* gives it to every team)
       mob: wither skeleton
       potion effect: wither (Every hit gives wither to the inflicted)
       attribute: (See attributes below)
   


Item attributes: Attributes that a certain item has like: generic.maxHealth, increases maximum health.

 generic.followRange, increases range that mobs will lock onto you.

 generic.knockbackResistance, increases knock  back resistance.

 generic.movementSpeed, increases movement speed. 

generic.attackDamage, increases attack damage.

Could be used in a syntax like this:

0: item: iron axe
 title: 'Example of syntax'
enchants:
 - damage all:5
 - dig speed:5
 - durability:1
attribute:
- generic.movementSpeed:add (or subtract):3 (you can use decimals)

A mob spawning syntax.

A syntax that allows trading with villagers and enabling villagers.
