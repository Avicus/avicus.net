Applications News

Rumors say that applications are opening soon, I can confirm this. More information will be given out on applications within the next week.

Atlas Updates

Our developers continue on working hard to push this update out. The administration, and development teams are still organizing testing sessions for the update.  Atlas is almost here!

Department Removal
The senior staff team will be reverting back to using a task / assignment system, and will be scraping departments. After a recent meeting, we have decided that this method is better to use during these circumstances. 

Overcast Closure

Unfortunately our fellow Minecraft server, The Overcast Network, will be closing. As this time slowly approaches, we will warmly welcome all members who wish to continue with a similar style of Minecraft. 

Avicus Community Slack

The Administrator team is still encouraging you all to join community slack, just remember to contact an admin to be added to it!

General Rules:
  • No links to pornography, phishing sites, keyloggers, scams, etc.(3 Points)
  • No harassing users in all chats(Depends how bad. Talk to over staff about it)
  • No rumors/drama. We're trying to get away from that.(1 Point)
  • Try to remain calm and civilized in situations.(1 Point)
  • No death threats or threatening to Ddos (3 Points)
  • We have a three strike limit. These strikes will be removed if the strike was given on an older date and the staff feels it has been a long enough time
  • Making a leaving thread will get you kicked out of slack. Unless stated that you do not wish to leave.
  • Each room has different levels of it being moderated. Low moderated means the channel is very lenient. Medium moderated means the channel will be regularly moderated. Highly moderated means it will be very strict with moderators .

Channels

  • #announcements(Highly Moderated) •Do not post in this channel unless otherwise told to by an admin. If you wish to discuss what is said on the announcements, please talk about it in #general
  • #competitive (Medium moderated)
  • #feedback(Highly Moderated)
  • #general(Medium Moderated)
  • #help (Highly moderated)
  • #map-making (Medium moderated)
  • #music
    • •Do not post Music that is harmful to others ears or disturbing
  • #news(Medium Moderated)
  • #random (Low moderated)

That's all for now folks!
