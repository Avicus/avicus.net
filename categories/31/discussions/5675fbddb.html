Goodbye. by Sevoo March 6, 2017 at 3:03 AM UTC

This has been coming for a good two months now and I feel I owe this to anyone that actually cares, so here goes, I'm going to try and make this short and sweet.

Goodbye, it's been an awesome three years. I've made friends, "enemies", but most of all memories (good or bad). This was my first server. I remember joining with my friend and playing Survival Games for hours with him, even though I would never win and pretty much died off spawn half the time. 

There are multiple reasons I'm leaving. For one, Minecraft got boring as a whole, especially lately. It's a very broken game with tons of cheaters, shitty staff on lots of servers and the direction the game was going was a completely different direction I was going as a player, and I think lots of people here can relate. Second, the one server I could always count on to lighten my mood up and have fun on (Avicus) got boring. It was the same recipe all the time. Nothing new. I tried to get into competitive but that pretty much died right after I made that decision. Third, and this is probably the main reason I left, is because I started playing Overwatch and it's really, really fun (even though I suck ass).

I'm not good at thanking people, so I'll just pick like five people I remember right now to thank with a short summary of why I chose them. 

Zaidms- You probably won't read this, but you've always been nice to me. I've always respected you as a player and you're always really fun to skype with. playing with you while you ban evaded constantly was fun xD.

Nathy- You're a really nice guy. I mean it. For the short time I was actually active in Cacal you were really fun to play with. I remember that one time we were talking about Game of Thrones together. You're also really funny. Good memer.

Lunacy- Favorite weeb by far. I've always respected you as a player because you can be a god with such high ping. It was really fun playing alongside you in Cacal and helping you carry us XD. You're also really funny and have decent memes.

Emily- Oh my god. You're fucking hilarious. You're really, really nice and can really put a smile on my face sometimes. It's always fun being a retard with you. Holy shit I can't stop thinking about that video you made XD.

Badger- I remember you as the one genuinely nice and sincere guy that I could always turn to. Sure, you've pissed me off and I've pissed you off but at the end it never got in the way of us getting along. You're a really good staff member while still being a cool guy. One of the coolest :).

To all the rest that I forgot, thanks for three years :).

Bye.
