Staff Spotlight #4 - LFT by Crazy_ July 1, 2017 at 8:07 PM UTC

Alright, so welcome back to the Staff Spotlights. This time, we've asked  LFT to tell us a bit about himself. He's a long time staff member, and is involved in many different parts of the staff team.

Introduce yourself (4 or more sentences)

Hey everyone, some of you know me as Robert, but some know me as LFT. I've played Avicus for almost 4 years now, and originally joined back when it was known as MC Zone. I've been playing Minecraft for a little over 5 years now. I am a junior in high school and I plan to be a Marine Biologist when I am older.

What are your hobbies?

My hobbies included, sports (basketball, baseball and football) & playing video games.

​What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?

Counter Strike Global Offensive, been playing it almost every day this past week or so.

What interests you the most in life?

This might sound dark, but the whole 'death' thing. I've always wondered how it feels, what happens, where the spirit goes and all of that.

What music/art/media do you like?

Honestly, it depends on the beat that the music is played on. I usually listen to artists like, 21 savage, lil uzi vert, playboy carti, xxxtentacion and other artists in the rap genre. ​

Where are you from?

Orlando, FL.

What’s your favourite thing about moderating?

For me, its interacting with the community (whether that is through teamspeak, discord, in-game or the forums) and the ability to joke around with my fellow staff members.

What’s your most memorable experience as a moderator?

I don't think I can pin-point a memorable experience, but I would say the day I was promoted to Moderator. Also probably the day I was promoted to Mentor.
