Leaving? by Centralking April 29, 2016 at 2:04 AM UTC

I'm sorry..

For my discussions
For my suggestions
For having fun
For joking around
For helping the community 
For reporting
Most of all, I'm sorry for my presence on this server

It is April 28th, 2016, 10:20 PM EST where I have decided to quit Avicus, in fact quit Minecraft in general since this is the only flame that was keeping me going. I was an Avicus member for 3+ years and counting, it was a wonderful experience in meeting new friends on this server and keeping in touch socially. 

I'm leaving due to many conflicts. It is a very long story, but all I'm going to say is to the senior staff team is have some trust on the fan base. They have been here the most obviously and if you want to keep this server going, listen to them, comply with them, and keep in touch with them. They know the drawbacks of this server.

I would like to give a huge thanks to Arigenn (Ethan) for giving me the chance to talk to him as it gave me the final motivation I needed to finally quit. My life is progressing with many difficulties and I needed this. It was fun keeping close relations with some staff here as well as many new friends. You know who you're. Once again, I'm sorry.
