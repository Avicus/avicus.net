Discussion #5: Teamspeak?! by Howsie August 24, 2014 at 1:08 PM UTC

Hi everyone!

When avicus was introduced, we also introduced a ton of cool new services for our users to enjoy and be part of. One of these was the teamspeak server, which for those who dont know is like a channel based Skype, where you can talk to people from all around the world who also play on avicus. You can also talk to the staff members who are always happy to help and support you and your needs or if you just want to jump on and have a chat with one of them. 

So I just wanted to ask a few questions about teamspeak and what you all think of it. 

Today's Topics:-

Do you use teamspeak and if not, is there any reason why? (Nervous, couldn't install etc) 

If you do use teamspeak, do you like the general layout and design of the teamspeak channels? 

Do you feel that the staff talk regularly enough to you or other players?
