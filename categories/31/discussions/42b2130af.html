Alright, let's get serious here. I'm gonna give my honest opinion now!
Also, keep in mind this is merely my opinion, yet nothing official or confirmed.
The following people I am about to mention are not organized in a specific order at all.

-- Polar_Express
He's one of the two Walls adepts I've got to meet until now (the first one being itaity). While his personality may be stubborn at times, he'd handle the rank quite well and I can smell good results coming from him, because there are already some fantastic Walls maps from him that have unique qualities and amazing features, every single one of them. All of his maps offer different gameplay styles that innovate and add more variety to Walls.

-- Merlinmaster
He has a really organized and impeccable building style, he's been making maps every so often and has shown to possess true Map Developing potential. His attitude is something he should work on a tiny bit but I can tell this could be an opportunity for him to change as a person for the best. He'd be a good choice as he brings up unique concepts in Map Making, and he's been bringing up pretty neat gamemode ideas lately as well.

-- Slicorn
He's been improved since last time he made a map. I can appreciate the sharpening of his building abilities in both aesthetical side and gameplay side. He offers a nice attitude but has also his downsides, anyhow, he'd be pretty efficient and hard-working, since that's one of his brightest qualities. He's a pretty good choice and he's been working good with maps lately. You can easily work with him when it comes to map making and he'd ensure you a fun time, trust me.

-- Zedther
Lately he's been showing off what he didn't in the past: Map Developing potential. I was truly impressed when Para first mentioned him, but now I can understand why, as he is actually a pretty good builder and he knows how to take feedback properly. He seeks ways to improve, as well, and his easy going attitude is pretty helpful as well. He's very active and his time zone is rather... interesting. I'm pretty sude he'd be a nice addition to the Map Developing team, so I also vote for him.

-- sweeeper
This guy works by the shadows. His building style is flawless and he is full of ideas and new map styles. His ability is truly impressive and he takes his time on making maps to make sure the finishing touch is worth it. His attitude is pretty friendly and he is capable of understanding what the word teamwork means on all its forms. He has been working a lot lately and I have seen it with my own eyes. He'd be a great addition to the team.

-- SweetMango
This guy is an exceptional builder. He knows what he's doing when he builds, and even though his map count is the smallest out of everyone, building Dredmor is enough for him to be considered for the position. He's very trustworthy, his attitude is pretty cool, he's very mature, capable of working efficiently, active, his time zone helps him, too, and he's already a Moderator, he pretty much knows what teamwork is and how to communicate around other Staff Members. I swear, I will never forget how fun it was to build Dredmor with Mango and Koo. Mango's boss.

Chill and Cool guys I also like:
-- Zintenka
-- resepignev
