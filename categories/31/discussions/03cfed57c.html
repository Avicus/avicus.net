Can You Get Them All? by Flebula September 18, 2014 at 2:09 PM UTC

So I was looking at a post by Sethskye about Nebula challenges. This something that I think would be great fun so I am going to make a list of challenges that go from easy to hard. I thought going down this list might make nebula more fun. Credit to Sethskye for my idea.

1. Get one kill.

2. Get 5 kills in one match.

3. Get 5 kills in one life.

4. Get 10 kills in one life

 5. Build a two storeyed house just outside of spawn with a sign saying "Hey there" at the door. Must be at least 10 x 10 blocks.

6. Place 25 signs with step by step directions to get to the other team's spawn.

7. Pretend to be a store clerk selling iron at from a chest with your own hut for 5 minutes.

8. Get a monument.

9. Get 20 kills in one life.

10. Build a protection building around a monument.

11. Build a skybridge to the enemy spawn/base.

12. On your monument, defend and  kill five people who were at the monument.

13. Rank to level 10

14. Get 2 monuments in one match.

15. Hold shift the whole way to the other team's spawn, straight through the middle of the map.

16. Get first kill on a game with 20 or more people.

17. Get and arrow kill from 30 blocks away.

18. Build a tunnel underground to the enemy base.

19. Get to level 20

20. Build an underground (Or below spawn-level) tunnel to monument and break monument.

21. Kill an iron player while you are not wearing armour (And not with a bow, not from hitting them off the world).

22. Kill a player with a bow shot from 60 blocks away.

23. Go to the enemy's iron supply, and make yourself iron armour.

24. Go to the enemy's iron supply, and make yourself iron armour, except this time, kill five people at their base.

25. Get all the monuments in the match.

26. Rank to level 30

27. Kill 30 people in one life

28. Only die once the whole match.

29. Don't die any times the whole match.

30. Kill 10 people with a bow in one life.

31. Build a skybase to the enemy monument and boat/minecart drop down onto the monument and break it.

32. Kill someone with your fist.

33. Break a monument with your fist. (Idea from sfkwww)

34. Rank to level 40

35. Incase an AFK defender in wood at the enemy monument, then break the monument.

I'm not finished with the list, and I should get up to 60-70 challenges when I am done, but give me feedback on what you think so far.
