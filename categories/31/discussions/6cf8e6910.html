Unofficial Survival Games Event by Posighdun November 15, 2015 at 1:11 PM UTC

Hello Everyone,


In a bid to increase the popularity of Survival Games and show everyone how fun it is i’ve gotten together with Gregory, Tazz & a couple others and organised an Unofficial Survival Games Event here on Avicus.


General Information


So far there is roughly 30ish people. Each Game will hold 10 people (At this stage) and the top 4 from each game will move to the finale. The first match will start off at 8:05am, Followed by the second match at 8:10am and the third match at 8:15am, all times are in EDT. We plan to have the matches finished by 8:45am but once all matches have finished then the finale will begin.


The matches will be randomly selected, i will put everyone's names in a random generator and the first 10 drawn will verse in the 1st match, second lot of 10 drawn will verse in the 2nd match and the 3rd lot of 10 will verse in 3rd Match.


The matches will be done on public servers, Sg01, Sg02 and Sg03. I will individually invite the 10 randomly selected players to these matches. We will all assemble in lobby and when i give the go move to the server.


If you are unable to attend due to any reason please contact me ASAP.


Maps are still being decided.


Prizes

1st - 7.5k Credits

2nd - 5k Credits

3rd - 3k Credits


Dates

The event will take place on the 21st of November, Saturday, at 8am EDT. I’ve tried to make it so people of all time zones can participate.


Registration closes 2 days before that on the 19th of November.


Rules

  • All Avicus rules apply.

  • Teams are allowed, but maximum of 3 players per team. Teaming up is not allowed in the Finale, the finale is a FFA.

  • For the Finale, Participants MUST have Teamspeak. They don't need to talk, they just need to be on it.

  • We will be keeping track of the logs, to make sure no one breaks the rules.

  • Talking in public chat is strictly forbidden. 

  • Try your best not to delay the matches, if you are participating.


Registration

Register here


We are very much still in the planning process but once we have an idea of how many people wish to participate then we can finalize it.
