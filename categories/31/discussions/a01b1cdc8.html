Well, I'm sure many people want to use Plug.dj on the go and on their phone as their browser on their computer probably lags them while their gaming, but here is a guide.

Requirements:

An iOS Smart-device (iPad, iPhone, iPod etc.)
Requires iOS 6 or later.

Step 1.

Go to the app store and search "puffin browser"

Step 2.

Download the application

Step 3.

Once installed, open the browser, follow the instructions

Step 4.

Type in the plug.dj website link on the search-bar, pick your room, and you got high quality music!
