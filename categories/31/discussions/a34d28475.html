Community Update & The Future Of Avicus by Posighdun May 8, 2016 at 4:05 AM UTC

Recently we have had some leadership changes within the Senior Staff Team, so we thought that now is the most appropriate time to make a community update and inform you all of what has happened and what is to come.

Retirements
Pelpelajax retired from Moderator & Translator.
PieZ retired from Moderator.
Rinn stepped down from Administrator.
Palaemon stepped down from Manager.

Promotions
Posighdun was promoted to Administrator.
Eteh was promoted to Junior Moderator.
Kyoal was promoted to Junior Moderator.
CrazyCreeper_521 was promoted to Moderator.
Palaemon was promoted to Senior Moderator.
Fouled was reassigned to Senior Moderator.

Regarding the manager rank
After discussing it with Ethan we have decided to remove the Manager Rank for various reasons. It won't be returning, the decision is final. We will be tweaking the Senior Moderators roles in the near future to make up for this removal.

Regarding Staff Applications
Staff Applications closed on the 1st of May but we are still dealing with some applications here and there. Please be patient, we hope to have all the responses out ASAP. 

What to except in the future (this month)
- Updating the rules.
- Some gameplay changes :^)
- Possibly a tournament. 
- Small changes to the policies.
- The staff system/structure being redone, to make work more effective.
- Other minor stuff.

As always, the Administrative Team is open to feedback and to opinions on a wide range of issues. Whilst we cannot incorporate every idea and opinion you have, we can take it onboard when we make decisions. We need your support to fix Avicus. If anyone has any queries or concerns please hit me up on Community Slack.

The Administrative Team is very excited for what is to come of Avicus and what the future holds. We hope you all are too :P

Regards,

The Administrative Team (Peter, Ethan & Keenan)
