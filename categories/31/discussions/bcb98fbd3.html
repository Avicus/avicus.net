AndrewJKim August 10, 2014 at 5:08 AM UTC

Alright, so, if your going to download a template, it's going to be bad I can tell you, because alot of other people would be using it also, making it show that you just downloaded one. IF you legit want to make one yourself, you sortof want to get well known with either Cinema4D/Blender/Maya (C4D is best for beginners, but takes longer to download, Blender is good but takes longer, and Maya is for professionals.) If you want a 3d banner, use those, and if you want a 2d banner, either use photshop (Make sure to use the video frame layer) or adobe after effects (Really easy to understand, and learn.) These are mainly for 2D banners. Just get well known with them, and then you want to make an intro. It's best that you learn and then do, not just try off the bat, because I've been thru that, and I've suffered thru my learning time.
NOTE: These are for minecraft intros and/or some other games that might work. I am not sure what program would be best for other intros.
