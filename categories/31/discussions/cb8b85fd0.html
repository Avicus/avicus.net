Here we are back with the second unofficial tournament.

BRACKET IS OUT: http://challonge.com/BarneyTazz2

Group stage is over.

General Information

The tournament will consist of 2 groups of 4 teams. Each team will play eachother ONCE with a 45 minute time limit. The best 2 of each group will head on into the Semi Finals.

The group stages will take place on October 10th and then the semi finals and then finals will take place on October the 18th. When signing up you need to be able to play on BOTH of the dates above.

An unlimited amount of teams will be accepted.

We follow Avicus' rule set.

Capacity
  • 8 teams - the maximum amount of players on a team is 30, which is the maximum amount of people on a current team roster.
  • Only players on the team page will be allowed. 
  • You can only play for one team.
  • On the pitch, teams may have a minimum of 7 and a maximum of 10. No spectators will be allowed - seeing as whole teams have to invited, only 10 at maximum may be on at once - noone will be allowed to spectate. If anyone is spectating after being warned, your team will be DQed.
Registration
  • Team registrations - https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1Qz1JNs8AyVSgW-MUWOEx5pIZLu-pWtn_oXZuzK2iH50/viewform
Important Dates
  • Registration opened - October the 3rd 8PM BST
  • Registration closes - October the 5th 7PM BST
  • Teams confirmed and group assignments - October the 5th 8PM BST
  • Group stages - October the 10th (See below for times)
  • Invitationals - October the 18th (See below for times)
Group Stage and Invitational Times

Group Stages:
  • First Matches - 5PM BST - must start by 5:15 PM.
  • Second Matches - 6PM BST - must start by 6:15PM
  • Third Matches - 7PM BST - must start by 7:15
Invitationals:
  • Semi Finals - 5PM BST - must start by 5:15
  • Finals - 6:00PM BST - must start by 6:15PM
If matches haven't started by these times, the team holding the start back will be DQed.

Maps
  • Group Stage Match 1 = Rendezvous New CTW
  • Group Stage Match 2 = Senex Scrim(MAYBE)
  • Group Stage Match 3 = Wahiz
  • Semi Finals = Senex CTW
  • Final = Wahiz
