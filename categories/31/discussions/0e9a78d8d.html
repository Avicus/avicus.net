Throwback Map Reassessment by Legoche July 20, 2016 at 6:07 PM UTC

Hello,

As you know, we've recently just had a throwback map reassessment. The current throwback maps have either been left Unchanged, Edited or Removed.

We are going to spread the throwback maps onto the other DTM servers. The main reason for doing this is so that hopefully more people will get a chance to play on other maps and game modes. Let's be truthful, alot of the current throwback maps aren't enjoyable to play and have bad gameplay. Although the community will uproar because of throwback's changes, it still needs to be done in order to make progress.

Maps Removed:

  • Abandoned Park 1
-The map was very difficult to defend on and usually ended quickly.

  • Fantasy
-The map was very big and laggy, favoring one team to the other.

  • Crusade
-The map was either very stalematey, ended quickly or was a killfarm mess.

  • Honeycomb
-The map had confusing gameplay and was extremely hard to defend on.

  • Frostbite
-This map was very stale and had alot of killfarming. It was also a very laggy map as players would break snow.

  • Justice 1
-The map was very hard to push and didn't have very good gameplay at all. It was very crowded and small with too much void.

  • Ravanged
-This map was very stale as players could destroy the monument's tower and leave the monument 30 blocks in the air. The gameplay was also boring due to the 2 islands being very far apart.

  • Rendezvous 2
-The map's layout didn't work at all for CTW. It was never defended and usually was a race to who could triple cap first.

  • Rigor Mortis
-The map was either very stale or rushed quickly. The map was very boring to play and had a bad layout.

  • Sandstorm
-The map was rushed too quickly and had bad PvP space. The saplings were very overpowered and the outer ring had no purpose other than to cause lag.


Maps Unchanged:
  • Fissura
  • Clay Wars
  • Gladiator
  • Halcyon 1
  • Rendezvous 1
  • Senex 1

Map Edits:

  • Bleened Rings
-Back monument has been removed.
-Middle has been remade to allow for control and to allow for more PvP space.
-Iron has been moved to the back.
-Useless paths have been removed.
-Map has been aesthetically made nicer.
-Height Limit has been lowered.

  • Campi Erbosi
-Spawn completely remade (There are chests with defense supplies, just like the original)
-Paths have been made wider to allow for more PvP space and a new path to the monument has been added.
-Monuments have been completely reworked and are an easier shape to defend.
-Middle has been remade to allow for more PvP space and better map control.
-Height Limit has been lowered.
-Aesthetics have been improved.

  • Castle Adventure
-The public version has been switched with the TE version which removed overpowered punch bows, added defense supplies and removed alot of useless clouds.

  • Formorgar
-The paths have been made wider.
-Iron has been moved to the front tower.
-One tower has been removed.
-Monument islands are no longer block placable.
-Middle has been made easier to control.
-Map aesthetics have been made nicer and height limit has been lower.

  • Luar
-Spawn has been remade and it is now harder to shoot players bridging to monuments.
-Diamond count has been lowered.
-Paths have been widened.
-Monument islands have been remade to influence defense.
-Frontlines now link to the side iron to make the map easier to push.
-From testing, most games had a defender or a mid skybridge.
-Map has been made aesthetically nicer and height limit has been lowered.

  • Senex 2
-Iron has been removed from the main island
-Monument islands have been remade to influence defense, they have also been moved closer to the main island.
-Four iron blocks have been added to each monument
-Map's aesthetics have been improved and height limit lowered.

  • Ternio
-Extra side islands added to influence skybridging.
-Iron has been made neater
-Map's aesthetics have been improved and height limit lowered.


Maps that will be edited in the future:

  • Quintus
  • CherrylandMC
  • Dust Devils

That's all, these change will take place in Atlas. Please do not start a flamewar, these maps may be edited slightly still but we are not reverting these changes. If you have any questions regarding any of these changes, feel free to message me or comment below and a Map Developer will respond.
