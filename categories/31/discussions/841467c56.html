Scenarios:
Soul Brothers: Before the game, 3 worlds would have to be prepared (let's call them UHC1, UHC2, UHC3). Worlds UHC1 and UHC2 would have the same seed, UHC3 does not. Nether would be off. One teammate is scattered in world UHC1 and the other one in UHC2 (with different starting positions). They can tell each other where the ores could be found, where's a mineshaft etc. Obviously, they can die during this period to pvp/pve. After 1 hour, all players would be rescattered in the 3rd world (as a team, now reunited) and game would progress normally.

Rewarding Longshots: When shooting and hitting people with an arrow from a variable distance, you will be rewarded with various different items.

  • 30-49 Block Longshots: 1 iron ingot

  • 50-99 Block Longshots: 1 iron ingot + 1 gold ingot

  • 100 - 199 Block Longshots: 1 diamond + 1 gold ingot + 1 iron ingot

  • 200+ Block Longshots: 5 diamonds + 3 gold ingots + 2 iron ingots


Paranoia: When mining diamond or gold, healing or taking damage, making an enchantment table or anvil, your cords will be broadcasted in chat.
