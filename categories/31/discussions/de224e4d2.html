Armour changes on certain maps! by Robin_DD_J October 24, 2015 at 4:10 PM UTC

Hello Avicus,

The Map developers have decided to start removing most of the leather armour on certain maps. All you now got is an leather helmet to show what team you're playing in.
  We decided to change the armour to provide better gameplay with having people making armour. We believe that if people start making armour gameplay will be better, kill farmers won't have it as easy as they did and rushers would actually get a challenge.
  These changes will soon be on the server :)

If you disagree with this, please explain why bellow. We're always listening to you, but please address the issue with somewhat kind words. Don't get too offensive.

Regards.
