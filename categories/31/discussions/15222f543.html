Hall of Fame [HoF] by Fouled September 21, 2016 at 1:09 AM UTC

From the release of Atlas, the Hall of Fame was delayed due to a problem with teleportation to get to the area. We've recently added portals with the use of command blocks to teleport to the HoF.

With this added, the Hall of Fame is now accessible, although there is a slight problem (doesn't change the fact that you can still access it). The yaw & pitch coordinates don't really work with command blocks so you probably won't teleport facing the correct way. But for now, at least you can get there.

Have fun.
