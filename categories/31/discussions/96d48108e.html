Community Update - May 22nd, 2016 by Arigenn May 22, 2016 at 11:05 PM UTC

Ω Community Update Ω

By  Administration team

Recent Promotions / Demotions

There have been quite a bit of promotions and demotions / retirements  since our last official community update.

- Tomqss has been promoted to Junior Moderator
- Wulfcaspice has been promoted to Map Developer
- Hete has stepped down from Moderator
- Sayah has stepped down from Translator and Referee
- vladthegreat100 has been demoted from Media

Tournament News

New tournament announcements have been made! Tazz has created an information post for the upcoming tournament called "Battle of Eternity". The event team has been working hard to make this happen, and finally it has been announced that the tournament will start on June 1st. If you have any questions regarding this tournament, Edans has made a Q&A post. 

"Ask the Senior" sub forum

Ask the Senior sub forum will be completely dedicated to allowing community members to ask any questions they may have for the Senior staff team. Please keep in mind that troll questions will be ignored! All staff member complaints must still be sent to support@avicus.net

Avicus Public Relations

A new twitter has been created to allow the senior staff team to advertise a lot more. This twitter will server as a contact resource to gain youtuber sponsors, and to furthermore show off Avicus's progress (lets get more players!) It is @AvicusPR

Shortened policies!

New shortened policies have been created by Slicy and Arigenn to make them more manageable, and easier to read. All staff are expected to follow these, and you may find them here. 

Avicus Community Slack

The Administrator team is still encouraging you all to join community slack, just remember to contact an admin to be added to it! Thanks to PieZ, we have official rules in place now that users will have to stick to, although they are quite straight forward. In addition to this Jahaj has been promoted to Community Administrator!

General Rules:
  • No links to pornography, phishing sites, keyloggers, scams, etc.(3 Points)
  • No harassing users in all chats(Depends how bad. Talk to over staff about it)
  • No rumors/drama. We're trying to get away from that.(1 Point)
  • Try to remain calm and civilized in situations.(1 Point)
  • No death threats or threatening to Ddos (3 Points)
  • We have a three strike limit. These strikes will be removed if the strike was given on an older date and the staff feels it has been a long enough time
  • Making a leaving thread will get you kicked out of slack. Unless stated that you do not wish to leave.
  • Each room has different levels of it being moderated. Low moderated means the channel is very lenient. Medium moderated means the channel will be regularly moderated. Highly moderated means it will be very strict with moderators .

Channels

  • #announcements(Highly Moderated) •Do not post in this channel unless otherwise told to by an admin. If you wish to discuss what is said on the announcements, please talk about it in #general
  • #avicus(Medium Moderated)
  • #competitive (Medium moderated)
  • #feedback(Highly Moderated)
  • #games(Medium Moderated)
  • #general(Medium Moderated)
  • #help (Highly moderated)
  • #map-making (Medium moderated)
  • #music
    • •Do not post Music that is harmful to others ears or disturbing
  • #random(Low Moderated)

That's all for now!
