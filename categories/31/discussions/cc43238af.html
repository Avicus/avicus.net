Staff Spotlight #6 - Sulorak by badgg November 24, 2014 at 6:11 AM UTC

1. What is your username in minecraft and what is your favorite game on Avicus?
Hey, my name is Sulorak, but I'm often known by my real name, Michael.  My name was made because I decided to spell a Swedish name backwards. My favorite gamemode is Nebula, just like everyone else, but I still have a sweet spot for Walls and Nexus, which I still take some time to enjoy. 

2. What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?
I'm a pretty busy guy outside of Avicus.  I'm a stock investor, Tae Kwon Do instructor, Fencer, Martial Artist and Debater.  In my free time, I really enjoy playing on my computer, hanging out with da bae, chilling on Avicus, coding, and working with networks and such.

 3. Where are you from?
'MURICA 
I'm from Oklahoma, in a college town of about 50,000 people full of hillbillies and rednecks.  Some people say they can tell I live in Oklahoma by my accent, which I still can't believe. 

4. Are you in school, or do you have a job?    
I'm in High School, but I have a job as a stock investor, hired by myself, and that is how I get my own money.  I quite enjoy it, but it takes up a lot of time.  When I grow up, I will be studying to be a lawyer.

5. Who or what inspires you the most in your life?
OBAMA!
Just kidding, my inspiration comes from my Government teacher, who I have known for many years, and I have looked up to since I was a child.

6. How do you contribute to the Avicus as a staff member?
Aside from doing general Moderating, I do a lot of networking-related things, and I also assist players who clicked on links put in chat that are hazardous.  I also do a lot of work with Staff management.

7. What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating or managing?
"Sulorak ur hot"  "Can you unban me?"  "DDoS?"  "r u hacking?" "can u ban this guy?"  "are u watching him?"

8. Anything else you want to mention?
I'm a country boy.
