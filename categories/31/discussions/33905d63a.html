Well... This is it... by Spoookeh April 1, 2015 at 9:04 PM UTC

Hello my fellow Avicus players,I would like to formally announce that I'll be leaving this Minecraft server... and not coming back. It isn't because I've grown bored or people have started ignoring me etc etc. I just feel like its time for me to move on. I have had some great memories here that will stay with me forever and I'm thankful to all of the great people I've met along the way, some off the top of my head:
Pizza334, Burdie (JustBudah), ImRaging, Sunny_ , RefuseToSink, Emmaye, D00ZiE, Gummi3, MLGPorky, vladthegreat100 and the list goes on and on and on and on (sorry if I missed out anyone... :))
Thank you to past and present staff members for creating a unique and compelling atmosphere and also thank you to the map developers for making Avicus run so smoothly over the years.Well,
I remember every day (well most days) since I was introduced to this server during the good old MCZone days when my friends (IRL) wanted me to play Sky Wars and I came across Nebula. Since then I have ranked up 2 prestiges!!!

Thank you all for making these 2 years a fantastic time for me... farewell Avicus!
