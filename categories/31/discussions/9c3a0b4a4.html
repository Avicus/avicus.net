RCMP_Mountie September 9, 2014 at 7:09 AM UTC

GitHub is a site used by programmers, where you share repositories, store programs, code, and track bugs.

On the Avicus side of things, you create an account and post any Avicus related bugs in the "New issue" or you can confirm/discuss about current issues in the "Open" section here: https://github.com/Avicus/Issues/issues
