Some important Announcements - April 1st by Arigenn April 1, 2016 at 1:04 PM UTC

Hello Avicus,
I have a very important things that I want to address on April 1st, 2016.
First off all, I would like to announce that me and peachy, after long discussions have decided we no longer wish to be administrators of avicus. Its a rather stressful job, so me and peachy will be migrating to a more low-key server called PonyCraft, where we have secured ourselves administrative jobs, due to how successful we have been at Avicus.
Unfortunately, on behalf of me and Peachy, I will be saying goodbye, and farewell to Avicus, and that our new Administrators treat the server well, and ensure that our server gets a lot better. On that note, our first Administrator that will be promoted is Iron, please congratulate him. Now, our second one will be a very successful moderator, for the first time in Avicus history, a moderator will be skipping senior moderator, and manager to become Administrator. We have chosen him based off of his great work ethic, his great attitude, and most importantly his compatibility with our other new Administrator Iron. These two as a pair will definitely fix Avicus as a whole, and I am very excited to congratulate both of them.
Along with this announcement thread, me and peachy have made a last decision before we leave, to create another new rank called Junior Administrator. This is an idea both me and peachy had for a while, and we believe that under the admins, this rank will work out great! It will act as admins in training, but in their free time they will be trying to get other servers friendship. Michael or Newcleus will be promoted to this rank, along with our very honourable demoted administrator, ParaSpider or ViceWatercolour! This means that our manager position is open, and we have decided that we will be promoted Posighdun, and GrapeSmoothie to this position.
Please congratulate all of the people that have been promoted recently, and even though it is very sad to leave the server, I know great things will happen, thanks!
Regards, 
Administration Team.
