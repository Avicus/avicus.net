Community Writer Applications


General Information

What are their tasks?

As Community Writer you get access to Avicus' social media platforms. You are able to post your own Creations regarding Avicus. From time to time you can get some insight what is going on in the Staff Team since we also post Sneak peeks.


Who is the Social Media Team?

Currently only Spartemex, Kylo and I are members of the Social Media Team. Before Mango and Jozz retired all the Admins were part of it. (We plan to add the new Admins).


Application Format:

  • What is your Minecraft In game name?

  • Which timezone do you live in?

  • Do you already have experience with posting on a Social Media Platform?

  • Write three detailed paragraphs about how you would contribute to the team.

  • Do you have Facebook/Twitter?


Criteria:

  • Excellent spelling, punctuation, and grammar.

  • Have some experience in posting or using Facebook and Twitter. This is not absolutely required but is a great attribute to have.

  • The length of your application. (Don't write a too short or too long application.)


Application Process:

  • Step 1: Application - You write your Application and send it to "avicus.socialmedia@gmail.com"".

  • Step 2: Reviewing - Spartemex and resepignev are reviewing all Applications.

  • Step 3: Results - About one week after the Applications close, all applicants get their responses, (We try to send them all out on the same day).

  • Step 4: Trial - If you are accepted as Community Writer you'll get invited into the slack and you can write your articles.


Schedule:

  • November 1st, 2015 - Opening of the Applications.

  • November 22nd, 2015 - Closing of the Applications. [Extended]

  • November 29th, 2015 - Transmission of the Results. [New Date]

  • December 16th, 2015 - Promotion of the Trial Writers. [New Date]

  • December /25th, 2015 - Christmas.


Questions:

Do you have Questions? Post them below and Spartemex and I will try to answer them.


Note:

This is not an official staff rank, but leaking can result in a ban.

Honestly,
Instagram would be a much better route to take than Twitter or Facebook. Instagram, Snapchat, and Pinterest are the most marketable social media platforms (just my opinion) because you can get a MUCH bigger fan base, plus there are easily hundreds of thousands of MC players on those. I believe you should really be focusing on a platform like Instagram, where you could post pictures of sales, videos of cool things to do on Avi, merch, and other cool gaming stuff in general. 
On Instagram it's about... 100x easier (not even exaggerating, lol) to gain followers too. You can follow 50 people every 15 minutes, at a limit of about a thousand a day. If you follow from other big Minecraft server account's "Followers" section, you can gain about 500 followers from every thousand followed (I'm not making this up, I've run two Instagram accounts just like this, along with a Twitter and Facebook account) which means in a week you could easily have 3500 followers. If 3500 people were seeing how cool Avicus was, there'd be at LEAST 2000 people playing Avicus in one week. 
On Twitter, you can follow as many people you like at a time as long as it's less than a thousand a day. But out of those thousand, about 5 percent notice you. It's INCREDIBLY hard to grow. There's also far less REAL accounts on Twitter, and people care so much more about Instagram. On twitter it would just be like "Oh yay another MC server following me" (again, same with Facebook) because, I don't know about you guys, but I don't CARE nearly as much about my Twitter or Facebook as I do Instagram, and Instagram's content is SO EASY TO MAKE.
And that's my rant. :)
