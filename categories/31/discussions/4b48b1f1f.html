TheMasterCow August 14, 2014 at 12:08 PM UTC

Please stop complaining about walls. We're doing all we can and us map developers are getting pressured so much because of these threads. Every moment we have we collaborate and attempt to help fix up the gamemodes and give them equal attention -- however Walls is the most complex because it needs several people to test it, needs lots of YAML checks, to make sure all the items are appropriate (some maps had this issue such as labeling items drugs or sex). Walls is getting it's attention, we slacked before but we're giving it our absolute best.
