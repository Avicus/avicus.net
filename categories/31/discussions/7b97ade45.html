Hello everyone! I'd like to announce that I will be hosting a party on the 25th of January, beginning at approximately 8:30 AM EST. This party will consist of the following:

-Plug DJ
-TeamSpeak
-In game fun
-Emerald giveaway... (I'll go in to depth below!)

This party is mainly going to be a hangout. I'd personally love to meet some new faces, have a few laughs, and just have an overall good time. The soul purpose and main goal that I hope to achieve in doing this is, to bring the community together. Never know, this may be a great opportunity to make some life long friends!

On to the giveaway details, at some point during the TeamSpeak call, I will be announcing a competition that will decide the winners of the giveaway. I'm not going to let you know what that competition is now... You'll just have to wait and see. The winner of this competition (if you requested to take part in the giveaway) will be awarded with a free Emerald rank.

So if you're interested and would like to join us, please do! I feel like most of you are really going to enjoy the time to just relax, chat with friends, and have a nice time. And if you'd like to enter for a chance to win the Emerald giveaway, please let me know down below. I hope to see you there!

Regards, -Jake
