Few Updates! by Zeusy August 4, 2015 at 6:08 PM UTC

Recently, the Senior Staff team has been discussing a lot about getting updates out there for the community to interact with, and support the staff team. Thus, we've got some new updates.


Suggestion Rooms on Teamspeak: http://prntscr.com/80s4ur

If anyone has a suggestion, you can get on Teamspeak and input/discuss your suggestions with players or staff members. All suggestions will be noted and discussed by Mods and Seniors, based off the majority vote, it'll get either implemented or denied. If any trolling goes on within the channels, you will get punished accordingly


AutoClicker is now a Permanent Ban. (Suggested by: Qwuiblington)

Although the post got locked, he made a good point. We've discussed and agreed that 15 days is too lenient for Autoclicker. Thus, it's now a Permanent Ban at first offence.


Inappropriate Names:

All names which have curse words in them will now be punishable, if you have any questions regarding a name, don't hesitate to ask a Mod+.


Have a wonderful day/night.
