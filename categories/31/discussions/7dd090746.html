New (junior) Map Developer! (jozzmcozz) by ViceTechnicolour April 26, 2015 at 4:04 AM UTC



After a lengthy discussion about a junior mapdev rank ranging from now to a year ago with several changes and finalizations, it has finally been implemented and our first guinea pig is jozzmcozz. He will be fixing YAMLs for us over the forum, being an informer for things, accepting maps at his will, and partaking in meetings/discussions. Not going too in depth of what a junior map developer is supposed to do, it's basically just a stepping stone for map developer or a rank for those who are seen to be worthy of helping out the map developers, but not yet ready/prepared to take on, in our eyes, the role. For something inbetween, this role was granted to the server and we will see a few junior map developers appear every now and then as necessary. Keep in mind, a junior map developer doesn't exactly mean you'll be promoted (but we'll of course see if they will be!)

Please welcome jozzykins to the team!
