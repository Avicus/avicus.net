Thankyou and Goodbye. by Legoche May 30, 2017 at 11:05 AM UTC

I have been playing this game for a very long time and I have come to the decision that now is the best time for me to leave.

I have been losing motivation to play for a while now and this game isn't enjoyable for me anymore. After taking a break from this game I realized how much of a waste of time it really is and that is why I am leaving.

I have risen myself in this game as a player who has been able to consistently stay at the top of the competitive scene and for that I am proud of. I have witnessed rise and fall of the staff, players, teams, myself and even the server. And to be really cocky, I feel that I have helped this server more than the majority of people. I rose from an average player to one who has been respectable in both the staff team but also in the competitive scene.

Sorry to anybody I have been a dick to, but I hope you realize that I'm not a dick to anybody unless they have done something to me or a friend.

I would like to thank everybody for this experience, all my friends, my teams, my subscribers, and everybody who made this possible. I had originally planned to write a long list saying thank you but if people want a direct thanks they can PM me, I will keep discord for a little while.

I plan to keep in contact with some of the friends I have made through this game (you know who you are), and I wish everybody here the best of luck.

I have something to say to you who don't enjoy this game. Why waste your time playing it? If you don't enjoy it you should just stop. This game has gradually grown more and more toxic and doesn't have a spark of enjoyment anymore. Take a break. You will not regret it.

Goodbye, and thank you. Enjoy yourselves.
