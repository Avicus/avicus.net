Discussion: Merging MCZone servers by ViceTechnicolour May 7, 2015 at 10:05 PM UTC

This was an idea of the map development team for a while, and I want to see what is thought here. This essentially would remove all 3 of the MCZone servers (01, 02, 03) and replace them with Throwback servers (01, 02, 03) applying to Nebula only. They would be showcasing not only just MCZone maps -- but older Avicus maps like Volcano, Fantasy, Mystic, Teres, Broken World... the list goes on. Any map that was in the old avicus servers before avicore, they'd be mixed into the rotations with MCZone maps.

This is to ensure that both ends of the playerbase are happy for missing maps, and so that the MCZone servers have more of a longevity in terms of players and fun factor.

Thoughts on this change?
