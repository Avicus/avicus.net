FAQ
Q: How long is the match?
A: Usually 1 & 1/2 hour.

Q: What timezone is this?
A: Eastern Standard Time (US)

Q: Are you allowed to strip-mine?
A: No, you are only allowed to dig straight 8 blocks if you hear sounds. You are not allowed to strip-mine intentionally for ores.

Q: Are sky-bases allowed?
A: Yes, BUT only until MU (Meet-Up), then you must come down.

Q: Am I allowed to kill others via Lava, Gravel, etc...?
A: Yes, but not before PvP. This kind of killing is called iPvP, just so you know ;)
