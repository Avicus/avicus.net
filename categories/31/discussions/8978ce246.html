What is prestige? How do you get it? Please tell me!

Mathematical June 5, 2014 at 12:06 AM UTC

You have to get Level 50 in a game and do /prestige
It gives you a star next to your name in chat and lets you continue to gain xp (At Level 50 you cannot gain xp)

Joao_Victor June 5, 2014 at 12:06 AM UTC

When you get Level 50 in any gamemode, you can do /prestige which will put a colored star next to your name, you can do this 5 times until you are Prestige 5 Level 50. 

The colored stars go like this:
1st = Pink
2nd = Lime
3rd = Aqua
4th = Light-Red
5th = Yellow

If you prestige in any games with kits, you will lose those kits until you level up enough to unlock them again.
