Game Modes Really? by Polar_Express August 19, 2014 at 1:08 PM UTC

Go Avicus! I see so many post about New Game-Modes, “What would you like to see” and Game-Modes “What would you change” and Game-Modes “What would you remove”. So many posts about game modes but we are overlooking the main problem, the current game modes have tons of work that is needed – before adding more…. Adding more game-modes is not the solution to the population problem on Avicus alone. People come to a server and they don’t just try out a game-mode and then leave, they play a few of them and see if the server is one they want to add. Currently If a new player logs into Avicus they have three choices, and currently we only have the population to support 3 most of the time. At peak CTH may have a game, but other than that Nebula has just under 200 players, walls about 40 and Nexus about 20-25 in games. Elimination none, CTH none, Avicade None. First do we need more game modes Yes! Can we support them currently No. The population is going to continue to drop through the week with schools back in and be a little better on the weekend. So if you add another game mode now it will most likely have traction problems no matter how good it is. My suggestion: Avicus must get the community / players talking about the current popular game modes. We should not jump the gun and add other games that are going to be buggy, have few maps, and a learning curve. We should without a doubt put some time into the following:
`
Nebula 1st:
`
Fix the /mgs  /friend issues: How can you make friends if you can’t add them. You need to be able to join the server and type /msg name, and whisper to them no matter where they are on the Avicus network.  People play because they like the game-mode they come back because of the friends.
`
Points Shop: Fun interesting things need to be put in this shop, get creative, what would make it fun? What about Team rewards in the shop not just personal? Think about what would help the defenders defending the monument, what about attackers – could they buy a super pick axe or an infinity bow on maps that don’t have those items? Make it fun…. Come up with great items.
`
Stats: We really should have a way to see how well we are doing in a match, kills, deaths, ets… It would add so much to it. What about a hero board that shows monument breaks / kills / fastest monument break time, etc – This gives a goal to hardcore players and friends. You need competition to keep it fun. I am sure the long time players of Nebula have even more things they would like to see, poll them and really see what would make nebula so much better. It is the bread and butter and I feel it has for the most part been put on auto pilot, it should be “The DTM game that destroys all other DTM games”
`
OCN has 1000+ online most of the time and Project Ares is the main game for them. It is super close to Nebula gameplay – focus on that. It is the big draw, Remove all the poor maps, put only the best maps in that game mode, rotate new fresh maps in every week and rotate some of the great ones that we already have.
`
Walls 2nd:
`
Fix the /mgs  /friend issues same as above:
`
Kits: The Walls kits need serious work: The kits are supposed to not be over powered, but still offer an interesting way to change the game and play style for the player. The maps and kits are what make the walls different on Avicus. The kits are what keep people playing to gain more exp to get higher kits and see how the new kit will make the game more fun. If a player gets to a certain level and works hard to get a kit and then that kit is no good or has no use in a situation that is a huge letdown.  All kits should be useful on some level and in some circumstance. I won’t go into a rant about each kit, but currently we have kits that do not work, some that are useless, and some that add no game play change at all. One kit is a single low level healing potion like you would find in a chest, awesome!.
`
Maps: The walls gets very little map attention until recently, and thank goodness for IZ and Crazy Clowns because no other staff member has bothered to make a walls map and have it published even if they published dozens of maps for other game-modes, it’s just a fact. Like Nebula and Nexus – The walls should have game Variations, but this is not being developed. What about borrowing ideas from “The Bridges at mineplex” or making a two team or 8 team walls map? How could we make the walls on Avicus be the Walls game to play? Even if you don’t like walls, if you are for Avicus, you should help develop it.
`
Nexus and the rest:  Nexus / CTH / Elimination / Avicade : We just need to do the same with Nexus / CTH / Elimination / Avicade that we should do with the Nexus and Walls. If we don’t like one game mode – incorporate it into another until the server starts to grow again. Take a deep look into the game mode and make it exciting then add new ones when we are happy with the state of the others. Develop the game-mode!
`
Lastly: Communication to the player base:
`
Avicus has really poor communication to the player base, it can’t be denied/ Just read all the posts where people want answers about different things, and no one gets them. “Things happen when they happen”. “Be patient” should be the Avicus motto. I will say this, With the New EULA changes, poor customer service will run good paying donators away and when the money starts getting tight Avicus will have a different view on keeping the player base informed, excited, and respected.
 Avicus went from 1000 players + on average to now only keeping 3 game modes going…  Servers with 10x the population keep the player base informed about what’s being developed and what’s going on, Avicus does very little of this.
`
  Everything said is to help Avicus – If this makes you mad or upset, think about why? I promise you being upset at my posts will not bother me in the slightest. I have not said anything that is not true or my opinion. I have not been rude and can back up everything I have said. I am pro Avicus and want to see the Server grow, but it should not take weeks to update maps, It should not take months to fix a friend or msg problem, and clearly we are not focused on making game modes better, we are taking the easy way out and just dropping in a new game.
`
Thanks for reading :)
