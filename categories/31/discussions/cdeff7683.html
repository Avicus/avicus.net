Thanks for everything, Cya [Retirement] by GrapeSmoothie March 9, 2017 at 12:03 AM UTC

Welp, its been fun but as you can see from the title, I am stepping down from my remaining staff position. Its been fun being part of the official team and my previous teams (Senior staff & Map Devs) but my time has come to an end.


The reason i’m retiring is because I haven't been the most dedicated person to the event staff for the past few months and the other teams since before August 2016 when I had a minor setback. Since then I've noticed I have lost a lot of interest and now I feel that I'm just wasting space for someone who will be more dedicated and fit for the position. To my successor I wish you great luck. To the Map Development team, Developers, Staff & Event staff I wish you good luck as well.


I wish I could say I would return but at this point it's highly unlikely. Life is getting crazy with Sophomore year, Friends, starting a job soon and Its all made it hard to juggle that and try to remain active here. Can't please everyone. Now I'm just focusing more on school and getting rid of my roles made it easier. I may stop by from time to time but I doubt id be active like I use to be. 


I'm not good with goodbye's but thank you everyone for making this server enjoyable. I wish everyone great luck here & in life. It's been a great 4 years here that has shaped my life in many ways. It has made a large impact on me and I will never forget all the people that made it great and developed my character into who I am. 


I love you all - GrapeSmoothie <3
