EULA Change: Experience/Leveling/Prestiging by _Gill August 3, 2014 at 2:08 AM UTC

Hey everyone! Sorry we are a little behind on the EULA changes. We are working on them as quickly as possible. A more "official" update will be posted soon enough.

For now, I have one major question to ask all of you players:
Would you like for there to still be leveling?
As of now, prestiging is purely cosmetic, and I would like for it to stay that way. This would allow donors to continue having their same perks. The only major difference would be the fact that leveling up wouldn't give you the opportunity to get kits (therefore leveling up faster is purely cosmetic). We would also add more prestiges, along with more stars and other symbols to show off. 
Also, this would allow everyone to keep their current level that they worked hard to get. There would be no resetting levels, which is my ultimate goal seeing that people have put hours upon hours into getting their prestige 5.

Comment below whether you would like to keep leveling, or whether you would like for it to be tossed. Your opinions do count! So please be specific on what you want!

Thanks, 
Max
