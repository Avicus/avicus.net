Flamingo Interviews #1 | Zedther by Flebula September 27, 2014 at 1:09 AM UTC

Real Life Name: Juha

What Is Your Favorite Game mode On Avicus And Why?: "Nexus: There is not much strategy required in Nexus, whereas in Nebula, you must use abstract reasoning in order to break the monument, or defend it. There are a lot of great people in it, a lot of them are easy to get along with and can become great friends, though in Nebula, people tend to yell at eachother a lot. I can easily get kills and kits are sort of reward for my efforts. I can PVP safe knowing my team will back me up if i'm in trouble, which is one of my favourite aspects of Nexus.
It doesn't matter if you win or not, what really matters is if you had fun <3"

What Is Your Hobby Outside Of Minecraft?: "Drawing: I love drawing, it's an inspiration to me, and i've been drawing since I was very young, I am a very good drawer for my age, and I have started to make graphic art on electronic devices for people.
Talking to People: I love talking with people all the time, that's why I am frequently on the forums, I also use social media a lot.
Sleeping: My favorite hobby, I love sleeping no matter what, it's my favorite thing to do, and I would spend all my time sleeping if Minecraft wasn't around.

What Are You looking Forward To On Avicus?: "I look forward to the update, I am super pumped for it, and I can't wait for everything to get really awesome!
I can't wait for the big community, and the revival of Avicus!"

What Country Are You From?: "Australia"
