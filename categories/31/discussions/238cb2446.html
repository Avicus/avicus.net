My Retirement. by Porkyyy August 5, 2015 at 10:08 PM UTC

Hey everyone, as some of you have already noticed and asked me about, I have decided to permanently retire from the staff team.
There are multiple reasons for this, and I won't explain them all, but for the most part it's because school is starting, and because of some issues in the staff team which I won't mention.
It's been an amazing 7 months being able to serve as a staff member and work alongside some really great people. This community is one of the best I've been a part of, and I'm glad to have met you all :)

Anyways, I'm not leaving Avicus at all. I'll still be active on TS, the forums, and ingame, but I just won't be a staff member.
Before I end this post I want to list a few people who I want to thank.
