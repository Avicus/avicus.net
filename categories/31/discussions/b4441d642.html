Some Ideas. by Vobaseball12 August 18, 2015 at 3:08 PM UTC

Hello,

I talked to Eric on TS for a bit, and he brought up some good points. I had some of my own opinions, so I decided to make this thread to see what the rest of the community's ideas were. Please put your opinions down below. Thanks.

Eric made a good point. "Why is the skill level of some OCN players greater than the skill of some new Avicus players?". He made points about TNT, and water. "People when they see TNT, they think of 'destroy my teammates' instead of 'strategic cannons'." This made me think. Why do people not know how to use cannons? I'm not talking a Claf here or a whip or sniper (this goes out to all those cannon guys <3) what about a simple whiskey or line cannon? My guess is the lack of TNT on maps. Sometimes, on TNT maps, I make a cannon and soon, people crowd around me in awe. The more people make cannons, the better chance of raising that "skill level" of the new players. What do you need to make cannons? Redstone and TNT. 

I talked to Sli, and mentioned two things. Why don't people make TNT maps, and why do people only make one type of map, really. These two things tie together. 1 reason is that people aren't motivated to make TNT maps because they are too focused on atheistics, and not gameplay as much. Let's take an OCN map for example. SSB, or SpaceShip Battles is a classic map. It does not have many atheistics, but the map is a favorite among regulars and tournament players. Please stop focusing so much on atheistics, and focus more on gameplay. We need more creative maps.I don't mean make the map ugly, but don't make it that the gameplay is so much worse than the looks. Mapdevs, I'm sorry, but don't just accept a map because it looks really nice. Test it vigorously to make the gameplay very important.

Thanks for reading this, and I hope you agree with some of me and Eric's points.
