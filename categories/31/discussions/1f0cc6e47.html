[SUG] BBCode and Signatures by Milki June 16, 2015 at 1:06 PM UTC

Title says it all; the implementation of BBCode and signatures.

BBCode is a way that makes formatting forum posts much richer.

For example, you could make text larger with [size=200]Large Text[/size], or make things in colors like [color=red]Red[/color] or to make hidden text to prevent spoilers.

Also, I'd like to see signatures implemented to give everyone just a small flair.
