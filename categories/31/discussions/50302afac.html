As most people have already said, saying "I am an admin, I will ban you." Is not allowed, since it is staff impersonation.

On the other hand, saying things such as "Please do not hackusate." And other things along that line are allowed. Players are allowed to tell people the rules. There is a fine line between staff impersonation and trying to help our players in general. But most of the players, including me, that try enforce the rules do not intend to act as staff, but just to help out the server.
