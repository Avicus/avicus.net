Social Media Applications by K_R_Cord June 7, 2017 at 3:06 PM UTC

Hello Avicus.

In the recent days, the social media team has decided to open up our doors and expand our team to become a full force in advertising the server. The past two months have been working on making the team move again and now we can finally expand. With this expansion, we will be splitting the team up into 5 different departments to maximize efforts. Every part of the team has open slots that are needed for the future. We welcome experienced and people interested in the subject of marketing as long as they are willing to learn and be active. You can apply for as many positions as you want but you will only be accepted for one if you fit the requirements. Here are applications and what purpose the different jobs.

Twitter Application
You will have access to the official Twitter account and learn the basics of marketing from the standpoint of the platform. This is a great job for beginners that just want to learn. 

Youtube Applications
You get to create videos that will be put up on the official YouTube account. You will be able to design, make and/or edit videos that will advertise the server.

Graphic Design Applications
You get to allow your great artistic skills show and help collaborate official Avicus artworks our social media accounts and other various needs.to advertise the server.

Instagram Applications
After a long hiatus, we are finally bringing back our official Instagram account. If you have a passion for advertising on the Instagram platform, this will be perfect for creating a brand for Avicus on the platform.

Advertising Applications
You will get to learn how to properly advertise Avicus on different websites and learn how to market the server itself while collaborating with different parts of the Social Media platforms. 

Good luck and I hope to see some fresh new faces on the team :D
