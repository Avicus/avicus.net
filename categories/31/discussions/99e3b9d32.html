What is the Avicus Newspaper?
The Avicus Newspaper is a montly newspaper that covers most of the most important information inside avicus.


How do I apply?
You have to send an application to avicusnewspaper@gmail.com with the following title [Application]-Your Username here.

The format is the following:
Name:
Age:
Languages you know(and levels)
Why do you want to be part of the team?

Tags:
[Denied] Your application has been denied for the following reasons:
-Reason 
[Accepted] Your application has been accepted and you have been invited to slack.

Basic Information:
We are going to have a TeamSpeak server in which we are going to work in, and in which you will get a Rank with most permissions.
