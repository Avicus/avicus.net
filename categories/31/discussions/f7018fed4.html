Porkyyy February 13, 2015 at 1:02 AM UTC

We are testing a new "hand picking" method, which will hopefully begin in mid March. Basically, this means that the staff team will be observing players in-game and on the forums who are helping others, and exhibiting the traits of a moderator (without impersonating staff), and we will keep an eye on that player to see if he / she is moderator material. If the staff team agrees on promoting said player, they will be given the Jr Mod rank, as well as be trained like any other Jr Moderator. As for applications, we do not know an exact date yet. Whenever we need more moderators, we'll alert the community. :)
