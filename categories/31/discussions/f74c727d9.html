Introduce yourself--
Hey I'm Sho, love me or hate me but I'm a moderator like I once was. I'm a pretty old player who's been around here for a while since mczone but other than that I've been playing this game on and off for a couple of years now and since I made plenty of friends in mczone that are still here to this day, so I like to hold onto them. Other than that I come onto ts pretty often so you can pm me or something if you need help I guess


​*What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?*​
I am currently excited about my summer break as it's been a while since I had a lengthy holiday and I definitely could use one. I've only got a couple of weeks left to go. :open_mouth:


​*What interests you the most in life?*​
The motivation to be successful and live a life that's actually worth living.


​*What music/art/media do you like?*​
I'm not too restrcited with music genres since I'll grab any song that I like regardless of genre, but mainly I enjoy EDM and chill vibes in general. As for movies and art, generally speaking I enjoy horror, dystopia and dark comedy, but I'll watch any show that actually has value to it. My favorite tv show is the man in the high castle and my favorite movie would probably be the silence of the lambs. Oh, and I also read creepypastas but that's about it.
