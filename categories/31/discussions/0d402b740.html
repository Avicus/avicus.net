What Is Avicus? by DiviXion April 20, 2015 at 2:04 AM UTC

Well I was thinking, what is Avicus? What does it strive for? I decided to make room for a small thought that shows what Avicus really is all about.

Adventurous
Avicus aims to be adventurous with all aspects of game play. Never something boring but if it does get boring, there are others to play. It invites players all around the globe to have an amazing virtual adventure that has never been seen before.

Vintage
Might sound off or so but the vintage; old. Irreplaceable great atmosphere. You can't duplicate a vintage item because it won't be original. Nothing on Minecraft can expect a 'vintage' server like Avicus. It's a one of a kind and if you try replicate Avicus and its game modes it offers, it won't be quite as good as the original one.

Interaction
Striving to interact with the community as much as possible and maintain a healthy and safe atmosphere for players who log in. Interacting and bonding with regulars and feeling newcomers as welcome as possible.

Communication
Communicating is very vital in Avicus. Without communication, there will be no notices and chaos will break loose. Hell will be upon the server but here at Avicus, we love talking and communicating about what we love. It may be about how to improve the server or how great the server is. One thing is for sure though; this is too important to lose.

US
As you can see, Avicus ends with US. It's all about US. We determine how Avicus will end; greatly or a slow and torturous ending. Community suggestions and friendships as well as good sportsmanship in public games all contribute for the US part. As the owners may leave to pursue their career, Avicus may end. Although, we can show how we want to end. Do we want it to be a living legacy as one of the greatest Minecraft servers or do we want it to be known as a failure? It all started with US and it's all about US right now. Everything will be determined by US.

Alright guys so that's what I thought Avicus could possibly mean in my own mind so if you think what it is all about, just feel free to post it :)
