Credits Just Became More Useful by ALM November 4, 2016 at 8:11 AM UTC

Hello Everyone!

The Seniors and I are happy to announce a new feature today that will make credits useful on a new platform, TeamSpeak!

That's right, you read that correctly, you can now use credits to create temporary channels in TS!



Just message the bot !tempchannel to get started. Once you run the command a second time to confirm, a channel will be created, you will moved, and be given administrator privileges in that channel.
These channels are set with a time limit, but have no fear, you can extend the channel up to as long as you want (at a price)!

The pricing structure is as follows:

Rank Creation Price Initial Time  Creation Cooldown Extension CostExtension TIme
Diamond 0 30m 3h 1,00010m
Emerald 0 15m 3h 1,00010m
Normal 1,000 10m 0 1,00010m

NOTE: If your rank is not listed here, you fall under the "Normal" pricing structure

We hope you enjoy,
Stay Tuned!
