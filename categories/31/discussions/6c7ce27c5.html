Welcome to our newest administrator, Fluenza! by Mango_ September 3, 2015 at 11:09 AM UTC

Hello everyone,

Today I would like to formally congratulate Fluenza (Jozz) on his promotion to the role of administrator! Since the retirement of Peachy and DoctaPop, the staff team has been run by Kylo, the senior moderators and myself. The vacant position was discussed thoroughly by Kylo and I to ensure we make the decision in the best interest of the server.

As many of the community members would know, Fluenza was previously an administrator for this network and put in countless hours to help the server grow as a whole. After leaving the team he continued to communicate with the staff team and community members extensively and it is a pleasure to welcome him back to the team. We will be meeting very soon to discuss plans for the server, and we can't wait to work with you all collectively!

Regards,
Mango_ and KyloCode
