Staff Spotlight on Acceqted

What is your username in Minecraft and what is your favourite game on Avicus?
My minecraft username is Acceqted and my favourite gamemode would probably be Capture The Wool as I love the maps and find it enjoyable!
Introduce yourself in 3-4 sentences.*​

2. Hey there! My names Acceqted and I’m a junior moderator on the Avicus network! Yes, this is going to be my second trial as a junior moderator but I hope to improve this time! I’m a defender for the Cronus team and love talking to people and asking how they are doing! I am a very excelled dance and usually dance around 18 hours a week. I love meeting new people, and if you ever see me online, don’t be afraid to drop by and say “Hi”!
​*What are you the most excited/obsessed over at the moment?*​

3. I’m definitely obsessed over helping the community out. I know it sounds nerdy, but I just enjoy helping out people. I like to get stuff done and talk to others who need someone to talk to. I enjoy supporting others and that’s why I am so fascinated with moderating. It’s a great way to help others and get noticed in the community. My fellow staff members and I are also currently obsessed with emojis on slack xd. So yeah, whenever we are in a nightclub. That is probably what we are talking about.
What interests you the most in life?*​

4. I find my elders inspire me in life. Their wisdom is extraordinary and the stuff they figured out fascinates me. Their knowledge about the past mixed in with the new inventions put together today, breaks my mind. I love talking with grandparents and their funny old stories. It’s a pleasure to talk to those in the past that are afraid of the future!
​*What music/art/media do you like?*​

5. I love to listen to different styles of music especially certain ones with impressive beat drops. It’s very nice to listen to while chilling out with friends or just moderating and playing minecraft. If anyone has any musical suggestions or songs I should listen to, please talk to me about it. Otherwise, as I said before. Dancing is an influence to my daily life. It’s basically what I spend all my time doing, I can express my emotion through just like others do thorough maybe writing or music.
​*Where are you from?*​

6. I’m from Canada (Eh) British Columbia. I surprisingly live near MidnightBeats, FrostedCupcakes, Arigenn and some others.
​*What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating?*​

7. The most common question I get asked as a moderator would have to be “When did you get junior mod” xD
