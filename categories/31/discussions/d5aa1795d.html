Plug.dj is Closed. by Derumisis September 21, 2015 at 7:09 PM UTC

Plug.dj is closed.

RIp.

Keep us plugged in!

Unfortunately, the costs of running plug.dj exceed the income that we are generating. In order to keep plug.dj alive, we need your help! Your donation will bring us one step closer to our goal - to continue operating and building amazing new features on the product we love.

Apple Music, Spotify, Google Music and Rdio are each $120 per year. Pandora is $60 per year. If we get 10% of our community members to donate just $1 per month, keeping the amazing plug.dj community alive would be just $12 per year! Our current goal is to raise enough for the next 6 months.

Without your help, we cannot continue to build the community we’ve all grown to love. We need to reach 30% of our goal by September 28th or we will unfortunately have to shut down. The average donation so far has been $10.77. Please donate now!
