Hack Detection Help: Signs of Hacks [WIP] by Stimulating February 25, 2015 at 4:02 PM UTC

Let me start off with, this is not to insult staff, this is simply to help out staff, new and old with hack detection and signs a player is hacking to help improve bans. Anything to add? Suggestions? Comment away.

Without Further Ado: 

KillAura/FF: 

-Always aiming at an exact point on the body. (It doesn't have to be the head)
-Keeps the target at 2-4 blocks away most of the time because of the kb produced. 
-FF can be good or bad, Nodus KA is really bad, vs like Weepcraft KA which is pretty good compared to to other common hacks. It varies how hard it is to hit the person using FF depending on the quality of the FF. Some FF hacks will toggle to the closest person to you in that moment, while some will stay locked to the same person until you change targets manually/toggle. 
-If it does change targets, it will immediately snap onto the next person. When using FF their shoulders will turn to hit them before their head fully turns, which can also be attained with high dpi and/or fast turns and response time with good aim, but that is generally uncommon. 
-FF can be multi-target. 
-FF can twitch and hit several people in one turn. 
-FF range can be expanded in better clients, usually to 6 blocks, sometimes 8. (Difference between 8-blocking and having a 8-block radius FF)
-Can hit players without looking at them
Notes: Jitterclicking can attain some of the same attributes as FF. 

Aimbot: 

-It's basically a worse version of FF, always aims at a certain part of the targets body. It shares many similar traits with FF, except for the most part without the auto-hitting and kb.  
-The user's body is free to turn wherever it wants, but the head stays locked on target 

Video of someone using Aimbot: https://youtube.com/watch?v=4sNuhLTeHF0


Anti-KB: 

-You take almost no knockback (kb) with decent anti. When the person is taking no kb due to their ping and/or lantency, they will usually stop, If it's bad, the lag will usually negate the first hit's kb and they will take no kb for just that first hit, like Juano in Nexus (</3). With Anti, they will slow down slightly when they get hit, but they will not stop moving or move backwards. If they stop moving when they get hit, it's generally not Anti. If they slow down, but don't stop moving, especially when critting, it is most likely Anti-KB. If it's just ms/latency or techniques, they will just drop down, not continue the forward motion, vs Anti, were they will get slowed down, but continue the forward motion. 

Triggerbot: 

-When set high it gives the same effect as jitterclicking, except due to the clicks being perfectly times by the program, it gives huge kb. Triggerbot also causes random headtwitching, so look out for that. 

-When set low, all it does it help deal extra kb, so be wary of that as well. It will look less sketch

-Blockhitting/comboing will get lots of kb and that effect 2nd or 3rd hit, Triggerbot and Autoclicker are the first hit. 

-If you jump and their head does ultra twitches when you jump, that's triggerbot


Note: Most Triggerbot users are easy to strafe, but will deal huge kb when you get in front of them. Do not accidentally take sprint resets (blockhitting, w-tapping, etc.) as Triggerbot's extra kb. If someone blockhits the first hit and gets lots of kb, it's probably not triggerbot. You'll eventually learn how to spot Triggerbot with experience. 

IMPORTANT: Triggerbot = https://youtube.com/watch?v=SYegol4Xksc 
                                                It will look something like that. If the person is good and out-cps/pings you to the point of garunteed first hit with a sprint reset, it will look similar. 

Autoclicker: 

-Same as Triggerbot, except it's client-side and not built-in. Only difference is that Autoclicker usually screws up the users dpi/sensitivity of the mouse and can cause pretty bad aim without headtwitching upwards. 

Cayden Using Ghost Client: https://youtube.com/watch?v=blZU3-BYf6Q 

Speed/Movement Enhancing Hacks/BSM: 

-Eat and block while moving 
-Sprints in every direction 
-Sprint/movement particles show when standing still
