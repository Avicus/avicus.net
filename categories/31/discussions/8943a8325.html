Hey guys!!

Senor here. So I just came up with a cool game idea. A lot of people have these "guess the" games, and I thought, why not make one up! So yeah! Mine is called guess the animal! Here is how to play!

How to play:

I'll say an animal's characteristics, or a sound that they make, and you guys have to guess it. When you make a guess, just put your answer down, and make up your own question! Here's an example.

My animal is black and white and eats lots of bamboo

Let's say I'm the person responding. I would say, "Oh, It's a panda!" Then I would make up my own question!

Do you guys get the whole scheme of the game??



Ok so here is the first animal:
