Avicus Needs You - DevHunt2014 by Arachadonic September 21, 2014 at 10:09 PM UTC

As any regular Avicus player knows  or even someone who glances through the forums, the server is currently in a state of disarray. The main reason for this is a lack of active devs due to our one and only developer, IvyCode, being busy in school which is fair enough and understandable. This is where you the community comes in.

In order for us to sort out all the major issues facing Avicus at the moment, e.g the current major downtime, lag, lack of updates/features etc, it goes without saying that we need a dev or devs and fast. There are some issues though when choosing a developer:

  1. Lack of funds: This is probably the biggest issue when hiring a developer as any developers will not work for free so finding one that will is vital as our server funds are dwindling from what I know.
  2. Lack of Trust: Can we trust this person to have control over console and other powerful commands that could potentially ruin the server? Trust is something that can really just be gained over time but if we hold interviews it shouldn't be too big a factor.
  3. Experience: If the possible candidate has enough experience and skill with working with plugins and the maintaing of plugins aswell as working in a team. This will be even harder to find with little to no budget.
So, these factors make finding a developer very very hard. Which is why I think we as the community need to stand up and instead of moaning an groaning all over the forums, do something to genuinely help the server. 

So I am asking every single person who reads this to use all of their contacts and resources to try find some good potential devs that would be willing to work for nothing but a rank and the gratitude of an entire community (with possible payment once more people donate when they see the problems are fixed). If you do find someone that might be interested in the job than you can skype me their contact details and/or their portfolio of past work and I will pass it onto staff members. The reason why I would use my skype is simply because none of this is official and I would rather I get bombarded with links and names than the busy high staff. You can find my skype on my profile here. Together guys we can do this.

*Note this is not official and I decided to make this thread as staff have not even though I suggested it multiple times. I hope this won't be locked or hinder my chances at the interview for moderator as I only have Avicus' best interests at heart and was sick of procrastinating*
