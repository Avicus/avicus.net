The community removal - Let's make a change! by Robin_DD_J October 17, 2015 at 11:10 PM UTC

Hello,
In our recent Map development meeting have we been discussing a lot of things. And one the things we'd like to introduce is something we call "The community removal". We're doing this to get the community's feedback on maps, if they would like to keep the maps or not.
  This thread will be rebooted every week with 4 new maps!

So, how does this little thing work?

Every week on this thread you will see 4 maps. These maps are being discussed if we should keep or not. Under me you will right now see four different maps. We, Map Developers would like you to share your thought of this map. Do you want this map to stay, or get removed? When you give ur opinion on the map, please do motivate why you think like you do. Maybe we wont have to remove the map and instead make a certain change to it instead!

The following 3 maps that will either be removed/stay:

Fantasy

We chose to discuss this map because in our eyes it's too big to provide a good aspect of gameplay.
We also chose this map because the monuments were poorly placed and came with confusion.

-------

Planks
In our eyes the monuments are poorly placed and is kind of hard to defend.
We also think that it's very hard to rush because of how the map is created.

-------

Port town 2

In our eyes the proportion of islands doesnt seem like it provides good gameplay.

This is when you can make a change, please motivate why you think like you do. Hopefully this will improve and create a better avicus experience.

Thank you!
