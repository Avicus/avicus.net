Regarding Screen Sharing. by Posighdun January 16, 2016 at 12:01 PM UTC

Alright so recently Tazz and myself have trained most of the moderators to conduct Screen sharing on players who we think are hacking. There have been a couple of issues recently so i need to go over a few house keeping rules.

If you refuse to be screen shared, you will be banned. No questions asked. Refusing tells us you are hiding something. 

When you are told to come on Teamspeak and NOT TO DISCONNECT FROM MINECRAFT, you don't disconnect,  you listen to the moderator. Logging off could get you permanently banned, regardless if you are hacking or not. 

When you are asked by the Staff member to come on Teamspeak and screen share do NOT delay. 

If you admit you have been hacking, before the screen sharing occurs, then we will be more lenient with your appeal. 

Listen to every instruction the moderator gives. Refusing to do something a moderator says is seen as you hiding something and a permanent ban could follow. 

As the rules state, if we find any unauthorised clients you WILL be permanently banned. 

We will be cracking down. I have a list i am going through of people who have been a bit suspicious recently, if you are asked to screen share then stay calm and listen to the moderator. 

- Posighdun & Tazz
