Hello,
I am posting this week's Community removal as Tazz is unavailable to make the new post.

In one of our Map development meetings, we discussed a lot of things. And one the things we'd like to introduce is something we call "The community removal". We're doing this to get the community's feedback on maps, if they would like to keep the maps or not.
This thread will be rebooted every week with 4 new maps!

--------

Last week's results are following:
Sector Six: Keep
Crescent: Keep
Verdon Fortress: Remove
Clearmont: Edit

--------

So, how does this little thing work?

Every week on this thread you will see 4 maps. These maps are being discussed if we should keep or not. Under me you will right now see four different maps. We, Map Developers would like you to share your thought of this map. Do you want this map to stay, or get removed? When you give your opinion on the map, please do motivate why you think like you do. Maybe we wont have to remove the map and instead make a certain change to it instead! And remember to always put the tag "keep, edit or remove", otherwise we Map developers may not understand your point and we get confused if you want to keep it or not.
