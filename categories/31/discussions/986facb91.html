To all TeamSpeak Users. Please read. by tel6331 September 13, 2014 at 10:09 AM UTC

Okay, so there has been a lot of talk about DDOS recently on the TeamSpeak server. Some people have been talking about it in a joking fashion, however, some others have been using it as a threat and a form of harassment. I would like to announce that speaking of DDOS in the team speak can now be punishable since I have had multiple players come and talk to me about being scared or even quitting Avicus because of this. We don't want to be a hostile community but after giving this some thought, it has brought a negative impact and a stormy cloud onto the community iteself. We want to be a friendly community. This is now, frankly unacceptable. 

So to sum up, all threats about ddosing, ddosing itself, or "booting somebody off" is now forbidden in TeamSpeak unless you have some issues regarding possibly being ddosed yourself. The act of talking about this can result in a tempban of around 1 week or more depending on the severity of the situation. However ddosing itself will be a perm ban no question.

If i find users are evading these rules, action will be taken no matter who you are.

-tel
