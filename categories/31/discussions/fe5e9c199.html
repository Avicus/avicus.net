For our second staff spotlight, we take a look at one of our newer moderators,  paulna.

Introduce yourself
Hi my name is Paulina, but I also go by Pac or Chips. I am 17 years old and I have never been on a roller coaster due to my fear of heights. I've been playing Avicus for 4 years or so, this is not my first account. I've been a moderator for about a month now, pretty fun!

What are your hobbies?
My hobbies consist of playing softball, minecraft (and some other miscellaneous games) and making texturepacks (which I only do when I'm motivated to do lol).

​What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?
Nothing in particular, summer is here tho, so yay!

What interests you the most in life?
I'm interested in music and animated movies, so if anyone has good reccomendations for either, hit me up :)

What music/art/media do you like?
Believe it or not I like any genre of music! As for media I only use Snapchat, Instagram and Twitter.

​Where are you from?
I'm from Texas :)

What’s your favourite thing about moderating?
Since I've only been a moderator for a month, I feel like I have a lot I haven't had the chance to experience. I'd say my favourite thing about moderating right now is getting to really meet a lot more of the community.

What’s your most memorable experience as a moderator?
Like I said prior to this question, I have yet a lot to experience as a moderator, but my most memorable experience at the moment would be passing my trial.
