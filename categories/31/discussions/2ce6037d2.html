[NEW] What happened to the Nebula gamemode by Javipepe March 18, 2016 at 12:03 PM UTC

Hello there!

Recently there has been a small change within our servers naming and apparently it has confused some of our player base, and bedazzled some of our regulars. Nebula is no longer nebula, but nothing has happened at the same time, really.

For a more streamlined experience, for organization's sake and to fix some stats issues we had, the Nebula Gamemode has split into the following gamemodes that used to conform this gamemode: Destroy The Monument, Capture The Wool and Team Death Match. 



All the maps and the server names are still in place, so this shouldn't mean any change on the way you play to the worse, only to the better. Any doubt / concern / carrots in pickle jars you may have by now, feel free to post below and I'll try to answer as soon as possible. 

Cheers!
