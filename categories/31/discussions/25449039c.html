This is just to clear things up. Yes, I am leaving.
But not for the reason some people seem to think I am.

^ That is not the reason.
The ACTUAL reason I am leaving has to do with real life.
First off, i feel as though i am not doing as well in school as i should, and i feel a large portion of that is due to spending too much time dedicating everything to avicus, and i'm not really getting anything out of it, so i feel like there's not much of a point to it anymore. I also haven't had enough time to myself to exercise, or hang out with my friends, because of how much time i spend on various avicus platforms.
Second of all, minecraft as a whole, and avicus especially has just gone to shit. More often than not there are not staff on to help with hackers, and i have to try to take it upon myself to try to get a staff on and get evidence, and i see no point to doing this, as nothing is getting any better. too many of the staff are inactive and/or in some cases incompetent. Not to mention avicus has just gotten boring and repetitive, and too many people in the community have just become complete dicks and/or mod tryhards who do their best to piss you off.
If you still think I'm leaving because my staff application got denied, you're wrong. I just feel as thought all the time i spend dedicating to this server is having a negative impact on my life and i am getting absolutely nothing out of it.
And yes, this time I am leaving for good. The only time I will be on is if i am playing with one of my friends, because thats the only time I ever have fun on avicus, cause it simply doesn't have the spark it used to.
