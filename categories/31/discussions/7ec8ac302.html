The Walls rotation was not changed, but all the maps received general improvements, headed by Micah. They are:
The height limits were raised to bring back skybases and give more strategies on these maps:
-Lands 
-Garden
-Village
-Hawaii
-Mt.Blizzard

Lapis Lazuli was added to make enchants possible before the Walls fall on these maps: 
-Village 
-Garden

The kits were completely redone. There are now 14 kits and the changes are here: 
-Builder gives 32 oak logs.
-Miner gives an Iron pickaxe with efficiency 1.
-Engineer gives 32 redstone, 8 tnt, and 4 sticky pistons.
-Heavy gives a chainmail chestplate, and chainmail boots.
-Pyro gives leather boots, 4 fire charges, and 1 blaze rod with fire aspect.
-Archer gives a bow with power 1, and 32 arrows.
-Cook gives 20 steak and 2 golden apples.
-Warrior gives a chainmail helmet, and a stone sword.
-Scout gives a drinkable speed 1 potion for 8 minutes and a splash potion of speed 1 for 2 minutes.
-Fireman gives  a water bucket, and a stone axe.
- lumberjack gives a stone axe with efficiency 1.
-Cleric gives 2 splash potions of instant health 2.
-Beatmaster gives 5 wold spawn eggs and 32 bones.
-Cactus gives an enchanted book with thorns 1.

Builder applications have been re-opened, and will not be closing (until further notice).
For more information, check the thread: https://avicus.net/forums/91e44537d

A map testing list has been created, and it includes maps made by the community, and builders/mapdevs. Map testing events will be held semi-regularly.
The new rotation update schedule has not been finalized, but it may range from weekly to bi-weekly, or something entirely different.

Thank you for reading.
