Going inactive (A leaving post of sorts) by Tynvale January 8, 2016 at 3:01 AM UTC

What is this thread about?

This isn't a post to notify all of you that I won't be here for a couple weeks due to a holiday, this is more of a leaving post. With my mindset at the moment I don't know how I feel and I can't come back if I say I am leaving; therefore I am telling you all i'll be "going inactive". Slowly I have been decreasing my playtime on the server and some people have noticed it and told me. I guess I am just saying that I probably won't be a regular anymore.

Because of all this I am ending my Top 5 skype and making a new one to continue talking to people from this community.
Add: tynvale.community

I have had to urge to do this for a while and the only thing stopping me was the community. I love talking to lots of you guys on here and you don't realize how good a small community is until you try and put  yourself in a bigger one, like I know tons of people in every game I join on Avicus and I lose that on other servers.

During my time in this community I have mad a lot of friends and I am quickly going to acknowledge that by saying some words to a few of them, if you want to skip reading them feel free.

With this I became more comfortable talking to you and the roasts started heading my way. In all seriousness I can't thank you enough for putting up with me, being there when I needed to talk to someone and just overall being a great guy!

Most of Apollo: Other than my own team Cronus, I loved talking to the majority of you. I would point out the people to personally thank them but I don't want this thread to be too long even though it is already. I just want to say thanks for all the good memories and for letting me sub for you once or twice even though I am trash! xd

There are a ton more thank you's I can say to people but I needed to keep the thread somewhat short.

What does this mean for my channel?

It will continue as normal, doing funny moments and other random videos, I just won't be making my channel Avicus based anymore. This means that the Top 5 series as well as the others have been stopped, which before I go any further I want to say thanks for what you all made that series become. The Top 5 series blew up my channel in a way I never thought was going to happen, and without it I would be where I am today. I understand if a lot of you stop viewing my channel but if you want to keep in touch with me, honestly keeping up to date with the channel is probably the best way!

So if you'd like to keep viewing me that's up to you, but if you ever lose my channel link for some reason here it is: Click me!

Maybe my final goodbye, I guess?

It feels so crazy that I am about to do this, but at some point all good things come to an end. I did a lot, spent a lot and played a lot on this server and made friends I won't forget anytime soon, so thanks everyone and be sure to stay in touch with me in the Skype I listed near the beginning. I will reply to every comment I can on this thread and lastly, I wish I put this much effort into school essays or something because DAMN this is a lot of writing!
