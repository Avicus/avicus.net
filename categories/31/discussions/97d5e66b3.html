Staff Spotlight #8 - K_R_Cord by JDKL November 15, 2017 at 9:11 PM UTC

Hello everyone! Continuing from where Crazy left off at, we are at #8 in the Staff Spotlights. Today, the Staff Spotlight is on  K_R_Cord!

Introduce yourself (4 or more sentences)

My name's Brandon. I am a project coordinator for Disney in California. Australian born and I have a certificate in coding. I am a fan of the Star Wars universe and yes, I will be seeing the new movie on Christmas.

What are your hobbies?

My hobbies change a lot. In Australia I was a very active in sports. I played Rugby and Football(Soccer). Here in America I have found a love for biking. L.A is an amazing place to bike and I love taking new routes every few days. I also like to game. I found a love for Sandbox games. Current games I like to frequent are Subnautica and Starbound. I play Minecraft once in a while and I am currently learning Pokemon from a terrible teacher.

What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?

Excited? Maybe a little off topic, but I don't find myself excited for really anything. It sounds dark but I am happy to wake up. I guess the day ahead of me is exciting. I get to talk to people, go to a job I sometimes love and I learn something new. If it was an event the new Star Wars movie is pretty exciting. 

What interests you the most in life?

Knowledge. I like learning something everyday. It may be as little as taking a faster or slower route to work or even learning a new name. 

What music/art/media do you like?

Led Zepplin is always a great sound to go back on. Not really an arts guy. I like to consume all types of media. I mainly like Sci-Fi books though.

What’s your favourite thing about being the Head of the Social Media team?

The ability to work on a project with PieZ again. Nothing against anyone else on the team but any work I have done with some of my closes friends is always a gift and even if he isn't the smartest person in the world he is always amazing to have around. 

What’s your most memorable experience as a Social Media team member?

Can't really say there was one huge memorable part of being in this team. I just do a job everyday and learn off of it.
