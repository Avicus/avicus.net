Hey everyone! The Plug.DJ team will be organizing an Alternative Rock music event.  This will be the regular Friday event.  The event will be held this Friday (tomorrow), at 6:00 PM EST, 3:00 PM PST or for those in the AEDT timezone (Saturday) at 12pm AEDT. There is no specific end time for this event!     

What do you do?
If this is your first time ever using plug.dj, you will need to sign up. Go to their page here:  https://plug.dj/dashboard   Follow the instructions to create a profile.
Keep in mind that once you create a username, you cannot change it in the future, so do not impersonate others.

I already have a profile, where do I go? 
We have an official plug.dj hangout. If you're already logged into your profile, go to this link right here! https://plug.dj/avicus  
Once you "plug" into the hangout, create a playlist of songs by searching up your favourites on YouTube, and then join the wait list! Once it's your turn to DJ, everyone in the hangout can dance, listen, and have a good time listening to your song!  

What Type of Music is allowed?
For this event, we will encourage users to play Alternative Rock to fit the theme! But please nothing inappropriate, violent, gory, or racist. Get creative with what you share!   

Who is hosting this event?
 The event will be hosted by any Plug.DJ team able to make it.

We hope to see you there!
