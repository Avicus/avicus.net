Technical Difficulties by Posighdun March 2, 2016 at 9:03 AM UTC

As I'm sure you are all aware we are having some technical difficulties with the Server and the Forums. A lot of glitches have arisen, we are aware of some of these and our developers are working as fast as they can to fix these issues. In the meantime please bear with us as we try to fix these glitches.

To help us fix these glitches even faster please report any glitch regarding the Forums and the Server in the github. Alternatively post some glitches you have noticed in this thread, glitches you know shouldn't be there, and myself or one of our staff will alert the developers. 

A list of all current glitches that have been picked up;

Website
- Manager tag is white. 
- Admin tag is no longer there. 

Server
- You do not receive credits for kills or for objectives.
- Ranks do not show up in all staff chat, /list and in punishment messages.
- No sound when you kill someone, even when enabled.
- Staff do not show up on stafflist.
- Donators cannot pick their team.
- Team colours are not displayed in chat or in tab in the starting phase of the game.
- /friends is broken.

This issue should be fixed within a day or so maximum,

Thank you and sorry for any inconvenience. 

- Avicus Senior Staff Team
