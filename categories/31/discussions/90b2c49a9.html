Holiday PlugDJ Party! Now! by Spartemex December 22, 2016 at 2:12 AM UTC

Hey guys! Many of you will probably remember the weekly Friday PlugDJ events that we had a few years ago. Sadly, PlugDJ went out of business, but it's back! And so are events!

So, I'm very happy to announce with the support of the senior staff team a Holiday Music party this Friday! The event will start at 6:30pm EST. Bring your fun holiday songs (remixes are welcome). There's no set end time, people will just filter out. Please attend! We have a great time every year and I hope to see you there!

If you don't know what PlugDJ is, it's a fun way to share music and hang out in a virtual dance club. To make an account or to just check it out, go over to https://plug.dj/avicus/. Everyone's welcome.

Feel free to post any questions below.
