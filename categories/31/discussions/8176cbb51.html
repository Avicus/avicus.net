Sky Wars has been closed by ViceTechnicolour December 23, 2016 at 8:12 AM UTC

Unfortunately, we have decided to cease current operations and close the Sky Wars server from the network.

Why is this?
1. Low Play Counts - The server was not active enough to warrant keeping the game.
2. Competition - The server offered substantially less than any other server with Sky Wars.
3. Failed Monopolization - Our monopoly is objective-based games. When mixed with Sky Wars, it didn't work out.
4. Lack of Continued Development - Sky Wars is hard to dedicate to, and the project requires a lot of work to keep it not stale.

What will happen to it?
It is likely that we may go with the original idea (the secondary result from the old polls) to add it into Arcade. However, it will be known that it will lack a lot of features and just be more of an Avicus thing mixed in to add Arcade variety. It will not go into Frenzy as a last resort to revive Frenzy, either.

What can I do about this?
The decision has been made, and we will not reverse it at this time. I have talked to an administrator as well as held a map developer vote, in which we all determined that the game mode would be removed.

What did you learn about this?
I am now head map developer, and I will advise against projects that are not priority. I will also advise against projects where the primary goal is keeping players rather than getting new ones that also keep players, because keeping players with a suggestion as such is not a long-term solution to our player count problem. We will continue further with objective-based games such as DTC and work further in that direction, which Avicus is mainly for. This doesn't mean we won't experiment with new things, however.

Other games such as Frenzy will be evaluated for player count, and whether or not anything else can be done with it.
