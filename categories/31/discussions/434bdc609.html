10 Fun Facts about Avicus. by NewVoltdrive April 5, 2015 at 4:04 PM UTC

1. The icon used to indicate whether a player is staff on the forums is the same logo that is the symbol for the teams tab on he website, just shrunk down.

2. The most expensive badge is the Greek letter for Omega, which is referencing the fact that badges were added in the Omega Update.

3. Avicus now has every gamemode that was in MC Zone except Ghost Squadron and Sky Wars. (And I believe that Ghost Squadron might be an Arcade game.)

4. KyloCode (formerly known as IvyCode, formerly known as funkystudios.), izjust4fun, and Telitubbies (formerly known as tel6331) are the only remaining staff from MC Zone.

5. Avicus has seen over 1,000,000 unique users in it's entire history.

6. One similarity between Avicus and OCN is that Show your face to the world! is the #1 topic on both forums.

7. If you jump off the map on the map Venomous. (TDM or DTM) you can see the phrase Turtle Love in glowstone under the bedrock.

8. KyloCode has a friend named Terrascape, which is the name of the very first version of this server.

9.  Although HG was said to be renamed to Hunger Games, if you look in the Games tab, it is actually called Hardcore Games.

10. The only Nebula tournament this server has had so far was not won by a team from MC Zone/Avicus, it was won by a team from OCN named Nodus.
