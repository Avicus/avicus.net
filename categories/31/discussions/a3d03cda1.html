Mathematical September 22, 2014 at 11:09 PM UTC

There are many tricks you can do in pvp but one of my most favorite trick is to strait line combo. Im still learning, but I think the way to strait line combo you must find a pattern to either enhance youre knockback or a way to delay your sprint. You can do a trick(I think?)called hybrid blocking. How I do it is by blockhitting when you come in contact with your enemy(Don't always blockhit, when you can't reach him do a normal hit.) You can also do this by hybrid sneaking or sneak tapping, this is exactly the same as hybrid blockhing but instead of blockhitting you shift and hit when you come in contact with your enemy. These tricks will enhance your knockback and at the same time delay your sprint causing an illusion from your enemy's POV to look like your hitting him from like 8 blocks.

The_Detonator_ September 23, 2014 at 12:09 AM UTC

Ok, going to take up the position of being the first person to tell you how to stay consistent, rather than how to pvp.
If you take, say, a weeks break from pvp, the first day back you CANNOT expect to be pvping well. During this day, I'd say to just practice your strategies such as strafing, clicking fast and your rushing routes around the map. 
If you're just playing inconsistently i'd probably say do some variety and try different strategies. Doing the same thing over and over will likely become inconsistent as the strategy will have changed the more you do it (Kinda hard to explain what I mean, just ask for clarification if you don't understand).
