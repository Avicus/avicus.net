Frenzy has been closed by ViceTechnicolour December 25, 2016 at 10:12 AM UTC

Unfortunately, we have decided to cease current operations and close the Frenzy server from the network.

Frenzy was unsuccessful and will be halted and either moved towards Arcade, or removed entirely. This doesn't mean that Frenzy may be used for future tiebreakers or other gamemode concepts: it's just that the traditional and standard Frenzy will not be available for now.

We apologize to those who liked this gamemode. We have cleansed this out along with Sky Wars to offer a primary focus towards our gamemodes that we are the most well known for, and shift towards success in those.
