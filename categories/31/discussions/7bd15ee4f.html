Why is Avicus not Growing? by Polar_Express August 25, 2014 at 3:08 PM UTC

Folks,

I would like to know your opinion on why Avicus is not growing and what we can do to help, and maybe the staff can chime in and let us know some things that are going on behind the scene, if they wish.

The reason for my asking is that I know with the EULA changes ( we are 25 days past the  deadline ) some servers are not going to make it ( some will step up and take it to the next level and do great! Some have already made the changes and are are doing great), and I would hate to see Avicus go down. I also see that Avicus has never regained its stride that it once had when the name was McZone and at this rate it never will, IMO. I see other servers growing, I see other servers having so many people playing that sometimes its hard to get a game if you are not a Donator and so many players you have to turn player viability off. I see other servers already make the EULA  changes and even add more to the content to encourage more players in the last 30 days.

I used to never play on other servers but now I do. I have a long list of reasons, but this is not a Rant page its and Idea board. I also do not feel this is out of place as, not only am I Donator, but I am also a Map contributor and a long time player , with many hours played.

I also see that many of the Avicus staff are doing all that they can, but it seems like some process, or some method in place is holding the mods back as well. For example, the map devs have been super busy posting maps, working on YMLs, and even getting the new rotations ready - as far as they can. They have gotten the community excited, but then many days pass and nothing, so we get discouraged. We even get a 2nd thread from them with a date on it as to when the Rotations should go into effect, but nothing happens, and we are well past that mark by days. I see lots of Polls about maps and game-modes, lots of Mod recognition threads, and lots of very good and quick answers to problems on the forums, but yet  not much is getting done and not much server growth at all - it feels like we have less people... @ 10:30pm est , we had 99 people on the server the other night. Only two games mods had people Nebula and Walls, and Walls had only 1 game going. With that said, I know some servers never break 100 people online ever, but Avicus used to be near the 1000 - 1400 player mark most of the time and Avicus is the server that is not growing, and I fear will not be here as long as we would like, if we keep going like this.

Every server has a basic set of problems and each successful server over comes these obstacles, and none of them should be a crutch for reasons a Server is failing or falling when it in all respects should be growing.

All have :  
*Every Server has to Deal with School / College back in or School / College is out.
*Most have a development staff of 1 or 2 - until you get in the 5000 + mark in avg. playing
*Every Server / Network  has Real Life problems / Vacations / Day to Day responsibilities outside of Mine-craft
*Every Server / Network  has to make Game mode choices
*Every Server / Network  has to Manage a Staff of mods
*Every Server / Network  has to develop a way to promote their server cost effectively
*Every Server / Network has to find a way to pay the bills
*Every Server / Network must follow the EULA
*The big servers have not lost much if any population - so the player-base getting tired is also not viable. Mine-craft is not a 10 year old game people are tired of playing.

So with that said, what do you think are the core problems, and what can we do to help keep our favorite server , playing strong.... Where is the Break Down....

Thanks for reading.
