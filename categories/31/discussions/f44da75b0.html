PlugDJ Friday Theme Suggestion [In Testing] by vladthegreat100 June 4, 2015 at 7:06 PM UTC

Hey everyone!
As you may know that the PlugDJ Team has been inactive lately, we all have our reasons but one of the reasons is that we do not have many people attending.
We aren't too sure of the cause but we have decided to test out something that might be implemented in the future. So here is the testing idea:

The PlugDJ Friday Theme Suggestions!
Pretty much what this post will be is that YOU guys fill out a form with your IGN and what Theme you would like for the upcoming plug dj event!
 
We thought that we should bring in You're opinions into our choosing of themes.
How it goes is that every Thursday we will be asking you guys to fill out the form with your IGN and Theme you would like for the next plug dj event. On Tuesday the PlugDJ Team will be looking through the answers on the spreadsheet and calculate the total amounts of themes given to us as a recommendation. The Top 3 voted themes will then be pulled out and the Team will be getting a final decision out of the Top 3. The decision for the Theme will be given out 2 days before the event (Wednesday)
The following criteria will be taken for choosing Themes:
    1. How many times has the Theme been used in the past weeks?
     2. How explicit will the Genre be in lyrics and videos?
     3. How many people voted for which Theme?
     4. Which one is our preferred Theme?
If you were not able to get the words with the link attached to the form here it is: http://goo.gl/forms/rcxK8kyVJh
Votes will erased and reset each week for fair voting.
No theme will be played two weeks in a row.
Thanks for voting! The PlugDJ Team Appreciates it! :)

If you have any Questions please ask below :)
