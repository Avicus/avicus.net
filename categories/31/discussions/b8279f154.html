New Survival Games lobby! by ViceTechnicolour April 12, 2015 at 12:04 AM UTC



A lot less restricted and a lot more features, with aesthetic and gameplay pleasure, we have brought you a new lobby courtesy of Playman and Tanpiku. This has things that the one previously failed to do, and in general the old one was kind of cramped. The new lobby hopes to bring a larger crowd and make waiting fun, introducing a difficult parkour course and (to come) a jump pad minigame. The YAML is up as "SGLobby" on maps.avicus.net if you wish to view it, and we appreciate your time!

A little group to get up on the server and play a few maps would be much appreciated, work went into this!
