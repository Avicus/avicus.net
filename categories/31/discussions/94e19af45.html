Well you guessed it! It is time for another one of my forum-game discussions! I have quit making the "discussion starters," and have switched to making just forum games! So todays game goes down like this:
  • First, You should answer the person's post above yours, put it in italics or underline your answer
  • Then after answering the person's question, make your own! In this case, I'm asking to write down a certain car, that you want to be rated by other users. Write your question in BOLD!
 The rules for this game are the following:
  • No spam (it's annoying)
  • List any car, it could be from the past, present, or near future
  • If you want, post your image of the desired car you want someone else to rate/review

  So yeah! I hope you guys enjoy this edition of my forum games! Remember the directions/rules, because they will make it easier to enjoy the game! Have fun!
