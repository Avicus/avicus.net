My internet is terrible as well.
What I try doing is unplugging your source of internet (your router) and everything in the plug where it has been plugged in.
Then, wait for about 5 minutes or so, and then plug everything back in.
Click on the power button, wait for all of the lights to turn back on (on the router) and then reconnect your laptop / PC back to your internet.
If that does not work, restart your PC / laptop and try connecting to the internet again.
Hope I helped!
