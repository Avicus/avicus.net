The former Administrative team always noted how they were in contact with a few Youtubers to help promote the server, but that never came to fruition.

But again, nothing much came of it.

I want to ask the current Administrative team what is being done this time round to make sure Avicus has a constant flow of sustainable promotion for the server. 

What is being done to make sure advertising doesn't fall flat again.

Also want to open the floor and create a discussion out of it. Positive or negative.
I feel genuine constructive feedback can be a crucial reference point for the staff moving forward.

dev_revs August 12, 2017 at 11:08 PM UTC

FINALLY

Okay, I can access the forums now

Let us start with the youtubers. From the start, every time I read about the admins using youtubers to advertise, I either laughed myself to sleep or jumped out my window. It is a strategy that is useless and will not work at this stage we are at. You need to bring players from sites, not youtube. Not to mention the cost some of these guys want, its overall just not worth it. I do not think it will be in the future either, unless someone comes along who will willingly do it and is popular.

On the other hand. We have had some great applicants to join our advertising team and I am very excited to work with them. I see a light at the end of a tunnel for us, which could be big. Also, I have plans for each department of the team, and we are going to be strict to make sure people are doing their jobs to the best of their abilities. Some new things include
-Social Media meetings for certain departments to prepare for upcoming events
-Having gameplans before hand and not asking things to be done the day of
-Avicus YouTube is coming back into play
- Advertising is being tested in multiple different ways, not youtubers.

There isnt much I can go into detail about the advertising at this point, but if you have any more questions please dont hesitate to ask. Hopefully I was able to answer your question.
