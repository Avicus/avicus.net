Resurrection. by OPTOBAT October 5, 2015 at 2:10 PM UTC

Hey everyone.

Firstly, I'd like to clearly state that this post is NOT to be condescending, ordering or any type of bossing around, i just think it is so crucial and beneficial for the server that I announce my suggestions.

The Problem:
Anyway, for the first time ever today, I saw Avicus drop below 10 people. There were between 4 - 10 people online for about 30 minutes. Now this is pretty concerning to me for a few reasons. I joined Avicus in it's golden prime, when the server peaked at counts of 600 - 800 players online. Even at the start of this year we were topping the 350 mark.

But 4 people. 4??

http://imgur.com/XUTWmWG

Many people, including myself, have been of the view that Avicus has been heading downhill over time, slowly but surely and seeing single digits today is showing that the server must be cusping collapse and rock bottom.

I have been around almost 2 years to see amazing characters, people, achievements, games and staff come and go, lost in the sea of people Avicus HAD. But now we really need something to jumpstart the server's life again, like real soon.

Resolutions/What Can We Do?
I know not everyone will be in full agreement with me, but the way I see it Avicus is hanging on in the Minecraft world by a THREAD. If we do not up the player count really soon I feel like the server will kind of be smouldered and not really noticed anymore. 
My suggestions vary and have probably been mentioned before, but I say we generate some ideas and stretch them to the max in order to gain recognition for Avicus.

My suggestions include:

YouTubers: Everyone who uploads Avicus videos, PLEASE push your passion, publicity and encouragement towards the server to maximum extent - try to get people to join you on the server

Reviews & Comments: There are THOUSANDS of websites on the internet which list Minecraft servers, along with reviews and comments to write. This goes for all of us: we should get out there and begin writing about our server, show off what we have to offer as a community and structured server. After this discussion I am going to go and write a handful of reviews for Avicus on a dozen different sites or so, just to put us out there. Think, if we all do it, people HAVE to see and come.

Socialize: Similar to the above idea - tell people, either on social media, your real life friends, on websites, the whole deal, just throw out the name Avicus to anyone willing to listen.

Seize The Moment: The first time on any Minecraft server is a defying one for the future. If you don't like a server first time, then you'll likely forget it and won't bother coming back. I say we try and be welcoming and chatty to any new names and just keep our notorious positive community flowing at its best.

Vote!!:
The staff have made it literally as easy as possible to vote for the server, so vote!
All you do is click the link that comes up in the lobby or click on the voting emerald and then enter your vote at the site.
The more votes a server has on a site, the more publicity and popularity it gains. Remember that. So please remember to vote every possible day you can.

These factors, I'm sure are enough to build our server a foundation of recognition and pull in just enough people to spark it back to life and MAYBE, just MAYBE we can return to the old Avicus I remember.

If you have any extra ideas or suggestions on how we can up the player count, please do not keep them to yourself, comment below and i'd be happy to add your idea to the list.

Edit: Guys, I am adamant that the staff are straining at maximum capacity to help do these things, if anything this post is to the general community, lets ALL go out and rack up the player count :)

Thanks for your time guys, see you round.
