NOTICE: Forum URLs Are Changing by ALM January 21, 2016 at 8:01 PM UTC

Just wanted to give everyone a heads up that we are doing a major backend overhaul of how the forums work. All that you nee to know right now is that the URLs of forum discussions/categories are going to change a little. This means all of the references to other threads that you have made in the past will be potentially broken. We will announce the new URL scheme later today and a date within the next couple of days. Stay tuned!
