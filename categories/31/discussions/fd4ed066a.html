Issue Regarding /report. by Porkyyy March 28, 2015 at 1:03 AM UTC

Hello everyone!

As some of you may have noticed, the /report command has been very glitchy lately.
Whenever you report a player, your game will either crash, or you will time out. However, the report still goes through.

To avoid this issue, we ask that you use the command /TELL (Moderator name) (Message) to inform us of any rule breakers.

Please note that to use this command, you must either be spectating the game, or you and the staff member must both be spectating, or both playing in-game. You cannot use this command when you are in-game, and the staff member is spectating, since it will not deliver the message.

We apologize for the inconvenience, and we hope to fix this issue soon!

-Porky
