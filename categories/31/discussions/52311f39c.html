It's been a Long time since iv'e last made an official Forums post.. and it's been an even longer time since i've done one of my dicussion starters! For those of you who don't know who I am, I used to be known as canuck_pro, and when I went by that title, I used to make these things called 'Discussion Starters.' They used to be pretty popular on the forums! I would usually ask a question to the forums audience, then I would say something like 'now it's your turn!' Now I think it's an appropriate time to bring the discussion starters back! So let's Kick things off with a bang!

  Today's Question is;



  Who Would Win?




 This is how the game is going to work:
  1. First I'll say two characters/things/people and I'll say 'Who Would Win This Fight?'
  2. Then  It's up to the answerer (One of you guys) to decide who would actually win the fight.. you can add extra comments too :)
  3. Finally, it's your Turn to create your own match up!

  • The matchup could be with Mine craft players, Different kinds of Animals, Celebrities who don't get along to well... It could be anything you put your imagination up to! 
  • Just try to refrain from using inappropriate things such as: Parts of the body, or Drugs..
