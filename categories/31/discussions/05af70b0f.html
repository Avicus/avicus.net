A Few Notes About Statistics by ALM October 6, 2016 at 6:10 AM UTC

As you may have noticed, with the Atlas update, we released a new stats display page on the website. For a while now this leaderboard has been filled with hackers and people who have logged in for five minutes then left. 

As of about an hour ago, some of this logic has changed:
  1. From now on, the default sort for the page is by kills, which should show a better ranking than the original default of K/D. (Note that the K/D sort option is still in the menu)
  2. Also, banned players are now fully hidden from any rankings display, so only players who are allowed to join our servers will show up on the page.

I hope this small update will help new players who want to take a look at the rankings of some of our top players.

Also, one more thing to note:
Yesterday, I added a feature to the edit toolbar to easily tag players (WITH AUTOCOMPLETE!). This is sure to help some of the confusion surrounding tagging.

As always, we ask you to report any issues you may find to avicus.net/issues. We received out 1,000th issue today (thanks ProfessorUtonium!) and we have closed over 900 since we started tracking issues this way.

Thanks,
keenanjt,  ALM,  Javipepe, and  Elly
