hasl July 13, 2016 at 8:07 PM UTC

We can all agree voting is a great, proven method to advertise, since the more votes you have, the more inclined people are to try out the server and the higher you are on the servers list.

The "reward" for voting though is what the major issue is. There is 2 ideas that usually occur:

1) To avoid voters becoming too "wealthy" or "overpowered" over non-voters, low rewards are placed on the voters, so that it takes more votes to actually matter and make a difference. This would cause people to not really see the point in voting, and don't vote, which is a bit of a let-down for the owner (Kylo).

2) Or they can offer a large reward for voting, which would cause more voters, but a loss in the balance of the players. Such would give the server more votes, but would really effect the players and gameplay. 

It is about finding the balance between what people want to receive from voting and what would not give a huge advantage and affect gameplay too much. 

As Kylo runs the server he can decide what he wants it to do; either choice 1 or 2, possibly even find the middle ground. There is no "correct way" to do it, just opinions and ideas.

It is about the middle ground, and this where the balance of voting lies, you've just got to find it!

-

From observation I would suggest Kylo wants the server to be balanced, EULA compliant. So I have whipped up some ideas that should match his criteria, I hope they will at least be discussed. :)

Voting reward ideas:
  • Crate keys - Let's be honest, the crate concept sounded really successful on paper, but when we discovered we had to pay to open one it was completely disregarded. This seems like a huge waste to me so why not reward voters with keys to unlock their many, wasted crates? Perhaps incorporate this into the lobby?

  • Top voter leaderboard - This would add a touch of competitiveness to voting, giving you the urge to make voting a daily habit. I haven't got an reward ideas for this at the moment, but I'm sure there are lots of possibilities. A forum badge or something would definitely be satisfactory.

  • Nexus/CTH kits - Basically the concept is that you will get special kits for voting; a random one per vote which lasts 24 hours. The kits shouldn't be extremely overpowered, but should include some fancy perks that'll make others jealous. I'm sure the MapDevs can brainstorm some pretty cool kits.

  • Vote reward multiplier - So here me out... "[VOTE] 'Player' just voted! Vote in the next 40 seconds to receive X2 rewards!" Not sure if that's going to attract people to vote, but who knows.

-

Avicus has many unique gamemodes, there is no faction style one where you can claim your kits or whatever. It's maps constantly rotating, so common voting rewards are going to be ruled out. It's difficult to have perks/rewards on Avicus, so you must give the dev team some leeway when it comes to this.

-

TL;DR - Avicus is EULA compliant, strives to have gameplay as fair as possible and has unique gamemodes meaning rewards are limited. I listed some of my ideas.
