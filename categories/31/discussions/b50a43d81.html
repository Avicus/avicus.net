Avicus PUG Discord by Wahiz December 15, 2017 at 11:12 AM UTC

Hello! Myself and falz have been putting together an Avicus PUG discord for the past couple days. More information on how the elo works you can find in the discord! ----> https://discord.gg/B3NaRRD

To sum up what a "PUG" is, it's basically 10-16 players (doesn't matter who) join a queue for the gamemode they wish to play. I dice is rolled and two captains will pick from the remaining 8-14 players. A scrim server will be booked and a map will be picked. If you win you will gain elo, if you lose your elo will go down. All matches will need proof of who won, and who lost. (Screenshot) and then tag all 10-16 players who played. 

Example: @chayana, @donalmtrump, @OCDs, @falz, and @dev_revs beat @patrikano, @EDJM9, @AtditC, @Xuph, and @Taxe

Our first season has already begun so make sure to hop on and grind some elo :D

We will be in need of some moderators to help update elo, and make sure no one is causing any problems. Feel free to message me on discord if you want to help.
