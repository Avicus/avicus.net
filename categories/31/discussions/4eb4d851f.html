? Prest wha ?
Prestige is when you reach level 50 in any Avicus gamemode. Once you're level 50, you can do /prestige and this will give you a star next to your name. All your levels & kits are reset.
There are 5 prestiges, and each prestige has a different color:

Prestige 1 - Pink Star
Prestige 2 - Green Star 
Prestige 3 - Light Blue Star 
Prestige 4 - Red Star 
Prestige 5 - Yellow Star

The star is represented by an asterisk [*]
