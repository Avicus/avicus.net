FAQ
Q: How long is the match?
A: Usually 60 minutes.

Q: What timezone is this?
A: Eastern Standard Time (US)

Q: When does a match start?
A: I post separate threads for each match, look there.

Q: Are you allowed to strip-mine?
A: No, you are only allowed to dig straight 8 blocks if you hear sounds, and in high "y" levels for cobblestone. You are not allowed to strip-mine intentionally for ores in low "y" levels, such as y11 for diamonds.

Q: What is rollercoastering? Are we allowed to do it?
A: Rollercoastering is a form of staircasing from y32 to bedrock level, like normal staircasing, but dig back up to y32 and repeat. It is ALLOWED. 

Q: Are sky-bases allowed?
A: Yes, BUT only until MU (Meet-Up), then you must come down.

Q: Where is meet up?
A: At 0.0,0.0
