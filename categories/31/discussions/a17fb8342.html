To start off, these series of topics will revolve around what 10 items you would take with you for certain scenarios. There will be one every 3 days, if we get enough posts for this one.

  • No swearing
  • Keep it on topic
Lets start off with something easy. 

What 10 items would you take with you on a deserted island?



Example:
Item - Reason
1. Water purifier - Purify water
2. Radio - Signal support

6. Knife - Skin fish
7. Gummy snakes - To eat when there's no food
8. Flint and steel - to make the fire
9. Hatchet - Cut limbs off the trees
10. Fish catching net - To grab more fish
