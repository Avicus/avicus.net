I'm sorry... :( by Zintenka May 27, 2015 at 9:05 PM UTC

Sigh... okay... my behavior has not been good lately. Pertaining in regards to the subject of MapDevs especially...

I was disrespectful and came off as coldhearted... I was rude and I often responded with "A lot of people on the MapDev don't deserve their ranks". True or not, I should've kept this opinion to myself. 

Its just that after almost 2 years of work in Avicus and old.avicus I got weary and felt that my work was not being rewarded enough. After some time I got deep-rooted anger and frustration. And it eventually exploded into snarky comments and rebuttals. 

Just know that this isn't who I truly am, I can be a kind and fair person. Its just that when I work this hard and I get nothing to show that my work is appreciated then I get angry. 

I should've gone about this a different and more peaceful way. Contacted the head MapDev or something. Instead, I let it pile up inside my heart and bad things soon happened. 

I just wanted to say I'm sorry to all the MapDevs. I really am :(

I apologized to most of them via skype, though I have to wait for em to respond...

The mods that were promoted: I might not know the reasons why you were promoted. I might not agree with the reason if i were to learn the reason. But the point is that I should've kept my opinion to myself, especially when it causes stuff like this.

But im really sorry and would like to apologize for my behavior. Hopefully we can put this in the past and move on
