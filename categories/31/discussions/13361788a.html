[Evolve] UHC Match #29 by Cyclops July 30, 2015 at 3:07 PM UTC

Welcome to the details/info page on Match #29 ([Cto6] Tower Defense/Devils Watches).

Match will be held on July 30th, 2pm Eastern Standard Time.

Whitelist will be turned off 45 minutes prior to start.

Tower Defense: One block of obsidian is placed in each corner and teams have to defend their block. If their block is broken, they're out of the game. Respawns are on.

Devil Watches: One (or more) player(s), often the host, is designated a "Devil" and will communicate with players, offering them deals. These deals may range from exchanging hearts and items, to sacrificing hearts and items for information about players. The deals will almost always require difficult decisions to be made.
