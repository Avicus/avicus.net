[Updated] - Clay Wars, Justice 2 & Proelium by Ogrey April 14, 2015 at 9:04 AM UTC

Hello members of the community.

The reason I brought up this thread is because, as you may or may not know, the maps I have listen in the title of this thread (Clay Wars, Justice 2 and Proelium) have been recently removed from rotations, however, all the Map Development team is trying to do is give you the best experience we can afford with maps. After all, you're the ones that play them, aren't you? So do we, but you guys are our main concern, as I've always believed.

So, here goes the question. Are you happy with these maps being removed from the rotations?
What are your thoughts on this? Are you willing to keep them, with or without changes?
If any changes, which? or perhaps you're okay with these maps gone for good?

Keep in mind I am only and exclusively asking for these three maps, comments regarding other maps will be ignored.

I need to know if you guys want them back or gone, and if back, with or without changes?
If any changes, which?

Please add as much detail and information as you can, as it will be more beneficial for yourself, because I'm the one that will have to work with the information you provide.

Thanks and have a very nise day.

Ogrey April 14, 2015 at 9:04 PM UTC

After examining all the feedback clearly I brought some small changes to these maps, and they are:

Justice 2
- Elevated the curved lanes, monument islands, water fountains and island with special bows.
- Moved spawn island closer (about 10-15 blocks) to the main islands.
- Custom loadouts. (Red/Yellow stained clay + stained glass)
- Reduced amount of Diamond Blocks.

Clay Wars
- Pushed monument islands a bit away from spawn.
- Custom loadouts. (Orange/Lime stained clay + stained glass)

Proelium
- Back Monument was completly removed.
- Left and Right Monuments were pushed forward, instead of being next to spawn island.
