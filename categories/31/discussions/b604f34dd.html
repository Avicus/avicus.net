Avicus Homepage Picture Submission by Arigenn February 14, 2016 at 10:02 PM UTC

Hello,

The Developer Team would finally like to update Avicus's home page on the website, and will need new pictures for this. We will be asking you, the community, to submit photos to us, so we can use them in a slideshow like presentation on the avicus home page. Submissions will open up on February 14th, 2016, and will close on March 12th, 2016.

All pictures must :
- NOT have any player names included
- NOT have an FOV over 100
- NOT have a GUI
- MUST have the dimensions of 1250 x 400

Pictures must be submitted to this google form 

Enjoy!
