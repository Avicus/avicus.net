Rendezvous 2 has been removed by ViceTechnicolour June 1, 2014 at 4:06 AM UTC

It was the worst playing map in the rotation, regardless of the pub rotated version with the feedback we got it just turned for the worst. A lot of people hated it and once we made the changes of removing the monument protection, the games started dragging on endlessly to the point I had to end it myself. I don't know how it can be fixed but it just does not fit in with DTM. Until we get more ideas on how to fix it, it's out for now. Please leave all opinions regarding that map to yourself.



As a mapdev, it hurt to look at and Evocal's first words were "oh my godddddd" as we agreed to remove it from rots.
