I am new to Avicus, and already, I have collected a few... infractions.

I don't really care about a few of them, one was a mistake, and another I don't understand, but the one I want to address is the tempban I got.

A few days ago, I was tempbanned for an hour for combat logging. It makes me feel really bad to have that on my profile, because no one will believe my stats and I will be looked down upon by the public. 

In short, it was kind of a rage quit. There was this guy, who was better than me, who killed me over and over and finally I got fed up, so when he hit me off the world, I void logged, right in front of two Avicus mods. 

I felt really bad for doing that, because I hate it when it happens to me. I also feel like I lost the trust of some of the mods, and I could never actually get to know them without this divide between us. I want to say sorry. Sorry my emotions got the better of me, and sorry I broke the rules, in front of two staff members as if I was a common noob. 

I'm sorry. I really am. I hope the players on Avicus will forgive me. ;-;

Oh, and btw, this isn't an appeal so don't tell me go to the support page or anything.

Thanks! And sorry!
