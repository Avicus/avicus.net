Avicus Memories: The Golden Days by OPTOBAT April 2, 2016 at 6:04 AM UTC

Hey everyone, at this point in time, most of you probably won't know/remember me, but to those who do, hello! :)

I have been a happy member/player of Avicus since late 2013 and along the way have accumulated many, many memories, videos, friends and experiences which have all been absolutely golden.

As some of you who know me will know, I have been heavily inactive from Avicus the last 12 months and the future is looking pretty much the same way. As more time passes, I will soon just be cruising through life, and will likely play less and less, so in commemoration of my favourite, most best times on Avicus, I wanted to share with you all some of the photos, videos and snippets I have gathered over the last 2 or so years. :)

Anyway, the photos of this colossal post are packed together for you to scroll through on a created album on the link below. Just FYI, the vast majority of the screenshots you will see will be from 2014 - Early 2015, and when you are finished looking, if you want, there will be links to VIDEOS from nearly 2 years ago as well.

Looking through such an in depth, reminiscent past time of experiences with all of you is amazing and it's really interesting to see the old players, maps, lobbies and games that were around before. I hope you enjoy looking back as much as I do, and I'll hopefully see you all in game in the near future :)

Peace - OPTOBAT

Okay so, that's my collection - for now - of some of my most cherish memories on a glorious server that helped me grow up with that little bit more happiness, knowing I had such a connected, joyful community to go online to and that each game was another memory stacked up. 

I shared all of these things, not to show off, or advertise, or waste your time, or any of that, but simply because I want everyone who does know and remember me to go back and reminisce on some amazing times we all shared, and for those who are new to the server to just know how great this server has been and has evolved to still be now.

Thanks everyone, see you all around :)
