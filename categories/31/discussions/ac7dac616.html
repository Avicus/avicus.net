The community removal | #2 by Robin_DD_J October 24, 2015 at 7:10 AM UTC

Hello,
In our recent Map development meeting have we been discussing a lot of things. And one the things we'd like to introduce is something we call "The community removal". We're doing this to get the community's feedback on maps, if they would like to keep the maps or not.
  This thread will be rebooted every week with 4 new maps!

--------
Last week's results are following:
Fantasy: Remove
Planks: This was removed by Blazikenite because he had people in-game complaining about the map really hard. Please contact a Map developer if you have an issue with this, and don't go crazy on us.
Port town 2: Keep, we might do some slight changes to the monument placement, but do not expect anything.
--------

So, how does this little thing work?

Every week on this thread you will see 4 maps. These maps are being discussed if we should keep or not. Under me you will right now see four different maps. We, Map Developers would like you to share your thought of this map. Do you want this map to stay, or get removed? When you give ur opinion on the map, please do motivate why you think like you do. Maybe we wont have to remove the map and instead make a certain change to it instead! And remember to always put the tag "keep, edit or remove", otherwise we Map developers may not understand ur point and we get confused if you want to keep it or not.

The following 4 maps that will either be removed/stay:

Dust devils


We chose to discuss this map because in our eyes it's too big to provide a good aspect of gameplay.
We also chose this map because the monuments were poorly placed and becomes an extreme stalemate if a defense is set up.

-------
Honeycomb

In our eyes the diamond makes the map very unfair.
We also think that it's very hard to get to the gold blocks on ur own island.

-------
Halcyon


In our eyes the map is way too small, maybe remake it to a competetive version?
We also think that the monument islands are poorly placed and becomes an extreme stalemate.

--------
Teres


In our eyes the map gets rushed way too easy and usually ends in the first 5 minutes.
We also think that the map is too big to provide good gameplay, and people usually end up rushing.

--------

This is when you can make a change, please motivate why you think like you do. Hopefully this will improve and create a better avicus experience.

I'd also like to say that the system will be added for this week, I'm sorry I did not implement it in the last week.

Thank you!
