[Redacted]TeamSpeak Update by VenomousViper April 18, 2016 at 4:04 AM UTC

The update is being reconsidered.

In light of our recent promotions a few updates will be done in the near future:


  • First of all, we will be making the nightclub chats donator only. This will help clear up much of the offensive behavior occurring on TeamSpeak as well as helping the server with donations. Since all of this behavior would take place in the suites it gives the TeamSpeak server more of a positive attitude.


  • Due to public nightclub chats being deleted we will be adding a Spanish Nightclub Suite.


  • We have also updated the Team Chat requirements:

    • At least 10 active players who are not banned.

    • At least 10 logged scrimmages.

    • 10 Players with over 24 hours of ingame time (who are not banned).

    • 10 Players with at least 2500 kills, or 100 objectives (who are not banned).

    • Official consent (At Least 2). (In the event that a Head of Teamspeak sees fit, we can approve/deny your request for a channel.)

    • Failure to be active on your channel will result in a reconsideration of whether it should remain.


The Cursing allowed chats’ rules will be updated to balance out the change with the Nightclub Chats. The chat will be set up where 18+ conversation can take place however none of the offensive behavior that is currently allowed in nightclub. More information on the specific rule set will be give out at a later time.


EDIT: We will be testing this for about a week or so.


-The TeamSpeak Development Team.
