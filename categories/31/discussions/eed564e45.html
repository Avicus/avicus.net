3. Making tournaments for other games than DTM, there was a post about this not too long about, but it got a lot of hate from DTM players that find walls dumb. I understand that some of you don't like walls because it takes too long or what ever, but we have DTM tournaments a lot more than we do walls. Plus Avicus can't survive on only DTM. Advertising other games would be very helpful to Avicus. And it gives the walls people to get something for us, not just you guys. I also saw some DTM people that liked this idea, so I'm not just hating on DTM people, most of you are nice. <3
