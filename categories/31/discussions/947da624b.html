Avicus and the recent mojang announcements. by 130R June 6, 2014 at 4:06 PM UTC

For the uninformed, mojang have recently said that their eula has always forbid the sale of ingame items, which could seriously affect avicus and many other servers. However there is still uncertainty over the extent of this ruling - while selling 'a diamond block' is obviously against this, are ranks also banned now? What about things like particle trails, or special perks such as team choosing in minigames. These aren't specifically minecraft items but are still part of the game... sort of. Another interesting concept is whether or not the sale of bukkit plugins, which are of course the code of a developer and not mojang, is allowed by this.

Whatever the final details are, it will have a big impact on avicus. What do you think of this decision and do you feel it will damage the server?
