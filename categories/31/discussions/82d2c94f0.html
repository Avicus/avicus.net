Goodbye... by Spartemex March 29, 2016 at 11:03 PM UTC

Hey guys! I pretty much have already left Avicus, but first I'd like to say some parting words. This server has been amazing, and it's really been more than a server to me, it's been a community. As any senior staff member who has read my applications, you know how I preach about how Avicus is a great community. Sure there were ups and downs, but it was really fun. I remember the old days when I would be on plug.dj with Cinnamon Croc, or doing a staff interview for Jozz. I guess I just wanted to thank you guys. All of you have made Avicus what it is. I feel I did my part to make it better, and now I'm moving on. I'll be on from time to time, and I'll try to stay in touch, please feel free to talk to me on the community slack, but I think my time here is over. Avicus was really fun for a really long time, and I loved being as involved as I could. I think my only regret would be that I wish I could be leaving with a "Retired" tag, but you know how it is. Being staff was great, and I loved working with everyone. Again, thank you... to the staff who work to keep the game fun, the devs that make it work, and the players who make the server real. Lastly, I would just like to make one MASSIVE thank you. Resepignev, you have been one of my best friends on the server. We worked together as mods, reffed tournaments, and you made my build server possible! Thank you. Again, feel free to contact me on the community slack. I'm not trying to leave Avicus in the dust, it's just that I'm not playing much anymore.

       As a good note to end on, and a reference to The Outsiders, "Stay Gold Avicus, Stay Gold"
