After lots of failures, I think I got it! Any testers? :o

What you do is you make your PvP KD 100%  and then make your KD a percentage relevant to that (Say your PvP KD is 4 and your KD is 3. 4=100% and 3=75%) in order to find the difference between the two KD's (amount of suicides). You will then subtract the KD from your PvP KD and the percentage you get is the percentage of your deaths that are suicides. 
Kills: 60
Deaths: 20
PvP KD: 4
KD: 3
Suicides: 5

4-3 (100%-75%)= 25%
.25*20= 5 suicides
Since you're taking your PvP KD KD without suicides and subtracting your KD KD with suicides, you're taking out both KD's and just leaving the suicides.
Say x= KD, and y=suicides 
(x-y)-x= suicides.
