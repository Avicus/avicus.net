_Phyre August 5, 2014 at 1:08 AM UTC

Suggestions v1.2.22:

Bow tactic, by: Overcast, and MCSG: Using the bow's ability to quickly fire, and knock someone back, gives you a huge advantage to get a Death Blow Combo. Scene: 

An enemy is running towards you... Pull out the bow, about 10 blocks apart shoot your target causing them to get knocked back (While in mid air) Use this opportunity to get a combo that will sure to destroy the target! (Same with the fishing rod) Tutorial:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3leu0NXOSSc (Using
the fishing rod same thing as a bow!)
Sword:
 
Using a sword, a powerful weapon. Strafing: Strafing is turning around your target, while hitting them, and possibly not get hit back. By strafing you are allowing yourself to get around your target and get hits that your target may not get due to (Combo's, Side strike). However, to defend this, click as fast as possible, and if you are in a combo, turn around and run until you are comfortable to fight again. When getting into a strafe, using block-hitting if you cannot get hits on your target (While at the same time possibly hitting them). Keep in mind that Block hitting slows you down, so DO NOT use it to chase, or fight if you have the upper hand! "Block hitting in general": Simple but effective method to use while in - Tight spaces, Trapped in a corner, or while being combo'ed. It is self explanatory, most of you know this.
Tutorial:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_kt7D-tR9YU
Void kill:
 
Void kills is self explanatory, knock a person into the void. This is effective when fighting against a really Over decked players, by knocking them into the void. Using the bow, when someone is crossing the bridge, use the bow, and aim as best as possible and hope to knock them off! 
If you need more tips and tricks, or general pvp suggestions, Please tell me! :) Hope this helped!
