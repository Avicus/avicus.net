We will announce (on this forum topic) who will be streaming the upcoming event/tournament matches. Therefore, there is no confusion as to who's twitch account to go to when the event starts.

The media team is here to allow everyone to experience the excitement of official events though there are a few rules;

- Any team member caught watching streams whilst playing in a tournament match to gain a tactical advantage (whether in first person, third person or with a delay) will be punished accordingly.
- Any player caught watching the streams and communicating with one of the teams playing will be punished accordingly.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Be sure to follow everyone on twitch to never miss out on streams, thanks!
