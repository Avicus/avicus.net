  • I covered the monument in obsidian first, on all sides, you find the obsidian by the iron behind spawn. Tell me who would mine multiple blocks of obsidian without getting noticed?

  • Below the monument I placed wooden slabs, as you know, you can't pillar up on these and they are more effective than pressure plates as a pressure plate is mined in a second or two.
  • If you look behind me, you will see I placed trap doors on the wall too, and wooden slabs above it. As you can't pillar on a trap door you will not be able to do pillar to the monument. 
  • You don't need to cover your monument, it should always be fully visible as an example on my picture.
