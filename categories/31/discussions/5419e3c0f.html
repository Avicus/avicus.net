Staff Spotlight #5 - Crazy_Creeper521 by Protel May 4, 2016 at 10:05 PM UTC

Another staff spotlight, please welcome crazy to the stage!


Introduce yourself--

Hi, I’m Crazy. I’m a fairly new player to Avicus, but most definitely not to Minecraft. Some of you may or may not know from the Overcast Network, where I’ve played for the last few years. I’m 17, and I’m in Grade 11. I have 2 part time jobs, and take part in Track & Field. Post-secondary I plan on studying photography, and later becoming a photographer. I’ve been told I’m now the 3rd moderator to both moderate Avicus and OCN at the same time, which I guess is neat.

​What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?

Books. I’m reading this really good one now, not sure if it’s the last in the series next or not. Then I have the final is a trilogy to read, and the latest Rick Riordan book to read.
I’m also super excited for a school trip I’m going on in mid-May. It’s organized by the French class at my school and a nearby high school. We get to go to Montreal for a few days, and experience the Canadian French culture. We get to watch the Opera & Cirque du Soleil, visit Old Montreal, and of course, eat Canadian French food.

​What interests you the most in life?

Music, photography, and books. If I had to live on a deserted island with only these 3 things, I think I’d die happy. Well, maybe lonely, but that’s a different matter. 

​What music/art/media do you like?

Typically I like most forms of music, but if I had to select a few favourites, I’d have to say Shawn Mendes as my absolute favourite. I also really like Illenium, gnash, Luke Christopher, and Witt Lowry. SuicideSheep is my go-to if I ever want to just listen to some music, but don’t really care who it’s by.

​Where are you from?

Canada, eh.

​What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating?

I guess stuff like “can you kick this player?” comes to mind, but it doesn’t happen often enough for me to consider it a frequently asked question.

Not sure if this one is related to moderating in the slightest, but if I had a nickel for every time someone asked me this, I’d be rich by now. It would seem when an OCN player sees me online, he presumes I’ve quit OCN and come to Avicus. So the question I get asked the most is: “Lol did you quit OCN?”
