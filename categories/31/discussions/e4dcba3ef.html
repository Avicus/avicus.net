Ravish Brothers - Created by RileGuy and Lavish

Intro:

Greetings from Cal and Riley, we are excited to announce the Ravish Bros are extending into a full on build team instead of just the two of us. After making two maps together, we decided it would be the right thing to do as we would like to work with more talented builders. The team will be building maps of any gamemode together but while this is happening, Riley and I will still be building our own maps occasionally off to the side, while still helping with team builds. If you would like to join, the application template is down below, the deadline for submitting applications is 5/2/15. Responses will be sent out by 5/4/15. The email to apply is, ravishbros@gmail.com. We are accepting 12 members to start off with. There is a possibility applications will open up again after they close depending on following events.

The name originates from our two ign’s; RileGuy and Lavish. The Ravish Bros was first created after Riley and I built our first map together with the help of others,

Folkvangr (Nebula DTM):

https://avicus.net/forums/ac368b87f

After building that map, we decided we work great together and created the Ravish Bros. The second map we have created is called

Arcadia (Nebula CTW):

https://avicus.net/forums/6a8f1c7a9?page=1

We are ambitious and creative in our ideas, but hope to incorporate others ideas into future maps hence the build team being created.
