Keyloggers by Pelpelajax November 19, 2014 at 6:11 PM UTC

Right guys, we have recently seen an increase in people sending key loggers in game. Now right now you might be asking your self, what is a key logger? It is the following; Someone sends you a link. You click on the link and this is when it gets dangerous. From that moment on, every little detail; Passwords, Accounts, Credit cards, you name it, gets logged and is recorded on that key logger. If you see someone post a suspicious link in chat, /report them straight away to alert staff on, don't click on it and if you do, run an Anti-Virus chat.

~Pelp
