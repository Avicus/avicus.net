What is your Avicus ID?
The Avicus ID shows you in order of joining, so funky would be 1!

How to get your Avicus ID:
Step 1:
Go into your profile edit page
Step 2:
Delete your Interests.
Step 3:
Put anything random in your interests box; it just has to be more then 100 chars.
Step 4:
Hit save profile
Step 5:
Your page should look like this: http://gyazo.com/ba06988c8983f54b80a5d8ffe394e2dc
Go to the toolbar and look at the address bar. The number after avicus.net is your Avicus ID! :D http://gyazo.com/4900a20b8eac72ae61208bfe2970bcd3
Step 6:
Set your interests to what they were before and save profile.
