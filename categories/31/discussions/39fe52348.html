-Becoming a regular on the forums and later, the server.

I understand this and cannot be more grateful.

This year I have learnt a lot from this server.
I have also made some extraordinary friends which is amazing!
I also never thought I'd have such a healthy and "close?" relationship with the staff and the community, it's so very awesome.



So thanks for an awesome year guys!
I am very grateful.
