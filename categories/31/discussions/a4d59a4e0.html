Introduce yourself (4 or more sentences)

What are your hobbies?

My hobbies include playing soccer, games, and watching films.

I’m completely obsessed with the Wonder Woman film at the moment. I’ve seen it 4 times in theaters, and I think they did a great job with it.

What interests you the most in life?

What music/art/media do you like?

​Where are you from?

I’m from California.

What’s your favourite thing about moderating?

What’s your most memorable experience as a moderator?

I have not had too many memorable experiences since I have not been moderator for very long, but meeting new people and making new friends within and outside the staff team is pretty awesome. :]
