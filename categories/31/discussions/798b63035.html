Some of your time please. by Zintenka September 5, 2015 at 12:09 AM UTC

Due to recent events, my time here will be sporadic with long periods of off time. Eventually, I hope that I'll be able to phase out and lose complete interest here.
Or I might just regain interest and whatnot, who knows. All I know is that I need a break from here for awhile or forever probably.

Warning: This is NOT a hate post. 

I tried to stop myself from making this post and just "phase out". Quitely with no post. But that isn't fair to me because I think that for all that I've contributed and how well that I communicated with you guys and my efforts to help create an amiable and fair atmosphere... I think that I deserve a little more communication than just an abrupt demotion. 

I woke up and found out what happened as soon as I logged on. I talked to Snow about what has been happening to me prior to my demotion and he understood well enough while pointing what I did wrong and what I should've done. He said that everyone has their moments.

I accepted these facts and I admit that I should've handled things a little better. But this isn't fair.

Staff-Mods:
You guys are really great people to talk to. I may have handled the situation with Michael badly but the least I deserved was a talk about what has been happening and a notification directly to me instead of an immediate demotion. This is what everyone was talking about when there is very little communication. Talk to me about the situation before taking actions.

When i handled the TS affair before I was involved, I made sure that I talked and listened to both parties and I actively communicated and recorded both of 'em. No bias and a lot of communication. This example should've been followed.

Just before I was demoted, I had a talk with Snow about what was happening to me on the last days before I was demoted. I faced constant harassment and opposition from Michael's and DaGoldBrick's groups. I understand why I was demoted but the least I deserved was a little chat about why these things have been happening and what we can do to fix. 

Especially since I was really dedicated to the job and I handled reports quite nicely. I talked to people casually enough and was quite active. To just... demote me randomly... no notice no less... is rude. 

It took two Seniors to tell me what happened. They listened to my point of view and what has been happening to me recently.

And this could've all been avoided if certain people, were already banned from all of Avicus' services. I'm pretty sure if anyone else tried this nonsense they would be banned quite promptly. I am appalled and angered by what I have been witnessing. Despite the risks, these things could've been avoided if every staff member opened their eyes sooner (With the exception of a few).

The reputation... the racist jokes... It's so obvious guys.

Staff-MapDevs:
I thought we were friends. You guys didn't even give me a direct notice from Skype. Just a Gmail notice... It was abrupt just like the moderators were and I thought we were more friendly than this.

Not even a "goodbye" or a "Thank you for your service". Especially since this was the position that I worked the hardest for and that I was surrounded by friendly people. I feel hurt and wounded. 

I admit that I was a bit rude to the antagonizers. But not "Excessively", this happened over a period of about 2 days. And my responses were only a little insulting. Not overwhelmingly insulting. 

Besides the screenshots of the Pokes I sent to Zacc (Which can easily look like I started the problem. He didn't record his own voice and the responses that he sent to me.). I was called a "Pussy" and judged based on where I lived. I was heavily antagonized throughout the few days and I find it hurtful that I'm the one called rude.

Even if I was rude, I wasn't "Excessively" rude and I tried my best to dumb down the situation and ignore the rest of the posts directed at me. Well, thanks for letting me experience being a MapDev atleast... heheh... *Slouches*.

Players: Try not to give the Staff team too much work and flak, alright? If you want to make posts criticizing their actions... do them constructively. What they did wrong, what they're doing right, how should they improve?

Dregs: You keep dumbing down Avicus and pissing people off... One day, you might do this to the wrong person and you will have to answer to some or a lot of people. Whether it be one person or your government. Keep at it.
