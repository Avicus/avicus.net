Minor Nexus Changes by ViceTechnicolour December 27, 2014 at 8:12 AM UTC

https://github.com/Avicus/Maps/commit/28c15ffa7f4a97abf049e1835ab3921ae338a483

As with Nexus being how it is, we decided to distribute team sizes to be the same in most maps. 9 maps have been committed by @MonsieurGray to include more players. The former teamsizes carry less, but for more players on a server for more enjoyable games and more action the change was made. The maps affected are the following: Archange II, Arkashia, Canyon, Deserted Paradise, Dragonblaze Peaks, Dredmor, Edron, Scorpio, Travincal III. Any feedback is welcome.

Following this, a few changes were made:

After a long period of spawnkilling and complaints, @Fluenza has finally fixed the regions on Travincal II to prevent spawnkilling.

The author name for Travincal II has been fixed after it's overlook by @ParaSpider. It's creators: now instead of creator: so the author shows.

Colors have been added to the chat messages on Travincal I (such as entering spawns). White -> red by @ParaSpider.

And one final (important) change; I've added an extra 3 seconds to spawn protection on ALL nexus maps. Spawnkilling is a recurrent issue and you still need just a little bitof time; 5 seconds isn't always enough to escape spawnkillers plus it is difficult to regen if you do happen to get camped. This duration should be long enough to prevent further spawnkill issues.
