Staff Spotlight #3: jozzmcozz by Howsie August 24, 2014 at 1:08 PM UTC

Today I'd like to present to you a staff spotlight from a valued member of the senior staff team and senior moderator to the Avicus Network jozzmcozz! 

1. What is your username in minecraft and what is your favorite game on Avicus? 
Hi there! My name is jozzmcozz, but please call me jozzy ^-^".  My Minecraft name originated from my Runescape account, which was inspired by my mum actually. 

My favourite gamemode on Avicus would have to be... Mhmm... NEXUS! :D
Teamdeath match has always been my favourite game types, whether it's in Call or Duty of Minecraft =D
However I still play Nebula every now and then, just to warm up for the big day ahead :P

2. What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment? 
Avicus!! (I know it's sad, but I do have a life =D). I love playing basketball, as I am a very sporty person. I've been playing bball for 5 years now? Yeah! 10 seasons :)
In my free time, I like to draw random things! Whether that's drawing my cat, myself, my basketball ring. You name it, I'll draw it! (Well, try too)

3. Where are you from? 
The one. The only. Aussyland.
That's right, I'm sure most of you know though. I'm an Australian (which is obvious if you ever hear me talk). 

4. Are you in school, or do you have a job?                      
Yes I am in High school. High school is definitely better than primary school though! The electives I have chosen are:
- Game Making
- Web Development / Animation
- Art
- eCommunications (photoshop, dealing with programs etc)

As you can obviously see (from the list above) is that I'm extremely tech savvy.

I don't have a job, however I'm going for one soon! =D  

5. Who or what inspires you the most in your life? 
Tony Abbott. Full stop; top bloke.

6. How do you contribute to the Avicus as a staff member? 
My role on the Avicus Network is to work with the Managers and Directors from an official point of view. I work really closely with the Junior Moderators (with a couple of others) to make sure they do the right thing! Hopefully we can increase the promotion to Moderator rate :D. I now also deal with support email (Yes, no more delayed responses)! However with my recent promotion to Senior Moderator, it means I have to sacrifice my twerking time in the lobby; for being AFK in the lobby ;-;

7. What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating or managing? 
"Can you unban me?", "Mr Mod, how do I apply for mod?". Fairly basic questions. 

8. Anything else you want to mention? 
Yeah one quick little thing. I just want to thank the members of the community and the staff members who have supported me throughout my time as a staff member, it's truly been a bumpy journey. I couldn't of done it without you! Much love <333 (No homo)
