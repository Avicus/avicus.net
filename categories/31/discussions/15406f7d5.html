Staff Spotlight #6 - Cammy by Crazy_ July 29, 2017 at 5:07 AM UTC

In light of recently being promoted to Administrator, we felt that giving  Camemes this staff spotlight was a a pretty cool idea. With that being said, I'll allow him to introduce himself.

Introduce yourself (4 or more sentences)
Heyo, I'm Cammy, I'm 14, 15 in August, and I'm also admin now so that's cool. I'm currently in year 9 in high school so that's cool. I've been apart of the staff team for about 18-19 months now. Apart from that idk what to put here lol.

What are your hobbies?
I'm really passionate about volleyball, I've been playing for about 2 years now and I really enjoy the sport. Any spare time I have outside of school or studying is spent practicing/training. For anyone who knows volleyball terminology, I play back court but can also play opposite if needed.

​What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?
I'm really excited for the volleyball state trials soon. I've been working towards trying to make the state team recently. That's pretty much it lol

What interests you the most in life?
I've always been interested in biology. Human anatomy and the way the human body works is truly fascinating and just adds to the complexity that is human life.

What music/art/media do you like?
It depends on what I'm doing or my mood. If I'm studying or just need quiet, I put on some classical music. Recently I've been getting into nightcore music so send help xd

​Where are you from?
Like I said earlier, I'm from Australia. If you really care, south east Queensland.

What’s your favourite thing about being a staff member?
Being able to work with a bunch of different people from different places. It's one of the main reasons I'm still here is the diversity in our community.

What excites you the most about becoming an Administrator?
Being able to do more for the server. The ability to work with more people and do more to help out.

What’s your most memorable experience as a staff member?
Too many to count. There aren't really any standout memories that I can think of. If you're ever interested in hearing my stories of wisdom, pm me and we can have story time with papa camemes. xd
