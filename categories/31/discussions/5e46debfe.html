Yes, sorry, I didn't notice this! All you have to do is upload the photo you want to imgur (Recommended) or any other photo website you want. To do that, just go under the box that says "Upload images" and click on "Computer". Then, go into the folder that has your picture in it (If your picture is in your "Pictures" folder in Windows, go to "Pictures") and click on the picture you want. Then, click "Start Upload". ONce you do that, click on the box in the middle of the screen with your picture in it. Then, once the image takes up more of the screen, go to the web address / search bar at the top of the screen under all your tabs. Copy all of that and then, finally, click on the "Insert Image" box in your comment editor (Next to Insert Link), delete the http:// (you already have it in the web address) and paste the web address into it. 
Hope I helped! If you run into any more problems, just ask me!
