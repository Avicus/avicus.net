having trouble not swearing? by toketrain May 10, 2015 at 5:05 AM UTC

if you are like me, you may have trouble not swearing on the server. it's hard to control sometimes. for a while i tried to just not swear, but it didn't really work. i quickly made a filter for myself that replaces swear words with something else. now when i'm on the server, i won't swear and get more infractions. horray.

==========================
1. download autohotkey
2. right click on your desktop, select new > autohotkey script
3. paste in the following:
:*:hell::heck
4. (optional) make a folder with as many copies of this as you need, replacing the words with different swear words each time.
5. when you double click on the icon, it'll start replacing the words as you type them.
==========================

note: this can be a little buggy when used in minecraft. sometimes it doesn't replace the word, but i've found it works 80% of the time.
