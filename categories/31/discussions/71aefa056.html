hpux July 18, 2014 at 6:07 AM UTC

When staff applications open, you may apply under the forum section of "Staff Applications". Make sure to read all the criteria and guidelines. If your application was accepted, you will be given an interview on TeamSpeak (Avicus TeamSpeak IP: ts.avicus.net), and if you pass the interview you will be given the staff rank of Jr. Moderator. After proving yourself worthy of being a full pledged staff member, you will be promoted to Moderator. Good luck!
