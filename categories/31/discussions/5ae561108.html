Slang Words Used On Avicus

          There are many different types of "slang" words used on this server. Some are meant to be funny, some are very rude. Here is an extremely long list of slang words and definitions that you will probably get bored of reading.


1. Lol - Translates to "Laugh out loud"
2. Lmao/Lmfao - Translates to "Laughed my ass off" or "Laughed my f*****g ass off"
3. Nvm - Translates to "Never mind"
4. Rofl - Translates to "Rolls on floor laughing"
5. GG - Translates to "Good game"
6. eZ - Translates to "Easy" usually used to say someone is bad at pvp
7. BG - Translates to "Bad game" usually used by bad sports.
8. Bae - Usually means something like best friend
9. Bb - Baby, Best buddy, etc.
10. Iloominatey - http://imgur.com/gallery/xhkQm
11. Gtfo - Translates to "Get the f**k out!"
12. Ur - Means "your" or "you're" 
13. K/Kk/Ok - These all translate to "Okay"
14. FTW - Translates to "F**k the world" or "For the win/world"
15. Brb - Translates to "be right back"
16. Tbh - Translates to "To be honest"
17. Gtg - Translates to "Got to go"
18. M8 - Exactly what it sounds like, mate 
19. ATM - "At the moment" or "At this moment"
20. OMG/OMFG - "Oh my god/gosh" or "Oh my f******g god/gosh!"
21. TDM - Stands for "Team deathmatch"

Rules for Avicus

          Avicus is a server with many simple rules. Following these guidelines will give you a clean infractions list. I will only be sharing a few rules, the ones that I think are the most important.

Bullying

          Bullying is perhaps the worst way to break a rule. This is harassing, abusing, and being rude to other players. This can result in many different punishments depending on the severity. Don't do it. It will build a very bad reputation.

Hacking

          First of all, never ever ever ever ever accuse another player of hacking in chat. Many do not know this, but it is against the rules. It gives the hacker a chance to turn off his hacks when a Staff member join the game to spectate them, letting them roam free. If you believe a player is hacking always use /report (player) (hacker).

Advertising

          Posting another server's IP ingame, on the forums, or anything like that is strictly prohibited. Advertisers are people who don't care for the server, they just want more players on other servers. Nothing else to say.

The rest are available at www.avicus.net/rules

Having Fun

          Always have a clean atmosphere. Be nice to players. One thing you shouldn't do is "mini-mod'. This is when a player acts like a moderator and sometimes even impersonate as one. But, this does not mean players can help enforce the rules. Make sure that other players are having as much as fun as you are.

Ranks

         Have you ever wondered why there are people with the tags Gold, Diamond, or Emerald? Well, those are Avicus' donation ranks. In order to find out what each rank does, go here http://avicus.net/shop

Anyways, Gold is the cheapest, Emerald is the second cheapest, and Diamond is the most expensive.

Gold - 25 USD
Emerald - 50 USD
Diamond - 100 USD
