When you are reporting players. by hpux June 10, 2014 at 1:06 AM UTC

When reporting players for hacking, I recommend specifying the type of hack, and only report them once. If you report a player with something like "HACKING! Mods pls come!" your report will likely not be taken care of before other ones. The same goes for anything you are reporting players for. Only report players once. Simply report someone for "Anti Knockback",  "Hacking - Aimbot" or "Spawn Blocking". Yelling/swearing in, or spamming /report will not be tolerated. Please consider this while using the /report system.
