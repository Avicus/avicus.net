Rotation Changes [Throwback] by Fouled June 23, 2016 at 4:06 PM UTC

Hello,

As you know, we've recently just had a throwback map reassessment and we are starting to edit the maps in order for them to have better gameplay. After the maps have all been edited, throwback will be removed shortly after. We want to spread the throwback maps onto the other DTM servers so there would hopefully be a balance in the amount of people in each server.

The main reason for doing this is so that hopefully more people will get a chance to play on other maps and gamemodes.
Although the community will uproar because of throwback's removal, it still needs to be done in order to make progress.

If you have any questions regarding this, feel free to message me or comment below and a Map Developer will respond.

Edits:
Quintus: Replace the diamond armour chests with blocks, make the pathway entrance a bit larger and decrease the amount of monuments.
Sandstorm: Remove the saplings at mid and remove the outer ring of the map.
UPCOMING EDIT: Remove some blocks surrounding the monument

NOTE: Do not start a flame war or you may get punished, this is your only warning.
