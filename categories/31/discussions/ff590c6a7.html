TeamSpeak >> Support Rooms by Pelpelajax February 9, 2015 at 7:02 PM UTC

Hello, as you might have noticed, we now have support rooms on TeamSpeak.


These rooms are like a Help-Desk. If you have any questions and/or concerns, you will move in here. A Moderator will then come and help you to the best of their ability. If you abuse these rooms, you will be banned from TeamSpeak. Please only come here if you need anything. It is not a place to talk to other players. If you have any questions and/or concerns, feel free to ask below. I will do my best to answer them. Have a great day :).

Just a note; this isn't for your life problems. Purely Avicus related.
