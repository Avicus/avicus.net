Community Social Media Applications


General Information

What will your tasks be?

As Community Writer you get access to Avicus' Social Media Platforms, which currently include Twitter and Facebook. You will be put into one of the two social media's, and will be asked to participate in making regular posts on your assigned social media.


Who is the Social Media Team?

We are a team of Avicus players that are in-charge of updating Avicus's social media. This team is run by Arigenn, resepignev, and Spartemex. The purpose of this team is to encourage Avicus players to check out our social media's, and to encourage the social media community to check out Avicus.


Application Format:

  • What is your Minecraft in-game name?

  • What Timezone do you live in?

  • Do you already have Experience with posting on a Social Media Platform?

  • Write three detailed paragraphs about how you would contribute to the team.

  • Do you have Facebook/Twitter?

  • If accepted, which social media would you like to participate in writing for?


Criteria:

  • Excellent spelling, punctuation, and grammar.

  • Have some experience in posting or using Facebook and Twitter. This is not absolutely required but is a great attribute to have.

  • The length of your application. (Don't write a too short or too long application.)


Application Process:

  • Step 1: Application - You write your Application and send it to "avicus.socialmedia@gmail.com"".

  • Step 2: Reviewing - Arigenn, Spartemex, and resepignev are reviewing all applications.

  • Step 3: Results - About one week after the Applications close, all applicants get their responses (We try to send them all out on the same day).

  • Step 4: Trial - If you are accepted as Community Writer you'll get invited into the slack and you can write your articles.


Schedule:

  • January 31st, 2016 - Opening of the Applications.

  • February 15th, 2016 - Closing of the Applications.

  • February 16th - Transmission of the Results.


Questions:

Do you have Questions? Post them below we will try and answer them.

This is not an official Staff Rank. Although leaking can result in a ban.
