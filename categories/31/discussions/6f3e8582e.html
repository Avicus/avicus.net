How to Insert Image in Forums! [MY WAY!] by _Laugh_ June 11, 2014 at 9:06 AM UTC

1. Make an Account on Imgur. [http://imgur.com]
 
2. Once finished Click "Images".


3. Click "Computer" and select the Image you wish to upload. A pop-up should appear from above.




4. Click the Image in your "Images" a popup should appear.





5. Click the Image again to get another Pop-up. Copy and paste Link.




6. Come back here and click this Icon.




7. Remove http:// inside. Then click and paste the link inside.


8. Press "Insert Image" DONE! :D

Note: It's best to type your message first then type. :)
 
Thankies and Hope this Helps :)
