Ogrey November 18, 2014 at 10:11 PM UTC

Volcano, Mystic and Fantasy offered poor gameplay/were rushed quite easily (we don't want 5 seconds long matches).
Halcyon was removed due to it being too similar to Dynamo from OCN.
Ravaged was removed due to it being submitted to our forums without the permission of the first (and main, I suppose) author.
Teres and Wasteland were recently removed because they didn't offer the best gameplay (Teres having these OP bows and matches being just unbalanced) and Wasteland having complicated gameplay with just one monument.
