Recent changes to SG by ViceTechnicolour April 8, 2015 at 8:04 PM UTC

Though this mostly won't affect this wave, I've been working a bit on SG to make some changes. This fixes the dominant issues with survival games and some other things that were necessary to attract players or even "internal" things such as making YAML more attractive and easier to use. Some of the changes that I have performed are documented here:

General Changes
Revert the new SG lobby (the one with fences and flatland).
Make wooden swords more common in chests
Remove duplicate max: 24 in SG ymls
Fix teams: spacing on all SG ymls
Fix colors that were not standardized (orange)
Remove Elimination entirely

Survival Kingdom
Grow the wheat crops by spawn
Bonemeal the area by spawn

Moonbase 9
Make the YML use spaces, not tabs

Survival Games 3
Fix YAML indentation
Change radius for spawn
Disallow building in deathmatch

Survival Games 6
Fix YAML indentation
Fix author misspelling

Dry Bone Valley
Credit all the map authors
Remove sign advertisements
Remove spawn pods
Fix sign misspellings
Disable fire ticks

Survival Games 1
Remove spawn pods
Add a few more chests
Fix YAML dupes

Survival Games 7
Credit ALL authors / contributors
Remove spawn pods
Add jump pads by spawn

Remnant
Lower spawn radius

Holiday Resort
Lower the lag amount
Trim region file junk
Remove spawn pods

Solar Frost
Add night vision
Remove spawn pods

Forsaken Ascension
Remove spawn pods 
Change spawn radius

Rugged Lands
Fix missing stair
Remove spawn pods

Gaia
Patch the very bad lag
Remove spawn pods

Insanity Cause
Make it easier to leave spawn
Remove spawn pods

Chernobyl 2015
Add 19 more chests

Breeze Island
Hide a chest inside the ocean turtle

Excavation Zero
Change spectator spawn
Remove spawn pods

Ocelot Cove
Ha! What ocelot cove? I removed it.

Flaire Woods
Lower radius
Remove spawn pods

So... that's basically it. Just a recap from the revisions page/commits pushed. Thanks! <3
