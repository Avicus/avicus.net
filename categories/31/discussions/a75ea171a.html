hasl July 18, 2016 at 6:07 PM UTC

Yeah as Junr stated above there are two methods to get a Junior Mod trial. The first is...
  • The application process, this is the standard process of Application -> Interview -> Trial. Ryan (Rinn) introduced the system where staff apps are to open three months after the trial period is concluded, so theoretically applications should open August or even September. However the senior staff team can decide to keep them closed for an extended period of they feel it's necessary.
The other system is...
  • The staff referrals, which is essentially 'hand-picking'. The conclusion is based on majority vote on the thread after a period of time. I believe some examples of this are LFT, WQMBAT and Mqderator.

Hmm, seems like I've covered the basics of it. :)


N.B: Some of this information may not be 100% accurate due to the fact it is based of my knowledge of polices from when I was staff last.
