Community Update 4/18/16 by Protel April 18, 2016 at 11:04 AM UTC

Hello!

So recently some unexpected changes have occurred within the staff team; I am going to clear up any confusion anyone may have and sum up what happened.

Promotions
 - Fouled and I have now been given the opportunity for the Manager positions, as the previous Managers (Newcleus and Iron) have unfortunately retired from the staff-team; I have assumed the Community role and Fouled has assumed the Server Development role (more to be explained in the future).

- Edans and Javipepe have been promoted to Senior Moderator. 

- GrapeSmoothie has been re-promoted to Senior Moderator.

- VenomousViper and Edans are now our new TeamSpeak developers, (following the retirements of our previous Managers).

- VenomousViper has been promoted to Moderator. 

- Sho has been promoted to Moderator. 

Retirements/Demotions
- Newcleus and Iron have retired from the staff team.

- SirCyaniide has retired from the staff team.

- FrozenSolstice has been demoted from the Moderator position due to inactivity.

- TimaeusTestified has retired from the staff team.

What's to come?
We are looking forward to keep making Avicus better, and you should definitely expect more changes in the near-future for just improving Avicus as a whole. We want to ensure that you the community are satisfied and we will strive to do our best to achieve proper community and staff-relations, fix server issues, acknowledge suggestions and much more. 

Thank you for understanding!

~Side note~ 

LOVE YOU TO EVERYONE WHO HAS RETIRED RECENTLY, YOU'VE ALL BEEN GREAT TO WORK WITH <3.
