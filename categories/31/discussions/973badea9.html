Welcome Ladies and Gentlemen to the return of UHC's on a whole new, up to date, and improved beauty named after [Evolved], quite the new form from the last server. I am quite excited for the match, and I couldn't have done it without the help of Juanooo for hosting the server and doing the construction work along with others that contributed (you guys are on the walls in game :u, too many to count lmao).
Now, with this said, let's get right into the action.

Server Info: https://avicus.net/forums/2b210ed35

Date & Time: Saturday, Feburary 13th / 2:30PM EST

Scenarios: 
Slave Market - Pre-game, bidders are randomly chosen and given a certain amount of diamonds. With these diamonds the bidders will buy "slaves" (the other people who weren't chosen to be bidders). The bidders can buy as many slaves as they want. Leftover diamonds may be used in the match!

(Vote) Moles - Teams of 4/5, at midnight someone is randomly chosen as a 'mole'. The moles objective is to work with the other moles to kill their own teammates and win. Moles have special chats and commands to help communicate with fellow moles. Moles may go into their own teamspeak room, Skype call, etc... if they have been discovered. They are given special items to help them kill their team.

Information left empty will soon be updated and this thread will be bumped indicating that it has been updated. I will usually do this early posting on some occasions so you may leave free time during indicated days to play!
