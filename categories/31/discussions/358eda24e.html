Senior Staff Live Q&A - Saturday 4PM EDT by JDKL September 24, 2017 at 5:09 PM UTC

The senior staff team will be hosting a live Q&A with the community on Discord, this Saturday (9/30/17) at 4 PM EDT (9 PM BST). This is expected to last approximately an hour. We will use a Google Form as our method of collecting the questions, which will be provided at the beginning of the Q&A. In case you miss it, we will record and upload it to YouTube. 

If you are unable to attend the Q&A and would like a question answered, please fill out this form: http://goo.gl/forms/SZP16oj5nqSNPT6U2. We will answer these questions on the day too; however, questions sent through the Google Form given at the Q&A will be prioritized first. 

If any questions are directed at an absent senior staff member, they will respond to it after the Q&A by text. All of the missed directed questions will be available for viewing soon after.

Troll questions will simply be ignored. We encourage you to prepare appropriate and Avicus-related questions.
