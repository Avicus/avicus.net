Ogrey June 1, 2014 at 2:06 AM UTC

Welp, I remember when I was just a regular, my first map was Justice.
I didn't take too much time on it, cause my friends UniqueAir and Meowfrees helped me making it, and we were so happy to see it got accepted!
I tried making a map of my own, named Evocus. It got denied by no reason whatsoever (not specified in the post, however I could tell it was bad haha, that was back in my nubby days) and it did take some time to be made.
Later on I spend a whole night (I didn't sleep) making Svargrond, a 4 teamed Nebula map but the comments weren't that good...
What am I trying to tell you with this?
Never give up.
Your map got denied?
Keep trying.
Denied again?
Try again. Never give up.
I never gave up, stood strong, looked for building tips, trained myself.
Look at me now, I've become a Map Developer.
I never thought I'd make it this far!
Maps need to be original, take your time on team, image the gameplay it would have, think twice before releasing it, add more things, add detail, shape, structures.
There's over 100 blocks, I'm pretty sure you guys know what to do with them. Just use your creativity and impress us :)
