  • Annihilation (TNT Large)
  • Ruins (TNT Medium)

  • Havoc (TNT Medium)
  • Subjugation (TNT Medium)
  • Extinction (TNT Large)

  • (Suggest more...) 
Requesting special features:
  • Crazy Sapper: A special permission to use TNT and redstone; Is granted automatically but can be revoked by a moderator or Jr.Mod. 
  • Battle Master: Special permission to use dispensers. Can be revoked. Is granted after 25 kills with TNT.
  • War Martial: All Redstone and TNT usage (Redstone repeaters, comparators, wooden buttons and plates, redstone blocks, etc.). Can be revoked. Is granted by getting 5 objectives and 50 TNT kills.
Rules: 
  • No purposeful spawnkilling. Punishable by a 3 warn then a 1 month temp ban. Second offenders will be PBed.
  • No purposeful greifing. Punishable by an automatic perma ban.
  • No hogging the TNT supplies or stealing. Punishable by a kick.
  • Do not killfarm, if the enemy is beaten to submission with TNT cannons then rush the objective. Punishable by 3 warns then a kick. 
  • Do not accuse anyone of greifing if they're using TNT in a remote area to make the space under an objective deep or open. Punishable by 3 warns then a kick.
  • Any other Avicus rules apply here too!
Remember, you came to this server to have fun not be kill joy.
