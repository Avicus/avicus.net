How to search the forums with google! by steven5703 May 25, 2014 at 11:05 PM UTC

(YES, if you are wondering, I DID find this on OCN)

In Avicore, they removed the handy search option.

But you can use google to search! Type this in google 'site:avicus.net/forums [topic name]'.
It might not show up on the first or second page, but just keep looking and you'll find the topic :)
