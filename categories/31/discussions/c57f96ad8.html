FINALE

Hello my children!


Hope you had a wonderful day of Avimas. Through these past days, I've realized the comments you've guys posted. I wish to thank everyone who remained positive regarding Avicus and the future of it. May I wish all of you a happy new year.


So it's the 25th and here is a quick recap of what we've showcased the past 15 days. Our first main introduction was DTC. You can find DTC in the Destroy rotation soon. Our next showcase was the lobby which will go away next Tuesday. Afternoon all that, we showed all the new gadgets and credit items you can obtain. Those morphs and sound effects and colored badges are all live now so go out and spend. You can also now access the Maps page via http://avicus.net/maps. 


About Arcade, Arcade is still being given its final touches and the Map Development team is currently working to push new maps and new gamemode concepts for this server. Expect this to be open between January and February. Just a quick recap though, the current arcade rotation is planned to have Minotaur, Red Alert, Lanes, Ghost Squadron, Parkour, and Spleef. Stay tuned for more detail by the Map Development team


For one final note, I would like to thank ALM, Kashike, and ProfessorUtonium for being the best elves I could have ever worked with.


Stay safe and have a good Avimas day!

DAY 9/15

Hello once again children! Ho Ho Ho!

Day 9 has finally been reached and I have a very special present once again! Following Red Alert, our NEW arcade gamemode will be Ghost Squadron! The goal of this game is to hunt down players who will be invisible. You will not be able to see other players but you can use your tool kit to hunt them down. These tool kits are fishing rods, enchantment bottles, and a bow. Another difficult thing for this gamemode will be the maps. All maps have traps that will tell everyone where you are such as pressure plates that light up redstoneblocks to notify everyone! This gamemode will be in the Arcade subsection which will be released with everything else on the 25th! Here is a sneak peak of one of the Ghost Squadron maps you will see:
