Blitzkrieg, Survival Games, and Skywars? by Shrimpy January 26, 2015 at 10:01 PM UTC

Survival games used to be one of my favorites when this was MCZone as well as Skywars; I'd play these two with friends A LOT when they were around. I kinda miss them on here and would like to see them make a return. These two may attract a lot of people and may also offer more options for what to play than what we already have.

Aside from bringing back games, I have a suggestion for something new-ish: Blitzkrieg. It's basically a quick deathmatch with teams. I know there's Nebula/Nexus/CTH and all but this is a little different. Players could start on a small map and would battle to the death until there is only one team standing. Surely players will take a liking to it because it's intense to play/watch and it's also quick, so players won't get bored and leave instantly and can just wait a minute or two until the next round starts.

This could be its own thing or it could be implemented with Nexus in map rotations to change it up a little. 

It's fun, it's fast-paced, the games are quick; I think this could work. 

Let me know your opinions.
