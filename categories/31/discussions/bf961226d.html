guys anyone know how to upgrade ranks as i want to play in the nebula but it says i have to be upgraded anyone know what that means?

Please note that you're only required to have a premium rank to join games when they're exclusively full (or exceeding maximum player capacity on both teams).

If you purchase a premium rank you'll be able to join full games and you also are given a credit multiplier (you earn credits faster).

To purchase a rank you go to https://avicus.net/shop and follow the steps given.
If you have a problem with your purchase you can email support@avicus.net or contact a Staff Member (perferibly a Senior Moderator or Administrator) for assistance.
