[Robo UHC] Match #24 > Finished < by Cyclops July 21, 2015 at 11:07 PM UTC

Welcome to the details/info page on Match #24 (UHC Reboot: Slave Market/Moles).

Match will be held tomorrow, July 22nd, 2pm Eastern Standard Time.

Whitelist will be turned off 45 minutes prior to start.

Those who showed to the UHC feature testing will be getting pre-whitelisted on my behalf. ^.^

Moles: Each team will have a Mole on their team. The moles job is to slay their teammates, along with the help of the other moles, so essentially, the moles are a secret team. Moles will have their own chat to communicate. Each mole can choose one of ten kits. If the mole successfully kills their team, or is discovered, they can join a call with another mole in the same situation.
Moles are set at night :D

Slave Market: Pre-game, there will be an X amount of players to be slave owners.randomly chosen. These players get 20 diamonds to bid on the "Slaves" (left-over players). Any remaining diamonds kept could be used in the match, so you can have a ten-man team, or go solo with 20 diamonds.
(For this game, every slave owner will have a starter teammate at random). 

Server Info: https://avicus.net/forums/6aaf332fa

Cya ya'll there, happy killing.
