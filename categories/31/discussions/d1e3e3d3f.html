Anime Night! Season 2 :D by Cyclops June 21, 2015 at 8:06 PM UTC

Anime Night was a group created by Me, Doozie and Zieno last summer. Its a group where we start a skype call, and start watching Anime at Midnight (EST). The whole group votes on whether it should be a Movie/Series, and which one. 

We either watch a full movie, or 2 episodes of a series each night, depending on the choice for that night. (If a series is voted, we watch that until finished).

This group is only done over the summer, and I shut it down until next summer.
