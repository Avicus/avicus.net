Don't rush the Map Developers please! by Fouled February 28, 2015 at 6:02 PM UTC

Alright, so. Frequently in the past few days, we've been receiving alot of messages from map creators saying stuff like "Did you test my map yet?", "Did you finish my YML yet" and much more. I'm not calling anybody out or anything, but i'm just insisting for you guys to wait patiently. We also have alot of work to do, managing rotations, accepting/denying maps, fixing YML errors, helping others and many more. As an example, Evocal/Astronis had to wait 2-3 weeks for Justice I to get accepted, and he didn't bug any member of the Map Committe (because there were no Map Developers back then).. We are trying to work as fast as we can with the most effort we can put in, and lately, we've been way more busy than usual. Just try not to rush us please! :)
Thanks and have a wonderful day!
