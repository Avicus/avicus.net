Staff Spotlight Comeback!!!! #1 [SnowSX3] by tel6331 March 15, 2015 at 12:03 PM UTC

Right, we had a recent staff meeting where a vote was made to bring back the staff spotlight on the forums. I am glad to say that it is back and will happen every month!!!..... maybe....... Well anyways, I would like to introduce SnowSX3.

1.     What is your username in minecraft and what is your favorite game on Avicus?
Sup Mates!
My name is SnowSX3.
My name is a combination of Midtown Madness 3 and the SSX snowboarding series. 
The “S” on Snow is capitalised to represent the first “S” in SSX
The “SX” is the rest of SSX and the 3 comes from Midtown Madness 3.
My favourite gamemode on Avicus has to be the Walls, great team-based gameplay comes from it!

2. What are you most excited/obsessed over at the moment?
I just got my hands on WWE2K15 so I’m playing through that at the moment.
I’m probably most obsessed with Avicus currently, I’m on it Moderating and playing everyday.

3. Where are you from?
New Zealand.

4. Are you in school, or do you have a job?  
I’m currently in my last year in high school.
I also have a part time job at a hotel.

5. Who or what inspires you the most in your life?
I don’t try to emulate other people, I just try to be a better person day after day.

6. How do you contribute to the Avicus as a staff member?
I am very active as a moderator, every time I join Avicus I’m always Moderating.
I always help others and respond to questions quickly and efficiently.
I’m also not afraid to say what I think, if I think something’s wrong, I’ll speak up about it as opposed to just sitting in the background while the problem gets worse.

7. What are the most common questions you get asked while moderating or managing?
"How do I apply for Mod?"
"Can you ban soandso? They’re hacking."

8. Anything else you want to mention?
I want to thank the community for giving me the massive amount of support I received on my way to Mod. It meant so much more than you think. Thank you guys.
Also, my Skype’s “SnowSX3” if you have any questions regarding Avicus, flick me a message!
