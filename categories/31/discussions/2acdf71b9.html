Avicus Community event PlugDJ


With the recent return of PlugDJ this event is designed of course not only as a way to have fun and interact as a community but to revive the PlugDJ community!


The event will be a song contest , with the winning song receiving Music god on Ts.

All music is allowed but anything deemed inappropriate by a staff member or un-needed can be skipped at any time. All songs will be recorded in a strawpoll (we can check all songs played in PlugDj) The community will then vote for their favourite. Once done the event hosts and any judges selected will go away and select a winner as to avoid any bias or rigging but the community vote will be play a huge if not main part though.



Rules:

1.No playing songs in the DJ history Within 1 hour

2.Any genre is allowed unless it's an event (for this event we are allowing all music but refer to rule 5)

3.No playing songs over 5 minutes. (exceptions can be made)

4.Impersonation will not be tolerated.

5.If a song is deemed inappropriate or un-needed by a staff member they have every right to skip it.

6.Follow rules in section A found at https://avicus.net/rules


The event will last roughly 1-2 hours (for the music god rank) depending on amount of players and so on.

If you play multiple songs within the time you'll have to message me which one you want to use in the poll.
