Staff Reassessment Day by hpux March 29, 2015 at 5:03 PM UTC

Hello everyone...again!

Yesterday, the staff team gathered for a meeting, and we discussed the results of the Sr. Staff meeting, as well as some other mini things that aren't too important. However, there was one thing that many of you would have guessed is coming. Well, it's here! On Saturday, April 4th, at 6 PM EST, the Senior Staff will be hosting our second Staff Reassessment Day. This means we will demote each and every Moderator, and discuss their strengths and weaknesses. After we discuss one Moderator, we will take a majority vote, which will decide whether they will receive their rank back or not. The Senior Staff will be moderating during this interval, but hopefully it will not take very long. If you were wondering why we decided to open staff apps so soon, this is why, and you probably already guessed that.

Have a great day/night!
