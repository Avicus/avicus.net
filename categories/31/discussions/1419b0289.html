Spam/Useless/1 Answer Question Topics.... by chaibrit1 December 7, 2014 at 7:12 PM UTC

So I've been noticing a lot of Spam/Useless/1 Answer Question Forums Threads that have been locked recently, so I'd like to help minimize these threads...

Spam/Useless Threads 

If the topic is something that we can't discuss (because there is nothing to discuss) please don't make the thread. Ex: I just went to the mall..." 
"I just went to the mall and saw someone fall into a fountain! Now I'm dying of laughter xD"
No one can really discuss anything other than "k." It's a personal update and it's just pretty useless. Remember, the forums is not a social networking site. They are a place for everyone to be able to discuss and relate. 
-And if it's a message to just 1 or 2 people, don't post that either. Just use Skype, tell them in game, anything just not on the forums. 

1 Answer Question Threads

Now these won't necessarily get locked unless the question is answered, which it usually does. If you have a question, you can ask in game or ask a staff member on Skype. Your best luck is asking a staff member on Skype if you don't trust what the people are saying in game. They're always there to help and they'll probably give you more detailed answers if you ask them on Skype, not on the forums. If you don't know any staff members' Skype, a lot of them have it on their profiles, so just check! 
Ex: If you recently bought a rank but didn't get it yet, ask a staff member or friendly people in game. Also, check under "Avicus Help" because it answers a lot if questions.
