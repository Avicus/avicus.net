What does it mean by kill %
So I'm not the only one controlling this story.

Zac and Frosted have control and they can use their assassins

(Heat/Sli/Frozen/Raging) 

The kill percentages are the percentages of the assassin killing them.

Example:
FIyHacks - 50%
Frosted sends Sli to assassinate me

I flip a coin
Heads = successful kill
Tails = failure

the coin lands on tails

my character survives the assassin


edit:
Forgot to explain Kill Tokens

Frozen and Zac can use kill tokens to either assassinate a player, capture a player and send them to a dungeon to raise the % to 100%, save a player trapped in a dungeon, or resurrect a player
