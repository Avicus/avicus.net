A document regarding all of this will be released tomorrow. This is just for suggestions and anything else that should be added.

There will be 16 teams and 4 brackets/groupings. 4 teams are in each group and they play 5 matches and get points from what place they get. 1st place gets 3, 2nd gets 2, 3rd gets 1, and 4th gets none. Whoever gets the first and second highest amount of points move on.

Each team will have of 2 players consisting of a 2v2v2v2.

The group's first place winner go into group A semi-finals where they play 3 matches for the highest point score. The group's second place winner will go into group B for the same reason. At this point, 8 teams have been eliminated (the teams who got 3rd and 4th for total score).

After that will come the Finals which are the first and second place winners of both group A and B for a final map on Grotto Arena.

From there, the first place duo gets copies of GARY'S MOD off steam. 2nd place gets 2.5k credits, 3rd gets 1k credits, 4th gets 500 credits.
