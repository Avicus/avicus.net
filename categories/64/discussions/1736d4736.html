Competitive Avicus by Zegita March 26, 2016 at 10:03 PM UTC

Let's be honest - the server receives an increase in players whenever a tournament or an official event is announced. This server needs to remember that competitive is what keeps this server living. I mean yeah there is public Avicus but that can get boring from time to time whereas a tournament actually motivates you to do well and achieve something good.

I remember in both tournaments the player count rose from when there was no tournament (about 50 players on) to 150-200 players on when a tournament was on or any type of official event. Now I'm the type of person that gets hyped up when an event is happening purely because it is fun to take part in or even just watch on streams as it is interesting.

So what I believe is that maybe the developers should focus equally on the public side of things on Avicus but also the competitive side of this and fixing problems like for example the scrim servers that disallow teams to scrim on and so they have to go somewhere else to scrim on for a start. Of course remember the problems such as the lag that occurs there and there but I think that has been reduced from what it was before so this does show the developers are making progress but we have to support them along the way.

The best ways of making Avicus' competitive popular is by doing the following (IMO):

  • Create more tournament maps! - Builders or Mapdevs, you seem to be creating public Avicus maps when public doesn't really need them but what we do need is tournament maps for either DTM, CTW or TDM.
  • Streamers/Youtubers - These guys seriously help a lot I believe streamers are vital for when a competitive scene is around as observers would love to watch what is going on.
  • More teams - Don't be afraid to create a team even if it is just a group of your friends in real life or even in the game. Even if your team is bad (most teams are bad from the start especially if they are only starting out) that doesn't matter because you can improve your team over time and see how you guys perform in a tournament and then judge yourselves and keep going. The best teams are the ones that stick together for a long time!
  • Organisation - Organisation is key - if the organisation is sloppy and bad the players who are playing will get annoyed with you and they will just not want to compete again or even not even bother with the tournament.
Finally, fix anything that goes wrong with competitve e.g scrim servers being down this is a major thing that developers should be onto ASAP because this prevents teams from playing and then having to go somewhere else which is annoying for players and teams.

Also comment down below if you agree or disagree or what your thoughts on competitive Avicus is - thanks for reading :)
