CTW Map making contest RESULTS by dev_revs April 2, 2017 at 8:04 PM UTC

-Event: Capture the wool map making contest!

Description: Recently I have noticed the lack of CTW maps in the avicus map repository. In scrims, the only map to really play is Hold fast, and in public matches, they're are like two-three playable maps. In total eight CTW maps are on avicus. I have decided to host a contest where players will compete with their map building skills for a prize.

Map grading rubric: Maps will be graded on a 100 point scale.

Gameplay- /48 points
-Size
-Pvp Space
-Cover
-Players opinion
-(More)

Aesthetics-  /42 points
-Pallette/Blocks used
- Layout
- Height Variation
- (More?)

Originality- -/10 points
-Creativity
-Originality

Show hosts-
 dev_revs
ALM

Judges-
 PieZ
profbananaslug
ProfessorUtonium


Rules- You are allowed to work with multiple people on a map, but the prize will not have to be divided. You may steal other maps ideas, and make run-offs. You may not re enter a map that has been in the avicus repository within the last 12 months. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to ask. At least 4 maps must be submitted for the contest to pursue. You may not submit more than three maps.

Prize- The winning map creator will get a 40.00$ steam giftcard, if multiple people made a map, the person who submitted it will receive the gift card, splitting it is up to the main submitioner. 

How it will work- *UPDATE!!!!!!* Maps will be submitted on the "Map realeases" subforum with [CONTEST] in their title.
*EVENT WILL BE LIVESTREAMED* We will provide the players needed to test the maps also, during the event to see how they play.  When you submit the map, please submit it just like you would on a forum post with the proper information and everything.

XML, Downloads, pictures, ect-  All these things must come with the map submitted, if you fail to get us an XML or anything we need to play the map, your map will be disqualified, I suggest sending to a map dev(not Lego or Seth) and ask them to run it on the development server to make sure everything is in tip top shape because these won't be played until the event.

The map must be usable by avicus and you give your guarantee to never retract it.


Event start- April 2
Event end- May 16 11:59pm
Event day- May 19 6 EST

If you have any other questions, please ask down below, hope to see a lot of talented maps being submitted.

Please Congratulate Xuph, Bloodoh, and imryaan on winning the CTW Contest Map Making with their map, Apollo 13. Second place goes to Clijmart cellish Sweedie with their map Cake. Third place goes to _DISTRACTION_with their map Insanity. We hope to see all map makers for the next Map Making Contest!
