This post will be updated with any changes so be sure to check it for future updates.



Information
  • The gamemodes will be CTF, 5CP and CTW
  • We will accept as many teams as possible, with 8 hopefully being the minimum.
  • Tournament will be single elimination with seedings being based off last tournament and the official's opinion (sorry but there's no better way to really do it). 
  • All rounds will be BO3 with the final a BO5.
  • Maximum of 18 players can be signed up with a minumum of 12. 
  • Maximum of 8 players on the field with a minumum of 6. 
  • Please feel free to apply for referee or streamer below, it really helps if you have past experience too.
  • We will be using Avicus teamspeak where the seniors will be providing team channels for all signed-up teams. 
  • We will be using Avicus scrim servers so I would kindly ask that you do not book any for the dates of February 4th and 5th.
