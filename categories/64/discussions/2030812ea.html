Unofficial TM The Wool by Posighdun May 10, 2017 at 3:05 PM UTC



Zicuri and Myself are hosting an unofficial tournament soon.

Gamemode: Capture The Wool

Format: The format is a double elimination which will occur over the period of 2 days. 

Match Information: A minimum of 3 players must be fielded, but a maximum of 5 players can be fielded. Max people you can register is 7 (subject to change). Note you do not have to have a team on the teampage, just fill out the registration form. This way there can be more teams as members from teams can split up and form smaller teams.

Matches will go for no longer than 30 minutes, with an 15 break in between. 
It's Best of 1. 

Rules:  https://avicus.net/rules 

Bracket: http://challonge.com/d6qvokti 

Discord: https://discord.gg/pAhbWmE

Map Pool: Hydroin, Holdfast, Cake, Light Pool

Important Dates:
Registration opens: May 13th.
Registration closes: May 18th. 
Day 1 of matches: May 20th, Saturday.
Day 2 of matches: May 21st, Sunday. 

Times: You can choose between 2 times, 10am EST (9.30 check in) or 2pm (1.30 check in) EST

All other information and times will be posted soon.

We are looking for referees. Hit me up in discord if you would like to ref. We are not looking for streamers.


Any questions then ask below
- Posighdun and Zicuri
