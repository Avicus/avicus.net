Community Update - (Tournament Edition) by Posighdun June 5, 2016 at 4:06 AM UTC

Ω Community Update Ω

By  Administration team

Introduction

Because we don't really have too much to report on this week, Ethan and myself decided that we would focus on the tournament. Yesterday matches 1 to 5 kicked off and 10 teams battled it out for spots in the invitations. This update will go through the results and a few other stuff.

Tournament Reults for Matches #1-5

Match #1
Team Allah vs Arcadeus (2-0)
The Crazy Beaners vs Endless Aptitude (2-0)
Cacalmarin, Apollo and Varsity all won by default due to some no shows.

Match #2
Team Allah vs ScorpionS (2-0)
The Crazy Beaners vs Varsity (Wasn't recorded)
Cacalmarin, Apollo and Endless Aptitude all won by default due to some no shows.

Match #3
Apollo vs Endless Aptitude (2-0)
The Crazy Beaners vs Cacalmarin (2-0)
Team Allah, ScorpionS and Varsity all won by default due to some no shows.

Match #4
Apollo vs ScorpionS (2-0)
Endless Aptitude vs Varsity (0-2)
The Crazy Beaners vs Team Allah (0-2)
Cacalmarin won by default due to their opponent being disqualified.

Match #5
Apollo vs Varsity (2-0)
Endless Aptitude vs Cacalmarin (0-2)
Necromancy vs Team Allah (0-2)
ScorpionS & The Crazy Beaners both won by default due to their opponents being disqualified.

Issues

Everything went perfectly, we were organised before hand and that organisation payed off. I'm sure the players that played can agree that so far this tournament is the best we have hosted. My only real issue is, which you could probably see from the above results, the no shows. It's not acceptable. If you don't show up I don't understand why you would register. I can't really say anything to Group A to change anything beause half the matches are already done but to Group B, China Baby Toes, La Vigen, Eternal Cross, Cronus, UbeR, Team Ungespielt, Xenarch, Jalapenos, Sagrevyum and Black Hole I would kindly ask that you be prepared for your matches on the 18th and 25th of June and try to not no show.

Live Streaming

Yesterday we streamed most of the tournament matches live on Avicus Official which is located here. . I want to give a massive thank you to ULTIMATE7FALCON and to JustBudah who did some professional quality streaming for Avicus Official. Also a thank you to Pokerface for providing us with an Overlay for Twitch.

Conclusion/Next week matches

Next week matches 6-9 will kick off and then the following week Group B matches will commence. Good luck to all the teams to play in matches to come and lets make this a tournament to never forget.

That's all for now!
