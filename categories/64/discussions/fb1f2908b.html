Poll: Regarding the next tournament by PokerFace October 18, 2017 at 9:10 PM UTC

Question: Would you be ok with a 5v5 TDM tournament?
  • A) Yes (list reasoning) (4)
  • B) No (list reasoning) (6)
  • C) Depends (list reasoning) (1)
My reasoning for having it as a 5v5 is because if it were to be a regular sized tournament, there would probably be only 6 teams (from Avicus) who would be able to meet the registration requirements. If it were a 5v5, this would give smaller teams the opportunity to play, make it easier for people to create teams, and the big teams can split into multiple teams. Would you rather have a small tournament with lots of teams or a big tournament with a lower amount of teams? We will have the maps edited to suit a 5v5 so after all of this, if you still disagree with the decision, leave your vote and a detailed response as to why. Please keep in mind that we will still have 8v8 for the next tournament. This tournament is to mainly be used as an extra tournament and an incentive for smaller teams to develop while still giving everyone a competitive environment. 

When voting, please include the letter of your choice in your response. Submit your vote through a forum response.
