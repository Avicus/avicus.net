How does one rent a server? by amJewel March 12, 2017 at 7:03 PM UTC

So I've heard that you can rent a server for scrims/practices, that sort of thing. How do I do that? Do I ask a dev when they're on, or is it some other process?

dev_revs March 12, 2017 at 7:03 PM UTC

Hi, if you look at the top of your screen, you see tags such as "Play" "Stats" "Competitive". If you click competitive a drop down menu will appear, then click scrimmages and it will bring you to the reservation page where You click reserve on the time-frame you like. PS- You will need to be leader of a team to do this.

Spartemex March 12, 2017 at 7:03 PM UTC

Yeah, you must be a member of a team to reserve scrimmage servers, like dev_revs said.
