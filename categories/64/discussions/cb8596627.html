Streaming for Invitationals | Discussion by Spoookeh July 7, 2016 at 6:07 PM UTC

Hey there!

We'd like the streams for the invitationals to be as professional and as high quality as possible so we'd like as much feedback from you, the community, as possible.

Firstly we'd like to ask your opinions on what channel we should be streaming on. Would you prefer streams of  ALL the matches on multiple channels or one main streamer on AvicusOfficial (https://www.twitch.tv/officialavicus) allowing more viewers to engage with one stream?

AbusiveUser has also generously created a new overlay to use (http://imgur.com/a/J9DZK). If you'd like any improvements to be made to this including team logos we'd be happy to make adjustments. 

This might come across as fairly controversial but we have been discussing the use of mods such as enlarged nametags and damage indicators to give viewers a better idea of what is happening in the match. Personally I would love to see this in team fights, checking the player's HP and guessing who might come out on top. I understand the argument "If media are using it why can't we?" might pop up but it's not like streamers will be allowed to go in-game and play with these modifications enabled. 


Feel free to discuss the points made below and anything else you might have to help us improve the streaming quality.

See you in the invitationals! ;P
