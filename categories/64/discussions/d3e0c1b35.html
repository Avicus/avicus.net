Unofficial Event 3 by Tazz December 28, 2015 at 12:12 AM UTC

Hello

After a long break, Lgmatias, BmB and myself have decided to host an event. This event will be a Staff VS Regular match which will be a TDM Bo3. 

It will take place on the 2nd of January at 7PM GMT, which is 2PM EST. 

The confirmed maps are SupplyTDM and ActrixTE. The third map will be announced in the coming days. The maps will be played as 8v8.

The teams are unannounced but if you are one of the following ranks you are eligible to play: Admin, Senior, Mod, Jr Mod (at the time of you asking), MapDev, Jr MapDev, Official and Advisor. However, if you are staff, you can not play for the Regular team. Teams will be chosen by the two team captains - Tazz and Lgmatias  will captain the staff team, and another player, who will be announced very soon, will captain the regular team. If you are a staff member who wants to play and is available, please msg Tazz on the Slack and he will add you to the list. The regular team leader will consult any players that he is interested in. He will choose 14 players. 7, along with himself for the first map, then 7 along with himself for the second map, and if it goes to a third map he will choose 3 players from one team, and 4 from the other team - this will be down to the leader. If you have the Retired rank, you can play in either of the teams - post a comment if you are interested in playing for the staff team. If you do not post one in the next 24 hours, you will not be considered for the staff team and only the regular team.

It will take place on either the Official Servers or Scrim Servers - TBA.

The current ruleset for the Avicus League will be followed.

Thank you to Lgmatias and BmB for making this possible.

Regards

Lgmatias, BmB and myself.
