Walls of Steel Walls Tournament 20$ prize by dev_revs August 22, 2017 at 8:08 PM UTC



Hello! I am now announcing my second tournament,Walls of Steel! This tournament will have your best walls players battling it out to see who will be crowned champion! [Banner soon] This tournament has been planned for a while and I am ready to host!


Gamemode: Walls


Server: Avicus


Tournament format: The tournament is going to be single elimination, and the team winning will advance to the next round to play the next set of winners. (2v2v2v2) NOTE- We will only accept a multiple of four for the amount of teams, sign up early!

My goal is 24 teams. I highly encourage staff or people who arent on the competeive side to play this for fun with a friend!


Team information: Matches will be 2v2, Rosters will not be confirmed until the day of, but I recommend signing up early to guarantee your spot. On the day of the tournament, if you need to sub a player in BEFORE your team plays the first game, you may. Teams should use discord but may use teamspeak if the ref agrees to follow them. You cannot show up on the day of the tournament and expect to play, one player must of signed up in advance.


Challonge: TBA


Team registration: Leaders apply here. https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdR85O7w2a82L9aGL24Eq6GemMzZc5wnjjXCLe4xPJkHmkBlg/viewform?usp=sf_link


Spectating: Event-staff and players playing the match are the only ones permitted on the server when the match is being played. Streams will be available.


Starting times: Teams will be given a maximum of 5 minutes after the scheduled time to strat, and get their team ready.


Disqualifications: Official tournament rules apply. All avicus rules still apply as we're using an avicus service. When a decision is made by our refs, it is final. We will not disqualify a team without there being solid proof. We will pause matches to discuss potential problems, if they arise. In a situation of an outburst between two teams, or a disqualification, we ask team leaders to handle it in teamspeak/discord with officials.


Map pool: Please vote on the walls maps you want played. Maps will be selected by a random generator to decide what will be played. The TOP 6 maps with the highest votes will be put in the map pool.

http://www.strawpoll.me/13756268



Important dates and times:

First rounds

Finals and Semis

Sunday September 10th or Saturday September 16




PRIZE: As of right now, the winning team will each get 10.00$ each. If anyone wants to donate/discuss a prize please contact me
