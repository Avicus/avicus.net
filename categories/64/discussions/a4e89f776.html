Tomqss' Unofficial Tourney by tomqss October 24, 2016 at 11:10 PM UTC

Hey.

Seeing that the official tourney won't come for a bit, I thought organizing an unnoficial tournament would be a good idea to keep this a little less dead.

Teams: https://goo.gl/forms/X3gbOKrdgFLsBZra2

Referees: https://goo.gl/forms/xL1jfjMPoaPlRzdv1

If you want to stream contact me on skype (tomqssmc) or on Slack.

 

Some information:

-8 players in field. Up to 18 registered. They don't have to be in your teampage, list them in the form. You can make a roster repair after registering by messaging me on Skype or Slack.

-The gamemodes are DTM and CTW. In qualifiers, you're playing 2 times each, against 4 different teams (Bo1). In invitationals, you will play both (Bo3), and if needed, the third match will be one of both, randomized.

-The quantity of teams will depend on how many register.

-First round is going to have a Swiss Elimination (You're gonna be matched up against teams with a similar record) format, and invitationals, single elimination.

-Maps are TBD. You will be able to veto until you have 1 map in every instance.

-Dates for qualifiers are 19 and 20, and dates for invitationals are 26 and 27.
