We know, When a tournament is annunciated Avicus gets x2 or x3 more users than normal, But why? Because Competitive is what most people want.

How to get more people then?

-Advertise Avicus "públic" players about Competitive aspect, Broadcast messages like Walls tournament.

-Advertise Tournaments on Other PvP communities.
-Work on more TM maps and not on public ones, many people complain of the lack of variety & gameplay of most Avicus maps.
-Better Prizes.. ( As Walls TM )
-Experienced Refs for Officials Events.
-Practice servers for teams that Register on tournaments ( Just the ones that really want them )
