Hello all! Under no official order, the media team has taken over the responsibility of mega scrims. We will be attempting to host them every Saturday at a reasonable time for all time zones (sorry aussie). These will be very planned out and will run smoothly and not take up all your time

DATE AND TIME:

11:00am PST
2:00pm EST
7:00pm BST

zihaw  [CTW]
Foundry[KOTH]
Senex[DTM]


Hosts:
kon333


How to play:
We will start adding players to the scrim server 25 minutes before start time, simply message one of the hosts to invite you(we will be inviting by player, not team). Once the draft starts, if you did not make it in time, you will be asked to wait to play til game 2. 


Guidelines:
The hosts will basically pick two captains for the whole mega scrim, we will play three matches total. If you refuse to join a players team after he selects you we will simply ask you to leave the server. Team captains may agree on draft obligations (ex: team A gets first pick but Team B will get 2 picks next.. ect). We reserve the right to refuse participation to any player. Spectators are allowed as long as they will not give out tactical information, and not type in global chat.

TeamSpeak: We will be using teamspeak for this event, you must be in the channel to play. Teams will be split into two team channels not being used at the time.
