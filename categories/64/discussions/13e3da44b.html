Hey there. Today i am introducing a new tournament, it's called: Bloodthirsty,


This tournament will be TDM. I'm doing this to train for the upcoming official tournament. As far as we know, it will be TDM so why not? Let's start.


System: 3 Game Guarantee


Gamemode: TDM

Roster Sizes: 8v8 (6 minimum players)

  • You may use substitute players as long as they are not already playing on a team that is in this tournament.

Bracket & Schedule:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1q55qBRrA-iM0_az26-rN7E0wJtsGp-4BFMnwcv8y6jc/edit#gid=0


*This will be updated regularly, it is your responsibility to check this out regularly. Updates will be posted on here as well.


Map Pool: [7 maps]

  • Injustice

  • Emperial

  • Xena

  • Actrix

  • Supply

  • Plaza

  • Lutose

Map Veto:

  • Best of 3

  1. Team A will veto one map.

  2. Team B will veto one map.

  3. Team A will choose one map.

  4. Team B will choose one map.

  5. Team A will veto one map.

  6. Team B will veto one map.

  7. The last remaining map will be played if necessary.

  • Best of 5 (Finals)

  1. Team A will veto one map.

  2. Team B will veto one map.

  3. Team A will pick one map.

  4. Team B will pick one map.

  5. Team A will pick one map.

  6. Team B will pick one map.

  7. The last map that hasn't been vetoed or chosen will be counted as the default win.

MATCH SCHEDULE HAS BEEN RELEASED

Hello everyone the match schedule has finally been released! As I promised to all of you, the time is reasonable for each and every team participating. According to the preferred timezone given by your team leaders, nobody should be playing past 12:00 MIDNIGHT or earlier than 6:00AM. To view the schedule just go to the bracket document and look at the first sheet. You will see it on the right side of the bracket. 

Things to Consider:
  • The winner of game #1 (Big Boyz vs. Baller Boys) will play immediately after that game is finished against China Baby Toes. There will be a few minutes of break in between these 2 games, depending on how fast the game goes.
  • Some games may say Saturday according to GMT time, but it could very well be a Sunday in your timezone.
  • The time that is next to your team name is the time that you will be playing according to the timezone that your team leader gave me. If your timezone is not the same as the one that your team leader gave to me, please talk to your team leader to figure out what time it is.
  • On Sheet 3 of the spreadsheet, there is a timetable for all of the teams. You can use this to see how I came up with the times of the matches.
If there are any mistakes please let me know!

Maps: 
  • Injustice has been added to the map pool.
  • In the event that Plaza, Lutose, and Emperial are not ready by the time the first matches start, we will be left with Actrix, Supply, Sentry, and Injustice. (4 maps). A new veto system may have to be created later on.
  • If worst comes to worst and we do not have at least 5 of the planned maps to be used in this tournament, we will add in a "Wildcard map". This map will be a completely random TDM map (Not Nexus) that is available on the Avicus repository. You may choose to veto the "Wildcard map" if you like, but you may risk vetoing a map that your team is strong at. 
Servers:
  • If the developers allow us to use the official servers, we will use them. If not, we will contact each team to book scrim servers at a designated time.
Good luck and have fun everyone! 

Edit:
  • The time for Eternal Cross vs. Cronus has switched from 11PM GMT to 2PM GMT.
  • The time for Big Boyz vs. Baller Boyz has switched from 3PM GMT to 7PM GMT
