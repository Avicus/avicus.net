Status of the Official Team/Behind The Scenes by PokerFace October 18, 2017 at 5:10 AM UTC

Hi everyone,

I believe the community needs an explanation on everything going on with tournaments and the official team, so this is what this post will be about.

Tournament preparation I would say is almost complete, but things have been running slow, especially with the recent demotions/retirements of 3 officials. Although this should not be an excuse, we are doing the best with what we have to work with.

Battle of Eternity II:

As you may be able to tell by the name, the next tournament will be a TDM tournament. However, due to the recent disbanding of three of the largest teams (Allah, Horizon, PureBlox) we have decided to host this tournament with a 5v5 format to give smaller teams the chance to develop while still attempting to retain the competitive atmosphere Avicus has had in previous tournaments.

The maps will most likely be Crossfire (without scoreboxes), Actrix, and Injustice.

Some of the issues causing us not to have the tournament released yet are:
  • Dates of the Badlion tournaments.
  • Maps have not been optimized for the tournament.
  • Implementation of new features.
  • General slow progression.

New Features:

During this time  I have been experimenting with possible new features to enhance the quality of the tournaments. I will give a brief explanation of each of the ones listed below.

  • Map Veto Visual Representation


As you can see in the picture there is a redstone lamp wall and some levers in the front along with visual representations of the maps. The idea is that when you pull the red lever, the lamps in the back will turn off indicating the map which has been chosen to be banned. The idea for the green lever is that with the help of XML, in chat it will display a message saying which map has been selected. The main purpose of this is to make the veto process more organized to avoid doing the vetos through messages. It also enhances the livestreams.

  • Advanced Statistic Tracking

This is just an example of how a match page with statistics would look like (disregard the names and numbers). As long as there is a stream/chat logs with full evidence of all the pvp encounters, this can definitely be a possibility.

Event Staff Applications:

We have received all applications and are under the process of reviewing them. We should be wrapped up with this in a few days.

Official Promotions:

There definitely will be official promotions, still debating on who to be promoted.

Questions?

If you have a question about something that wasn't addressed, feel free to ask!
