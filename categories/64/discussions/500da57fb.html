Welcome to Beauty and the Gamemodes tournament. Here you will have all the basic information about it.

DISCORD:   https://discord.gg/GA8KPqK


Information
Tournament will be played  in 1.9+
The tournament consists of a single elimination.
All matches will be best of 3.
Team size will be 5v5.
You can not register more than 12 players and less than 7 players.
In the match, you can not have more than 5 players and less than 3 players.
We will be accepting 16-32 teams.
The vetoing process will be decided by playing a match of "Sumo Pawer Edition (5v5)"
We will play the tournament on 2 different servers.

Dates
Signups: February 14th
Teams confirmed: February 18th
Qualifiers: February 25th
Invitationals: February 26th

Maps
CTW: Abstract, Wrap, Wallop
CTF: Space Race 1619, Assembly, ?
KoTH/5cp: Bamboo Valley IV, Foundry, Erth
DTC: Nèa Archí, Cuckoo TE, Purpur Seroc
Tie Breaker: Boom TE

Forms

Referee App: https://goo.gl/b1BV5w (8 referees)
