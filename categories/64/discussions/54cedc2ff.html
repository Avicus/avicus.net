The Vote by Tazz May 8, 2017 at 4:05 PM UTC

p.s this is not an official post

Hey boys, ready?

The whole procedure of the ban was messy. I wasn't there that night Zaoki got screenshared, so LFT didn't know if what he found was an autoclicker or not. It turned out it pretty much was, however we couldn't 100% confirm he was autoclicking, as only an autoclicker was found. This however, was defined as a punishable offence in the ruleset, whether we could prove he was using it or not. His autoclicker also matched up with the many alerts he set off in 1 match x)

Therefore, officials were unsure of what to do as PureDogs had got in their heads and they were unsure whether what had been found was an autoclicker. This was because the officials were still waiting for my confirmation on what was found, so PureDogs had time to type and make up explanations.

Nothing was being done after a few days, Zaoki was unable to go on his computer to attempt to prove himself. 

Kyoal then decided with Posighdun it would be a good idea to create a vote. This vote was an experiment for the officials, by Kyoal. He likes to impress Posighdun. Kyoal instantly voted for no dq, pressuring other officials to do so as they want to make what seems to be the right decision.  Then I woke up and they were getting disqualified whatever I voted.

Kyoal thought it would be a good decision to make an experiment on the officials, when he was one of the only ones stopping a dq from happening earlier that week.  And still to this moment, some officials are not aware of the vote being an experiment by kyoal.

After the decision was public, and it had been announced, there was (rightfully so) a shit storm created by the community.  This lead to officials who voted for no dq either feeling 1 of 2 things: 
  • realising the decision they made
  • wanting to change their decision, although it was too late, because of the shitstorm created
This made everyone realise we as a team messed up, causing everyone to post why they voted for no dq publically. Most people did, Kyoal didn't however, and was refusing to even tell the official team.

Hope this clears shit up

*ps* LFT was the ONLY official to vote for a DQ.
