Suggestion the Official Team by ViceTechnicolour July 10, 2017 at 6:07 PM UTC

This is a suggestion made out of desperation- and I know that for a fact, this would never be approved because the people who run tournaments would be the ones who need to improve it, and typically would ever voluntarily remove themselves from power (not saying that this is comparatively bad to a complete control over a team, per-se, but it's just what I'd believe).

I was thinking that we need to contact literally any server that's not ourselves to pull people who know how to run a tournament. Running a tournament requires a whole foundation, and if any of the pillars are broken, it won't be successful. I notice that complaints get lodged against the officials, and realistically, that's going to happen- but it shouldn't happen for the same and/or basic things for every tournament.

Meeting the desires of the community is crucial for survival. Being that competitive is one of the few features of the server that lets it stand on its own two feet, we need to take drastic measures in every way possible to preserve our teams, players, and reputation. As a disclaimer from many players, myself included, we feel that compared to any other server that offers competitive Minecraft and similar gamemodes- they are objectively better than what Avicus does. We've been doing tournaments for a long time now, and I can't really find any more excuses to use anymore.

I am suggesting that we contact people who have flourished in developing tournaments to run our own tournaments, and have the officials essentially just help supervise/plan it with them. We need improved communication, better planning, more marketing, better reliability, improved staff quality, and a lot more that could go down the list- and honestly I can't expect the officials to bring all of that together and stop the problem at the source, while other servers have been able to do that quite easily.

Rather than tripping on our own mistakes, it should be possible to contact someone from RMCT/Stratus/OCN/Badlion/RFW/etc. to help us work out the kinks in the tournaments, even if it's only a temporary solution.

I apologize if it seems like I'm frustrated- I've just poured my heart out in feedback for 3 years and I feel like now it hasn't really been listened to or even considered. This has been the first feedback I've delivered since 2 months ago.
