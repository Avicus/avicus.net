steven5703 June 8, 2014 at 4:06 PM UTC

If it's hosted by a company, then you should have an FTP server and mutlicraft pannel. Click on "Files > FTP File Access" when logged into the pannel.

Download a free program called FileZilla (MAKE SURE YOU DOWNLOAD CLIENT, NOT "SERVER")

Complete the installation process and launch the program.
Then at the top bar enter the username and host given to you at the FTP File Access screen on Multicraft. Then type in the password and port. The port should be 21. And the password is the password that you use to login to multicraft itself. Hit enter. You then should see a bunch of folders and stuff.

Now back to your download. Download the hub, drag it to your desktop, and unzip it. Now open up filezilla. Make the window for filezilla be half way covering your screen. Delete the 'world' folder, and drag the new hub from your desktop into filezilla and rename it to "world".
