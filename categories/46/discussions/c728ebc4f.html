Pick-up Scrimmage Information Hub by PokerFace August 23, 2015 at 10:08 AM UTC

Hi. So just recently, we had pick-up scrimmages where various members from various teams all go on the same scrimmage server and just have a good time and scrimmage. I hope to continue these events and below will be all the information if you would like to join these pick-up scrimmages. 

Application Format: 

Team Name: 
Team Tag: 

* Please do not change your team tag often so that I can invite the teams easier.
* Please add an application to this post even if you have already participated in the first one.

Schedule:

Typically, we will try to have these events every weekend and i will bump this post every time that there will be a pick-up scrimmage. I will try to give at least 3 days in advance for every event.

Rules: 
  • Keep the spam in chat to a minimum.
  • Be polite to others.
  • Basically general server rules apply.
*Failure to follow these rules may result in your team not being allowed to participate in pick-up scrims.

Procedure:

Will be updated when other things have been finalized.

TeamSpeak: 

As of now, we will be using the Avicus general chats however, separate rooms might be provided to us courtesy of the Avicus Network. If that doesn't happen, I will find some other alternatives. You may list your ideas as well. Or volunteer your TeamSpeak server if Avicus can't provide us with some rooms. 

Google Doc: 

I will be making a Google Doc recording some things such as rosters, scores, etc... starting from the next time we do this as organization wasn't the best. 

Staff:

Currently I am the only person running these events. I won't be able to run every single one most likely so I will ask a few of you personally if you wouldn't mind helping to run the event.

Current Teams:
  • Cacalmarin   [AMEN]
  • Fortissimus   [BOOM]
  • Berzerk         [BZRK]
  • Apollo            [BEAR]
  • Furium           [SCM]
  • Cronus           [CNS]
Basically, what happened today was great. Players from all different sorts of teams gathered together to play a few competitive matches against another team. At one point, there must've been 30 people on that scrimmage server.Please show your support and hopefully this can continue. Thanks!
