How to get a Santa Hat by Numberz_ December 13, 2016 at 1:12 AM UTC

Heyy, 

I just figured out how to do this myself and I know that some people on here might not know how to do it either. 
  1. Just goto https://namemc.com/ 
  2. Search for you Minecraft username.
  3. Then click on "known skins" 
  4. In the top right corner select the photo of the steve in a Santa hat.
  5. Then hit "apply", which will redirect you to the Minecraft.net site where you sign in a update you skin!

Enjoy the hat!
