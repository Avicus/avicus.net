@xArcturus Okay that looks cool. But what is Capture the wool? What do you need to do on it?
Capture a given amount of wool blocks from the opposite team and bring them back to your own team base (or also known as Victory Monument). Wool rooms are usually protected and you need to retrieve 1 wool from each given color in the respective game.

For instance, in Arizona: CTW, Pink team has to retrieve back Lime and Green wools, while Yellow team has to retrieve Blue and Cyan wools. You'll need 1 of each color and place them back in your victory monument to win the game!
