1. You need to be unique. (There's such a big space on YouTube, I'm looking for someone who stands out in the crowd. Don't submit if you post only Minecraft videos - for example). 
2. You need to have at least 500 subs. (I'm sorry, I know people don't like it. But I also don't like going through 10000000 of submissions, I need to put a limit somewhere - This seems fair).
3. You need to be active and posting videos. (Don't submit if you last video was 3 months ago!)

Since I have nothing to lose and meet basically all the requirements I decided to give it a shot and enter! It may not mean much to you but I would really appreciate it if you left support on my post by leaving a like on it. Here's my post: Click Here

But to be more unique, I am going to push myself to do what I was going to do eventually right now. I want to integrate more videos/games into the channel, this will cause there to be less Minecraft videos but to be honest it was going to happen eventually. I want to be 'free' with my uploads and have people that are happy with whatever I upload. Caring more about me and my personality over the game that I play. I will still play Minecraft a lot, but probably wont record it as much and I hope you can understand my point of view with this.

If you also meet the requirements, go for it. It could change your life. Thanks for taking your time out of you day to read this and good luck to anyone else entering!
