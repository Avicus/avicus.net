Civilization VI by AstroPizza October 22, 2016 at 4:10 PM UTC

I'm in love.

I got the game on launch day (Friday) and i love it i love it i l0ve it.
The music as it advances through the ages really creates that feeling of your civilization advancing ; their achievements along the way almost purely represented by the music itself.   The graphics although they had lot's of complaints about being 'too phone like' or 'too cartoonish,' after about 3 hours playtime i began to love what i thought would take a lot longer to adjust to , the new fog of war system by far being my new favourite feature graphic wise. The new districts system creates a depth that Civ V never had , making the player have to plan/think harder than (in my opinion from playing Civ 3-6)  Any other civ game. The new city state system as well is extremley well made in my opinion , making the used to be meta of stealing workers and glitching city states to take them now in to a much welcomed powerful ally. That's not to say it doesn't have it's fare share of things that need fixing , bad minimap , poor ai algorithim , bad border growth system , not the best at notifying you of nearby events etc and multiple small bugs. The majority of these will be fixed in hot patches however and i much look forward to the game that's left.

May write more here later but there's so much i could talk about.
It's fair enough to say i'm addicted with 14 hours already , kill me please. (Don't worry it was just for the first day Mr Pizza will be back on avicus like usual tonight :^)  )
