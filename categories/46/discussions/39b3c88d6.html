Hey man! One of the best ways to play on DTM (Destroy the Monuments) maps only would be to join one of the DTM servers or throwback. Currently I believe that there are 3 dedicated DTM servers with DTM01 being only 1.8 and then one throwback server. You can check https://avicus.net/games to see what servers are up and running and see what maps are currently being played.

Another tip would be to use /server to go to a specific server that you want it is a pretty easy way to get around the Network.
