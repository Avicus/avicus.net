Challenge #1
"The Passive Challenge"
In this challenge, you must complete atleast one objective in Nebula, like a monument.
Oh, not so fast. You cannot hurt the other team. You can't hit them, you can't use lava buckets,
you can't knock them out of the world, you can't use water buckets, and you can't spleef.
You must be crafty and figure out a way to get an objective without hurting the other team.
Good luck!
