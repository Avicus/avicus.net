HEY GUYS!!!!!

Recently we have had a big breakthrough in making competitive Minecraft a legit thing! Many of you who are active on the OCN forums may already know about this, but this subreddit is focused on making the competitive side of Minecraft PvP easily accessible to more players. It lists events that are taking place on dozens of servers around the world, and just how you can participate in them. Avicus may not have shown its own competitive side yet, but they are very welcome to start with the collaboration of these other servers. Avi mods and coordinators, I advise you to reach out to the leaders of this subreddit and leaders of other servers to start up. It may help Avicus gain a larger player base!

I do not take credit for this in any way, but it is my duty as a competitive Minecraft player to spread this message to any and all who would be interested! I hope to see many of you active on the forums and in game!
