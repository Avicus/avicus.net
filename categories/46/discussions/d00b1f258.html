If you want a mouse under $20, I'd recommend the Redragon M601. Overall it's a good mouse for gaming, though it does feel lightweight and the side buttons don't feel sturdy. That's what happens with a mouse under $20. Honestly I would spend less on the keyboard and more on the mouse, mainly because I don't see a point in getting an expensive gaming keyboard. For me, any keyboard does the job, but the mouse would probably be more important.
