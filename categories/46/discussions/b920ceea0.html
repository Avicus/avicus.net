Posighdun July 21, 2015 at 8:07 AM UTC

It's up to the moderators to decide the amount of time they believe a tempban should be. We don't have a set punishment for each offence as the scenario's often vary but we do have set guidelines that suggest the punishment for most offences but depending on the situation the guidelines sometimes aren't useful. A big factor that any moderator takes into account when deciding a expiry date for a temporary ban is if the player has already committed the same infraction, multiple times. If that is the case then that player will receive a longer temporary ban and if it continues it may even warrant a permanent ban.

Usually they way it works is we will get a console report, then watch you to see if it happens again. If the console reported it once, and we see it happen (we can't see all the way into the void, but if you're death message doesn't pop-up, and the console detects it again, that's a pretty sure sign) we tempban. It's up to the moderator how long to tempban someone (within reason), because after all, guidelines are guidelines. So, some may ban them for a day or two, others three or four.
