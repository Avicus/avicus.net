FrozenSolstice February 14, 2015 at 2:02 PM UTC

This isn't why it is illegal, but it is very bad for your health and you can get problems with your hands from it.
On the topic of jitter clicking, I feel the need to state some of the problems that may arise when using this tactic too often.

Arthritis;
You probably know what this is.

Carpel Tunnel Syndrome;
Carpal tunnel syndrome is a painful disorder of the hand caused by pressure on nerves that run through the wrist. Symptoms include numbness, pins and needles, and pain. Pregnancy, arthritis or repetitive hand movements can cause carpal tunnel syndrome. Splinting and medications can help, but sometimes an operation is needed.

Various muscle disorders;
These can include spasms of the muscle, twitching numbness and other side affects. Unlinke carpal tunnel, some of these muscle disorders can affect not only your arm, but other muscles, like your eyelids and mouth.

There are probably more, but I'm not going to list them all. :P

This is why I only jitter when I HAVE to.
Of course, this is just some information, you choose what you'd like to do.

NOTE; These disorders and such do not occur after jittering once, you'd have to do it extensively.
Also, these side affects can cause more serious health problems, like epilepsy.
