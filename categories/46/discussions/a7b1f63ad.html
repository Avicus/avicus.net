This is an idea that occured to me a few days ago and what I would like to ask your Opinion about.

What do you think about a Quidditch gamemode?

 As in original Harry Potter, every player would be flying. 3 strikers, 2 batters, 1 keeper and 1 seeker. the Quaffle could be a enderpearl, that gets thrown as normal and caught by hitting it. if another player has the ball and you hit him, you get the ball. The Bludgers could be Ender eyes, that get hit away with sticks and do 4 hearts damage, when hitting a player. The Snitch could maybe be a gold nugget that also needs to be hit to be caught.

Eventually you could also give some bonus items, like snowballs, that freeze for 3 seconds, or a speed boost, or a short hint where to find the snatch.

So what do you think about this?!
