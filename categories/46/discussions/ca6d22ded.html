Bunnies in Minecraft! by lulle265 July 5, 2014 at 8:07 PM UTC

Minecraft Snapshot 14w27a adds bunnies to the game!

Some informations on the update
Multiple skins, including one which will only show when named "Toast" - Screenshots
They drop rabbit feet, used to brew potions of leaping (II)  - Screenshots
They also drop: rabbit hide, which can be crafted into leather with 4 pieces; rabbit meat, which can be smelted for cooked rabbit, which restores 5 hunger points
Sheep now drop mutton, which can be smelted into cooked mutton
Bunnies can be bred and tamed using carrots 
They don't reproduce on their own
Bunnies will flee from players
A rare variant: killer rabbit of caerbannog, has a 1/1000 chance to spawn, it will attack players - Screenshots
Bunnies will eat planted carrots
Skins: all types of natural bunnies: white, brown, gold, black & white, salt & pepper - Screenshot

Huehue bunnies.
