While I agree that some skybases may be fairly easy to take out, from my experience before the height limit changes at least 50% of the skybases I faced were at or very near max height. Attempting to tower up to the skybase required gathering a couple stacks of blocks to safely tower up, and a couple stacks extra to compensate for being knocked down by the barrage of potions, arrows and TNT. Attacking skybases from the side offered a bit more protection but still necessitated quite large amounts of blocks and still had the hindrance of an arrow barrage. 

I'm all for people thinking of creative ways to win, but we must take caution when considering the hight limit, skybases tend to excessively stall the game. Just think from the perspective from someone who died earlier in the round. Now they have to wait for the survivor(s) to deal with the skybase(s) before being able to play the next round. 

If the height limit is to be raised, it would at least be nice to have some kits with an anti-tower purpose. 

TL;DR: I don't hate skybases, I just hate stalling. If you relax the height limit add some good anti-skybase kits.
