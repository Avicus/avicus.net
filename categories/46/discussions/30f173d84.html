Hey guys, for about 1 year 3 months I have been compiling best times for maps on Avicus into a document on my desktop. I am making this post so people can refer to the list of best times and records if curious, but also for people to post new records they have achieved on maps. To put your time on the list please follow the guideline of; screenshot of map time (displayed on tab), type names of people who got the objectives (if someone gets more than one objective write x2 for example), type the name of the map and previous record. If valid I will then edit the post and add it to the list :) I do not have screenshots for the times I have currently collected as there are so many, although you will have to trust me that all these times are correct- and I would have no reason to lie anyway. Also, some of the IGNs on the list are likely to be outdated as this list has been compiled over a long period of time. Enjoy the list and feel free to ask any questions below :)

ViceTechnicolour August 27, 2017 at 6:08 PM UTC

By splitting up the monuments ;)
So you are able to bridge and break 5 different monuments? ._.

Takes 10 seconds to mine an obsidian monument.
Takes about 8 seconds to ninja bridge from one side to the next.
Takes 7 seconds to ninja bridge from one side to the monument, and about 14 from one to the next.

So, you figure that Aviiia took 30 seconds in breaking monuments, and took 14 + 14 + 8 (a back, mid, front)
Assuming you and Aviia broke it roughly the same time, that means:

It takes about 66 seconds (slight margin of error) to get the monuments and and bridge over the void.
However, that doesn't include navigation. Since it was done in 90 seconds, we're going to do 90 - 66.

90 - 66 = 24 seconds. That is roughly the amount of time spent running on solid blocks in the map.

While a stretch, it may be possible. I just feel like it is implausible given the math for it: you also have to account for enemies, arrows, any mistakes, or whatever else. It just seems too perfect.
