It's over now. Winners



Hello! Some days ago many staff members were really interested in creating events to bond a little bit more the staff and the players, instead of being distant. I took it into practice and decided to make an UHC for the whole Avicus community. It will be hosted on Amsterdam, EU (prooftested by US guys who haven't experienced more that 100ms to the server). Without further due, let's get into the info.

That's cool, give me more info:

  • Hour: We are starting at 13:00 EDT (really useful link for conversions), so you should be 20 minutes before. If you are not in the server 5 minutes before (12:55 EDT) you will not play, this is the one and last warning.
  • Server IP: javipepe.pw
  • Map Width: 1500x1500
  • PvP: FFA. PvP will not be enabled for the first 10 minutes. When there are only 2 players left or the 85 minutes are over, there will be a deathmatch in a small arena
  • Time: 85 minutes of normal play at maximum, then 5 minutes of deathmatch maximum
  • Observing: You will be able to spec on death, however you won't be able to talk in chat. Also if you /msg a player who is playing at the moment giving tactical info (which I can in fact check) you will be banned from specting any other tournaments hosted by me
  • Conditions: Players banned on avicus are not allowed to play. 


    • Rules:

  • 1. Hacking will result in ban and blacklist from any other future community events
  • 2. Annoying spam will result in a verbal warn, then if done again, ejection from the event
  • 3. Someone being disrespectful will get a verbal warn, then he'll get kicked from the event
  • 4. This rules are subject to change and will get a post on this thread for any other change
  • 5. Playing means agreeing with this rules and accepting the consequences of breaking them
  • 6. Use common sense for all you do

    • I want to play! How do I sign up?:
