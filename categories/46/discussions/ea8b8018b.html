Murder Miner Gamemode Idea by AlexTheEpic99 April 4, 2015 at 9:04 PM UTC

3 Teams

  • Sheriff
  • Murderer
  • Innocent
Murderer:

  1. Objective:
To Kill Everyone Else in the game.

    2.  Weapons

All The Murderer Has is a Diamond Pickaxe with enchantments that is instantly 1 hit KO.

    3.  Upon Death:

The Game Ends Once the Murderer is killed.

Sheriff:

  1. Objective:
Figure out who the Murderer is and Kill him, or protect the Innocents

    2.  Weapons

All the Sheriff Has is a Bow With Infinite And Is a 1 Shot KO

    3.  Upon Death:

If the Sheriff is killed, he drops the bow and is out for the game. At that moment innocents have a chance to pick up the bow, thus becoming the Sheriff. (THE MURDERER CAN NOT BE ABLE TO PICK UP THE BOW)

    4. Killing an Innocent:

If The Sheriff Happens to kill an Innocent, both the Sheriff and The innocent who was shot die.

Innocents: 

  1. Objective:
To Survive as long as possible and not get killed.

     2. Weapons:

The Innocents do not start with anything. BUT if the Sheriff Dies, An Innocent may pick up the bow, thus becoming the Sheriff

     3. Upon Death:

When The Innocent Dies, he is out of the game, simple.


MORE INFO:

It is a one round game meant to be played in small maps (4-6 chunks).

The players are told what they are before the round starts.

It is a 2:00 Minute timer, but every time the Miner (Murderer) kills someone, the timer is extended.

Anti-Tk: Innocents Can't harm other innocents but Sheriff Can

There is to be 1 Murderer, 1 Sheriff, and Atleast 5 Innocents.


I Know what you are thinking:

Ripoff of TTT!?!?! No. It is not. It has sorta the same concept except in this, weapons are not scattered, and innocents do not have weapons (unless picked up from Sheriff's dead body. And the only way to tell whether someone is a Murderer or not is to SEE them kill someone, so the Murderer has to be sneaky.
