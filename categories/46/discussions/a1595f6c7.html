cinammoncroc000 August 15, 2014 at 4:08 AM UTC

@goldenwalrus Minecraft can only register 2 hits per second on an entity (excluding high levels of poison and wither,) but in PvP, there's ping and internet connection. When you click on a player, it communicates to the server that the player you hit took damage. However, not all the clicks will register depending on your connection to that server.

In other words, you send hurt packets to the server, that will deal damage to the opponent; however, if your connection is less-than-optimal, not all those hurt packets will be delivered to the server. So, the more cps you have, the better the chances that those hurt packets will be delivered since they are being sent at a faster rate. At least, that's how I think about it. If you have a good connection however, you can still get pretty nasty combos with just 6 cps.

On topic: I use CoolerMaster's Storm Recon Gaming Mouse, but Logitech, Razer, and RAT have pretty cool mice as well. All depends on your preference. :)
