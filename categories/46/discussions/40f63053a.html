Can vouch for the 750 ti, very good price/performance/watt card. Shame it costs $180 in Australia. The 260x (which is $110 here) is also a good option but only get it if you can't afford the 750ti or if it is considerably cheaper. 
Once you go lower than that you start losing performance/dollar and you would be better off just going with the A10 7870k or 7850k which is both a CPU and a GPU.
