http://www.badeggsonline.com/

Ended up playing this a lot during school hours to pass the time, and eventually it became more than some simple flash game. I've been playing it a lot for the past 4 days, and if you don't know what it is, it's a combat game oriented around eggs. Though there are premium perks, they did a pretty fantastic job balancing everything and it's super fun, so I'd recommend registering and playing some 1v1 matches against other lower levels to build up your XP. Very customizable too!

To get higher levels, you participate in matches and kill people. It's very strategical and there's quite a few maps to try out. I got hooked on it and also would like to know if there's any bad eggs players on here!

If you don't want to register but still try it, you can play as a guest but nothing will save. I'm on level 19 so far, and will be hanging out in 1v1 matches for a bit. Post your username below and I'll send friend requests:
