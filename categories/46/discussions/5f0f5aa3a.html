SnowSX3 July 23, 2014 at 10:07 AM UTC

To answer your questions:
We find computer games easier to do for a longer amount of time because it's physically less demanding than participating in a sport for a few hours.

Playing sport for a while requires you to move around and keep moving for a long time, which gets tiring no matter how fit you are.
 Where as computer games require much less physicality as all you're doing is sitting in your chair concentrating on what's in front of you.
