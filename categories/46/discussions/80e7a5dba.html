dentmaged August 17, 2016 at 12:08 AM UTC

First of all, find a position that you find fun and that you're good at, so defence, rusher, midfield, etc.

Second, get a good PvP K/D, play on servers like Badlion to improve your PvP skills.

Third, make a post in the Looking for Team section, and say basic things like your preferred position, any past teams, can you talk in TeamSpeak, your PvP K/D, your Skype.
