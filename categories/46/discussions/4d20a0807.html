Myv December 6, 2014 at 11:12 AM UTC

Ok.

I have a PS4, so here we go:

1. A great game that I started with was The Last Of Us. If you like shooting games that are slow paced but with tons of action, buy it. I recommend it a lot. The multiplayer is great, and it plays at 60fps, which is huge.

2. Another game I am currently playing a lot is Watch Dogs. It is a lot different to any other game around, but is really fun and has a great campaign while also blending decent multiplayer features. There is a lot to do on Watch Dogs - it is a great rival to GTA.

3. There is a free to play shooting game called Blacklight: Retribution which is a multiplayer - only game that is great. Basically, you get the game for free, but if you wish to buy any gun upgrades or armor upgrades, you must pay at a small cost. However, I have a great loadout on that game that I only spent £3.99 on, which is great instead of paying £40 quid+ for the game with free upgrades. It's really fun, and the multiplayer is great, but it is also challenging.


I also recommend that you get The Crew, COD Advanced Warfare, Far Cry 4 and Assassins Creed: Unity as they all look great and would make good Xmas presents.
