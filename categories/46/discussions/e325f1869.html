Pokémon Showdown Tournament!

Hosted by dev_revs



Hello everyone! Announcing the Chill Corners first Pokémon tournament, Pokémon showdown! http://pokemonshowdown.com/


Tournament Format:

This tourney will be best of three in a double elimination bracket, giving you a chance at redemption if you fall out round one. You will be using random teams from Generation 7 and below


Date of Tournament:

November 11, 5:00pm EST


Requirements to enter:

  • Must be able to make the above time, and plan on staying for 1-3 hours.

  • Be in the Chill corner discord with the “Pokémon” rank

  • Willing to be the Pokémon master


Tournament Registration:

https://goo.gl/forms/0jfAmQZcmp8jCspy1



Tournament Prize:

The Pokémon Master will receive a special rank in discord, along with another prize still being decided. (possibly money?)

I don't want to play, but want to help:

Summary:
On tournament day, we will have rooms where you and your opponent will be moved into to face each other and play. Please be prepared to spend 1-3 hours on this tournament. Some matches will be livestreamed by dev_revs. We will ask for honesty when it comes to who won the game, don't be a loser. We hope you decide to sign up and enjoy this tournament! Our Discord https://discord.gg/vjedCKW
