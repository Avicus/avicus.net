Windows 8- 

1-Press the windows button on your keyboard and r at the same time and you will get the run tab open, this is basically a search bar.

2-Type %appdata%, roaming, .minecraft

3-If you dont already have one, make one or open your minecraft go to resource packs in options and press open resource pack folder.

4-Download the resource pack

5-Drag or copy and paste the resource pack in the resource pack folder.

6-Your done!

Windows 8 tutorial only scrap number 1 and do number 2 in the search bar.

Sevoo January 21, 2015 at 2:01 AM UTC

There are alot of things people would not like, for instance the sounds, language, and the stay-still enchant glow. Here's how to fix it!

Enchant glow-Go to your resource pack, go to textures and go to misc. Take a texture pack that has default everything and go to that texture packs misc folder. Take the "enchant_glow.png" and "enchant_glow.mcmeta" and put them into this resource pack's bin folder.

Sound-Go to my resource pack and delete the sounds folder.

Language- This is really easy. Go to languages and if your language is set to Us English (Put it on that for the colors and custom names), put it to Canadian english.

Hope this helped!
