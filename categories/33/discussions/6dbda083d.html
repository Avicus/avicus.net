New gamemode: RFM by lulle265 June 22, 2014 at 3:06 AM UTC

RFM (race for monument) is basically a game of nebula with 2 or more teams. There is a monument in the very middle of the map, and the teams have to try to destroy the only monument.

(Optional)There can be (a) monument(s) at your own side, when it is destroyed you lose or don't respawn or something like that.
