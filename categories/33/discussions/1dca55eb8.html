About Rendezvous 2 (CTW -> DTM) by Ogrey May 31, 2014 at 8:05 AM UTC

Okay, I'm getting tired of people complaining over and over again about Rendezvous 2, so here's what happened:

Why isn't it CTW (Capture the Wool) anymore?
Back in MCzone, Nebula did support what we know as CTW. However, in Avicus, CTW is no longer supported, so you won't find it anywhere in the current Avicus.

Why was it transformed to DTM (Destroy the Monument) in Avicore?
Rendezvous 2 was originally a CTW map, so in order to make it work, I changed it to DTM.
I basically removed the wool chests and added monuments in the rooms. That's it.

Why was it added back? We don't like it!
Well, according to me, a lot of people loved Rendezvous 2. I was pretty dissapointed to hear CTW was no longer supported so I did the following:
-Tried to locate ParaPenguin.
-Asked him for permission to edit his map (CTW -> DTM).
-Edited the map successfully, then it was officially ready for when Avicore would be released.

However, players do not seem pleased with this.
I did take my time to make Rendezvous 2 come back to Avicus, because it was originally not planned to be added back in Avicore. I did it because I thought everyone would think of it as something positive, seeing an old map come back to Avicus.
Anyways, it's up to you:

Should it stay? Should it go? Want it to be modified?
Please, tell me what do you think about it.
