Extinction (TDM gamemode) by MerlinMaster August 19, 2014 at 4:08 AM UTC

First, this is a Team Death Match suggestion. 
I was thinking, what if there was a TDM that wasn't just to see how many kills you can get.
Maybe make it to where you kill people and they are on your team? 
Have you ever played Sharks and Minos in elementary school?
Well, if you haven't:
1. Have players stand on one end of the room
2. Have 1 tagger
3. Have the payers run across the room and try not to get tagged
4. And keep going until there is 0 minos left
Avicus Version:
1. Make it the teams like the walls, to where when the games starts everyone is forced into joining and no one after that can join until the game is over (like the walls)
2. Split the teams as evenly as possible (like the walls)
3. Donors can't choose teams for this
4. Have a good size map
5. Start the game
6. Probably make it last as long as a Nexus game
7. When you kill someone they get transferred to your team
8. End 
How do you win?
The team who has the most players at the end of the round wins.

What do you think?
