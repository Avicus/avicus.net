Mooch24 August 8, 2014 at 3:08 PM UTC

Kill farming is a legitimate tactic. It's the idea of holding a choke point so the enemy can't advance in whichever direction you choose.

The big problem is spawn killing. Spawn killing is when your inside of the enemy spawn and attacking anyone that appears. The thing people forget is the enemy team needs a clear route outside of spawn.

An extremely common map to kill farm on is Quintus. This here is a diagram showing what is okay and what isn't. 
The blue shows where it's okay to stand. You can be anywhere in the main area or on just that part of the corridor. As long as the enemy team has a chance to move out of their spawn freely, without being attacked by bow or sword, and move along the green lanes it's fine. If they're blocked from either exiting their spawn or moving to an iron area, it's considered spawn killing.
