How to help AVicus by FirstSintax September 14, 2014 at 2:09 AM UTC

Hey guys, I’m going to make an unofficial list of things you can do to help avicus and its community.
I’ll start from simple to complicated.

You don’t have to be a donator or mod to help! Here is what you can do to improve avicus:

1.Stay active - this is the simplest one, more people online, more people say “wow this server is popular!

2. Make friends - especially to new members, if you have a welcoming attitude, more people will come back for fun!

3. Follow the rules - Read the rules here http://avicus.net/rules it shouldn’t be hard to folow them 

4. Contribute to community - This can range from reporting bugs to making topics like this.

5. Advertise on server websites - as suggested by y0urm0m1

6. Make up for your infractions - The reason I made this was to make up for the trouble I caused to the server.

7. Map making - It takes a ton of effort to make maps and a ton of effort to get them into rotation. The more choices Map Devs have, the more maps you and friends can play on!

8. Have fun - the main reason avicus is here is to make the best of your freetime.

If you have any more ideas don’t be afraid to post.

Thanks, cri’ing Sintax
