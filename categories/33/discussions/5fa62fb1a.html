(Walls) Let me say that the new maps are very intricate, they are works of art.  The problem is that they are too complex that a person just starting out gets lost and feels overwhelmed as people rush around grabbing one of the dozens of chests.  Heck, I get lost on the new maps.

It is 9:40 CDT, or 7:40 PDT.  Prime time for people on the west coast (and MDT) to be playing, but NOBODY is playing Walls.  I can tell you with great confidence that before the new maps were introduced there would be at least 20 people playing Walls at this time.

Summary:
Old maps:  20 players
New maps: 0 players

While you're at it, fire is still not working right.  Please fix fire so that (1) fire spreads, and (2) flint & steel works again.

Suggestions:  Reduce the emphasis on grabbing diamonds.  Reduce the number of chests, a person with iron armor should have a chance at wall drop.  Reduce the number of chests - too many powerful items in them.  Fix fire so that it spreads.  Fix flint & steel.  Increase the frequency of the Tetris maps on the rotation, people know those maps since the subsurface is the same on each (great idea by the way).  Maybe put the jungle and/or walls maps back into the rotation...

Thx.
