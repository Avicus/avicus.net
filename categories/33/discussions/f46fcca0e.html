[Avicade] Skydive by ViceTechnicolour June 24, 2014 at 2:06 AM UTC

You spawn on your each individual plane, and have to jump down while flying. Gather resources from randomly generated chests in your air balloons, then once you hit the ground fight everyone. You are only able to PvP once you hit ground. You spawn at Y: 256, and work your way down on each colored air balloon. You can only enter your own balloons.
