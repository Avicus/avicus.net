Porkyyy June 2, 2014 at 12:06 AM UTC

You are allowed to kill them no matter what.
However, if they are in their spawn, you cannot hit them unless they hit you first.
But if they leave the room in which they spawn, you are allowed to hit them first.

Lieutenantpants June 2, 2014 at 12:06 AM UTC

You're allowed to kill people if you're in the other team's iron, as you're defending yourself. You are not permitted to kill newly spawned players in the spawn building, however. As long as you are in their iron and actively seeking out the objective (not just killfarming newly-spawned players), then I don't see why you would not be allowed to do so.
