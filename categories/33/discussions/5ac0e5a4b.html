walls - poseidon vs barbarian by Murphh May 26, 2014 at 2:05 PM UTC

So...poseidon has become the new op kit in walls since the new release.

Before players would complain about barbarian being too op because it added more strength. It was op and wasn't even the highest level kit you could get.

But now...barbarian is the highest level kit  (44), but it's properties have changed.  It doesn't become active until you kill someone.  This is a disadvantage since before I would use it to kill a diamond person while being iron.  Now my kit is useless until I kill someone.  But that was the whole point to dominate on the first kill when running into the middle.

And now poseidon, a mid-level kit (14), has the regen properties where your hearts regen pretty rapidly while in combat.  As long as you're in water you are regenerating hearts.  This is a complete advantage.  So now everyone is spilling water buckets around the maps and pvping in water!!!

Noboby uses barbarian anymore...now poseidon is the new op kit.

Please bring the old barbarian back so we can fight poseidon.
Thanks for listening. :)
