Lately whenever I reported, the mods always tell me to show evidence. But I said I didn't have evidence. So they left. The next time I had evidence, but they said I had to show it to them. How do I show it to them? Soz I am a noob

Mooch24 July 5, 2014 at 6:07 PM UTC

You can take a screenshot using F2 and then post it to Imgur (www.imgur.com)

Or, you can use an easier alternative such as Gyazo (www.Gyazo.com) or LightShot (app.prntscr.com). They're quite easy to use and can give you links to pictures that you take quickly and easily. I highly recommend LightShot.
