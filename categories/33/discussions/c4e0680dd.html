kycrafft February 3, 2015 at 4:02 PM UTC

Basic overview:


Warrior:
Nothing special

Archer:
Bow with punch 1, 32 arrows, wooden sword

Medic:
Gold armor, gold sword, splash potion of regen extended, splash potion of healing

Snake: 
Stone sword,chance of giving poison 1 each hit

Sniper:
Wooden sword, speed 1, bow with power 1, 32 arrows

Scout:
Stone sword, speed and jump boost 2, no fall damage

Engineer:
Stone sword, 3 tnt

Barbarian:
Diamond sword, mining fatigue 1

Spy:
Stone sword, invisibility

Heavy: 
Chain armor, stone sword

Freezer:
Stone sword, snowballs (3?) that give a person slowness 2 for 3 seconds when they are thrown

Enderman:
Stone sword, 5 enderpearls
