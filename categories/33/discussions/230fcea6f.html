The Suggestion:
I just want to suggest a new Minigame for the Avicade. I call it "The Run to Escape" It's a mix of Red Alert and Minotaur.

The History:
Well I made a Map for Minotaur and I asked myself: Ss it possible to have no minotaur in minotaur? Sounds wierd I know :D, but then I asked in the forums (http://avicus.net/forums/5d3be79fa). DaBaderMC liked the Idea and helped with the name. Then I asked Evocal via skype and he said (he is not 100% sure about this): "If the Minotaur dies the game would end." So if you count 1 and 1 together, you'll see how it now is, it won't work.
So I suggest it as a new game.

The Gameplay:
It would be like Minotaur, you have to cross the finishline before all others, but with the aspect of Red Alert, you can't kill each other. So you also can say it's Red Alert without block disappearing and you have to cross the finishline before all others. Or simply a Race.

The Maps:
The Maps would be cool. You can add different aspects like climbing a hill, doodle jump, obstacle parcours, etc. I made a map, that I mentioned above.
