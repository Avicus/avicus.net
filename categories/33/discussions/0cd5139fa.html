I like what Pie said, if its in the game I'll use it. :) Also the definition of bow spam is often lost. Bow spamming is: when a player draws their bow just enough to allow the arrow to be launched anymore is just half/quarter charging, which is very useful to gain an edge over an opponent by putting their strafe off tempo. Bow spamming can be used to keep opponents away until you regenerate a bit and their health is slightly depleted.
