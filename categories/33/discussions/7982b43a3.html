[New] Potion PvP by ViceTechnicolour July 17, 2014 at 3:07 PM UTC

PotionPvP is a new minigame held within Nebula that is a team deathmatch, except you spawn with potions and it is rather different from the other games. Documentation for this will not be stored on docs.avicus.net, however it's components are available on Pastie. The features of potionpvp are similar to those of what Kohi, Badlion, Strawhats, HCF, or other servers have except it's not 1v1 matches; it's your team going against them. This is not that much of an official minigame, yet it's enough to be announced. The maps for this are as follows, feel free to make them yourself;

  ~ Fantasy (This was converted to PotionPvP as it badly fit DTM)

The YAML is the same as Team Deathmatch, however the exclusiveness is within the removed drops, inability to edit the terrain, sounds upon killing players (using trigger functions, similar to OiTN), and of course the potion decked inventory. The Fantasy inventory layout can be found here, and the cropped out features can be found here.

Special thanks to testers IvyCode, Ingigo, and MLGtino.
