[Nebula GM Idea] GnR (Grab n Run) by Cyclops May 27, 2014 at 3:05 AM UTC

Jubby & Mustard? came up with this idea a while back but it never got much feedback, then I sprinkled a few ideas in.

There are X amount of minutes. There is 1 Obsidian block in the middle of the map, both teams are trying to break it and bring it back to their base. Although, the game is not over yet. Within those X amount of minutes, the other team is able to try and steal the monument from the other team and bring it to their base.
Everyone will have Diamond Pickaxes (Eff IV).
If the block is lost or thrown in the void, it shall respawn in the middle of the map once again.

Any feedback or suggestions are much appreciated.
