JOHN_CENAAAAAAAA October 22, 2014 at 2:10 AM UTC

I have a second monitor, so I'll answer these.

What do you use it for?
I use my second monitor to keep an eye on forums, Skype calls, TS, and other programs, it's a real help.

Is it really worth it buying another one?
If you have the budget, totally! I couldn't live without one, but I can't say the same about others.

What are the good things on having two monitors?
It helps you keep an eye on everything that's going on, and just more efficient in general.

What's a good monitor?
I personally use the Acer S231HL, but it's a little expensive and it's also 1080p. If you're on a sharp budget, get a 720p monitor and it works just fine.

Has it changed anything since you got it? 
Definately! It has by far changed my efficiency and ability to keep things in track, in progress, and on time.


Hope this helped!
