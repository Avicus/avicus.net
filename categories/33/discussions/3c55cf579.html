ProfessorUtonium January 21, 2015 at 12:01 AM UTC

How does the point shop work?
How do you get points?
Can you see how many points you have?
Do points expire?
The Point shop or the "Nebula Shop" is the nether star on the 9th slot of your inventory. 

The point shop is used to give you items for the kills or the amount of monuments you get in 1 game. Points don't carry on from one match to another (Cronus to Apollo) 

You can see how many points you have by right clicking the nether star

Points "expire" once you leave the match you're in(leaving from mczone01 to hub)
