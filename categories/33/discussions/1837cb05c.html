COD Ghosts Cranked (Nexus Add-on) by Aydran May 28, 2014 at 1:05 AM UTC

Cranked: 
Cranked is a gamemode from COD Ghosts. This gamemode is similar to TDM but with a few twists. 

How it Works: 
Every time a player gets a kill the player is given a 45 second timer, after each kill the timer will reset, if the player fails to get a kill within the time limit, they die. And the first team to reach 70 points wins. (Each kill is worth 1 point)

Cranked Effects:
When a player becomes "cranked", the player will receive a speed buff and a strength buff.

Maps: 
The maps for this gamemode have to be 2 team maps, the maps can either resemble the COD maps, or can be the current 2 team maps. But the maps have to be small to medium sized since the gamemode is meant to be fast paced.

Kits:  
This is an addition to nexus so the kits can be the same. If you want to see the Cranked gamemode in COD for reference go here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K3RriEJCiZ4

Any suggestions are welcomed, and also this gamemode may need a bit of work.
