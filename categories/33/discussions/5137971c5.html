A: Small Maps
-Small maps are simple to get across to the battle unlike alot of other maps where you spend half the game running to the other side and end up being ambushed by people waiting to kill you, either way. Small maps are the key to having a pvp filled game and not wasting time to just run across the map half the game.

-This one is simple. If you dont have such a beefy computer, the map will make you lag, Alot. i know this from past experience and even the Avicus lobby, the lobby is over done, something that was fixed when it was never broken. The old MCzone map theme was great, small, simple NOT LAGGY.
