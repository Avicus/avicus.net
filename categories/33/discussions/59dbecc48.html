LightsOut -- NEW MINIGAME! by Fouled June 6, 2014 at 11:06 PM UTC

First off, i don't know if this mini-game idea follows correctly in the section used but whatever..
This mini-game is called LightsOut and i think would follow in a popular game. The idea of this game is there are 2 teams:
- Dark
- Light
The main theme of this mini-game is that there is 2 teams that fight it out, with normal weaponry and kits that they would get. Each map would have NO light source or containment. 
Each person gets 3 stacks of torches, their goal is to light up an entire map in order to map the map full brightness and win the game.
Once a teams side of the map is FULLY lit up, with no containment of darkness the game ends.
GOAL------- Light up the opponents side of the map with torches to make it fully bright with no darkness with kits and PVP styles.
The other team CANNOT break the torches that were placed.
KITS AND MORE GAME PLAY VIEWS WILL BE ADDED SOON!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
EDIT: Must stand near each torch for about 4 seconds before placing another one.
