Discussion - Sprint Key by cinammoncroc000 November 18, 2014 at 4:11 PM UTC

Hey guys, I thought I'd spark up a discussion.

So today's topic will be the sprint key. If you don't know, this was added in Minecraft 1.7 and allows you to re-initiate your sprint if you hold the sprint key down.
It may not sound like much, but this has practically changed the face of Minecraft PvP. Before, it was difficult to initiate combos and whatnot because of the double-tapping (which was a bit buggy at times and caused trouble,) but with the sprint key, it is so much easier to perform advanced techniques.

What are your thoughts on the sprint key? Do you use it? Are you with or against it?

Personally I am a big fan of the sprint key, as it has made my PvP experience (and Minecraft experience in general) better. It also lets me parkour with much less hassle (parkour with double tap... *shudder*) However, I understand how people would disagree because the skill gap is much smaller now.
