here goes:
  • Beastmaster~
  • Level: 8
  • Starting items: 3x Wolf eggs. 5x Bones.
  • Ability: 33% chance of getting a wolf egg and bone after killing a player.
Please start this kit with more bones. It normally takes 3 bones give or take to tame a wolf  - with my luck anyway...

  • Snowman~
  • Level: 9
  • Starting items: 10x Snowballs.
  • Ability: Hitting enemies with a snowball gives them blindness/slowness. 33% chance of getting 2 snowballs after killing a player.
I do not know the timing on each part for the blind/slow, but please let slowness last a little longer than blindness. It could be used to slow runners and to get away making it useful. If the blind is the main part of the kit it will be rarely used.

  • Spider~
  • Level: 19
  • Starting items: 1x Stick.
  • Ability: Right click your stick of webs to shoot a cobweb. Constant Jump 2 effect.
Just want to make sure this kit has a web cool down of a few sec. filling the map up with webs can be problematic in lots of ways.

  • Smelter~
  • Level: 21
  • Starting items: None.
  • Ability: Right click a furnace while holding any ingot/uncooked meat to instantly smelt it.
Can this Kit come with an Anvil? Seems like that would make the kit really cool on some maps.

  • Cookiemonster~
  • Level: 28
  • Starting items: 5x cookies.
  • Ability: Right click a cookie to gain a random positive potion effect. 25% chance of getting a cookie per kill.
Can the player start with 10 cookies? some maps do not have a way to make more cookies... also can the effect last longer than 6 sec? can we go for 15 sec+? I have used this kit and it is really tough to get any benefit from such short buff periods, it most of the time just turns into a chore that is not worth remembering to do.

  • Wither~
  • Level: 32
  • Starting items: 1x Stone hoe.
  • Ability: Hitting players with your hoe (scythe) has a 50% chance of giving your enemies the wither effect.
As this kit stands today its really hard to get any use from it. the damage is way to low and the switching weapons to the hoe and back most of the time turns out badly... and if you hit and no effect the other guy is going to start the fight ahead.. If the kit is designed like it is today it will not be used often.

  • Ghast~
  • Level: 33
  • Starting items: 1x Fireball.
  • Ability: Right click your fireball to shoot fireballs.
As this is a new kit it may be designed great, but if the fireball is anything like the fireball on missile wars it will be pretty useless. If you have to stop, place aim and shoot/hit - I would rethink this kit.

  • Maniac~
  • Level: 45
  • Starting items: None.
  • Ability: Gain strength from your kills.
Can this STR buff last longer than 6sec?  10-15 sec?

Last may I suggest:
 a TNT bow Kit?
Bow and 5 TNT arrows get 1 TNT arrow on player kill 
small cool-down on shooting arrows. like 3 sec. would be great if the arrow was able to act like an arrow and not TNT , so it could stick to  a skybase etc...

My thoughts:

Beastmaster needs more bones, say, 8x or 10x bones.  You should have a very good chance of getting three wolves, with five a lot of times you only get one or two wolves.

Lumberjack:  make the axe so it does the same damage as an iron sword, plus the wood cutting abilities.

Archer:  give the bow punch 1, or if possible give it a random attribute like maybe 1/3 chance punch, 1/3 chance flame, 1/3 chance power 1.  No need for bonus arrows with kills.

Sonic:  It has to have a time limit, else people will just run around avoiding the attackers. Maybe a five minute time limit?  Give me a permanent speed 2 boost and I can make a game last over an hour.  Speed 2 is a 40% speed increase.

Assassin:  Same thing, speed 1 (swiftness 1) is a 20% speed increase.  Don't make it permanent, make it a potion with maybe a five minute time limit.

I don't think Spider would work as a kit.  You can't give somebody unlimited spider webs.

While we are talking about Walls:

1. Fix fire so that it spreads.  Burning trees / skybases is a part of the game.

2. Change it so that leaves can decay.

3. Get rid of the "no shooting over walls before wall drop" rule.  Before that rule was made it was a race to gather stuff and build quickly, of you could always build further back.  Honestly, ask the more experienced players if they like this rule and you will hear a strong "no, get rid of it".

4. Stop saying that going to the alternate world (when killed by a spectator) is "glitch abuse and you go there we will ban you".  It is not glitch abuse.  As long as you are not holding up the game why not go there and have fun?

5. Stop accepting new maps.  There are some really good ones out there (thanks to Polar, Itaity, etc), pick ten and use only those maps for a few months.  Let people get used to the maps without having to learn new maps all the time.
