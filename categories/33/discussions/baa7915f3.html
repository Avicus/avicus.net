I haven't played 3 yet, but...

Justice 1 was great (for me) because of the limited ground space, therefore I could abuse the Punch I bows and my aim to the most. It was great for kill farming, but it led to unbalanced gameplay. Still quite a good map, though.

Justice 2 was probably meant for sword fighting and larger team battles - there's a ton of space to strafe, place blocks, etc. I could see an all out war happening on this map. It's pretty awesome, and it still comes with the Punch I bows for crowd management, more consistent and rewarding bow shots, and so on.
