Short forms and gaming/Minecraft language. The ones that I know if I have missed any please leave them in the comments.
Short forms                       
  • Lol>Laugh out loud
  • Ik>I Know
  • Cya>See ya
  • Gl>Good luck
  • Hf>Have fun
  • GG >Good game
  • BG>Bad Game
  • OMG >Oh my god  
  • Ppl>People
  • Idk>I don’t know
  • GJ>Good Job
  • gtg>Got to go
  •  KS>Kill steal
  • TBH>To be honest
  • IMO>In my opinion
  • ATM>At the moment
  • AMA>Ask me anything
  • Brb = Be right back
  • Idc = I don't care
  • Idek = I don't even know. 
  • Wbu/hbu = What about you/ how about you.
  • Wyut = What you up to
  • Weut = What everyone up to
  • afk = Away from keyboard
  • tp = Teleport 
  • pvp = player vs player
  • ijmtuspatttlpt>I just made this up so please add this to the list please, thanks
  • K>Ok
  • ty>theynk yu
  • yw>ur wealcom
  • rn>rite now
  • crayp>poop
  • pie>pie is excelent
