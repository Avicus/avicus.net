Keep Walls and Jungle maps on Walls! by potatoarmor August 16, 2014 at 8:08 PM UTC

I understand the desire to add new maps, but why get rid of these older maps?

These maps are great because success depends on which kit you start with and teamwork, at wall drop normally the best you can have is full iron armor, sometimes enchanted, sometimes not.

With the new maps at wall drop if you don't have full or at least partial diamond you are at a severe disadvantage, so the game becomes "who can get to the chest and get the overpowered loot first" and "who can get to the diamonds first"?  Seriously, games on the new maps are all about developing routines to get the loot and diamonds.

I'm not saying to go back to where the only maps are Walls and Jungle (and Village), but please keep them in the rotation.

And a suggestion for new maps - no diamonds except in the middle (like Jungle)!  Makes things much more fair at wall drop and lets you spend time on building things before wall drop rather than just going on a diamond hunt.
