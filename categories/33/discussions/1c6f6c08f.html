Brawling Basket Ball (Avicade) (ACCEPT PLZ) by Tigor101 May 31, 2014 at 4:05 AM UTC

Avicade needs some more games, so this is a basketball game with twists with two teams of 8 people. You can hit players to slow their offense/defense. Also to avoid trolling, the ball holder can have the ball for a maximum of 30 seconds (it's gets passed to a random opponent). A player can intercept a pass if the opponent accidentally passes the ball to you. Each shot made will be 2 points each and the first team to get to 20 wins. 

Kits:
Baller (lvl 1)- just an ordinary basketball player

Tall Johnny(lvl 25)- a tall basketball player who takes 50% less knockback and deals 50% knock back

Superstar(donator)- the MVP who runs fast with a speed 2

Notes: the ball is orange wool. Also, those who will say this isn't original this is the ONLY thing that I found when I looked up Basket Ball in Minecraft..... http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wo9-OJ9i24g and it is not fun at all. So, I figure this would be a lot better than ^.  Last thing, I did make a post back on the old forums but I'll delete that one.
