Battle of Heroes

There will be many different types of game-types within this game-mode. In this game-mode you will battle each other and team with each other as... you guessed it superheroes!
The game-types:
Battle of Heroes - This new game-type will pit players against each other in a Free For All arena. If you have a friend you want to team you can make a team of a max of 2 players. The person or 2 player team with the most kills wins. Any hero may be used.

Team of Heroes - This game-type will have 2-4 teams of 1 certain hero for each team. The team with the most kills wins.

World Domination - In this controller game-mode there will be several "hills" on the map. You score a point for every second you have control of a hill. There will be 2-4 teams on this one each team with one hero-type only. The team with the most points wins.

Survive the Villains - In this Game type there will be 2 teams one of villains that you can choose from and one of heroes you can choose from. The heroes have to survive the villains for 5 min. and the villains have to kill all the heroes before time is up. When a hero dies he/she becomes a villain.
