Unique Qualities (Feedback) by Titan16 October 23, 2014 at 12:10 PM UTC

So as everyone knows, the goal of Avicus is to consist of unique mini games, and contain nothing "bland." But from the feedback people have been giving us, It's coming to the realization that this attempt in making Avicus unique has been going downhill. I mean, I'm sure you all can agree. We now get an average of 130 players on at a time, when it used to be between 1,500-2,000 in McZone. So, we need to find ways to improve the qualities of Avicus one step at a time. Any feedback is accepted, unique or not. We need ideas. 

Thanks for reading. -Jake
