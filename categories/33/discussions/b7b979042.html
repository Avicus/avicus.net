A way to spice up Elimination by ClubberNugget May 27, 2014 at 6:05 PM UTC

Seeing that the Winter update is coming at some point soon, and from what I've heard, Eliminstion is not leaving.  So, with that update coming soon, i thought a bump of this topic was in order.  The goal of this thread is to revive elimination, here are some suggestions, It’s a mouthful but please read it:).

Disasters:

So Elimination is getting repetitive.  One guy wins the early blood bath and then dominates the game because he has a lot of cool stuff.  Well, if we add natural disasters, it could mix things up a little.  I’m thinking every time the total number of players are halved, a disaster occurs.  Here are ten ideas for disasters:

Tornado: A tornado tears through the map and picks players off their feet.  “Sky bases beware”. Lasts one minute.

Sandstorm: The map gets foggy and players take one damage every five seconds regardless of armor, “find cover!!” Lasts four minutes.  (This disaster only happens in dessert and beach biomes.)

Earthquake: The ground shakes, the map is torn apart, and every grounded player takes heavy damage, lasts five seconds.

Flood: Water covers the map to the top of pine trees, “miners beware”, lasts four minutes.

Forest fire: Lava randomly spawns around trees and the forest catches a flame. “Get water quick!!”  lava spawns, no time limit forest burns until its gone.

Famine: All trees die, plants die, water dries out and the worst of all, all animals die.  It stays this way the rest of the game.  “Hopefully you hunted for food beforehand”
Avalanche: Snowballs with sharpness 2 are thrown down the mountain, and if that’s bad, have fun getting buried in blocks of snow falling from the mountain as well.  Lasts three minutes.  (only in tundra and ice mountain biomes.)

Mob swarm: One night, the mobs decide to take over, they are everywhere, zombies, creepers, ender men, skeletons and spiders.  Players have nowhere to hide, they will fall if they dare face the mighty army of monsters.  Lasts one night.

Nether mob: The regular mobs hide in fear, for one night, the nether visits the regular world, that’s right, blaze, magma cubes, pig men and wither skeletons.  And the worst of all, a ghast, roams the map.  Lasts one night.

Rise of the Undead: Every spectator comes back into the game, they spawn with the kit they played with, they have one minute to kill a tribute, if they do, they come back to life.  If they don’t, they go back to spectating.  Lats one minute (Undead can’t do damage to each other.)  

Volcanic eruption:
lava oozes out of the mountain and burns the entire map, "better get above ground and quick." lava falls and stays for the rest of the game.*

Meteor strike:
a fireball hits the surface and creates a giant Crater the ground, pretty much kills any player that it hits (or heavily damages.)  there is also diamond and iron ores inside. "no, pain no gain."  meteor hits and then creates crater, no time limit.*

(New) Blackout: The sun died out!!!! Everyone is stuck with blindness 3 (Or whatever works), and now seeing is a difficult task.  (Lasts for 5min.)

(New) Hardcore time: Everyone’s max health is one heart!!! You better watch your step, one slip and you’re done, lasts for the rest of the game.

Note: many of these could be like the extreme weather mod (at least that’s what I think it’s called.)

Well thanks for reading:) please leave suggestions, let’s put elimination back on the map :D
