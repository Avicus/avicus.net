cinammoncroc000 September 15, 2014 at 2:09 PM UTC

- PVPing
Like other people said, strafing is generally the way to go. Moving around your opponent (with the a and d keys) is the basic way to strafe, but guides on YouTube and even on forums give better explanation.
There's also block hitting, where you block and hit at the same time. It reduces damage inflicted on you while still allowing you to attack. However, your movement speeds becomes horribly slower and your aim generally becomes worse, so I only recommend it in tight situations.
Plaza's PvP Guide:
http://avicus.net/forums/e3c991988

- Getting to monuments
Try to avoid making contact with enemy players. Your goal is to break monuments, not kill. Only kill the players if they really get in the way, like when they're chasing you. Use blocks to create bridges and wallsprint quickly across the side of enemy bases. Use the sneak key wisely to hide your nametag. Watch rushers like JKawesome, sfkwww, Sethskye, etc. and see what their tactics are. Generally you want to keep a sharp eye and have quick reflexes.
- Getting shot off the world as easily 
Well, on skybridges, you kinda just have to hope your opponent doesn't have the greatest aim. Try to avoid jumping on bridges without guardrails, because if you take a shot while you jump you are almost guaranteed to fall. Try stopping and starting your sprint to throw off your opponent's aim.
- Getting to know the map as well
This comes with experience. Go as a spectator and explore the map. Watch Avicus regulars play, because they know most of the maps' secrets.

I hope I helped at least a little bit! :D

JKawesome September 17, 2014 at 3:09 AM UTC

I made a rushing tips post a few months back, if you want you can go find it. I'll give some general rushing tips:

Always have a distraction
If you want to rush, it is always best to have a distraction. For example, a mistake a lot of rushers make is running to the monument and trying to kill all the defenders, which is pretty much impossible. However, you can use that guy to your advantage. Rush right behind them and while the defenders are distracted, surround yourself and break the monument. 

Don't go into a fight you can't win
This is a pretty straightforward tip, but it's unbelievable how many people jinx it. If you see a diamond guy, don't try to kill him...you can't, unless you're Cayden. Just run past him; if he chases you, spam blocks in his face and somehow hide. If it's a void map, clutch to the side and dig in. When they jump down you can knock them off.

Don't be seen; blend in
Again, another basic tip that people don't care about, or don't do well enough. If the monument is out in the open, wait until the defender is crafting, or distracted, or something. Don't run in there and expect to kill him. If you're not seen, its really easy to break monuments. I once played a Frostbite match that had a glass box with 10 diamond guys in it. I went in there and pretended to be a defender, then broke the monument without them noticing at all.

So, those are just some general tips that rushers should follow. Hope I helped
