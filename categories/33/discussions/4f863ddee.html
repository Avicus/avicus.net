[U]GoldRush - New GM Idea by ProfessorUtonium October 12, 2014 at 6:10 PM UTC

New Gamemode idea
2 teams:Red and Blue
Medieval themed maps
You can attack or defend. First to take the most gold is victorious
This is like CTW And instead you need to obtain the most gold blocks And bring it back to your base. 30m limit.  You can kill players or take whole blocks from enemies.  You can only hold 1 gold block at a time. If an enemy destroys the defense you can repair it. If you get a gold block you need to throw it in a hopper. This GM can be won by people who want to get kills or people who like to get monuments on nebula

Scoreboard: On the scoreboard it goes
Red
GoldB:-#-
Blue
GoldB:-#-

Equipment: Leather dyed armor
StoneSword
Bow Infinity
DPick UB3Eff4 
Stack of Stone, Glass, Wood
 

If you kill someone you get 1 gold nugget
 Max Build Height:4025 team limits
Can't place blocks on the side of the map
