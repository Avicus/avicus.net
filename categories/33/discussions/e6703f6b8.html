Jongh September 26, 2015 at 4:09 PM UTC

For me it just came naturally, after I was doing a ton of shift staircase bridging on hypixel skywars. After a while I could feel something in my fingers, like I'd want to go faster with my bridges but I didn't have the skill to do so. 

Then in a high stress situation (OCN ranked), I just spontaneously started to ninja staircase without ever having done it before, and ever since then I've had it, as well as normal ninja bridge.

If you can't seem to get it while practicing, I suggest you just play a lot more while trying to do a bunch of shift staircase bridging, and try to go faster and faster when doing it, which will bring you closer to doing a ninja bridge. Regular shift bridging won't really help that much, as it's not something where you can go faster in any way.
