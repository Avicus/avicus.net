JDKL November 8, 2017 at 5:11 PM UTC

Although it has been a while, the Administration team will continue to post on here to inform you of recent progress and administrative work. 
So here is our most recent work:

- Looking into new payment methods for the online Avicus Store. We are trying to cover a number of different methods of payments for our customers, so expect new payments gateways soon!
- The Avicus Instagram account (@officialavicus) is now a business account, to cater to our company's needs. This also allows us to promote Avicus across Instagram - promoted advertisements. 
- Improving Administrator efficiency. Recently, we introduced an Administrator Trello for better organization and rate of work. 
- Promoting voting more. 
- Organising and improving Moderation tools (guidelines, mutes (thank you to ALM, etc)). 
- Formation of the assembly team (more info here: https://avicus.net/forums/discussions/168791e0d).
- Working with Owners to decide the direction of Avicus.
- Looking at new ideas to improve the state of the Avicus store - better prices and perks. 
- Keeping consistency throughout all sectors. Small changes have been made to ensure all departments have the same work resources and workspace. 
- Working with the Outreach team (Kord) on our most recent advertisement. We have noticed gradual improvements in our player counts, which we are hoping to continue. A big thank you to Kord for his hard work in our current and starting advertisement.
- Check up on Content Creators. We are also looking for new ideas to further promote the rank.
