hasl May 22, 2016 at 9:05 PM UTC

What does my team need to do to get a ts channel?

    • At least 10 active players who are not banned.

    • At least 10 logged scrimmages.

    • 10 Players with over 24 hours of ingame time (who are not banned).

    • 10 Players with at least 2500 kills, or 100 objectives (who are not banned).

    • Official consent (At Least 2). (In the event that a Head of Teamspeak sees fit, we can approve/deny your request for a channel.)

    • Failure to be active on your channel will result in a reconsideration of whether it should remain.
