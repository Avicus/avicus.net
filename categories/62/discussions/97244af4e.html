Hello, 

The Qualifiers will take place on Saturday July 22nd and Saturday July 29th.
Below are the start times for both Group A & B on those days:


Group A: 
Check-in: Saturday 22nd, 11:30am EDT/4:30pm BST
Start-Time: Saturday 22nd, 12pm EDT/5pm BST.

Group B: 
Check-in: Saturday 29th, 11:30am EDT/4:30pm BST
Start-Time: Saturday 29th, 12pm EDT/5pm BST.
(All matches together will take approximately 3 hours to complete)


From the 10 teams playing in the group stages, 6 will advance onto a single elimination invitational, in which the winner will be decided.
