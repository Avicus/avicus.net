Team Channel Information and Applications by ProfessorUtonium December 17, 2016 at 11:12 PM UTC

Hello.

All teams must now apply for a team channel when there is no tournament happening at the time. This is to organize team channels and a way so players with upcoming teams can create a channel without going through the old process. For all teams with a team channel currently, they have been removed and you must reapply to obtain it. Once the tournament begins, all teams registered for the tournament will automatically be granted the team channel then removed once again for another application period for team channels. Team channel applications will never be closed (even during tournaments) and will be available for anyone with a 12 or more players in a team to apply.

Team Requirements (Banned players do not count towards the following requirements):
  • 12 or more players in the team 
  • 10 or more players with 12 hours (or more) of playtime
  • 10 or more players with 500 (or more) kills


Here is the application

Good luck with your application. All passwords and channel information will be sent to you via email.
