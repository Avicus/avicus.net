Banner by Fouled

Hello,

We are happy and excited to announce our 5th tournament, Conquest Inc. This will be a CTF & 5CP/KoTH tournament.

Tournament Format

The tournament will be a swiss-style tournament for the group stages, and double elimination for the invitationals.

4 teams at minimum & 8 teams at maximum will be accepted into the invitationals, depends on the number of teams accepted.

Both during the group stages and the invitationals the games will be bo5.

A single match will run until completion or 12 minutes maximum, leaving 45 minutes per round slot.


Map Pool

The Map Pool will be announced and finalised on the 8th of April, but refer to our WIP Map pool until then.

In the qualifiers, all rounds will have a set map set to each match, which will be announced with the teams being announced. We will have 5 maps in the tournament.

Team Information

One team may not have less than 12 players registered, and no more than 18. All matches will be 8v8. Teams will need at least 6 players to participate in a match - if they do not have 6 when the match is scheduled to start, they will be disqualified.

Match Time

Check in: 4:30pm BST / 11:30am EDT
Matches: 5pm BST / 12:00pm EDT

Registration


Team leaders will have to decide on a maximum of 18 players to register for the tournament. When registration opens, the team leaders will select 18 members of their choice to play in the tournament. Each player will have to confirm their own participation by pressing the 'accept' button in their alerts. You can register at the tournaments page when registration opens. That tournament will be created soon. If you have any concerns, please email officials@avicus.net. Bear in mind, you can not add alts to your roster so your team can meet the requirements.

Important Dates:

(Your team needs to consider that you could have to play on any of these dates when signing up:)


Tournament Announcement: 2nd of April

Registration Opens: 8th of April  

Maps Announced: 8th of April

Registration Closes: 15th of April

Teams Announced: 15th of April

Matchups Announced: 22nd of April

Group Stages: 29th and 30th of April

Invitationals: 6th/7th of May
