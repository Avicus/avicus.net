Hello, 

Registration for Race to Victory II has ended, and the Officials have accepted the following teams:

Note: You must leave your team name as listed above, which are the names at the time of registration closing. This is the first warning, after this you get 1 more warning then a disqualification. 

You can ask for your team and Team Leader (two per team) ranks on Discord now. 


Tournament Format:

The group stage will consist of 2 groups of 5/6 teams. Each team will verse every other team in their assigned group. After the group stage, the top 3 teams from each group will move on to the single-elimination final stage, with the 1st seed in each group receiving a direct spot to the semi-finals. 

The group stage will take place on Saturday July 22nd and Saturday July 29th for Group A and B. 

If you have any questions, feel free to ask them below and we will do our best to answer them.

Regards, 
The Official Team.
