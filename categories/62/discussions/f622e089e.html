Check in will be done 30 minutes before the first  match is scheduled to start. If you do not have any players in your team channel 15 minutes before the match is scheduled to start, you will get a no show. 2 no shows and your team is disqualified. If you do not have 6 players when the match is scheduled to start, you will also get a no show because you need 6 players to play. A referee should join your channel 15 minutes before a match is scheduled to start.
