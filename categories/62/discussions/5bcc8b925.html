After all the Blubber Blabber, we will be using challonge! The tournament is formatted with 7 teams in Group A, and 6 teams in Group B

Bracket: http://avicus.challonge.com/battleofeternityII

Match Times: 
Group A will begin at 11:00am EST and each team will play six matches. We are giving roughly a two hour timeslot for this.
                                  [4:00pm GMT]
Group B will begin at 1:00pm EST and each team will play five matches, again giving two hours, but will most likely finish earlier.
                                  [6:00pm GMT]
Things To Remember:
  • We will only wait 10 minutes after the scheduled time to start the match (except in extreme circumstances)
  • Please make sure you have your team rank in discord. It is required that you use this discord to communicate with your team. (https://discord.gg/KMpbZh8)
  • Please make sure you have someone show up at least 10 minutes prior to the starting time to do the map veto. 
Any questions you have may be asked below. Good luck everyone!
