Battle Of Eternity II: Tournament 7 by PokerFace October 29, 2017 at 9:10 AM UTC



Thank you  KojaCola for making the banner.

Hello, we are happy to announce our 7th tournament, Battle of Eternity II. The gamemode will be regular TDM with scoreboxes. Originally we were going to do a 5v5, but we decided that a change that big would be too drastic so we will keep 8v8 for now and try to adapt to a 5v5 scenario in the future. The tournament format will be determined based on the amount of teams that register, but we will most likely be having a group stage with the top teams from each group advancing to the invitationals. Match times will most likely start at 4:00 PM (GMT) and go on for a few hours after that.

Registration:

Register here: https://avicus.net/tournaments/battle-of-eternity-ii

Important Dates:

  • Registration opens: October 30th
  • Registration closes: November 15th
  • Bracket+Times Released: November 16th
  • Group Stage: November 18th
  • Invitationals: November 25th

Roster Information:

  • Minimum: 10 players registered
  • Maximum: 14 players registered

Match Information:

  • All matches will be 8v8
  • Each map is 12 minutes long 
  • Bo1 group stages
  • Bo3 invitational stages

Map Pool:

  • Arcadia
  • Plaza
  • Strike
  • Crossfire
  • Injustice
  • Endurance
  • Actrix
*Please note that some maps still have some technical issues, which should be resolved soon. Feel free to message any official or map developer about any problems you should encounter.

Map Veto:

Bo1

  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Remaining map is played.
Bo3
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Team A picks a map
  • Team B picks a map
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Remaining map is played (if necessary)

Tournament Discord:

  • https://discord.gg/KMpbZh8
  • Every player and team will be required to use this discord throughout the entirety of the tournament. Team channels will be provided.

Rules:

  • Refer to all the rules listed here: https://avicus.net/forums/discussions/9bdf40274
  • By particpating in this tournament, you agree to the ruleset.
