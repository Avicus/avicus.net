Roster Sizes for Tournament 7! by LFT August 9, 2017 at 9:08 PM UTC

Howdy!

The Officials have been talking and have decided that it would be best to drop the tournament rosters sizes a bit. We felt that 18 was too much and seeing as a bunch of teams have extra players that could play, and maybe even get a few extra teams out of it.

So, we've decided to drop the minimum amount of players needed to register from 12 to 8, as well as drop the maximum amount of players you're able to register from 18 to 14.

This is NOT an Official change for all future tournaments, however it will be if you guys like it.

Regards,

The Avicus Official Team.
