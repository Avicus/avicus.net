Tournament Blacklists by Tazz July 31, 2016 at 12:07 AM UTC

Hey,

As you have probably realised, tournament blacklists have now been implemented, thanks to ALM.

Currently, tournament blacklists block access to avicus.net/teams and /tournaments, which also means when blacklisted it kicks you from your team. This means you can not be on a team, and if you are on a team on an alt account and you play on a scrim server you will be permanently banned for Ban Evasion, starting from right now. If you are on a team on an alternate account and you can't leave that team for whatever reason, email officials@avicus.net as soon as possible so you are not permanently banned.

Unfortunately we had to blacklist around 45 players which is a shame after the most successful tournament yet, being last tournament. 

All tournament blacklists can be found here: 
http://hastebin.com/ebojeposef.css

To appeal a tournament ban please email officials@avicus.net. If you make an appeal on the forums you will instantly be redirected to there. Moderators, leave any tournament related ban to Officials please to save being complained at by me :D

The format of a tournament ban message is [TOURNEY] Blacklist Reason [LENGTH], and it will always stay like this from now.

Regards 

The Avicus Network Officials
