Scrimmage Improvements and Updates by ALM July 2, 2017 at 1:07 AM UTC

Hello,

With the recent way the scrimmage servers have been running, we have decided to change the system up a bit in order to fix some of the errors in the other system - such as not enough time for servers uptime per reservation or just problems with reserving them in general.

This system will incorporate the use of donator perks more and will obviously work for every user still. All you need is at least one donator to reserve the scrimmage servers (if you want the extra perks). Here is a brief run-down:



Max Instantaneous Servers | Max Servers per week (resets Sunday) | Max Length

Normal 1 | 5 | 45m
Gold 1 | 7 | 1h
Emerald 2 | 10 | 1:30
Diamond 4 | 15 |  2:30

Each level of donator rank will receive different perks in regards to scrimmage servers. The first number is the max amount of servers that will be able to reserved by a specific single team. Please do not use all 4 reservations at a single time to get access to 4 scrimmage servers as it's considered hogging the servers and will lead to some form of punishment (possibly a blacklist from scrimmage servers for a specific amount of time). If it becomes a big enough problem, the numbers will be adjusted. 

The second number is the max amount of servers able to be reserved per week. Users without donation perks will be able to reserve 5 scrimmage slots per week and so on as listed. 

The third and final number is the max length of time that the server reservation will run for. If you do not want the full time available in your perks list then you are also able to select a specific time such as a reservation for 30 minutes instead of 2 hours and 30 minutes.



NOTE: I will repeat this again as it's important: Please do not use all 4 reservations (if you're a diamond donator) at a single time to get access to 4 scrimmage servers as it's
considered hogging the servers and will lead to some form of punishment (possibly a blacklist from scrimmage servers for a specific
amount of time). If it becomes a big enough problem, the numbers will be adjusted.

If there are any other problems or concerns regarding the new scrimmage server system, then please be sure to post it below or message an administrator.

Regards,
The Administation & Development Team
