BOE II: Official Map Pool+Veto Process by KojaCola October 30, 2017 at 1:10 AM UTC

Battle of Eternity II // Tournament 7

Official Map Pool


The map pool of Battle of Eternity II consists of seven Team Deathmatch maps, which are as follows:

Crossfire by  Eteh Legoche


Plaza by PokerFace Bloodoh


Strike by Spoookeh


Arcadia by Zedther


Injustice by lgmatias


Endurance by  Passively


Actrix by keenanjt Tazz gobernment
 

Map Veto Process:

Bo1

  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Remaining map is played.
Bo3
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Team A picks a map
  • Team B picks a map
  • Team A bans a map
  • Team B bans a map
  • Remaining map is played (if necessary)

Notes:

  • Some maps may have errors, and will be fixed. Please report them to officials or map developers.
