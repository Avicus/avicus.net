Introducing Myself + AMA! by shaarpie July 18, 2016 at 6:07 PM UTC

Hello Avicus Community, I am Sharpie.

I started playing on the Overcast Network on 2013 (not on this account) and that's pretty much the only server I played regularly. Sadly, it seems like it's coming to an end soon. I had great times playing and refereeing in there, but I guess I have to move on.

Unlike many OCN people that will move to Badlion, I won't. Because what hooked me up in there was the small, friendly community. I think Avicus has a small friendly community too, at least from what I have seen. Sure there are jerks (also in OCN), but for the most part it's good.

Also Avicus is the closest thing to OCN that you can play right now (although I don't really like Throwback maps and that's the only thing that is played :/).

As for personal information, I am 17, going to start my last year of high school (12th grade for muricans) in September. I am from Spain and I like playing sports and going out and doing all those social kind of things, but I also like to spend some time with my computer :p

Well, go ahead, ask me anything :)

EDIT: i will be more active here once OCN shuts down. Until then I will spend my time in there :)
