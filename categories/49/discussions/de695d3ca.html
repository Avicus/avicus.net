Guys,
Sorreh all you guys,
But now I'm seeing obligated to leave Avicus. I joined May 27, 2 days after Avicore update (Avicus 2.0). In another server a guy invited me to play Nexus, he passed me the IP via skype and I joined Avicus. First, I played Nexus, it was beautiful. I felt very good and funny playing here. 2 or 3 days after it, I registered to Avicus website and forums. In that moment, I joined the best community in the world, based on a server and with awesome guys. But, in this community are awesome guys and, other guys....
 I was part of Avicus for a long time, doing that day and day passed, I feel more special and fine in this community. I felt inclusive as a part of the Avicus Family. But days ago I had problems with the school, but, what is so mattering school?! To be honest, my problem wasn't only the school, was my friendships, my relationships, my real family, and all that make us really special.
So, I chose to leave Avicus. I don't know if you noted that for the last 3 or 2 days I wasn't online in-game. But now is the really decision of quit, leave a big community that was very special for me and now I feel this like my family. Understand me, leave Avicus is like leave my real family. But I can't stand it anymore.....
