*Opinions?
You're a great mod. Back in my trial, I used to ask you lots of questions and you didn't just answer them, you also gave me optional tips and feedback, which I really appreciated. :) That of course, makes you a nice person from my point of view. I had a few laughs when you were on TS and when we were moderating together, but you never heard them. :P

If you ever come back as Junior, what are your plans?
As a Junior, I will first thank each Sr. Mod and Admin that they gave me another chance and I'd probably just do the same job I did last time. But of course, I would try to always act as a Junior/staff member and be more respectful towards other moderators/staff members. If you want to know more, just read my application, you have permissions to do it.

What motivates you to play Avicus?
Back in the days where prestiges were still a thing, the prestiges of course. I always played and played just to get as much XP as possible. There were fun gamemods such as Red Alert, Minotaur, OITC (It had another name) and of course DTM with lots of great maps. (Quintus, Original Luar, Ternio, Ravaged etc.) Today, to be honest, the biggest reason I'm still playing is that I want to become staff members to help the mods with their inactivity problem and just to help other players who need help. If I wouldn't have that reason, I'd probably be very inactive and just come on at special events and scrims. Right now, I'm a little bit hyped about Arcade too. I know it won't get played very often but it's still nice to have it back!

I don't have one (right now) but if I must say one, it'd be 142 because it's at the end of one of my usernames. xD
