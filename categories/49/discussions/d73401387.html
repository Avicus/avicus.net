Hey

I made an AMA on my alt but I'm going to do one on my main now

It all started when Notal introduced me to Project Ares in late 2012. Within 6 months, I became a known player and I played in GOG with Vicious and Delicious. After that tourney, we disbanded and I joined a whole bunch of teams including Ambush (for a while, not long) and Escalate. I made good friends with Fire961 and N3XT_M3CH_P0W3R during that time. In November 2013, a VnD remake was created called EfficiencyV. It consisted of known octc players today including: Zenniker, Emosnail, freekkiller and Eulenspieler. However, things turned quickly and we were disbanded, but we had a shit ton of potential. I am still good friends with most of these today.

After that, I quit OCTC and started to play HCF with SectorOne, thanks to copito3011, Fire961 and N3XT. I played with ylo and emo on hcf at this time - this is map 13-14. I became quite known in the EU timezone and now we find ourselves in map 26 where I would consider myself quite a well known respected pot pvper. However, HCF became stale around map 19 and I started to play CS:GO. I started as Silver 3 in late August and I am know Distinguished Master Guardian (5 ranks from global).

I am here at avicus to get back into things, get my skill back and soon play in tourneys on OCTC and Avicus. My role is offence and I am now one of the strategists and offenders for Bad Company in the upcoming tourney.
