The Avicus Network by hcnem March 29, 2016 at 3:03 AM UTC

So, you're probably going to think this is some formal post where I announce something that means nothing to you until I explain it (see how I didn't say the "Hello" at the beginning), right? Well, not exactly. 

I've been sitting here as a staff member for almost three years, and all you've most likely come to know me as is "Peachy, the Admin." Just the guy who sits up in his little TeamSpeak office, acting like he's doing things for the server? No? How about the guy who seems like he runs the show behind the scenes, but you actually don't know anything about him or what he does? A bit more likely.

Well, let's start simply. My name is Ryan, and I live in the USA. I am currently a sophomore in High School who enjoys playing tennis, researching medicine, and competitive PvP. Okay, basics done. Let's move on to specifics. My job here? Well, my main priority is making you guys mad. Minor things that I do include handling promotions/demotions, executing changes to policies/rules, setting priorities and goals for the server, managing senior staff, listening to Zac in the support room, etc. The list goes on. Anyway, isn't this exactly what I told you I was? One of the guys who runs the show behind the scenes? Well, yeah. Nothing special, most of you could probably do it better than I can. Let's move on though, there will be time to trash talk me later.

Avicus. You've heard that name before, but you're not sure where, right? Well, yeah, probably from some advertisement on TV or something like that. What is Avicus, though? Is it a business run by a corrupt Senior Staff team trying to gain a ton of power on a Minecraft server? Sounds strange, right? Let's try again. Is it a metaphor for how there are lots of greedy and selfish people in the world whom you can't trust, but also how there are people who try their best to do good for the world? A bit farfetched, don't you think? Alright, one more time. Avicus is a community, upheld by unique and welcoming people. Avicus is a place for friendship, safety, and most of all, fun. Avicus is a place where NO ONE is left behind, ostracized, or purely disregarded, and also a place where EVERYONE has a voice and recognition. 

So, why don't we all work in cooperation to fix the problems that are blocking our path to prosperity? Put away the differences, accusations, and arguments in order to put this place back onto its position of glory. 

Now that I've turned off Gandhi mode, I'm sure you either think I'm crazy and should be demoted, or that I should just be demoted. That may be true, but I told you there will be time to trash talk me later, didn't I? Here are my final questions for you, though:
Who are you, and what makes you unique?
What is it about Avicus that makes you stay with it?
What, in your eyes, is the best path for the server at this moment?

THAT IS ALL AM DONE WASTING TIME
