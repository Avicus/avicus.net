Here is some basic FAQ(s) :) 

1st) How do I become Staff?
You may apply for Staff on the forums! ;D They just closed D; So, when they open, you may submit your Staff Application under the subform "Staff Applications" (:

2) What are the rules? 
Here ya go! ;D ClickForRules:D

3) Are Staff nice?
We do our best to be supportive and the best we can provide for you! In that case, yes ^~^ We try to be as approachable as we can! ;D Don't worry, we won't bite (:

4) People accuse me of hacking, when I don't?
Accusing other of hacking is against the rules, so if anyone accuses you of hacking, you may use /Report (: If they accuse you, and you don't hack, don't fear about it (:

5)  How do I report people?
You may report people on the forums on the Subforum! On the Left side, there is a Subforum Topic stated as "Player reports" In there,follow the guideline given to submit one, then create your report by clicking "New Discussion", then support your player report with solid evidence (: 

For Ingame, you may report a player if he/she is breaking th rules by using the command "./report (player) (reason)" so a Staff Member can arrive as fast as possible ! (:
