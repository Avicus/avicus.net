Will Cord ever be given access to rotation editing? Why or why not?

Opinions?
You were an okay mod (you had the rank for a really long time for some reason), and a fine mentor. I really have no doubts about you or your capabilities.

Will Cord ever be given access to rotation editing? Why or why not?
No.

Cord has retired. We do not rely on people who are no longer on the map development team to perform map development tasks.

Why are maps like Dust Devils and Halcyon still in rotations? In the previous rotation, they were the most enjoyable maps but considering they've been in rotations endlessly for the past year or so, they're getting a bit dry.
There's a reason that Dust Devils is still in the rotations, and it has nothing to do with Halcyon.

Halcyon was one of the nostalgic maps I added to rotations in order to somewhat appease the community, who I thought would have otherwise endlessly complained about the lack of Throwback in the rotations. It appears to have served its purpose until recently.

How often do you plan on changing the rotations?
I see a lot of people demanding rotation updates every week or two weeks. To those people, I have to ask; what do you want me to do? Completely redo the rotation every two weeks? If we had a hundred good maps of each gamemode, maybe we could actually do proper biweekly rotation updates. But, there simply aren't enough maps (unless you want them to be endlessly recirculated in and out, which is just an unnecessary hassle in my opinion.)

Keep in mind that I'm talking here about significant rotation updates. Of course, we will still constantly maintain the rotations by removing bad maps, and possibly adding newer ones. In fact, maybe this can be we will do: Instead of having a large update every month (certainly not every two weeks), we could replace maps more frequently in much smaller portions.

To definitively answer your question, the frequency of future rotation changes is currently undecided. My thoughts on the matter are described in the above two paragraphs.

If you have any questions about anything that I said in this post, feel free to message me on discord.
