Javipepe August 22, 2015 at 4:08 PM UTC

Basically I kept failing with the HTML and those kind of shits. How to get used to it?
Perseverance and patience makes a great programmer. Always think what you want to archive before getting into the code, imagine the design, the usages, think about what you really want to have and what would be second priority. Then start with the framework, before getting into the details. When you just don't know how to do something and you can't figure it out, Google it and go into w3schools links or stack overflow, they will sure have the best answer. 

Also it's also helpful to talk with someone that knows about it from time to time for better methods to do the same so your code isn't too messy, happens a lot and it's a headache truly.
