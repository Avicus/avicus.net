Howsie January 3, 2015 at 3:01 PM UTC

How did you get to watching MLP? :D
Jesus, thats one hell of a question.

Well, it all started around march-april 2013. Essentially by that point I was fully aware that it existed and there was a fan base outside of its demographic, but it never struck me as something worth my time or interest in. I essentially dismissed it really. Never rejected it, just didn't care much for it. 

So one day I found one of the songs from the show. (Winter Wrap Up) Being a fan of cool music and decent animation it was a very big shock to find out that I was actually enjoying it. One thing led to another and I was watching episodes of season 1. By the time I had finished up season 1, I was both incredibly confused and very curious about this new guilty pleasure I had discovered. 

I did the research about the fandom, the show, its background, poured through pages of the  MLP wikia. Essentially what I always do when discovering something new. Eventually I had become enough of a fan to finally become a member of the fandom (and while I'm not a great fan of the name) I essentially became a brony.
