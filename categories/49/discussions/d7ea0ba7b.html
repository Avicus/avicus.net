Project Redemption Spotlights #4 - dev_revs by BoldAndBrash November 29, 2016 at 11:11 PM UTC

Hello, guys! I'm here with the another addition to the PR Spotlights series! The interviewee is dev_revs!

What is your team name?
Our team name is Team Rocket.

What have you done to prepare for Project Redemption?
We have worked on teamwork, practiced against each other, and strategized.

Has your team played in past tournaments? How do you think this'll effect your experience?
Our first tournament was BoE, didn't go so well for us. I think now that we have experienced it we will be ready for Redemption island.

What are your team's major weaknesses and strengths?
Some of our major strengths are working together, having each others back, and having fun. Weakness is being a Spanish/English team.

What do you think of the teams you are set to face?
We are interested to see what the teams bring to the table. Home has a couple players who we have to watch out for. Don't know much about spacedeepers or labradoodles, but allah is going to be a difficult opponent and cennet will be fun to play against. 

Is there anything you'd like to say to your opponents?
Good luck to our opponents, don't cheat. Team Allah should start Liz so I can give her the L 

Any predictions for Project Redemption? Upset victories? Controversies?
Good luck to our opponents, don't cheat. Team Allah should start Liz so I can give her the L 
Their are a couple teams I think are going to be busts, some sneaky teams you have to look out for. I have my picks for invitational on who I think will make it, and we all will have to see. I was hoping Apollo would take first, but looks like ALM already did.. 

Anything else you'd like to mention?
Watch my tournament livestreams for group A and Prepare for Trouble. (make it double)
