Conquest Inc. Spotlight #2 - Muharnmad by BoldAndBrash April 21, 2017 at 12:04 AM UTC

What is your IGN and team? Muharnmad/InBlox/InLoc and my team is Bulldogs/PureDogs.

How many tournaments have you participated in? This will be our first avicus tournament!

Any team in particular you'd like to face off against? Apollo. I'm curious how we would do against them because I see them as a benchmark for invitational-level teams.

Any team you don't want to face off against? Not really. I guess I just don't want to be up against anyone that no-shows. It's really disappointing to get 8 players and think you're about to play only to find out the other team didn't show up.

How do you think you'll do this time around? I expect we will definitely make invitationals. The goal is to reach the finals, maybe win them.

Any teams you have a rivalry with? Not really. I guess the closest thing would be Team Allah because their roster is pretty similar to yirang (on OCN) and ours is pretty similar to PureBlox, and those two teams had a little bit of a rivalry.

What do you think is your team's best strength, and your best strength alone? I think our best strength is our pvp. Mine is probably that I know everyone on the team and their abilities, so it should be pretty easy to lead them.

What do you think is your team's worst weakness, and your worst weakness alone? A lot of us haven't played since OCN shut down, so communication and rotations might not be super easy. Mine personally is that when my team is losing I play too recklessly and overall a little worse.

What have you done to prepare? We've played a couple scrims, hopefully some more before the tournament.

Anything you'd like to say to your opponents? Woof
