Opinions?
You've always been someone I admired for your continuous dedication to the server even when things don't go your way. You seem like a great bloke who always finds a way to try help out. Can't wait to see what you do in the future.

Thoughts on the current staff?
I think they're alright. They're better in terms of activity but still small issues we need to fix up. 

Where would you like the server to be in 6 months?
If it was what I want it to be, a good active, quality staff team with strong leadership in the senior staff team. Constant map rotation updates to remove stale gameplay, new advertising to promote the server and hopefully a rising player base. Of course these are aspirations so it's going to require hard work from everyone to make it happen.

What areas does the server need to focus on the most in your opinion?
Advertisement has always been a struggle on the server. We had a real opportunity when Cord was head of social media. Finally we had someone who knew what they were doing and we blew it when those who needed to listen most, simply wouldn't.
