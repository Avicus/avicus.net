Oooh this is a great series of questions.

Favourite Mod? Least Favourite Mod?
I hate to be a party-pooper, but I'm really not a huge fan of picking specific favourites. I will hold high respect for anyone who does their job well with little to no complaints, and who will address any complaints they may have in a way that actually contributes to growth within the team. Any staff who either don't do their job properly without a good reason, or staff that complain about things without either coming up with ideas to improve the situations or explaining things in a concise and reasonable manner will always grind my gears. The thing I value most within a staff team is constant improvement and growth, as well as strong teamwork. You may allow this description to decide if you fall under a favourite mod of mine, or least favourite. ;p

Favourite Senior Mod? Least Favourite Senior Mod?
Similar to Moderators, as long as you perform your assigned duties without any major hassle or unreasonable complaints, I will respect you. If you exceed your duties, I will hold high amounts of respect for you. If you slack off for a poor (or no) reason, then that's when that respect starts to go down a bit. Senior staff do work very closely with admins, which I know from personal experience, so it's very important that Senior Moderators go above and beyond whenever possible so that they can be good examples to Moderators, and show that they also want the team to grow and improve. The senior staff that have this in mind will always have my utmost respect.  

Favourite Admin?
If we're talking about current admins, then all I can say is that all 4 are great. I can't even choose a favourite or least favourite, since all 4 admins have very specific tasks and responsibilities that they all carry out to the best of their abilities. I have spoken to all 4 in the past, and not once have I ever had any major, or even minor issues with any of them. All 4 of  them perform their necessary tasks, and do so in a considerable fashion. Sure, all 4 of them have made mistakes in the past, just like every moderator and senior moderator has, but I still hold high respects for all 4 of them due to the high amounts of effort they have put into keeping things alive here.

Least favourite staff rank?
Every staff rank has a purpose, whether that purpose be to create the maps that we play on, develop the necessary plugins needed for the server to function properly, or to ban rulebreakers so that the innocent players can enjoy their time on the server. Since every rank we have does indeed have some important purpose, I do not particularly have a favourite or least favourite, since they are all necessary for the server to keep going. 

Favourite staff rank?
Refer to the question before.

Most helpful staff member? Sassiest?
Well, the sassiest one is easy: I'm definitely the sassiest!! In all seriousness, this is fairly similar to the favourite staff. There have been many current staff members, as well as staff in the past who have shown that they're capable of providing a great deal of help to non-staff, as well as fellow staff members. It's very difficult to choose just one staff member, since every staff member has their own method of helping others that cater to different types of people. I could say "oh this staff member and that staff member are the most helpful because they teach me the most", but are they really the most helpful? To me, they definitely could be, but to others, they could be the least helpful because that person learns differently than I do.  

Opinions?
I'll admit, even now, you just provided me with some questions that made me really think about the current staff, as well as the current state of  the staff team. A lot of the information I provided above was information I thought of in the past, but a lot of it was also material that I just thought of in the past little while that I was typing all of this out. After typing out all of those answers, I've actually gotten a different understanding of my true opinions of the staff team, as well as the community as a whole, which really intrigues me. Because of this, I'm definitely quite impressed in your questions' ability to cause this. Apart from that, we have actually talked in TeamSpeak quite a few times, and I can definitely say that I've very much enjoyed those times. You are a very amiable and approachable person, and I admire the fact that you're always looking to socialize and interact more with people, especially fellow staff. Even in Slack, I always see you giving suggestions and gaining feedback from fellow staff on your own performances, which is something that I'm very much impressed by. As a moderator, you've always had great performances, and I always know that you'll only improve more and more.

Now, I'd say the highlight is being able to build relations with fellow staff as well as players in the community, so that I can create lasting friendships that will go on for longer than a couple weeks like they used to. Although I do not moderate for the sole purpose of receiving appreciation back, it is always nice to know that the appreciation is there, and that what we do is actually having it's desired effect.

How does Avicus' staff team compare to other ones?
This is interesting, since it's actually quite difficult to compare staff teams. Staff teams cater to the gamemodes and overall structure of the server itself, hence ranks such as "Builder", "Map Developer", as of course, specific Moderation subteams if applicable. Since Avicus is a smaller server that does not need those subteams, it's hard to compare it to larger servers I've been staff on such as Mineplex, due to how different the two are. Nonetheless, I find that the state of Avicus' staff team currently is actually decent, considering it isn't too unorganized from what I've observed, and it isn't too large or too small. It doesn't have unnecessary subteams or ranks, and each rank/position we have currently serve their own purpose that all relate to keeping the server going. There are many servers that are very different from this, some of which I have been staff on. While some of those servers do in fact have great staff, most of them do have flaws of their own, much life the Avicus staff team does have flaws here and there that make it imperfect. Every staff team has flaws, but I'm easily able to tolerate Avicus' flaws. I can also easily tolerate the flaws within the other 2 staff teams I'm in, hence why I'm staff on 3 servers and haven't ditched the other 2 for Avicus.
