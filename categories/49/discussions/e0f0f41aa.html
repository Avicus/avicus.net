Hey, I am Eric. I am also known as my current and past usernames:oriminecrafter, or6663, EricV4, ElectraXdroid, RQB, lmao_, and MapDev.
I've been on Avicus for around 2 years, I'm a really active player, and I really enjoy playing it.
I really like building, im building for many servers, some for money, some for free.
Today is my birthday and I'm turning 13, though im pretty mature for my age, however I do not take minecraft really serious unless it's in a competitive or serious situation, or when taking responsibility, therefore I'm not really mature in minecraft.
I am a regular map maker on Avicus and made some maps that got removed or still are in the rotations, I took part in making the current Avicus Hub and made the tournament lobby. 
I also enjoy PvP, I usually spend my "PvP time" on Avicus and kohi.
I enjoyed Avicus and played it with many friends I earned, on teamspeak, in-game, and I can say it's one of the best communities i've ever been on, that's probably the reason that made me stay for such a long time.


That's pretty much about me, but if you wanna know more, ask!
