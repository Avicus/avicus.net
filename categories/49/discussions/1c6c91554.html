Hello fellow avicus lovers,
I want to make some clarifications about myself since I'm sure many players have opinions about me.

So...about 2 years ago, I made a post explaining myself and who I am. 
At that time, I felt I received a mixture of support and disapproval.  
I stopped playing avicus for awhile because I don't like to be judged.
But...in the end, I came back and have enjoyed many hours on this server.

I have made some new friends and I feel it's time to explain myself again
and let you decide if you want to continue to welcome me into the avicus community.

In a nutshell...

I am old.
I am much older than any other players on avicus. 

I am a mother.
I have 3 fabulous children ages 14, 12, and 10.  We are a very close family and live in California.  
All of my children are extremely active on either social media, youtube, and/or online video games. 
Sometimes I play avicus with them or on our mc realm. More recently, they have all moved onto other games and activities,
but I've stayed on avicus because of the community.  My children are all supportive of my youtube channel and may start streaming with me soon.

I am a teacher.  
I teach English to K-5th grade students.  I work mostly with new immigrants to the US. They speak little to no English.  
It is a very rewarding job, provides flexible hours, and summers off.

Why avicus?
I started playing avicus before McZone with the WallsMc or SkyWars ip.  My son found the ip on a list server and we started playing together. Avicus has fun maps and enjoyable players. Every time I play avicus, I am always laughing at my mistakes and/or what other players are saying in chat.   Most members of avicus are friendly.  I try to treat players in a way that I like to be treated...with respect.

Looking forward...
I would like to continue playing on avicus.  

I am part of a team HOME on avicus with 23 players.  I will continue to be a leader of that team, unless members would like me to step down. In the past, I have built 2 maps for Walls that were accepted and put into rotations (Camp Creeper, Camelot). 
Currently, I am working on a Walls map (The Station) and a SkyWars (No Name Yet) map and hope to release pictures/downloads soon. I may apply for Media, if the staff is open to it.

Again, my only intention for playing on avicus is to have fun and relax when I have free time at home.

Thanks for reading.  Ask Me Anything.

Hi PieZ - Any questions are welcome! I realize in advance that some people may disagree with my comments.

How do you feel your school or school board(Which ever tells you what you have to teach the kids) is doing for your kids? I feel that the school board and/or state legislators are out-of-touch with reality. They haven't spend enough time in actual classrooms to understand the challenges that teachers face and/or the various needs of a diverse group of students.  They also haven't asked the students what works best for them.

Is it good enough or can it improve in some format where you are from? Sure it can improve, but the people who make those decisions are listening to the wrong special interests (ie. testing companies) and implementing some changes that are not helping students. Not enough time is spent on actual instruction and follow-up to make sure students understand a skill or concept before they are tested on that subject matter.

What do you believe is the best way to teach children in a school setting? Well, having a qualified instructor is the first step. Besides understanding the content matter, they need to be able to engage students and check for understanding to make sure students understand what/why they are learning.  Also, class sizes must be smaller (ie. 20 students max). Having more electronic devices available on campus for student use is only valuable when the teacher has had proper training (which most have not-this is a work in progress).

Are you stern or do you believe you are very free with your classes? No, I'm not stern at all...maybe a little lax, but I always make it clear what student behaviors, expectations, and outcomes are necessary for them to learn. Students are in charge of their own learning.  My classes are usually small groups between 3-15 students, so we are able to focus more on content and understanding.

Do you believe that Minecraft and other games have a role in the future of schooling with certain kids? Sure, I've read many articles online regarding teachers using mc in their classrooms.  Anything that makes content more accessible for students should be utilized even if it's in a game format.
