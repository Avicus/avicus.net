Lets start with a generic question...
How'd you come up with your name?

_Stormz December 13, 2014 at 10:12 AM UTC

When I was younger, I used to love watching videos on YouTube. I remember clearly that I was watching videos of The World Ends With You, an NDS game. I saw that the uploader's name had a "z" in it, and it sounded pretty cool. I tried to find different alternatives and possibilities on what sounds nice. I finally found the word "storm" to be pretty cool, so I chose it. 

Now, having the name "Stormz" was too generic for me. I needed another word in my name. This part didn't really have much inspiration, but I was just sitting down in front of the computer for a few minutes until I imagined the word "invade" next to "Stormz". I found this really cool, so it stuck with me ever since. I don't plan on changing it.
