@cinammoncroc000:
Dream job would undecided but would involve either math or CS, so maybe a programmer or a mathematician.
My mouse is a plain old Logitech mouse.
1+1 depends on how you think: It's 2 in base 10, 10 in base 2, and 110 in base -2. Jozz's vice president would be Peachy <3
@smitdalt: My favorite genre of music depends on the day: it could be classical, dubstep, or happycore.
Cake. All the way. <3
How did I get my username? Oh boy, that's a question that I expected but I knew would be a long story. You better be prepared.
First things first, I've always loved dairy products such as milk and cheese, and I have no idea why.
Now, the story starts when I was randomly searching Kongregate for Multiplayer games. After a while, I found a game titled "Realm of the Mad God." I took a look at it, didn't think much of it, and then stopped.
Later, I picked up the game again, and got addicted. At that time, there was a professional guild known as "Mafia" that played this game. They later changed the guild to "Cream."
Fast forward a few months, and I started getting bored of the game. My birthday was coming up in a few months, and I had heard of Minecraft being the new "thing," so I decided to get that for my birthday. I decided to come up with a good name. I thought of Cream because of the professional guild and also because of my love of dairy products, but I found out Cream was taken.
So, I thought, "what's cream made out of again? Right, milk." So I tried CreamMilk. Didn't work. Finally, I went with MilkCream.
And that's the story of my name!
