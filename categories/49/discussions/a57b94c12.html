Why did I retire from Administrator? 
At the time, it felt like the best thing to do. Don't get me wrong I enjoyed managing and dealing with escalating situations... the community seemed they wanted a new administrator. I admit, I have been bias before. I have falsely banned players. I have made many mistakes that couldn't be mended. All in all, I stepped down from Admin due to making way too many mistakes, alongside the communities opinion/feedback. I mean, if the community base doesn't like the admin who is pretty much running the show... it's best for a fresh person to come in 8^)

Why did I retire from Mod?
I was being relied on too much for the first couple months, even though I had no power. It got to a point wher I set myself a goal to assist the new moderators to develop skills... to be more independent - if you will. Before retiring, there were quite a few moderators who really became leaders amongst the staff team. Since my 'goal' was furfilled, my purpose as a moderator depleted. Additionally,  I was lacking moderation (got lazy with reports etc).
