Emerald, Gold, and Diamond are the standard donator ranks

Hey hey and welcome to the Avicus forum community! Hopefully you like it here :)

Gold, Emerald and Diamond are all donator ranks which are given to users who donate X amount of money to help fund and maintain Avicus.

You can find the shop at: http://avicus.net/shop
As you may notice, there are two different types of payment options available. The "Subscription" option lets you donate in chunks. For example; Gold is $5/month, Emerald is $10/month and Diamond is $20/month. You can cancel the subscription whenever you feel like it! The second option is a "One Time" payment. You donate the full price, and your donator rank lasts forever!
