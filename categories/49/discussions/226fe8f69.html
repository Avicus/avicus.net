[AMA] Tazz by Tazz December 5, 2015 at 9:12 PM UTC

For those who don't know me, I'm an Official and MapDev on Avicus. I'm probably best known for Toggle Sneaking in the Semi Finals of the first tournament; https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VPZuXKhk0No

I joined about 2 years ago but didn't really become active up until earlier this year. I came from OCN and then went to PotPvP and now I'm at Avicus for some reason. 

I don't really play public here because it is so easy - as a MapDev I focus on Nebula and more specifically Tournaments. I became Official because I pretty much told Vo that I was interested, and Pelp had told me that I could become Official whenever I like, because of my unofficial tournaments with BmB. I became Jr Map Dev after being jealous of BmB getting Junior Map Dev, and it turns out he got it because I got it too lmfao.

I would say that I am quite good in melee but I'm a lot worse at archery. I only really offend for my team, normally on my own but now seeing as I am the leader of Bad Company, I tend not to tunnel so much and let Jive do the honours <3

I will answer all questions but I might skip some - all of my answers will be 100% honest, so do not get offended if you ask for an opinion.
