It's a great project, and I hope you guys succeed with it.

I'm a bit wary about adding my maps to it, simply because
A) it means I don't really have control anymore of where the maps go, and
B) I don't really see people using my maps in a context where they are really needed. I doubt any PGM revival servers will get enough players to play 32 vs 32 maps at their full count, so there's no point in me giving any large maps up. Mini maps are simpler to give, I guess, but all my minis are more complex than average, and I don't know if people would enjoy them.

In terms of the avicus map stack, I guess I don't really know where people would use those maps. Atlas isn't public, and no other plugin accepts avicus xml. It's still a good effort on your part, though.
