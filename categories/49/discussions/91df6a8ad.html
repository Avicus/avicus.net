Click here and then make an appeal (hitting the big green button that says "New Dicussion" in the format of: 
  • Your minecraft username(most recent name if changed)
  • The reason you were temp-banned/banned
  • Why you should be unbanned from the server.
Wait for an admin/sr mod to reply and you will see if you are appealed! Remember be as detailed as possible!
