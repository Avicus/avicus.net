Crazy_ August 29, 2016 at 11:08 PM UTC

no just in general, rules, commands, etc.
TS - https://avicus.net/ts
Gravatar profile pic - https://avicus.net/forums/discussions/d5488b78b
Dev Log (for updates and stuff) - https://avicus.net/forums/c029e4189
Community Slack - https://avicus.net/forums/discussions/a9121e12c
Rules - https://avicus.net/rules/en (Note that spawn killing and void logging/combat logging is illegal here, unlike OCN)

Most of the commands are similar to OCN. A few to note are:
  • /nh <playername> - displays past name history
  • /sv <server> - shorter version of /server
  • /staff - displays staff online
  • /list - displays players online, which is great for knowing who has ranks and stuff
  • /t - toggles team chat. Use it to go back into global chat as well
  • /settings - opens a menu full of settings to configure ingame, such as death msgs, or toggling trails.
  • /tracker - tracks your kills & deaths. By default it resets after a match ends, however you can configure it using /settings
Clicking on the orange number next to a forum thread will allow you to view the thread from the last read point, where as just clicking on the thread will go back to the start.
