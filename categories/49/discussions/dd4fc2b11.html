I remember my first single-player day, like it was yesterday. It was a summer of 2011, my yard friends always talked about some strange game, Minecraft. I didn't want to start playing, till they told me, that it made of blocks and you can do everything there. OMG, SANDBOX GAME. 
I went home and installed cracked 1.7.3 beta version, created world with normal settings. I spawned somewhere on the beach near the forest. It was a beautiful day and I messed around with cute animals and square trees. But it started getting darker.
Wtf, night?! It was super dark, I didn't see anything and somehow went back to the beach. White shadow flashed in the forest. Who was that?!!! Am I alone?! No. Strange green someone without hands (!) walked up to me. Who are you, little cute mutant? KABOOM! It dropped some sand, as I thought. Then white shadow came out from the forest and started shooting me! (Later I understood, that it was Skeleton and Creeper, being who blown up my beach. WHAT'S WRONG WITH YOU, GAME?!)
I was super scared, began run away. Everything tried to kill me.
