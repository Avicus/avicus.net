My journey {No not retiring, yet. Ha gotcha} by hasl August 18, 2015 at 10:08 AM UTC

It's been a very long ride yet I haven't been to all my stops, I hope I never reach my last.

It all started a long long time ago in magical forest filled with *WRONG STORY YOU NUTJOB*
Once upon a time? *NO WRONG STORY AGAIN*
On sunny day in Kylo's bedroom? *NO DUDE WTF EWW*
I was walking past an enormous Peach named Yas *SERIOUSLY, ARE YOU KIDDING ME >.<*

Way way back in the days *Mmm, go on* I played on a server called VoidCraft with SkyBeats and others and it was a multi-gamemode server with next to no staff, I lived my youthful days there (I'm not like 420 years old now, at least I think i'm not 0.0) and anyway things were quiet with a base of 100 players and the next day AvalancheNetwork moved next door and we merged together and that's when I met TheMasterCow (Para) and Zintenka on AvNk which had Equinox (DTM) and I was hooked instantly. Things weren't whoop-dee-doo forever and soon AvNk collapsed and so did VoidCraft as well. Stranded homeless I embarked on exploring the huge lands of Multiplayer and came across this settlement named Avicus.

I wasn't a donator or forums user or a teamspeak addict, just your average user playing Nebula... until February. I was inspired by Mango (SweetMango back then) who went from a donator to mod right in front of my eyes, and that is why I visited the staff apps building in central Avicus, sadly my first app was denied and I felt a bit put down (Don't worry Jozz I forgive you ;D) but I saw __SkyGirl__9 get the first interview on the interview session post and that made me determined to get my name on there so I sat on abandoned hills of Avicade writing and improving my second application and submitted in the Avicus post on the final day. When I got a response for Reviewing I was over the moon and soon I got an interview, now looking back it doesn't seem that big of a deal for me, but back then I was speechless, I couldn't make the interview sessions so Andy (Tel) kindly conducted my interview when I was quite ill and would sneeze every few sentences :D, a few days later of me drinking coffee all night getting xp for my first prestige, I got mail from PeachyYas at 5am saying I got promoted and got the job! I woke up my entire family with my horrific singing voice singing my victory song goes like 'Don't stop me now haven't such a good time, just give me a ri-ng *voice crack*' 

Being a late Jr Mod I was never mentored or monitored so I made countless errors and thought I was going to get the boot when my 3 weeks were up, but no... I got another letter from PeachyYas saying I got promoted and I had room on floor #3 in Mod Hotel. Life was fine there meeting my room mates and learning their names, my best friend was Sesspee back then, we would stay up all night and roast marshmallows in the staff lounges. *You're taking to long, skip to your next major change* argh fine... let me see, what would that be? Ahah: Senior Mod interview.

It was a rainy Saturday in the staff lounge playing truth or dare with around 5 other mods 3 including myself were to have our interview anytime with the mighty Solurak. I was to go first and my ass were the flowers in Michael's hair smelly, anyway the interview was obnoxious and awkward for me and when I'd finished I went into a public channel with MasterClock and others and said my interview went crap. A few ways later on a MANIC MONDAY Iron said in Slack (a breakthrough technology of Social Media, Ikr bloody amazing fam) that in 10 hours results would be out, I sat there looking at the list working out who the five would be, no way did I ever think I would be one of them, until 4 am the next morning, which I nearly fainted, sung my victory song again (this time with no voice crack, well there was but don't tell anyone c;) and fell asleep just as I got downstairs, so I woke up thinking it was dream until I realised I was in another Slack group, had a sexy af new tag and everyone was saying 'Congrats Blizzem' and a miracle had happened.

Many staff meetings and heated discussions later here I am, and I can't thank every single one of you who helped me along the way, from a nobody to a Senior Mod, I mean wow the support was amazing.
I'd like to take the time to thank some remarkable people, sorry if I didn't list you- everyone says that, but I mean it :)
