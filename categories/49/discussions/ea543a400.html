How did you become director?
Its really a long story. I met Funky back in like Beta 1.6 or something. After a long time, I finally become Mod on his server (one of the few active ones). I then followed him to MC Zone where he promoted me to admin. Ive stuck around since then and been a help, so Ive finally worked my way up to director. Thats a very shortened version of the story.
Was it hard or easy with lots of time?
Umm, I don't know what the question, haha.
How is being director different than being a normal player?
Its a lot of responsibility and thinking. We come up with ideas for the server all of the time, and we always have to be kind and helpful to all players. Its important that we keep our calm, and always look for something to work on. Its a completely different experience in my opinion because I play for fun like 50% of the time, but I play to think of improvements the other 50%.
What do you (dis)like about being a director?
Hmm, I really wouldn't be playing Minecraft if I wasn't so involved in a community like this. So basically I like it all!
How does it feel to be a director?
In my opinion it feels great to be a director. I like being looked up to and I like being able to help out in any way possible.
Do you even director?
Sadly, I have not learned how to director. Ive been working on it for a while, but haven't exactly nailed the art.
What type of relationship do you have with Ivy?
Umm, we are amigos I guess. I don't really know, haha.
Which do you like more, a sphere or a cube and why?
Spheres, balls are much more fun to play with than like stacking cubes. But I also like cubes because of the way they stack and work together.
What's your favorite color and why?
Green. I don't really know, Ive just liked the color forever.
If you had to creatively use an  undangerous object as a weapon what would it be and how would you hurt your victim?
I would obviously use a whale or walrus. I don't really know, but it'd be pretty awesome beating someone with a whale.
Would you rather never touch a computer again or someone of the opposite sex again?
Never touch my computer again. Hahahahaha, I am very into my girlfriend, so I don't think that would ever happen.
What's your favorite type of music?
Country by far. Let the hate begin!
What are your theories on the Universe? :o
My theory of the universe goes like this: Bang, everything is made. Bang: Live begins. A few more bangs and we have Earth!
Would you rather want to be a wizard or an animal of your choosing?
I would rather be a wizard who could turn into an animal of my choosing.
