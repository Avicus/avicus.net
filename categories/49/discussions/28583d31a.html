Race to Victory 2 Spotlight #2 - Patrikano by BoldAndBrash June 23, 2017 at 5:06 PM UTC

Hit me up on Discord (boldandbrash#4517) if you want to do one.

What is your IGN and team?
Patrikano and my team is called Jalapenos

How many tournaments have you participated in?
2, Battle of Eternity and Project Redemption

Any team in particular you'd like to face off against?
Not really, maybe Horizon since we never got the chance to play them

Any team you don't want to face off against?
No, we're up for facing any team. It's always more fun to play as many different teams as possible

How do you think you'll do this time around?
I think we will do well, i'm not fully sure what place since I do not know if it's groups or single/double elimination but we are hoping for top 5

Any teams you have a rivalry with?
No, but Celestial have been boasting about how they beat us last time we scrimmed so it'd be nice to play them in the tournament. Apollo are also a fun team to play since they never go down without a fight

What do you think is your team's best strength, and your best strength alone?
I think my team's best strength is that we have started to communicate very well recently and always go into the match expecting the best. I believe my strength is that I can get the team to listen to me during games which allows for good rotations

What do you think is your team's worst weakness, and your worst weakness alone?
Our weakness is that sometimes we mess around too much during a match and end up losing to teams we usually beat, though we definitely will try not to do that during this tournament. I think my weakness is that I sometimes make the wrong decision during a match and then don't know how to come back from it

What have you done to prepare?
We have looked over some maps that will possibly be in the map pool and have begun to scrim teams on these maps

Anything you'd like to say to your opponents?
 We are better than you think. Oh and Rombey hacks and armored is a potato
