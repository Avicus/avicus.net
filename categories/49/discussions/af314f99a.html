Hello everyone!

Well, here I am. I can't believe it was one year ago that Avicus came out! I thought I better make this thread as I've starting playing the odd game of Nebula again (and am really enjoying myself!) and I never really said goodbye. Thanks to all those that have said "Hello" and welcomed me back to the server, it's really nice to see some people from the old days.

Maybe this is my return? Who knows, only time will tell.

Nice to be back!
