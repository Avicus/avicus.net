I'm sure you know by now by joelous February 17, 2017 at 5:02 AM UTC

I've decided to exhaust all my services from Avicus. I've been here for over 4 years, I've experienced almost every staff system that has been in place from when Howsie was Director; and it certainly has been very interesting to see the revolution of ideals from a management perspective. 

Main reason for this sudden decision was the amount of work I am anticipated to complete for school - as I just entered my final year. The expectations were much higher than I thought it was, so I need to focus on that. Besides, I want to play enjoyable games (Overwatch + Elsword, not Minecraft since it's boring now) in my own free time without being obliged to fulfill a responsibility. 

I'm quitting Avicus, and Minecraft. I don't see myself logging into Minecraft much anymore, although I may pursue a development approach to the game if I'm interested. That being said, I want to thank a few people who have been most influential in my Avicus experience - and I'm not going to list everyone since I would be going on for days.

Howsie Without you, I wouldn't be the person I am today. I wouldn't have confidence in my leadership skills, and I would have not been able to cope with responsibilities such as administrating a complex network. Your guidance in the early days still influences my decision making; I often wonder what you would do in a situation. I know there are some people here who look down on you since you like to be (apparently) pessimistic on the forums, but I don't care. I'm happy that I was able to be around when you were in charge. Adding you on B.NET and hearing from you put a smile on my face again - back to the times of the Sistahood prime. Let's hope we remain great friends, play competitive together (carry me please) and eventually greeting each other in real life. 

izjust4fun I will never forget you lol. Genuinely nicest person I have ever met online, and probably in real life (quite sad when you think about it). Alongside Howsie, you were my role model here on Avicus for over 3 years. You won't see this, but I hope you and CC are still together. I still need to hear all about it!!

_Gill Tinychat parties were the best, and you knew how to make the mood in the community lift. Stay in touch on snap, keep sending my drunk pictures because they are certainty reminiscent of the days where you took interviews while being tipsy.

DoctaPop  NolanDalton  UnkelBen As moderator, you guys were absolute legends to me. Always there to talk about random shit, great friends where we had so many memories (such as when we were promoted to senior mod). Pop I remember when we played The Forest in skype calls... I miss those tbh. We still need to continue our saved adventure! Nolan, I'm sorry, but I'm going to have to remind you: "Well, um, so, you basically got mod". Hardest working mod/senior I've worked with, shame you left so early - but we need to catch up somehow... :P

Xuph Continue to love nightcore, and I won't forget the skype music sessions we had - it sucks we randomly stopped. I trust you wholeheartedly, and you know why. Thanks for being a great friend. 

keenanjt You inspired and motivated me to start developing. Thanks for helping me when I was stuck, and your efforts and dedication to Avicus is exemplary. Even when the odds are against the network, you've somehow managed to continue the project. Even after the life of this community has died out - I believe you will go on to be one of the top programmers. I'm looking forward to read your name in an awesome project... and I hope one day I can join you. (I'm pursuing computer science majoring in robotics and web development).

Yoav. What can I say. Best friend on Avicus by far - and I still have your good luck charm of a dragon, on my bedside table. When you're out of boarding school please come back to chat, so we can have a video chat once more (but please wear a top).

Thanks everyone, 
You can ask anything here; what really happened behind the scenes; some secrets I didn't tell anyone (within reason) etc... but I won't be treating this as an AMA. 

Noodle God & Rock Lord signing out.
