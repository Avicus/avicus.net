Project Redemption Spotlights #1 - Tomqss by BoldAndBrash November 24, 2016 at 11:11 PM UTC

Hey guys, with Project Redemption matches just around the corner, I thought I'd introduce you all to a new series. It's essentially a community spotlight, but for anyone's participating in the tournament. I'm going to be handpicking the participants for this, and our first interviewee is Tomqss!

What is your team name?
Team Allah (PowerPuff Boys in Tourney 2)

What have you done to prepare for Project Redemption?
We've played a couple of scrims along with finishing our tryouts to have a full 18-man roster.

Has your team played in past tournaments? How do you think this'll effect your experience?
We've placed 9th in TOC, and 1st in both RtV and BoE. I think having won two tournies will be benefitial for us.

What are your team's major weaknesses and strengths?
Our greatest strenghts are the individual skills and the team ability to work together due to the experience we have. Maybe our biggest weakness can be memeing too much.

What do you think of the teams you are set to face?
I think Labradoodles along with Rocket are two good teams and I think the winner of that match will get into invitationals. However, also Cennet can upset any of both.

Is there anything you'd like to say to your opponents?
Good luck!

Any predictions for Project Redemption? Upset victories? Controversies?
I think Team Rocket will do better than what most people think and that Sagrevyum will also. About the controversies, who knows, it's hard to predict those.

Anything else you'd like to mention?
Thank you for giving me the possibility of starting this.
