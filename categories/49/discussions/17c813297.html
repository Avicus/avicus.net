Project Redemption Spotlight #3 - PokerFace by BoldAndBrash November 26, 2016 at 10:11 PM UTC

Hey guys, back again with another there PR Spotlight. Today's interviewee is PokerFace!

What is your team name?
My team name is Cacalmarin. We are one of the first Avicus teams to be created and have been around for several years.

What have you done to prepare for Project Redemption?
To prepare for Project Redemption, we had one scrimmage against Team Allah and are hoping to squeeze in one more with another team before the weekend.

Has your team played in past tournaments? How do you think this'll effect your experience?
We have participated in every Avicus tournament to this day and a couple of unofficial tournaments as well. I think this experience will help with our communication and strategy a lot.   Sometimes when teams pick up new members, it may be hard to recognize who is who. We have a general idea of certain team's tendencies and we are hoping that this will help us to win.

What are your team's major weaknesses and strengths?
Major weaknesses we have are coordination and strategy. Often times we cannot hold a choke-point and we often get overwhelmed because of it. Our communication needs some improvements as well, sometimes enemies aren't called until they are at the objective. I would say our strengths are individual skill. We have quite a few talented players and hopefully we can utilize these skills in our matches.

What do you think of the teams you are set to face?
I think the main teams that stand in our way are Apollo, Cronus, and Sagrevyum. Apollo has had the same core roster for a very long time and they are very well coordinated as a team. Their recent roster shuffle is something important to note as well. DaFrozenBlaze  has always been an outstanding support player. I remember when he used to play for Cacalmarin,he was the guy that kept the game competitive, even against top-tier teams such as Allah. Sagrevyum has always had amazing individual skills so I think they have a good chance making it to the invitationals. Cronus has definitely been growing stronger and I'd like to see what kind of battle they can put up against us.

Is there anything you'd like to say to your opponents?
I wish all opponents good luck and i hope to see a couple of upset matches!

Any predictions for Project Redemption? Upset victories? Controversies?
For Group A, I think Apollo and Sagrevyum will be heading on to the Invitationals, but Cacalmarin and Cronus have a chance to upset For Group B, I think it will be Team Allah and Team Cennet who will head to the Invitationals. There are a few new teams in Group B that I am unfamiliar with, but there is always the chance that they could upset.

Anything else you'd like to mention?
We hope to make I think at least to the invitationals.
