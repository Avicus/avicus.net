Race to Victory 2 Spotlight #4 - Spooks by BoldAndBrash July 13, 2017 at 9:07 PM UTC

What is your IGN and team? Spooks_9900 and I'm in Apollo

How many tournaments have you participated in? All of them, starting with Pensioners in T1 and Apollo T2-T6

Any team in particular you'd like to face off against? Not in particular, probably Horizon

Any team you don't want to face off against? ofc allah and puredogs, our matchups in previous tourneys have been really unlucky against them

How do you think you'll do this time around? Since CTW is our main gamemode probably fairly well. Everyone underrates us too because of what happened last tourney.

Any teams you have a rivalry with? Celestial because they beat us a couple of times whilst scrimming and seem quite cocky now; and horizon for obvious reasons lul

What do you think is your team's best strength, and your best strength alone? Probably how close we are as a team. We're more a group of friends and so can take criticism from each other well while knowing each other's playstyles

What do you think is your team's worst weakness, and your worst weakness alone? Working together such as ground pushing or team fighting even though we all have great individual skill

What have you done to prepare? Well the team has pretty much been completely re-designed following the last tourney so we've been trialing and adding new players as well as scrimming together fairly regularly. 

Anything you'd like to say to your opponents? ready for 3rd again
