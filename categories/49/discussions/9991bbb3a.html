Project Redemption Spotlight #2 - Legoche by BoldAndBrash November 26, 2016 at 12:11 AM UTC

Hey guys, back again with another PR Spotlight. Today, our interviewee is Legoche!

What is your team name?
Apollo

What have you done to prepare for Project Redemption?
Apollo haven't scrimmed recently since competitive avicus kinda died, but we've stratted for maps quite a bit and have some tricks up our sleeves :^)

Has your team played in past tournaments? How do you think this'll effect your experience?
Yeah, Apollo has played every tourney, we came 2nd in TOC, 3rd in RTV, 3rd in the league and 2nd in BOE. I feel this tourney will probably be just like last tourney, nothing special will really happen...

What are your team's major weaknesses and strengths?
Apollo's strengths are our strats and some of our players chemistry with each other. Our weakness is definitely communication, and this is likely because of lack of scrimming/ some players needing to improve.

What do you think of the teams you are set to face?
If I'm gonna be honest I don't feel there is a whole lot of competition in this tourney, Avicus teams are so far apart in skill, and nobody scrims so it's gonna come down to who has the better players, which is sad to think.

Is there anything you'd like to say to your opponents?
I feel that our opponents could all stand a chance beating each other however I don't think we will lose in the early stages.

Anything else you'd like to mention?
Cronus and Cacal better be ready for a re-enactment of last tourney.
