Seriousness: How did you come across Avicus? p.s we should talk more :)
Me and my irl friend (RyanPearson22) were looking for walls servers back in march 2013 we stumbled across early MCzone and loved the walls on it. Then we played nexus together, i met Ronloyd and Panda also shoutout to Zeus, Zero and 1Theodore1 who i saw regularly, made my first friends actually learnt to pvp on 1.6 nexus and eventually joined the forums.Through the forums i played in the MCzone tourney, got knocked out by merlinmaster and emmy's team then I left for a while when Avicus first started because they had removed nexus but then re-joined because i decided nebula was fun and i wanted to be on the non-donor leaderboards  (made it to 2nd at 1 point damn you fazer).around this point i also started Bad Company after a 5 month run, we grew fast and with the early roster i had some of the best times of my MC life but unfortunately summer 2014 we disbanded. I got banned for 5 months and in these 5 months i became the player i am now by doing a lot of Kohi and OCN, without my ban i would be no-where near as good as i am now and not have made many of my friends today so i am almost thankful for it. But hearing AV might have a new tourney (little did i know it would take another 5 months XD ) i appealed and was unbanned at this point i firstly joined Apex and made some more new friends namely the D00z. Apex disbanded so i created spin off team of BC called Blurred Dreams which invlolved a number of the early BC players and a few apex members, but it never really worked out I had a rocky road on AV for a while around here and was banned and unbanned a number of times with my infractions growing like India's population. Luckily Notal approached me and said "look i miss the old BC lets restart it again but properly, like the old one. So we did. That was the birth of today's BC a team most of you know quite well. It consists of about half old BC members and half new legends that have now become some of my greatest friends. This team became very good we competed in the tourney and got DQ'ed i almost disbanded the team and quit MC right there if i'm honest i was so pissed. But i realised we can still have fun with all the new AV teams growing and actually becoming more competitive i was happy to stay and keep this great team. 

I know you didn't ask for my AV history but ya know thought i'd give it ya anyway.
Also talking with you more would be great you should hit me up on skype (b.banno321)
