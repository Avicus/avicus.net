Hello everyone!

So let's start off I've been playing for about a year. My ex-friend showed me this server and I really liked it. We played for 4 months-ish. Then one day, he has full diamond armor and sword and Quintus and gets knocked off. He gets mad and leaves the server, and then I accuse him of "rage quitting". So that ended that.

I griefed his pyramid on a creative server. And then a few weeks later, I dump a bunch of books on his plot. And then he comes on. and he starts chasing a spamming me. Not good. 

So ever the past few months here, I've had some good experiences, some bad. The day before Avicore come out, I purchased Gold. I purchased Emerald shortly after. And after being inactive for a while, I decided to come back.
