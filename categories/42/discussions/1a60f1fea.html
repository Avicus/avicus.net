We highly encourage advertising the server on youtube, mostly for fun though. Many people have been asking what are the requisites for such ranks.
VIP: Around 5,000 subscribers- this is the one that people should aim for. VIPs receive emerald donor rank things- 2.0 xp multiplier. An example is Peckett- he will be making videos soon. 
Youtubers: Around 50,000 subscribers. This is for major youtubers that join the server and make videos for us. These people receive all of the kits possible. An example is Woofless.

These numbers are subject to change in the future. Also, they are rough estimates. Certain exceptions do exist.
