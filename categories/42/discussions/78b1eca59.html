New game mode by Humblares April 26, 2016 at 12:04 AM UTC

  • Objective: The objective is to break or destroy (with TNT) a core that has lava inside of it. 
  • Once the lava falls under the core a certain amount the other team wins.
only one Core
  • This game mode will include TNT for cannons or for destroying the other teams side for more leeway in the match. 
  • Timer? No there will be no timer the time will run out when the other team destroys the core in any shape or form. 
  • Large or Small map? Large maps will manly include this game mode better smoother play in the game , but some small maps can be included or sprinkled in to the mix. 
  • Why I think this will benefit this community:   This will benefit the community because I feel like the main game mode people play is destroy the monument and this would be a better and equally loved game mode to play and would be a nice change of pace.
