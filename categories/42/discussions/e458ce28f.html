Ok, if you want to make a good, decent-quality video, please avoid Windows Movie Maker at all costs. Yes, I know I recommended it to you yesterday, but now I remembered the horrible video quality I had on it a few years ago. Sure, it is easy to use, but not even considered an average video editor.

I would recommend using Sony Vegas or Premier Pro, either the legit or pirated version works fine. In my opinion, both are almost as user-friendly as WMM, so no need to worry about being too "nooby" for it :P If you need help, you can always search up videos of guides and stuff; those help a lot.
