MTRL Release v1.0 by Tazz April 21, 2016 at 8:04 PM UTC

Hello

As some of you may know, I used to be a Developer on Kohi and Avalance. I haven't developed in a while, and I started getting back into developing a while ago. My good friend Mr BmB's forge didn't work, and he needed a way to have Sprint and OptiFine. That is what I have created.

This is basically a client, however the only 'mods' are FullBright and Sprint. You toggle each one with 'B' for FullBright, and 'LCONTROL' for Sprint. These are unfortunately not configurable, however I am working on making Sprint be toggled by your Sprint button in your controls.

I am looking to update this with other mods. If you have any suggestions, please post them below. If you like I suppose I could make it OpenSource? GUI for toggled mods?

Proof of ownership: http://prntscr.com/av6h9o

To install just put this into your versions.

Download: http://www.mediafire.com/download/8gqeqrnl34og8cg/MTRL.zip

Thank You
Tazz
(p.s. and yes, this is allowed on Avicus as of 21/04/2016)
