Avicus Strat Roulette! by Speeded April 6, 2016 at 8:04 AM UTC

Hey guy toby here and im back with a new idea to spice some things while playing pub matches!

So what is Avicus Strat Roulette? Basically its kinda like based off of ChaBoyyHD's CS:GO Strat Roulette Video
Video Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wuuYDQmpNLM

So basically every 1st day of each month, players can try out these strats which can be listed down in the comment section below posts like this. (this can be done alone or with a friend on skype/teamspeak/ingame chat)

Examples:
(Position - Strat - Description)
Rusher - N.I.P. Crabwalk - Crouch/Shift rush to the monument or wool room all the way from your team's spawn

Midfield - Hit me baby - Everytime you get hit by a bow shot, type in chat "Hit me baby one more time"

Support/Bridge - Assassins Creed - You have to bridge/staircase, parkour and pvp while in F5

Defender - Catch of the day (1.9) - Craft a fishing rod and you reel/pull in any rushing enemy into your defense and kill them for golden apples, keep repeating this until you reach atleast 32 golden apples.

Comment down below any strat you can come up with (mostly for the fun and troll of it xD)

GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN!
