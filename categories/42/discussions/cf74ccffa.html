Lieutenantpants June 3, 2014 at 11:06 PM UTC

MAC Device

Ok. Open up a finder window. Go up to the "Go" dropdown menu in the upper left-hand corner. When this is open, press the Option (alt) key. A hidden option called "Library" will appear. Click on that. Open the "Application Support" folder. Open the "minecraft" folder. Open the "screenshots" folder. The most recent screenshot will be at the bottom of this folder.
