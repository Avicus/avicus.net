Hi, I'm starting this discussion on nanofictions. I am starting an Avicus Collection, and would like to you guys to enter.


A nanofiction is a piece of writing that is a very small story (AKA with limited words).

For your entry, you must follow these rules:

1. The nanofiction must be EXACTLY 60 WORDS
2. There must be a zingy, funny, ironic plot twist at the end.
3. It must be appropriate, only enter if you take it seriously.
