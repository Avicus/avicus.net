What is the Avicus Podcast?
The Avicus Podcast is a show with your host  shadowolfyt, SnowSX3 and 1 other guest appearance. The Podcast will contain at least 1 hour of news, fun, banter & memes.

It will be released on the Community Channel, which you can also conveniently find in this playlist. You can also get it on SoundCloud as well. Lastly, iTunes on the Podcast store is the last option too!
