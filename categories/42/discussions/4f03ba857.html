The Excalibur Saga by Zaayk November 3, 2014 at 12:11 AM UTC

The Excalibur Saga is back! As some of you know, it was the first fan-fiction Avicus ever had!

Anyway, here's a short summary of what you'll be reading:

There are two regions, Blue and Red, fighting for a mythical and all mighty emerald sword named "Excalibur". The only way to get the sword, because it is frozen in place in the middle of the "Field of Wood" (the field that separates the two regions) is to destroy the other side's energy source, a heavily guarded and highly powerful piece of obsidian. It's an action/comedy story that follows me and a bunch of old/current/new Avicus players! It is loosely based of Nebula DTM.

 Anyway, why am I keeping you waiting? Go ahead and start reading. I'm going to release a couple each week. Oh, and if you like the story, please tell your friends about it, I'm trying to get more attention towards it so more people can enjoy it. :D
