ImRaging May 22, 2016 at 2:05 PM UTC

To remove the 'GOTTEM' bow ding sound, go to the pack>assets>minecraft>sounds>random and then delete successful_hit.ogg

If you wanna enable bear diamond armour ( :^) ) then go to the pack>assets>minecraft>textures>models>armour and then there's a folder called 'Bear Diamond Armour'. Move the current diamond armour to your desktop or something and then put the files inside of 'Bear Diamond Armour' into the armour folder where the rest of the armour is.
