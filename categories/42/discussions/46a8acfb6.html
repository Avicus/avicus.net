goldenwalrus909 July 4, 2014 at 12:07 AM UTC

Nuu, unless you have a good server, just get the mod. Just scroll down the page until you see this, and then download the highlighted thing (No viruses, I scanned it). Then, you have to get Forge 1.6.4 (get latest installer). Adfly will come up here; you have to wait 5 seconds and click in the top-right hand corner where it says "SKIP AD". Then, install Forge by activating it and saying "OK". Then, go into your Run program and type in %appdata%. Once you do that, click on .minecraft and create a folder in there named "mods" (no quotation marks or capital letters). Then, drag the SPC mod from earlier in that folder you just named called "mods". Finally, go into your minecraft launcher, click "Edit Profile", click "Use Version:", then scroll to the bottom of all the releases until you see the profile with forge and things in it (I don't know the exact name because I uninstalled it, but you'll know where it is-- it stands out). I know this seems really confusing and over-whelming, but I'm sure you'll get it :3.  Good luck!
