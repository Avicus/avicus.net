[SUG] Co-Leader or Head-Leader by Myv June 5, 2016 at 7:06 PM UTC

Hello, BeanFive or Myv here.

Now, as a leader of a team, you can promote other people to be leader, which is all good. But, it could get out of hand- someone may be a bell-end and kick the other leaders, meaning they have successfully made a full takeover of the team.

As much as no-one wants this to happen, it is a possibility, because some noob may want to try to be funny and sabotage a team before a tourney.


The idea of a Head-Leader is that they cannot be kicked, because they were the founder of the team - they can only leave of their own accord and no one can force them to leave. They can still kick or demote leaders and kick members, but the normal Leaders cannot kick him/her.

On the other hand, you could also have Co-Leaders, which can kick Members and other Co-Leaders but not the Leader, because this is unfair.

Opinions on this? It would be really helpful imo.

Myv
