Resource pack: Green Pack by Sevoo March 14, 2016 at 1:03 AM UTC

The pack was based off of Danteh's green New Year's pack, but I decided to redo almost everything. The only things that stayed were the hearts and food bar, gapple, the buckets/lava/water and the fishing rod. The rest is either edited, taken from another pack or completely custom.

NOTE: Audio is very, very low. I'll be fixing that soon.
