It is definitely possible to no-shift staircase if you s tap or d and f tap, but bleeple/jitter bridging (just jumping and holding s while getting tons of cps) is not possible to do consistently without autoclicking. That being said, you can do it with like 12 cps and just get lucky every once in a while but you cannot do it for a long time consistently without autoclicking.

What I mean is if a hypothetical person, Jerry says he can jitter bridge consistently. He tries to do it 5 times. First time he goes 34 blocks, second time he goes 45 blocks. Third time 37 blocks. Forth time 28 blocks. Fifth time he goes 39 blocks. Jerry is probably autoclicking.

The other hypothetical person is Tom. Tom attempts to jitter bridge 5 times. One time he goes 12 blocks horizontally. The next time he goes 15, the next time he goes 31, the next time he only goes 9, and the last time he goes 14. This is possible without autoclicking.
