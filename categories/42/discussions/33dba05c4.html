Textures for Mapmakers by ViceTechnicolour July 17, 2017 at 12:07 AM UTC

Since resource packs are a thing in XML, and you can add custom textures to what you wish, I'm presenting non-copyright 16x16 textures that you can throw in to your map. All you need to do is replace a block that you want to use the texture with for your map, and then link it so that the XML recognizes it and converts players to see the textures you wish!

Blocks
 Splintered Birch Wood - add it to flooring along with normal planks to add a broken effect to your map
 Splintered Jungle Wood - add it to flooring along with normal planks to add a broken effect to your map
 Amethyst - want something special to mine to get a reward? add this in place of emeralds
 Aqua Wool - there's an aqua color on the scoreboard but no wool to go with. use an aqua wool as an objective!
 Tiled Brick - want your bricks to be more compacted together or mix up your brick builds?
 Cobble Brick - ever wanted something to fit together with your gray builds that's a variant of stone bricks?
 Dried Sponge - you can have a sponge and wet sponge, but what if you want to simulate a drought?

Specialty
 - Adds an X to an obsidian block to make it stand out (will make different colored ones like blue/yellow/green/etc.)
