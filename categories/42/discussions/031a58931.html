Hey, I have decided to host a KBWars tournament: "Knocked Out!"

To register: http://goo.gl/forms/3jeQ1Y1rSo
Registration closes in a week, in 26.4.2016.
The challonge will be shown up here once registration closes: http://challonge.com/KBWars.
If you register please be sure you can make the times below.
Challonge will be done by the number of players we will have registered.

Seedings will be done randomally.

The matches will be played in all 4 scrim servers which will be reserved by some of the teams that will take part. 

The matches will be played in the Bo3 method, and the player that loses is eliminated. // Single elimination.

The matches will take part in 8.5.2016, 6 PM GMT and the tournament will approximately be going around 2 hours. 

To clear up confusion, you play solo and it's not a team tournament.

If you sign up and then you notice you cannot make the time and the date please notify me with a subtitute that you find to play for you.

Prize is being discussed, but it's not the usual prize you're getting for events like this. :) wink wink
Rules
- All avicus rules apply
- Being disrespectful or innapropriate will get you disqualifed from the tournament.
- Hacking or using any illegal modification will get you disqualified and banned.
