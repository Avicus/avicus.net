BANM Competition! by Tynvale July 11, 2015 at 6:07 AM UTC

Once again, this is another thread to do with my channel. But this one is a little different...

On my channel I have a series called 'Building a Nebula Map' and recently I had a little issue with it. The world save doesn't work anymore so I had to start again. While I was trying to replicate what it looked like at the end at Episode 2, I noticed that it wasn't unique or creative. So I had an idea, I am going to be holding a competition for anyone to participate. It is going to be a competition where you come up with creative and unique ideas/blueprint for a map. Some of the map devs and I will be judging this competition and judge based on creativity, gameplay, etc.

You don't need to make a whole map, all you need to do is submit your ideas through pictures or even through video. I have a prize for the winner and if you really want you can work in a group but the prize will be split between you. The winner/s will also get there ideas used in the series and will be credited in the final build of the map.

I would really appreciate anyone who participates and the competition will end in around 2 weeks, so I can go on with the series!

So there you go, there are no limitations, so think of anything you want and feel free to have multiple entries/ideas.
