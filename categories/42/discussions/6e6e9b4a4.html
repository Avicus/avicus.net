The Printer   I looked at the clock wearily on the top of the wall. I was still typing up my report for history class, and didn’t realize that it was already 2:00 AM. I rubbed my face and tried my best to stay awake.    Much later, I had finally pressed the last key which meant I finished my report. A small moment of rejoice had blossomed in my tired body. I packed up my books and printed the history paper.   For some reason the printer wasn’t working though. I saw the wire stuck in the small socket. I checked if the switch was on, which it was, hence it should be working. I shook the printer and started hitting it. “Why won’t it work, I need some sleep” I screamed in my head. It felt like the cat caught my tongue, silence was stopping my mouth from actually speaking at all. I was just too tired.   I quickly jumped up on my bed and buried my face into the pillow, trying to forget about the paper. I slept for about an hour, until I heard a disturbing glitch noise coming from the printer.   I awoke shocked and fixated that the printer was making sound. I walked over to the printer and saw that papers were printing. There was one continuous message that kept printing out. “Check your window” it said. Even though I was really sleepy, that specific message made me feel like I was being watched. I quickly walked to my window to see a tall distorted brute standing at nine feet tall, just staring at me from a certain distance.   I quickly took out my phone and called 9-1-1. They eventually came and looked outside of the window to see if the creature was there. But as usual, the creature disappeared, when the police came.   So as always, they didn’t believe me and told me to get some rest. Now I know what you might be thinking, why didn’t I show them the papers?   If they didn’t believe me when I told them about the creature, they would never believe me about the message on the paper, they would immediately mark me as crazy.           As time passed I tried to clear my head from this madness. That’s when I remembered about my history paper. So I went to my computer and re-printed the report, and this time it actually worked. I tromped to the printer and was about to take the report, until I received a sudden phone call. It was from my neighbor, he had said that someone with a withstanding height is opening your door.  I hung up the phone, and tried to tell myself this was just a dream.         At that exact moment, I heard my front door creak open, and also heard the printer make that infuriating glitch noise. I saw that another paper printed right after my history report.           The paper showed a picture of a mutilated and slaughtered body lying on what appeared to be my bed. Under the abhorrent picture, there was a message. It said in a very clear font, “This is what you will look like when it is done with you”. Again, at that exact moment, I heard footsteps coming up the stairs, headed for my room
