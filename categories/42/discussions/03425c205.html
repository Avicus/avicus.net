Here you go, my guide to Pot PvP

  • When you run into somebody, don't just stop there, keep sprinting around them, and even through them, to throw their aim off.
  • 90 FOV would probably be the best, I don't care what people say about aim, it works just fine for me.
  • Use a nice PvP edited faithful edit, I use Eum3's edit.
  • Be as aggressive as possible, keep combo'ing until you reach about 2 hearts, quickly turn away, throw 2 pots, turn around, keep combo'ing
  • Keep practicing, you will only improve.
