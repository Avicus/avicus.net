For all new Avicus Players! by RightSide January 12, 2015 at 8:01 AM UTC

Because lots of new people are joining Avcius and not many of them knowing many in-game commands I decided to make this post to help out !

/join [You join the game]
/join blue [If you have a rank]
/rot or /rotation [Shows rotations in that game]
/leave [You leave the team not the game]
/report [PlayerName] [ReasonYouReportedThem]
/server [Do /server [ServerName]]
/hub [Takes you to the hub]
/stats [Shows in-game stats]
/m or /match [Shows how many kills or deaths for however long you have been connected to that server]


This is a few commands to help new people ! Hope this Helped .
Element.
