Happy New Year everyone! <3

My New Year's resolutions are to connect with this community more, keep my grades up in school, and just overall enjoy life and take the good out of everything, and everyone instead of the bad. :)

Since we're here, and it's been a rough year for Avicus, with many retirements, demotions, and bans. Everything I see on any social media site I go on it talking about how terrible 2016 was, and although 2016 was a pretty rough year there's a lot of good to take from it as well. Throughout the year we've updated to Atlas, which was really bad at first, and there are still some things that need to be fixed, overall the Atlas update was a huge step up for Avicus.

I've played on a lot of servers, and I've never seen one with such an amazing community. We have our problems, the people that don't like each other for whatever reason, but that's what holds us together. Without the community being as strong and amazing as it is on Avicus I would have left a long time ago. You guys don't realize it, but whether you are active, or inactive, or only talk to a few people, you are part of this community. 

My favorite thing about Avicus is the fact that even when we have 40-50 players on a good day, we all stick together and we do complain about a lot, but we still help in any way we can to keep Avicus alive, and improve it. I've seen many suggestions that are very good get better because of the communities ideas and constructive criticism, Let's keep that up. Help improve Avicus instead of complaining about everything we see wrong. Find a way to fix it, give ideas, be open to new ideas, and even if your suggestion seems stupid, suggest it, the community can help you improve it.
