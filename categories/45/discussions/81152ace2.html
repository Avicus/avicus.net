SO tomorrow is the release of this years GCSE results. In the UK, when you're 16 you take these exams and they pretty much set you up for your whole life. And tomorrow is when I, among many others, will find out my results... 

If you're getting your results tomorrow, good luck, and be sure to post yours on here, because I'm nosey.. 

And the grade boundaries are out, so check them out on the exam board websites.

(For anyone outside of the UK, the marking goes a little like this:

A* - around 80-90 (%)
A - 60-70
B - 50-60
C - 40-50 (pass - if you get lower than a C in English or Maths, you have to redo it)
D ...
E ...
F ...
G...
And so, but im not sure how many letters it goes to, worst I've heard of is a U, which is literally no marks.)
