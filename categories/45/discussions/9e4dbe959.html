Screw cancer, some of us want to buy a new phone, a new car, a new computer and whatever it maybe. But many animals and people fall under cancer, all they want is to win the battle. My leopard gecko happened to be one of the animals, and he did not win. All I'm saying is that us, people should be aware of such of these things. I could not help, he was still a baby, his anti-bodies were too weak to fight back. Even my snake is depressed, every sunday, I used to bring them out together and play with each other. Now my snake is confused and wonders, where my leopard gecko is.


RIP.

Andrew Jr.



Let's say you have toast for breakfast, but you don't want toast, well, you should be happy at least you get food, look at all the homeless, do you think they have food?
When you feel as if you're in pain, just because you didn't get the latest soccer boots, imagine all the people fighting cancer, they're in terrible pain, and they only want one thing. To win. I hope this makes you aware that, when you really want a video game, and you can't get it, think about all the people battling cancer.


I watched my friend die, with my very own eyes, not being able to help, helpless. I stayed up, not sleeping at all, just watching the tiny gecko arch its back in pain every few minutes. After a couple minutes, into the morning, 8:32 AM, Andrew silvano Jr. had exited my world.

Together, we can stop cancer.


Thank you.
