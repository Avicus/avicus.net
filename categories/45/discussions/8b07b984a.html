As my two lovely junior moderators stated above, you will have to be accepted through the application process as they have stated above.

But have no fear, you can still be staff here! There are 2 other ways of becoming a staff member, they may be very rare but if you wow us you may have a chance.

Referrals:

The referral process is where a staff member can nominate you to all other staff members. All staff members will then vote deciding your acceptance, if positive - you will be given the junior mod rank and granted a trial. If it is declined it will be a secret and only staff members at that time will know!

Hand Picked:

Even though this method is very rare, it is used sometimes. This process happens when only admins vote on a certain player. I personally have never seen this method used but legend has it, it has been done before :}.
