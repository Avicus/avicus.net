If I'm ever on incognito, I get it as well.

If you're on a normal browser (without being on incognito) and you're still getting this, you should get an AdBlock extension.

The one I use is called "AdBlock Plus" and it removes all of those ads.
  • Go on "Settings" (in your browser)
  • Click on "Extensions"
  • Click "Get more extensions"
  • Type "AdBlock Plus"
  • Download it
  • Restart your browser
