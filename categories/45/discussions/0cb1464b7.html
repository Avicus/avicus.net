Notices

- Make sure pictures contain NO nudity, pornography, sexual references, bestiality, religious, religious mockery, terrorism, hardcore gore, animal abuse and child abuse.
Failing to comply will result in the picture(s) being removed immediately and reported where staff may give a punishment.

- When uploading multiple pictures in the event that your desktop changes background whenever time, you may upload them all, but make sure they're either small and put together, or put in an Imgur album and link it, or link all the pictures without posting the picture here, but with the link to the picture
Failing to do so will result in the pictures being removed.

- You do not need to post your picture, if you don't want to, then don't.
