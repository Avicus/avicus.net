And before you say that leaving posts are not allowed, go away. I think I deserve this. 
Well what a roller coaster ride it has been! From Ingi showing me the old days of MCZone when we wanted a good server to play walls on.. To finding Nexus and Nebula and then playing them continuously.. From joining the forums just before the Avicus update to see what the fuss was all about.. To applying for moderator.. Doing that interview which was some scary ass shit and to then becoming a moderator on this unbelievably awesome server.. How far Avicus has drawn me along it’s pretty staggering.
If you hadn’t figured, yes, this will be my last post. I need to go. I’m moving into my last year of GCSE’s at school and it’s pretty important. I now have three jobs to work my way around, and now the summer’s up I won’t have time for it all. I may come on from time to time with alts.. But that’ll be secret. I cannot thank this community enough; how much I’ve changed. This staff team too is the best I’ve seen. The work rate is phenomenal. No one lays back. No one is lazy. Everyone co-operates together.
