I've been busy playing this game ever since I turned inactive here so I thought of sharing it with you guys. And here's a vid showcasing the game: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MTnVu42fTZs

You can either play on your browser on or Steam though I'd suggest to play on Steam since gameplay is usually affected by using the browser.
