Goodbye everyone by Tarheelkiwi June 14, 2015 at 2:06 AM UTC

Man I never thought I would be writing one of these.  But, here we are.  It's time for me to move on.  I have lost interest in Minecraft and have started taking my tennis more seriously.  Minecraft has become a major obstacle and has been in the way of my schooling and tennis.  All I thought about was getting done with my school work and my tennis to get on the computer faster and that has to stop. 

I would like to thank everyone I have come to know during my Minecraft days.  There are so many and this leaving post would be way too long.  Without you guys, I wouldn't have stayed as long as I have.  I will never forget you guys!

I would also like to thank the Sr Staff team for allowing me to serve as a Junior Moderator for this server.  You guys gave me an experience like no other.  Keep doing what you have been doing.  Also would like to thank the entire staff team in general.  You guys are amazing and don't let anyone tell you differently.

I am handing over my account to my siblings so if you see them on this server, it's not me.  Who knows what they will do ( goodby KD D: ).  Well this is it.  Goodbye everyone!


I have learned how to fly!
