Hey guys! Here I introduce you with a custom made mod made by, the Robot Pet Mod for Minecraft 1.8.x! This mod adds in robot that you can tame and have as a pet. It also adds some more items to the game. So who's ready to get minecraft a little more techy?
Features:
- Robot Pets (Male and Female robots)
- Copper ingots, tools, and block
