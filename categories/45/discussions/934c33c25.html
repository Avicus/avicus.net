So some suggestions could be:

-Find a new money system. Credits honestly suck. They are pointless to try to get, and even if you were trying to get them, what are you going to buy? A badge that isn't all that amazing, a morph that you can only use in the hub, where no one is for more then 2 minutes... A sound effect, that doesn't really do anything for you, and costs a lot of Credits. None of these are even close to spending 7 hours grinding to try to get. Find something that's worth it.

-A leveling system that can be infinite.
