Community Update. by Jake41901 March 9, 2015 at 11:03 AM UTC

Hey everyone.

Recently I've been receiving a little bit of negative feedback, regarding myself, and the Event Committee not "doing anything". Honestly, I can see why it would appear that way. But I just want to clear a few things up.

I made a post about an official UHC. That, is still going to happen. Along with that, more events are being planned between myself and the EC. However, I decided to hold off on hosting any more Community Events until the Nise Update is released. I don't remember informing any of you about this, so it is entirely my fault for some of you to assume that we're not doing anything.

In a nutshell, there is a lot of planning going on behind the scenes. However, I won't be hosting any more Community Events until the Update is officially released. So entirely, I hope for those of you that are convinced that nothing is happening on the Community Side of Avicus, we are working pretty hard. 

So if you notice that no events are taking place for a little while, this is the reason why. I apologize for the confusion.

Regards, -Jake
