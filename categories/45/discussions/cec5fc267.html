About me [LONG READ] by Camemes April 12, 2016 at 7:04 AM UTC

Heyo! 


So many of you know me as another moderator, but I am here to tell you all a little bit about me and who I am. I will try and make this as short as I can but try and stick around for the whole story.


 So, I was born in Eastern Australia on August 16th 2002, which makes me 13 years old now. However, disaster struck. When I was only 1-year-old, I got a very bad flu and (according to my parents) I was about to die, but seeing as I am alive now, I didn’t :) My parents got married literally days after my miraculous recovery.


 I ended up moving from Sydney to Brisbane and went to a state school. Something I want to get out of the way before I continue. I have a few allergies (6 to be exact) which include allergies to grass (you heard me) cats, dogs, pollens, dust and dust mites. These allergies mean I sneeze a lot, and I mean A LOT. I also get constant headaches and runny noses. A lot of people find this ridiculous but all that sneezing and headaches and stuff get’s me tired easily. I can sneeze up to 200 times a day, as crazy as it sounds, every word is true.


 Now where was I, oh right, I moved to a state school and to be honest, I hated every minute of it. Everyone thought I was sick as I was always sneezing and I never got to tell anyone why I was like that. No one wanted to hang out with a sick kid meaning I spend most lunch breaks alone. I was also the first kid to finish my work. Anyone who wanted to be my friend was only in it to copy my work. I always got the best mark in the class. People also bullied me because of this.

 

I then moved to a prestigious private school where there were some kids like me in some ways. I managed to fit in well socially and also academically, which challenged me unlike my previous school. Not much has happened after that, I began playing Avicus and Minecraft in general and loved it.

 

So that is a bit about me and who I am as a person. The reason I made this is explaining a bit about myself and tell people why I am always sneezing and stuff, it hurts when people laugh and don’t take me seriously when I tell them about my allergies and what it does to me. Well, I’ll see you all later, take care, cya around.

 - Cammy
