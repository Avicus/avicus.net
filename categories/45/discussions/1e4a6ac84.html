As many of you have noticed, I got demoted.
So I thought I'd talk about that today.

I was demoted for a number of reasons.
Inaccurate hack detection;
Rudeness, disrespect and arguing on the forums
Unsatisfactory forum moderating.
And possibly other reasons that I've missed.

I guess I'll put this here, it might be easier than explaining everything in a big essay..

( By the way, I'm laughing not because I'm finding anything funny, it's just what I do during conversations. I guess it's something I've always done.
I'm weird like that.
If you've talked to me on TeamSpeak you'd have probably already noticed that I laugh a lot so there you go xD )

I was demoted after being in the staff team for around 9 and a half months.

Promoted to Junior Moderator on 26/1/2015
Promoted to Moderator on 14/2/2015
Retired from Moderator on 28/9/2015
Returned to Moderator on 15/11/2015
Demoted from Moderator on 25/12/2015

It sucks to leave the staff team on a bad note via a demotion but that's life.


I guess my stupid mouth caught up with me in the end. I was never one to just sit by and watch when something stupid happens.
I always spoke my mind to anyone about anything, I wasn't intimidated by anyone and put my reputation on the line multiple times to stand up for what (I thought at least) was right. I guess I should have learnt when it was appropriate to talk and when to just stay out of it xD

I've had a ton of ups and downs during my time as staff but it truly has been a fun ride and I've appreciated every single moment I've shared with everyone during my time as staff. I've learnt quite a bit.


I have no intention of re-joining the staff team in the foreseeable future.

Thanks for listening.
