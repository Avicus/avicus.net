I fixed it for you.

Anyways, here are some things you can configure to get maximum FPS, at least I think so ;p


Video Settings
Graphics: Fast
Smooth Lighting: Off, 0%
GUI Scale: Small (but I recommend normal/large :P)
Brightness: I don't think this matters.
Fog: Off
Render Distance: 2 Tiny
Max Framerate: Unlimited
Advanced OpenGL: Off
Chunk Loading: Multi-Core (heavy on your computer, though)

Details: All at "Off"
Animations: All at "Off"
Quality: All at "Off"

Performance
Smooth FPS: On
Load Far: Off
Chunk Updates: 1
Fast Math: Depends on your CPU. I keep mine at "Off"
Fast Render: Depends on your Graphics Card, I keep mine at "On"
Smooth World: Depends on your CPU
Preloaded Chunks: Off
Dynamic Updates: Off
Lazy Chunk Loading: Depends on your CPU

Hope this helps! c:
