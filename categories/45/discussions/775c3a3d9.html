My Ahvecus challenge by fazer153 August 28, 2014 at 7:08 PM UTC

On Nexus or Nebula, you have to kill at least 5 people with your pointstreak shop.
But you can't knock him off the world or from a high place.
You can't use your sword AT ALL during the game. You can use your bow as much as you want though.
So pretty much when u MLG Shrek someone with the nebula shop the chat must say (person was slain by (person) using pointstreak shop.
Now go do it.
Edit: Oh yeah, i forgot to put that you must do this in one game, if the game ends the challenge resets.
Also, you can't glitch it like shooting with your bow then quickly changing it to the shop so the chat would say that you shot the person with your pointshop.
