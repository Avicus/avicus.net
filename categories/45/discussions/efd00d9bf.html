Step 1: Go to this website http://minecraft.novaskin.me/wallpapers

Step 2:  Pick a wallpaper you want

Step 3: Pick the people you want in the wallpaper

Step 4: When you're done customizing it, select a resolution you want your picture to be in (here's a picture of your options) 


Step 5: You can now pick a filter if you want, if not, just hit the green "Download" button

Step 6: Go to http://imgur.com/  and click on the cyan button "Upload Images" at the top of the screen

Step 7: Find your picture that you made, and wait until it uploads

Step 8: Click on image, then copy the image's url

Step 9: Paste the url in the "insert image" button on the forums

Step 10: Post the comment, and you're done ^-^
