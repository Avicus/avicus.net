I have to say goodbye to the server in game I will still use the forums once in a while and I will still be active on Skype if you want to contact me my Skype is on my profile. I am quitting because the server and Minecraft in general is just not enjoyable for me anymore. Thank you to my friends which most of you I will still talk to on Skype. Thanks for the memories bye for now <3 -99
