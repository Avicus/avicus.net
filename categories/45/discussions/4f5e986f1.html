I just woke up and all the clocks in my dream were set to 5:50 - I didn't even look at the time until now and I woke up. There was a person with a dead toddler as a face, rotted, trying to break down my doors and kill me. Parts of my family already died and it's almost entirely too real. I ALWAYS forget my dreams, but this one; every single hair, every single piece of skin, every move was real. Every step was so realistic. Everything was the exact same and eventually once he started chasing me I grabbed a pistol and shot it in the head 3 times; one in the eye, two in the brain.
It grabbed a box cutter and started shredding it's skin like cheese. It said "Help me." three times and everything turned misty as well as dead toddlers piling up around me. Suddenly, my entire family disappeared and I had a heart attack. All kinds of chest pains, my heart is racing, and for some reason my muscles are really dead. When I woke up, he was standing over me until I opened my eye again and he disappeared. I know it seems like a nosleep story, but every single thing I just felt I do not want to ever feel again, and I don't know the cause.
