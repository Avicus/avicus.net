cinammoncroc000 August 22, 2014 at 12:08 PM UTC

Well, if you look inside a texture pack's files, you'll see a multitude of folders that hold the textures. The "textures" folder will hold all the textures for mobs, items, GUI, etc.

If you take the tile 'diamond_sword.png' from one texture pack and put it in another texture pack, that diamond sword will appear in the texture pack you put it in.

It's really not that complicated once you play around with it. I'll give more info later since I gtg to school, if I can.
