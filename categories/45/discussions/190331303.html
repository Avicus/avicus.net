Goodbye and Thank You. by SnowSX3 February 24, 2016 at 11:02 AM UTC

Edit: Thank you so much for the support, I love you all :)


So the time has come. I'm officially leaving the Avicus Network.

Prepare for a long thread, but don't worry, it'll be the last one I make. Oh God, I just realised how bittersweet that sounded xD

I've basically been inactive for over 2 weeks now, I've only really still been active on the forums these days and I feel with university kicking into full gear, my forum activity will start to decrease as well.
Because of this, I feel that now is the right time to make this thread. 

During the past 2 years I've had the most fun here that anyone could have. I've laughed probably more than anyone should and I've had the privilege to meet and play with some of the most incredible people in the history of the world.
I've also grown as a person over the past 2 years and because of it and I couldn't be more grateful.

My time as staff was an incredibly humbling experience, I had so much support from so many people going into it, I just hoped I did them justice. 
Being a staff member for me produced some of the best and most challenging moments I've ever had on any minecraft server, and I appreciate that so much. I got to work with so many talented people and I got to help people and that's something I wanted to do since day one.

I have been at the end of so many jokes it's been hilarious. 

I never ever thought that test banning myself after my commands stopped working one day would ever be used against me for months and months after it happened. 

I also never thought that by simply helping Petlas tease FrozenSolstice one day on a shipping thread, that it would create the weirdest ship ever in Snowlas.

I've loved every single moment on this server and could be more appreciative of it. Good or bad, I've cherished every experience.
