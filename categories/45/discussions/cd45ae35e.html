Personally, as you can see... on the right is me. So y'all already know that i chose glass. 

The reason: Glass has a loud and distinct noise. If someone attempts to mine the monument, i will hear it when it breaks and rush to it. It also helps to set pressure plates because they are basically motion detectors. If someone steps on one, I'll know ;) 

The objective of my defense is to make it as noisy as possible. 

I also encourage you guys to break your bridges when building over to a monument.
