Hello. I am a YouTuber. Well, kind of :3
I really enjoy making the content itself - after getting a comfortable recording program (does not lag the gameplay/produces great quality video files) I have found that making videos is a billion times easier. However, I think the most important and the most enjoyable part about being a YouTuber is reading all the comments and looking through the feedback that people give you. Sure, h8ers gonna h8, but it's the nice comments and the constructive criticism that really do bring you up and encourage you to make more content.
Post your thoughts and what you think is the most enjoyable part of being a YouTuber!
