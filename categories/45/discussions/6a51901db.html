This is all my opinion here. People can have greatly varying tastes.

Spoilers Ahead

I thought this movie was terrible. I was at the drive in with my friends and this was the best option we had. The first 1/4 of the movie was quite interesting. It seemed pretty decent for a sci-fi movie that I originally thought was just going to be god awful.

I continued to stay tuned and followed the movie pretty well. As it progressed, it just seemed to get worse and worse. During the final shootout, it simply couldn't have been done worse. Literally. Having a flying magical genie appear would have interested me more than the ending. As she morphed into what seemed like a heaping pile of horse crap, my friends began to riddle insults. As she turned into some sort of cyber dragon, we figured she had settled in Jupiter or something crazily stupid. They should have just stopped the movie halfway and called it quits forever. The idea of the movie is great. It's a solid concept if you can actually throw in some nifty stuff and interesting plot points.                                                                                                                                                                                                                         The entire process seemed extremely rushed and there was no time to really think about what was going on. Once I could understand one concept that was terribly thrown in, something else supernatural would happen and it'd be completely absurd.
