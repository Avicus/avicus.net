[Music] Caleb's DAILY Music Hub! by caleb_gl August 11, 2015 at 12:08 AM UTC

Hello! Welcome to my daily music hub. This will contain my channel, along with links to soundcloud and youtube videos which contain music. WARNING: You might not like all of it.

I've started off with three favorites to start. It will continue to update daily!

SONGS:

#1 : Galantis - Gold Dust (ARMNHMR & DATHAN Heaven Trap Edit)
https://soundcloud.com/hypetunes/galantis-gold-dust
An amazing atmospheric song for the ages. This made me shudder and wish I had a friend next to me. 

#2 : Let Go - Sam Kraft (Scary Girls)
https://soundcloud.com/scarygirls/let-go
A acoustic, atmospheric song. Good for remembering old times (Also recommended for this type of music: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DDqNL0js0iU)

#3 : Dillon Francis - Bruk Bruk (I Need Your Lovin) [ben maxwell remix]
https://soundcloud.com/benmaxwellmusic/dillon-francis-bruk-bruk-i-need-your-lovin-ben-maxwell-remix-...
A DJ Snake type of song with a nice buildup. 

#4: Electric Feels - Fugue Flip
https://soundcloud.com/fugueofficial/electricfeels
A rework of Electric Feel by MGMT, and remixed by Fugue. Really mellow feel, sleepy song. 

#5: Gallant - Weight In Gold (Louis Futon Remix)
https://soundcloud.com/louisfuton/gallant-weight-in-gold-louis-futon-remix
A electronic track thats loud, boastful, and sexy tbh.

#6: LUCA LUSH - Velvet Girls
https://soundcloud.com/lucalush/luca-lush-velvet-girls-4
A combination of Daft Punk-esque vocals and octaves of melody, if that makes sense. Listen to it.

#7: Packie Perkins - out of ur clothes
https://soundcloud.com/pattyperk/out-of-ur-clothes
An interesting electronic track with a great bass line that'll fill you up. 

#8: Bee Gees - Stayin Alive (Stereocool "To The Death" Remix)
https://soundcloud.com/stereocool/stayin-alive
Stayin Alive remixed with a heavy bass, basically. Very cool use of the vocals. 


Updates will continue! Thanks Paraspider.
