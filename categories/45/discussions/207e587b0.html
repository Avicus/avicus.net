Because it really needs saving? People who aren't focused on the PvP aspect of it (a.k.a. the other 2 / 3's of the community...) think it's fine, including myself.

The articles are stating that they want to buy Mojang for 2 billion dollars, and it is quite insane to believe that Mojang is worth 2 billion dollars... It'll be worth keeping an eye on, but until Mojang makes statements, I wouldn't believe any of it actually happening.
