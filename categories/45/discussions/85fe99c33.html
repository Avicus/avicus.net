Breakdown VIP.
That's Drumstep. Dubstep has a half-step beat and has a lower tempo than that of drumstep due to it being 130 to 150 BPM (beats per minute) other than it usually being around 140-145 BPM. It is of a different genre and origin and is a sub of EDM (Both drumstep and dubstep). Drumstep is different and is Breakdown VIP due to the fact it uses between 160 to 180 BPM with a half-step beat, so essentially dubstep but faster tempoed based off the elements of Dubstep and Drum 'n' Bass. Dubstep is notorious for *chh shh* and drumstep is *chh chh____chh shh*. If you meant the original breakdown, it is located here https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1aov6gXO1Qs.

tl;dr VIP is drumstep, original is dubstep

Princezz_Dubstep October 22, 2014 at 4:10 AM UTC

Okay, to clear this up... Dubstep is a genre of EDM. There are a plethora of of EDM genres and genres of EDM. 


Dubstep has a larger BPM then most songs because of the tempo. It includes many sounds to make up of a song. Sfx, lead, bass, percussion, snares, kicks, main melody, counter melody etc. I could go on forever. 


Dubstep includes a drop, which is the main objective of Dubstep. The drop can elevate or stay calm. The drop is what makes it the best part of the song. The SFX is the little sounds that gives Dubstep perfection.

There are many genres of Dubstep. Such as, liquid Dubstep, chill step, bro step, hard step, jazz step, funk step, dark step, tech step etc The list can go on forever. 


Each genre has a different rhythm to how the song is played/ the whole genre itself. 
If you're looking for something slow and blended with a steady drop, but not too heavy. You'd go with liquid Dubstep. It's more relaxed, and liquidity. It blends with the other tunes it's played with, and it's more purified. It doesn't have a larger BPM. Much more moderate than other tunes. 


If you are looking for something upbeat( like for a video) you wouldn't pick Dubstep. Dubstep can be upbeat, but it's heavy and has drops involved within the song itself ( You can, but having the main melody play throughout the whole song, is much better (:) You'd more likely go with Electro, House, Garage house, Drum-Step, Electronic b, Progressive etc. There are many upbeat and uplifting songs to choose from (-: 


Dubstep is modified for its originality of intense drops and its unique beats and sounds mixed into the song itself. Going with a Electronic genre would give a better impression of upbeat and uplifting spirt (for videos). As it carries its melody throughout the entire song. Rather than Dubstep, as it would change to another whole rhythm. (:


Having a beat change is also sick, and I'd love to see Dubstep throughout your channel!
