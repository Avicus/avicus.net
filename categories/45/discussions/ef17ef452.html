New RP Release: Stimulating's Defualt Edits by Stimulating February 18, 2015 at 12:02 AM UTC

As you can tell by the title, I'm releasing a new RP for people to use, a default edit mainly optimized for PvP on servers like Kohi and Badlion, but can be used for normal play on other servers as well. 

Why Red Edit? 

Because in PvP when you hit another player, they turn red, so by making red armour, dia swords, etc. It makes It so the game has to process less, and actually gives you decently higher FPS when PvPing, the particles aren't laggy either.
