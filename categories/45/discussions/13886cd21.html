[GAME] What Map Is It? by Myv November 13, 2014 at 7:11 PM UTC

Welcome to my brand-new forum game, What Map Is It!1

After the startling success of my elder game, Guess The Avicadian, which ended up with 24k views (thanks everyone by the way <3) I decided to come up with a brand new guessing game, which instead of guessing an Avicadian, you guess a Map. I know there are less maps then players, which eventually means all maps will be covered, but you can give different clues for the same map.

The aim of this game is to, once again, give 3 clues, about any map on Avicus. They can be whatever you want, except stuff for like "Oh this iz where I rekt so and so with 8 hearts left gg" because that doesn't give any clues.

Rules:

It can be any map you wish, even ones that do not exist now, or basically redundant. 
3 clues only.

I will be around as much as possible again to update this and check answers.

I will start with an example (this should be quite easy)

 Clue 1: Very popular with most Avicadians
 Clue 2: 5 monuments need to be destroyed to win
 Clue 3: Map name ends with an "s".

Happy gaming and good luck!
