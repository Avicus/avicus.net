If you live in the city or close to a lot of noise, see if you can ask a parent to take you to the country, park, plantation, any calm place(Library only has people saying "shhhh").

So yea...After you are outside, just close your eyes and listen, what do you hear? 

I hear blue birds and squirrels running up and down the trees(Even jumping tree to tree). You could hear dogs, cats, flapping wings, or a tail wag.

Now open your eyes and sit down in the grass(Not in a place with a ant hill,animal droppings.)

Now feel the grass with your hand(when your down please wash your hands).

It could be soft, hard, spiky, or just something weird. 

Now if you sat down in a chair and just thought about all the sounds and feelings you heard and touched, You could think about anything, it could even change how you see things now.

Yea....I really just said all of this. hum...Wow...I'm a weirdo. Well, doesn't surprise me. This is just something I saw before that some one else did, and I gave it a go, I stayed outside a lot longer than two minutes after I did it a few times.

So this is pretty much just let go and relax, don't think about how you look or what other people think or say about you, just think about the animals and the sounds :-)
