The "Thank You" Thread by Tynvale August 8, 2015 at 1:08 AM UTC

To a lot of people, this kind of thread may be weird.But I don't want to have to leave the community to say thanks, some of you may feel the same way. So my idea for this thread is to make a list of people you want to thank in the community and if you ever need to thank more people you can edit your post!
I'll start:

Cyanide: I was lucky to have met someone like you. You're like my online brother and I bet a lot of us can say that about you. We need more people like you in this world, and I want to thank you SO MUCH for everything you have helped me with. I know that you will always be there for me, online or not. I want you to know that I would do the same for you. I could write paragraphs on you but I am going to keep this short! Keep doing what you are doing because you are helping so many people.

Harry: Where can I start, you are an enjoyable person to be around and I didn't expect to connect with someone like you. I have had some great times with you on TS and I am looking forward to having some more. I know that you can be a bit immature at times, but everyone is at some point. You're a great kid and I love having you as my sidekick!

Eric: You and Lokigh were the first people I ever talked to in TS. That was a big moment for me and I will never forget it. How I was talking to Lokigh for like 10 minutes then you finally realized that your mic was muted, little did I know this was the start of a friendship I would have with you. I was so happy when you asked me to make a map with you and I WENT CRAZY in skype when I found out it was accpeted (You can confirm!). I look forward to spending more time with you and just remember "tunnel snakes rule".

Snow: My fellow Aussie! I have had some great laughs with you, at you, etc. And somehow my bad jokes make you laugh! I love being in TS with you and having some casual banter because you know it is all in good fun. You are a great person and a great mod! I can't wait to bring you to events with me (remember the joke we made xD) and I look forward to the future and getting to know you more.

Sesspee: The better half of the leaders in Cronus. I love having some great banter with you about Snow, we seem to connect in a different way. I feel like we connect on a more professional level, I guess that's why we made a team together! I like coming to you all the time because I can't organise stuff myself (scrims, etc.). I can't wait until Cronus plays in our first tournament I look forward to experiencing that with you and the rest of the team!

Jozz: I haven't talked to you a lot compared to others but you still seem to help me out a lot. There is just something about you that is liked by everyone. Probably because you know how to MUZZ! You seem to be really chilled and relaxed and I hope one day I can meet you IRL (maybe if I go to Pax Aus). I would be really surprised if anyone had something against you and I think it will always stay that way.

Porky: Even though it is rare that I can actually talk to you on TS I feel like you and me have a connection. I don't know if it's that both of us do YouTube or if it's our personalities but something is there. Our channels have been growing together and I love to see that. You are a great person in general and I know I can also come to you if I have a problem. I would love maybe doing a video together in the future! 

Petlas: Probably the only girl I have actually had a conversation with on Avicus TS. You are a nice person and a nice mod. I hate to say it but when I am on TS with you, Snow, etc. I can actually see that Snowlas could be a thing. You will deny it but you both seem to connect. But I am no love expert so I will stop!!! Just keep being a great mod and a great person in general! 

Grape: Like Porky, I don't get to talk to you much on TS but when I do, or even listen to you, you always seem to put a smile on my face. You have a great personality and your screams are the best to listen to! Keep being you and I hope that we can talk more soon!

Laggeh: You PvPg0d. I love when we are on team speak and get to talk/play together. You make me look bad, mainly because I am, but you also have a great personality. Keep working on your YouTube because I know once you have your commentary set, you will go FAR.

The Cronus Team: You are all an AMAZING bunch of people. I am so happy to have all of you in the team and I think that this team has potential. We all seem to mix well and I love that about us.

There is SO MANY more people I want to thank but I will gradually add them!

Now it's your turn to thank people...
