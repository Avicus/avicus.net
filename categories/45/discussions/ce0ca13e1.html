Ending the night the LA Kings have been eliminated from the 2015 playoffs with a 3-1 loss to the Calgary Flames. Calgary returns to the playoffs for the first time in six years and will play the Vancouver Canucks in the first round.

Ottawa Senators need one point to clinch a playoff spot after beating the New York Rangers 3-0. Ottawa's next game on Saturday against the Philadelphia Flyers where they need just one point to return to the playoffs for the first time in two seasons.

The Winnipeg Jets have also made the playoffs after a 1-0 loss to the Colorado Avalanche. This will be Winnipeg's first time ever in the playoffs since re locating in 2011.
