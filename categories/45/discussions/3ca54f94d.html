Hello, everyone.

I know by now, you've probably completely forgotten about me, but anyways, I think it's about time I made this considering this is one of the only times when I've had enough time to type out what I want to. xD

Let me start off with an apology. Ever since my demotion, I started acting like a total dick (for lack of a better word). I was discouraging, argumentative, and didn't even once think about what I was doing. I did nothing but set a negative connotation upon myself and towards the whole community, and I deeply apologize for that. And although this may not be necessary, I'd also like to apologize to the staff team for disgracing you with my presence. I had no leadership skills, was a depressed mess, and I don't know why I didn't recognize that earlier. So, sorry if I did nothing but screw you all up.

I'm also here to say that since school started and the crew season is still going on, I've stopped playing Avicus, and Minecraft as a whole. I may still pop on every once in a great while, and I'll still try to be on the forums, but I'll be extremely busy every day up until Summer. I know I made a fake leaving post back when I was an attention-seeking moron, but rest assured, this one is real. So, goodbye, Avicus! I'll miss you and the great community you have.
