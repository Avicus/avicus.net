                                                              December 12th 2014.

Today is a special day. Why? Well, because today is my One year Anniversary on the Avicus Network, Its hard to believe that back when I first joined, I hated everyone, and all I cared about was PvP. But, as my play-time increased, I got to know some people in-game, and my love for the server gradually increased, But when I saw that teamspeak broadcast message, I immediately hopped on, My first few times on it, It didnt go as well as I thought, but I kept at it, and the more I went on, the more I felt at home. But everything really changed when Staff apps opened around march, I made an application in literally 25 minutes, and submitted it, And I felt good of myself, I felt that I was doing something to benefit everyone, however I never thought I'd get Senior mod, just regular mod.

So, what is all this about? It's because I love this server and it's community (While it may be bad at times :))))) It always gives me an enjoyable experience, even after a full year. (Or 2,000 hours of play-time) And the people I've met here, I'll never ever forget, They are some of the most amazing friends I've had.
So, Thank you. Whether you like me, Or if you hate me, All of you made me stay here for a full year, and I've had such an outstanding time here.
Heres to 2 years and an even better 2015!
