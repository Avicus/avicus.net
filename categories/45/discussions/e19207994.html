cinammoncroc000 June 20, 2014 at 1:06 AM UTC

Well, there's a lot of things to do in Minecraft (like a lot of people said.) Let me try and come up with some examples...
  • PVP - Killing other people for fun. I don't really know any "pros" but there's definitely some out there
  • Building - One of the main aspects of Minecraft. Build teams like Gazamo and Mithrintia, and building experts like AwarriorVenom and the Paras :3 are a few examples
  • Survival - Another main aspect of Minecraft. I consider some people on the HermitCraft/Mindcrack servers to be pros. Xisuma, for example, is a pro in my eyes. He looks through every mechanic of Minecraft (even going through its code) and tries to come up with ways that will use these mechanics to his advantage in survival. He's even good in building as well :O
  • Parkour - Another part of MC, though a bit minor. There are a lot of people that are good at it ;)
  • Redstone/Command Blocks - I classify them as two different things, but SethBling did/does both. There are a lot of people who work with these things. I classify them as two because I see redstone as mechanics using pistons, t-flip-flops, basic/complex circuits, hoppers, etc. Command Blocks is more about using the commands themselves. However, you need redstone to use Command Blocks, so they are closely linked. Some people are good with redstone and not command blocks, but most people who are good with command blocks are also decent/good in redstone.
  • Progammers/Modders - These are the guys that either make servers/plugins for servers, and the guys who develop modifications for Minecraft. I don't know any specific people for modders. However, we all know who created Avicus/core ;)
