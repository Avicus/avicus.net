How did you become such a great builder?
I was always an artistic person, in anything, I draw a lot (Patterns, Builds, Schematics)

So after a lot of practising and tips from other great builders, I developed my own style.

I bought Minecraft when it was 1.3.2 so 3 years ago? But I began building seriously around 1 and half years ago.

At the beginning, I copied shapes and designs form other builders, and then, made my own shapes using bits and pieces from builds I found here and there.

After a lot of time, I could make my own stuff :)

World Edit played an huge role in that aswell!

If you know how to //copy, //flip, //rotate you are off the a amazing start :D (Because you can play around with build more without wasting much time, thus, developing your creative mind faster)

If could go more in depth about building if you ask me, but I think this will do haha
