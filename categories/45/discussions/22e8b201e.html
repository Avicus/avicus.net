It has taken me a long time to build this and even longer time to get footage and render it (because am such a nub), but I think I am finally ready to realease it.

With a building time of several months I am showing you my FPU.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Fwy4jUJUoM

For those who don't know what an FPU is, it's basically the most complicated part in a CPU. What makes it so complicated is that it doesn't calculate with normal binary numbers and integer numbers, but with floating point numbers instead. 
In 32bit:
Greatest number: 3.402823466.....E+38
Smallest number: 1,175494350.....E-38

My FPU can perform addition(and subtraction by adding a positive with a negative number),Multiply, Divide and calculate the squareroot.
