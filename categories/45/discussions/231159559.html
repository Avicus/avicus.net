With the 1.8 speculation of name changes being available, lots of people want to change their name.

Sometimes your name can be taken, so take a look at this URL and check if the name you want is being used.

  1. Enter this URL:http://www.minecraft.net/haspaid.jsp?user=
  2. Enter the username you want to know if it's being used after the "equals."
  3. If the page it takes you to says:

True: Username is taken. ._.

False: Username is not taken! :P

Hopefully this helps :D
