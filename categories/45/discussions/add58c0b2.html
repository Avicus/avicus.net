Brief summary: A rising threat known as the Xanadeu Empire devoured and conqured many nations. However, Overcast and its allies had them beaten in the first war. Still, the nations allied with Xanadeu had bitter taste towards its victors

Soon after the war, Tribes of the OVercast were united. Avicus which was known as the McZone Kingdom reformed and turned into Avicus. Avicus was now divided into 2 districts, Old Mczone which is the orignal size of Avicus and Avicore which was the newer part and territory that was gained after the war 

Overcast and Avicus were brand new nations, each nations that trained pvpers in order to defend their nation. In order to train them, both countries use map simulators to train them rigorsly in different areas. The map simulators cycle maps and this encourages vannila pvp. This in return gives out the best pvpers ever known.

Chapter 1- The Conflict

It has been 5 years after the great war and Avicus was enjoying its newly gained territory after the war (Avicus did not participate in the war but they got the territory that was abandonded after the war) "Today, we have a feast, to celebrate this newly given territory!"- said Ivy. Everyone at the party cheered. D00zie, a general in the Avicus army, went off to the shores to patrol. Then, they saw some new trade ships that they never seen. IT was the Overcast.

The Overcast sent ships to open up trade in Avicus. Both Overcast and Avicus never really heard from each other and so they were, well a bit curious of each other. Then, an Overcast'an and Aviccanadian got into a fight. This fight sparked a battle(not a war) between Overcast and Avicus.

News spread across both nations, both servers competing to be the best. They trainned hard, gathered arms, and were prepared for war. There was some skrmishes in both sides. 

Then, both nations at the same time declared war. D00zie was shocked but he said to his men "we are dealing with one of the most best people in this region. Lets show them what we got"

"YAAAA" The people shouted. HottoDoge, prodigy and son of HattoWolf and general of the OCN army "We are dealing with an experienced and probably one of the best country we had ever faced. Be prepared for war!" "AGHH" they shouted with pride. Both armies began mobilzing and gearing up for war. D00zie led the navy towards the Overcast. He looked with his eyes and saw ships. "They are from the Overcast, be prepared to fire" There was a lot of ships on the horizon. Then, the Avicus army saw masses and masses of ships "This is not the Overcast Navy. This is Hypixels and Shotbow's invasion of the Overcast..." D00zie said.

The Battle for Quintus 

Avicus: 2,300 men
Overcast: 3,100 men
Hypixel: 7,650 men






Peachy and his forces landed on Quntius beach. The city was atop of a long steep hill. Hypixel forces have it pretty well defended. Once they landed, Hypixel unleashed hell. They bombared Avicus forces with tons of Arrows. There was barely any cover in the beach and so, they had to dig Trenches.

Meanwhile, Hotto and his navy landed near Senex 1. They encountered little resistance because they caught them up by surprise. Hotto sucessfuly captured Senex 1 and was now advancing towards Qunintus City to recapture it.

D00zie's forces encountered tough resistance. His vice- commander,  EmeraldSword in the battle of CherrylandMc, both of them fought bravely. Emerald ordred to keep firing upon enemy troops with tnt cannons. " Shall we sword?" said D00zie "My pleasure." replied EmeraldSword. Both lead the charge and kept pushing foward. No matter what or how many forces were there, the odds did not matter to them. Both succesfully lead their men to the battle. Emerald kept fighting and he went on. He wielded his diamond sword like he knew how to use it. While he was running, he charged and went striaght for the Hypixel general. Hypixel troops were slashed and killed due to his sick sword skills "be careful!" said D00zie as he was blocking a sword attack " Ok i will-" Emerald then looked and he realized that he had been shot "SWORD!" screamed D00zie and he went to go check on him. "Fire" he said again and this time, in front of D00zie's eyes, arrows were pelted on EmeraldSword. about 60 of them pirced through his armor. D00zie was outraged. D00zie then ate a dorito chip on the floor. He was in MlG status mode.
