R.I.P David Bowie+Alan Rickman by rememes January 15, 2016 at 5:01 PM UTC

So i'm really surprised that none of you made this post So I decided I would. 

Recently David Bowie and Alan Rickman both passed away from cancer at the age of 69.
David Bowie was a very famous English singer known for some amazing songs, they were quite eery at times but still amazing. Here is one of my favourite songs "Starman" which he was planned to perform this today as his last ever song performance. Alas he passed away. Here's the song and I hope we can all play our due respects.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRcPA7Fzebw

Moving onto Alan Rickman.
Alan Rickman played the role Severus Snape in the film series of harry potter. His acting was so good that he was hated in real life by Harry Potter fans, until the last one of course. Where we find everything out. The fact that two legends like this passed away around the same time makes me realise that my idols that I have been growing up with will eventually pass away. I hope you guys can pay your respects, I know this isn't the greatest place. But I really felt the need for this.

Thanks.
