My Retirement by FrozenThorn November 18, 2015 at 5:11 PM UTC

Hey everyone,

So some of you may be wondering why I do not have my moderating rank anymore, no I did not get demoted I decided to retire.

My decision to retire was thought about by me for a while and I came to the decision to retire for a number of reasons, most of which are to do with school. It is my last year in school (I am 16) and it is coming up to a really busy time so I would hardly be able to moderate and then from around Christmas to June I would be busy with revision for my final GCSE exams. This would result in extreme inactivity and a probably demotion so I thought that it was probably best to retire sooner rather than later.

I also retired because moderating was becoming extremely stressful along with juggling Controlled Assessments and revision, another aspect as to why I retired; In addition to this, it felt like it was right for me to make this decision and I just want to be a normal player for a while.

There are also other reasons that I would not like to disclose, however I will still be around for as long as I can; I will not be leaving just yet, sorry for those who are disappointed about that :P.

Lastly, I will hopefully be back to moderating around June, I may change my mind but for now that is what I, and others have thought about.

I will certainly miss moderating and RIP me joining full games/choosing a team xD

RIP in Resepignevs
