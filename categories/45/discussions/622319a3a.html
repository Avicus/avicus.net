Crazy_ March 1, 2016 at 5:03 PM UTC

I like it a lot. It added a positive spin to the game, making it more challenging now. I've been playing a lot on SMPs, and I love it. In terms of PvE, I no longer feel invincible, and can't rush every mob I see. Now I have to plan which mob to attack first, and time my hits/shield blocks to take the least amount of damage.  

This will defiantly add an interesting twist to PvP. Shields have the ability to block arrows, different weapons do different amounts of damage and extra perks (like the knockback effect with a sword) with different amounts of cooldown. On top of that, pots can be attached to arrows, which could be interesting to see (imagine what could happen if you could heal a team mate by shooting him with a health 2 pot arrow). There's also elytras, which will give the element of flight to PvP. There's also some nifty enchants, like the frost walk and mending.
