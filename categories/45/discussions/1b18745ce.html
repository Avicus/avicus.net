Farewell by Upsoar February 6, 2015 at 12:02 AM UTC

Heyo guys! To be completely honest, I would’ve never thought I'd be making one of these posts, but here it is. I will be leaving Avicus, possibly Minecraft in general. I've had a recent incident that made me realize how foolish I have been. Things in-game have been affected by more important things in life. Real life. The fact that I had even thought, "Wow. I should've done this 'in-game thing' instead of this 'school-related thing'," is evidence that I didn't have my priorities straight. I should never had been concerned about things in-game if it meant taking away from anything in the real world. I've had problems with this before, and I had sworn to myself that I would never let it happen again. Sadly, I haven't been able to reach a balance between being an active member of the community and focusing on school and other things. With that being said, I hope all of you can understand how grateful I am for everything large and small you've done for me. The amount of people I want to thank is too great for me to include specific names in this, but my thanks go out to everyone reading this. Not just the people on TeamSpeak. Not just the people I talked to daily. Everyone. Without all of you, Avicus wouldn't have ever been what it was to me. 

Keep Soaring - Upsoar
