I enjoyed the film. I know some crazy lore nerds are angry about how the story was different from the game, book, etc. though when seeing this film I went in knowing it was going to be different and I was ok with that, but I thought they did a good job at trying to keep it somewhat simple and close to the original story. Though I do feel if you didn't know what was going on you were kinda loss with what happens with medivh and how fel came into the universe and why it gives the orcs strength, etc... I will say the CGI was fucking amazing and it felt realistic. I thought there were some cheesy lines though what do you expect. I personally enjoyed the movie a lot though it is a bit bias since I have played "WoW" for so long and enjoyed it. 8/10 for me.
