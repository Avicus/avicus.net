Sorry to bump, but I am currently in the process of making a map with Mels and others.

What exactly is DTE?
Destroy the Environment. It's where you and the other team have to destroy the other side at a certain percentage.

Or like a specific landmark(s) that's part of the map that needs to be destroyed. Databasin and many other maps are great examples of these.

If you're going to make it the whole island, I'd suggest making the percentage for completion < 25%.

A landmark can be < 75%
