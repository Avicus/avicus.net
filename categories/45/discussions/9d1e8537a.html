Yes, you can buy capes off the internet. How it works with capes is that a few days before Minecon happens, they email you cape codes (URLs directed to minecraft.net with a page for adding your cape by logging in). Depending on how many tickets you bought is how many capes you get (1 ticket = 1 cape). You can get a really good profit off this for selling our extra capes like I did, but one warning, some people tend to sell them for a ridiculous amount of money on eBay and whatnot. However, there has been rumors that this year they aren't doing capes, something different, this has not been confirmed, as well as I do not believe that Mojang would stop doing the capes idea for each Minecon from this year to so on.
