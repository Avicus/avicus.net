Reducing ban evasion by Or8 November 1, 2016 at 1:11 AM UTC

So yeah me and junr came up with a way to reduce the amount of ban evasion by I'm guessing quite a lot. 

http://m.imgur.com/a/kRZYS


Basically what it is is


You would have a blacklist of ip's and a whitelist of accounts


When a person is logging on it would first check the ip blacklist, if the Ip is there than it would check the account whitelist, if the account your logging in with is on the whitelist it would let you log in, if it isn't on whitelist and your ip is on blacklist than it wouldn't let you log on.... 


A staff members brother was hacking on the server so this would be a good way of stopping his brother from ban evading if he ever gets an alt, but it would allow the staff member to log in cus his name would be on the whitelist (keep in mind you don't have to add everyone that's not banned to account whitelist because it will only check for you of your IP is banned, or is on the blacklist) 


This obviously won't stop all evasion but it will reduce by a lot considering how small of an amount of people that could probably change their ip.
