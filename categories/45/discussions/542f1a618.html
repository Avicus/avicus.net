SnowSX3 December 7, 2015 at 11:12 PM UTC

I was a regular on the Overcast Network when one day Howsie posted an AMA explaining how he was a Manager on this server called the Avicus Network.
So I hopped on to see what it was like.

I remember being disappointed initially because I had about half a second of bow lag between me clicking my mouse and the arrow actually firing. That first impression wasn't the best so I didn't go on for a month before coming back.
When I came back I made my first topic, introduced myself to the community and settled in. The more I played the less bow lag I seemed to be getting, which was good.
It was at this time where I was becoming less interested in Overcast and more interested and involved in Avicus.

That was almost 2 years ago  Now I'm one of the more experienced Moderators on the staff team, I'm well known in the community and I've made friends who have had an impact on me both in-game and real life who I'll treasure forever.
