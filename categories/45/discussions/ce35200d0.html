BourbonBonobo March 26, 2015 at 4:03 AM UTC

As a person who has lived in europe, and has travelled to London numerous, times, I would recommend,  the following:

  • The tower of London: This is a must buddy. there is so much history, and beauty to this castle. I would seriously try and get your spot to go on one of the tours with the beefeaters! It is really cool! 
  • the British Museum: This museum is pretty awesome. I would say that you'll need good walking shoes.. The museum is pretty big!
  • Tower Bridge: This magnificent treasure of england, is truly amazing. Just walking across it is really cool!
  • Go to the London EYE. Personally, I hate touristy places, but this one is truly awesome. Nothing beats a 360 view of London from above right?
  • I would strongly recommend, going out and seeing the Imperial War Museum!
  • Hamleys Toystore: Ok, you might not be into toys anymore, but this toy store is freaking huge! I mean 6 stories tall of toys, candy, giant teddy bears, and Lego
  • Here's a good tip for london: Get an oyster card (transportation card) This thing will take you anywhere, you can take the tube, or the busses, or anything that goes on wheels and requires the oyster car! 
Hope this list helps <3
