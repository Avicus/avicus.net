Milki September 9, 2017 at 3:09 PM UTC

No. I think there are two big reasons for why Wool Stalling should not be banned.

1. Defenders outnumber Rushers. This is straightforward; the wool is closer to the defending team's spawn than the rusher's team's spawn, so not only are there more people available to defend, they respawn somewhat close, so dying isn't too big of an issue. So already, rushers are disadvantaged. They need everything they can get, including stalling.

2. It's not as big of a problem as you think it is. Fire your arrows; you can probably hit someone in the wool room by shooting. Because of this, you can't stall in a decent amount of maps, including:
  • Revolution. Probably the biggest one where it's not a problem, since you can hit the rusher pretty much no matter where they are inside the wool room.
  • Sunset Town. 4 teams means that rushers will kill each other, almost eliminating the problem. Also, defending isn't good on this map anyways.
  • Green's Farms. Large and long wings leading up to the wool rooms make stalling pointless, as no defender is going to come up there anyways.
  • Apache. For the same reason as above.
I'm sure there are more, but I can't think of anything else right now.

tl;dr: wool stalling is fine, it's not broken
