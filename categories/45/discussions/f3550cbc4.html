What is Election Day/Electoral College?

For those not living in the U.S, here is a brief run through. U.S chooses the presidential candidate based on electoral college. In short, each state have except 2 (Maine and Nebraska) have electoral votes so if one candidate wins the majority of votes in that state, they get all the electoral votes in that state. There are 538 electoral votes in total and if one candidate wins 270, they win.

Voters may wish to vote early but Election Day is November 8 and polls open from 6:00 AM- 8:00pm.  I strongly encourage people to vote despite how pointless it might seem.
