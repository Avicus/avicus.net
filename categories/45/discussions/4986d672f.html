Alright so let me explain what Xmouse is:

What it does it makes that some buttons on your mouse presses or does a function on your computer. Mainly toward gamers, and content producers. What I usually do is set my back page (button 4) to nine. So I can quickly switch to an axe rather than not having my index finger on the hit button.

I'm pretty sure I asked someone this a long time ago and they said it was completely fine.

But I have a feeling someone can do some wrong with it.

You can map different buttons to different buttons on your mouse rather than just your keyboard. I can pretty much just put left and right click as just left click and theoretically click 24 times a second, which is a huge disadvantage toward others.
