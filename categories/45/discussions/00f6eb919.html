1 year as mod AMA by Goodnighht October 10, 2017 at 8:10 PM UTC

It's been a full year as of 2 days ago. I meant to make this then, but I've been busy and forgotten about it. lol

Anyway, Sunday (October 8th) is a weird day for it. It's overall happy because I was promoted to Moderator then, but it's also a very depressing day for me. Last year on the same day I lost my best friend for 2 years. (She's not dead, friendship died) Anyway, the reason is my fault and in the few months after I put a ton of pressure on myself. That was a bad thing for me personally, but the way I coped with it was throwing myself into moderating, which helped Avicus I guess. Most of you don't care, but Avicus/video games have been a way for me (and many other people) to get away from the real world. 

There's not a main point to that paragraph, but to make a point, please just be nice to everyone you meet on the internet. Obviously, you have friends that you beat on each other, but when I see people meet someone new and be mean to them, it's almost annoying to see that. You don't know what other people are going through. Some people are mean on the internet because that's the only way they know how to cope with sadness or frustration. The best way to deal with those people is to either ignore them or be nice back instead of yelling at them. Yelling back at them and being mean doesn't help anything besides make both of you more mad... 
Big thanks to  moqah and  xNiia for helping me through that. Love you guys lol

On a more happy note- Ask away lol I'll answer just about anything.
