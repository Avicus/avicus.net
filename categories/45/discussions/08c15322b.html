How many mice do you/have you had? You speak as if you've tried every mouse ever. xD
I've tried like 5 of the most high end mice and 8 of the most high end keyboards. This is only in shops though. 

I have a Razer DeathAdder, but getting the Mionix Castor soon. I'm convinced the Castor is the perfect mouse for me especially because I have small hands and a palm grip which is perfect for this mouse. It also comes with very grippy plastic and a it weighs 93 grams which is pretty damn light for mice. It's kind of like having a wireless mouse without a battery, but it doesn't feel hollow, it feels incredibly well built, which is also a must for me because I tend to abuse my products. I also need it for the red and black colour theme I'm going for.

I have the HyperX Cloud II Headset. Very nice headset and the best value one you can get. 

I also have the Logitech G710+ which is the newest member in the family. Liking it a lot so far, but the orange part kills me. I'm spray painting it red soon and experimenting with some mods to hopefully make the gaming keys I use have a red backlight instead of white. I'm also getting some glossy-white paper to make the backlighting a lot brighter. Black mat- plastic is very bad for backlighting as it drowns the brightness out when reflected on.  Glossy white is a lot better.
