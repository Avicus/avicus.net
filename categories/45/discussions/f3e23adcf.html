A little tip...

Create a timetable for a suitable amount of work each night with an hour before you go to sleep free to relax and do whatever you want. Don't do hours at once, because after 45 minutes your brain starts to get tired and won't register the information. So do half an hour of work - take a break - then get back to it.

These are the most important exams, but also one of the most important times of your life so don't let revision rule your world.
