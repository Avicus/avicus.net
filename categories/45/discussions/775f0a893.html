3 facts: 2 truths, 1 lie ~ Forum game by 0Ll June 3, 2014 at 12:06 AM UTC

Hey everyone!
I had the idea of bringing in this well-known game into the Avicus forums.

The game is called 3 facts: 2 truths, 1 lie, and as the title says, you basically, just say 3 facts about yourself, 2 of which are real and 1 is fake. The next player has to guess which one is the fake one and keep the game going by making their own set of stories for the next person to guess and etc... pretty simple, right?

This game will hopefully help us learn more about each other and unite the community a little bit more.

So... I guess I'll go right ahead and start this! :D
