Inappropriate names: I don't understand why. by Cynthesis April 22, 2015 at 9:04 PM UTC

I'm going to be completely honest here, I've looked at the network's infraction history many times, and I don't understand why so many people have such profane and/or sexually-oriented usernames in Minecraft. If you're someone like me who's real dedicated to keeping this network a clean place, then I immediately /report them, and these people have all been tempbanned, so for that I give a loud applause to the staff :)

Still, why is this even an issue in the first place? On a well-organized network like this one, if there's an inappropriate name in the death messages/chat messages, then you're gonna get tempbanned: no questions asked, and given that incentive, I still saw someone yesterday with a racist username and skin. If anyone wants to even try to give me a reason, I don't want to hear it, because it's NOT a good reason, whatever it may be. Thanks for reading, I really hope this issue becomes clear to everyone working to make this community as good as it gets.

P.S.
To those who think those kinds of things are funny, let me tell you as a fact set in stone that it will cause more provocation and less amusement.
