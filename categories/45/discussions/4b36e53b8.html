My faithful edit by Pavlo December 29, 2014 at 7:12 AM UTC

Hey guys, I'm sharing my personal edit of the faithful pack that I use whenever I play.
It's based on the 64x64 faithful pack, however I have also changed some blocks to 512x512, and most of the items to 512x512. 
I would like to say that I do not credit this as my own resource pack, as the textures and items have been collected from a wide range of other packs. Saying this, I have still customized some things myself. e.g the swords were taken from the R3d craft pack, and I changed them too my own liking. I also changed some of the particles myself and the explosion texture. The only block I did myself however, is glass and it's basically like the faithful glass, except at a higher resolution.
