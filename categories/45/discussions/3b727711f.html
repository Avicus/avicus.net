Windows 10 - Technical Preview by Derumisis January 26, 2015 at 11:01 PM UTC

Well, Windows 10 is underway and it's Technical Preview is already released. I am with the Windows Insider Program and I am typing this with the new and beautiful Windows 10. All pictures have been taken by me. It has it's bugs and it's stripped from features currently, but I am going to show you wonderful features.

THE TASKBAR
Currently, it's not fully customisable, but here is a picture to get you to know what you will be expecting: http://i.gyazo.com/e9de25c5a66717a3c0e5b69b3b6fc53b.png

THE START BUTTON
I'm sure most of you loved the Start Button from Windows 7 and below; and finally, it is back. NEW AND IMPROVED: http://gyazo.com/589db61589dd90142c16b2b476eca101.png

THE WINDOW
The window borders are VERY SMALL and have changed by a lot: http://gyazo.com/7ed3b0aaa8eae97d72824d8e0817e792.png

GRID SNAPPING
One of my favourite features, so say you got 5 windows open and you really want to organise it. Look at this: http://gyazo.com/0b7523b27da5795bd53222dfee3ee7da.png
You can fit: MORE THEN 2 WINDOWS ON A SCREEN PERFECTLY.
