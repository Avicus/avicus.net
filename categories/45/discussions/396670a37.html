Bridges Tourney! by mustafa February 9, 2016 at 9:02 PM UTC

So this Sunday at 1 pm EST, me, Netcb, and Atdit will be hosting a bridges tournament on mineplex. For those of you who don't know ow what bridges is, it is a game in which there are 4 teams each with 10 players. You have 10 minutes until bridges to other islands form. The last tram standing wins. The game actually will be hosted on Mineplex, Atdit can create a private server, and teams must sign up. Reply to this form if you would like to participate. Since this is supposed to be a competitive match, team captains will be chosen. I will be a team captain but if you would like to be a team captain or sign up together with some friends, just leave all of your names below. First come first serve!
