MLGtino January 8, 2015 at 9:01 PM UTC

DDoS.
It stands for Distributed Denial of Service. It is completely illegal and could bring your ass right to prison in most countries if you do enough damage.

I wouldn't get involved in this stuff, so just be aware when people say this kinda stuff.
To elaborate a bit more, it's when it floods your router with packets to slow down your internet. When a website is ddosed, it's sorta like you refresh the page. Except the difference is that it requests the page x amount of times per millisecond, which is insane. For example, if you set your booter to request a page, let's say, 100 times per millisecond, then it's basically like refreshing the page 100 times in a much less than a second. That's pretty damn lethal.

Edit: When I say flood your router, I mean it's someone else flooding your router. Also, ddos slows the other person(s)' internet as well.
