This was mine:
Name of show: Sonic Underground
Channel Name: Tiny Pop or ITV1
What was it about? Well, we all know Sonic. That blue hedgehog that goes super fast. Here is the story: Dr. Robotnik (Eggman) takes over Mobius (the place where it's set) and runs a tyranny across the land, robotisizing (turning people to robots) who don't obey him. Queen Aleena the ruler who was taken from the throne had 3 kids, Sonic, Sonia and Manic is told a prophecy by an Oracle, who says that one day the three will take down Robotnik and restore the land. But there was a price, to split up from their children and not return to them until they overthrow him. So she takes them to 3 different parents, then a few episodes later they get together on their quest. Robonik also hires 2 mercinares called Sleet and Dingo to help, but they failed. Miserably. WATCH THE SHOW IF YOU WANNA SEE THE STORY. IT'S ON NETFLIX.
