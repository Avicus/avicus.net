So we got a lot of ppl transfering to or nearing highschool and middle school.

Well here is some advice, focus on your studies. Don't let ppl at school disturb you.

I learned that i needed to get stronger in highschool, ppl who looked like they could lift 200+lbs (And they can too.), its pretty damn intimidating. 

When you're in highschool in the Big Apple or New York City especially in Bronx, you need to especially get tough and hardened. Gangs run rampent and teachers curse... oh yes they do... 

The gym teacher brought out this weird machine that i only see in TV, a barbell; bench pressing. 

I needed to often be spotted with the 45lbs or the weight would cave in my ribcage. 

But as time progressed, ppl noticed that i was getting "brolic". 

Previous school enemies backed off more often and started to respect me; especially after i had a fight with a very arrogant and popular kid. 

My advice is to: Stick to yourself, focus on your studies, be stronger, and due unto those of what they've done to you. 

Highschool isn't about getting popular or hanging out with friends. If you go down that lane, you'll most likely end up a highschool or college drop out. 

So please, stay in school and keep out of trouble. 

And congratz to the ppl going to college, I hop you guys get the dream jobs that you guys want!
