GITHANOS3 March 6, 2016 at 4:03 AM UTC

What is the SWC?
A: The SWC stands for the Spleef World Cup. It's an annual spleef tournament that puts the best of the best against each other in hopes for attaining spleef glory!

Q: SWC IV?
A: Yeah, the fourth Spleef World Cup. The first two World Cups were held in 2012 and 2013 on SpleefLeague, the third was in partnership with MultiCube in 2014.

Q: What's the server IP and forum address?
A: swc.cubecraft.net

Q: How do I register for the SWC forums??
A: Log onto the server and type /register [your email], from there you'll get an email with further instructions. Original explanation found here: http://swc.cubecraft.net/threads/change-in-the-registration-system.190/#post-2424

Q: How do I join the SWC?
A: Go onto the server and do /joinswc and you will automatically be added to the qualifications ladder. This command will be available as soon as the tournament started.

Q: How do I play spleef?
A: Type /s, /spleef, or you can select an arena from your game menu.

Q: Why are some arenas unavailable?
A: Some arenas can only be played on if you visit them by finding them on the map! The vast majority of the normal arenas will be available by default, in contrast to the usual 3-5 arenas open initially. This is so we can aid in the qualification period.

Q: What is Super Jump?
A: It's a Parkour run and jump style minigame. It's played between two players and it's a competetive race to reach and end goal. Like spleef arenas, many need to be discovered.

Q: How can I find my friend?
A: You may find friends and foes alike through a friend/party system (To be implemented) in which you can chat and toggle visibility so you can explore the spleef world together.

Q: What's with chat?
A: Chat is separated into numerous channels to prevent excess spam from the large influx of players. You will be in, by default, a specific channel when you join the server. Through your spleef menu, you can navigate towards chat options and find numerous channels to join. Find one your friends are in or one you'd like to participate in and you're all set!

Q: How will the tournament be organized?
A: This will be explained in a section further down the thread.
