Just remember that it's all fake.
These movies thrive off trying to make something fake look real.

Once you get your head around the fact that it's all not real then you should be sweet.

If you really are having a hard time sleeping, read a book for 20 minutes before bed,
keep your phone or any other electronics away and drink a glass of warm milk.
This will help you brain realise that it's time to sleep and it'll help your body relax.

If it's a matter over the mind sort of thing then get a night light for a bit until you feel comfortable with going to sleep again.
There's no shame in it and it'll help you mentally.

Hope this helps :)
