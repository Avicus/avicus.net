Extremely disappointed. I've always considered it one of the better servers still in action, regardless of past incidents with this one. 

Likely nobody will read this, but I'd like to just share my part of the story for a minute. 
Overcast was a breeding ground for new and experienced players in the technical aspects of the actual game and PvP as a whole. Many of these players have moved on to greater things because of this. My personal experience with the server dates back years ago when I started playing to gain a better understanding for the tactical part of DTM and DTC gameplay. Then I began meeting with some regulars on the server who, while appearing intimidating, were very helpful in giving advice to me to become a better overall player, not to mention being very friendly and accepting to someone completely new and fresh to the idea of cannons, skybridges, the whole core idea, you name it. I still talk with many of these people today. From there, I learned to participate in big tournaments with people far above my own skill level on both Overcast and Avicus, winning various awards with said teams respectively. I've met more people than I can remember and I've grasped a new level of tactical and technical abilities in game, making me who I am today. I wouldn't be me if it weren't for what OCN brought to me. I'd probably still be building fucking houses in singleplayer if I hadn't stumbled into the right places and met the right people. 

This all being said, the same goes for Avicus. Avicus wouldn't be the same without OCN. We've learned from them. We've adapted to their mistakes, embraced their players when possible - allowing new ideas to come in and new players to come out, and let's be honest, a large majority of our influence is off of them. Saying we copied them is a glorified, Avicus biased way of saying that we influence each other. If OCN and its players were non-existent, we wouldn'y have TNT maps and skybridgers until much later than they popped up now, most likely. And please tell me something, how many OCN players have been staff on this server, and still are? While this may not apply the other way around, much of our community was/is part of theirs. I can name at least 20 players off the top of my head that were regulars on both servers and within their relative communities. We used to rely on each other, now I don't know what this means for Avicus, only time will tell.

But seriously, I think it's crazy that they're doing this. it's like something from a dream - or a nightmare more appropriately.
