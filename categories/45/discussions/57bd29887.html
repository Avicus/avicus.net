For posting a picture on a thread, follow this guide:

1. Grab the image that you want to post and its link.
2. Click the top right icon of a picture, most likely this one: 

                                                                                                                                                                               ^ This one

3. Now insert the image into the area where it allows you to do so. For some links, you may need to have the ending ".png" of the link in order for the picture to show.
4. That should be it! Remember you can also post the link on the thread so other people can click on it and view the picture in a separate window.

Hope that helps.
