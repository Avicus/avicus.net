Why Giveaways Can Be Bad. by BoldAndBrash July 28, 2014 at 4:07 PM UTC

So, many Tubers love doing giveaways for their "adoring" fans.... Well, giveaways aren't always good. I'll explain why.



Why It's Bad For The Subscribers.
There could always be a possibility that the guy who says he is doing a giveaway may just be saying that for subs. He'll continuously say "I'll do the giveaway next week but you have to subscribe to me!" He may just keep saying this for subs. Though, this one is easy to avoid. Just hit that unsub button!

Why It's Bad For The Tuber.
"FAN": *subs*
Tuber: "FAN" has won a minecraft account from my giveaway! Gratz dood!
"FAN": *unsubs*
Basically, the fan might just sub for the prize then unsub if he wins it.
