cinammoncroc000 October 28, 2014 at 1:10 AM UTC

Screenflick.

Honestly that's going to be the best recording software out there for a decent price. Screenflow is also an option (comes with editing capabilities, too,) but that's well near $100. This is only around $30 and it is extremely fast and efficient. I experience no frame rate drops when using it ;)

EDIT: If you have good enough frame rates with Quicktime Player (should be installed on your Mac already) then you could use that, but for most people it's downright terrible.
