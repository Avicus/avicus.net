Secret Santa: 

Basically, whoever enters this will be given someone to get an online gift (Credits, badges etc.) and also you will receive a gift from someone else who has you. Somehow you could leave clues and they could slightly guess from the list of players who may have you. You have to keep it a secret or it ruins the game!

Dragon Escape Event:

How this works, is 1 person will be the dragon (AKA Santa) and it will just be a last man standing vsing the Santa (Dragon). Little idea :D
