[FUTURE SUGGESTION] Elimination DTM! by lactify July 10, 2016 at 7:07 PM UTC

Hey everyone! Recently, I just thought of a new gamemode Avicus could possibly create in the future. It is called Elimination DTM. 

The idea is simple: Basically, there are 3 teams or more and each team will have a monument and the idea is to destroy it like normal. Once your team monument is destroyed, you are out and cannot rejoin until the match is over. 

So really, it is similar to the Mute Cities (4 team DTMs) in the sense that one team will be victorious, however it could make the players more competitive as if your eliminated, your out for the rest of that particular game.

Now I am not an expert around the gamemode plugins, XML, YML and etc, but if this gamemode would be possible to implement onto the server, I would be more than happy to help.

Let me know what you guys think! 
If you still think my thread lacks details on how it will be played, let me know and ill edit accordingly.
