Differences between 1.7 and 1.8 by veryangerydoggo April 30, 2015 at 6:04 PM UTC

So, earlier on I was comparing 1.8 and 1.7 and here are the differences I found:
- Enchantment glint is more visible in 1.8.
- Potion bottle caps are enchanted on 1.8.
- One can get higher FPS on 1.8 if they are using Java 64 bit.
- On 1.8 the player's skin goes red when hit while on 1.7 the player's armour goes red when hit.
- Blockhitting is visually different.
I may have forgotten a few..
What differences have you noticed?
