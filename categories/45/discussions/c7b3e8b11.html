I have to agree on this, but no one can say what I'm about to say isn't true without being a complete idiot.

Razer is overpriced. 

Razer uses cheap materials (plastic mostly).

Razer is overrated and over hyped.

Razer uses their "gaming switch" in the BlackWidow, but in reality they are just cheap knockoffs of Cherry MX keys, specifically Kaihl. Most gaming brands (ex Thermaltake) use this switch to reduce prices and thus make an affordable mechanical keyboard. What Razer's done here is INCREASE the price by making it a selling point. In reality these switches are a lot cheaper than Cherry MX, which is made in Germany compared to China. They are also a lot heavier, and I know this because I've experienced both Cherry MX Blues vs Razer Green and Cherry MX Brown vs Razer Orange. Razer's just playing a game on you, and right now, they are winning by a lot.

I agree though, some people like these defects (imo).

And I have to admit, Razer stuff look AMAZING.
