Goodbye! by Teckky June 1, 2014 at 6:06 AM UTC

Well, where to start.  Lets start at the beginning. I joined two - three years ago on the Hunger Games server, there wasn't a hub and it was just a bunch of separate servers. Back then I was a noob and didn't realize that I had stumbled upon something special, an amazing group of people that would later change my life in ways that I couldn't have dreamed of.

I played Walls with my friend, and it was so fun! I remember it would be a pain to get on the same server and we would have strategies to get on the same team. "Okay cody join in, 3, 2,  ohh dammit you missed it, quickly re-join". We had so much fun together, and around that time was when my true love for Minecraft it's self flourished, now I'm not talking about passionate love, "if you love it so much why don't you marry it".. no no no, not like that.

Later I remember all my friends going to Jonah's house, and they would be amazed, and I would show them, and we would download mini-game maps, and all play together. So later I invited my good friend Jonah to join MCZone and that was the day KooDaLord joined the community.

I played, played and played and finally got fairly known within the Avicus Community. I started making friends (Howsie, Jozzy, Hannah, Andy, Yoav, Gabby, Oby, Nolan, Tino, etc.) and I really felt like I fit in quite nicely. (Now thats what I love about the Avicus Community, we are sooo accepting. Theres no oddball in the Avicus community, no extra puzzle piece.) \

Now let me get back to my original point. I was having fun, but my additions became worst and worst, and I began to stay on my computer for hours and hours on end. 

I would just like the thank everyone who has put there time into Avicus, as you have changed my life, and all of you have changed me. Thank you Keenan, I cannot believe how far you've got, but you deserve it!

Sooo... Tomorrow I will be leaving Avicus..
