[Forum Game] The Unfortunate Wish by Mango_ June 3, 2014 at 8:06 AM UTC

I heard about this game somewhere else, and I thought it would be fun to post it here! Anyway, here's how this game works...

I'll post a wish of mine (for this example I'll use "I wish I had more money"), then the next person to reply has to say something along the lines of "Wish granted, but all the money you received was counterfeit and you got arrested". In the same post (underneath their response) that person will post another wish, and the game continues.

You may make them as ridiculous and humorous as you want, but please keep all wishes and responses appropriate. I hope that kind of makes sense, so to start it off I'll post one:
