So, I was not on avicus for almost the entirety of last week, and here is why.
I was on a holiday to cambodia!!!!
and DAYUM it was awesome, but for now ill only post a few pictures from the second last day in cambodia. Since I am running short on time to post the rest.

So on the second last day we went to the kulen mountains
https://www.google.com.sg/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=5&cad=rja&uact=8&ved=0CCcQFjAE&url=...
And there I swam in a large waterfall area, It was definitely one of the best times I have had in my life.
I also went psycho/crazy that day. I made one heck of a decision.
So I did 3 things there.
1st thing was that I climbed so high up the waterfall that I reached an area where the water no longer touched me and alot of foliage flourished, It was as If I was in a movie.
It is kind of hard to see me but I am the one that is highest up the waterfall there.                                         After that I saw a guy jump off the cliff to the left most side of the screen, and it looked....awesome.
I knew I DEFINITELY had to do it. Even though i was kind of scared since i cant see under water and it is almost 5 meters up. While swimming towards it, someone did a front flip off the cliff, and I couldnt withstand the voice in my head telling me to do dat shit.
So i climbed onto the cliff, went mental, and forced myself to do a flip while jumping. Luckily I had my mother taking photos the ENTIRE time, and by that I mean she had 1GB of photos, within 4 hours.
Before that, here is an awesome photo of yours truly
So here is the "time lapse" of me doing a front flip

Well, I hope you enjoyed reading about my adventures in the kulen mountain.
Ill be sure to tell you everything about the other days as soon as possible.

Have a great day Avi-Community :D
