Goodbye by Zintenka March 20, 2015 at 11:03 PM UTC

Two of my posts were archived. The first post was semi-understandable. The second archive was uncalled for since i did not instigate it and made some edits. 

All i ask for is for no one to archive and let me have my peace. This really has made me see new things. 

My reasons for leaving:

  • #1: Bad decisions caused the server to lose player count. I do not know who thought it was a good idea to get rid of CTW but it was a very bad marketing decision. 
  • #2: Degrading of avicus' community and poor moderating decisions. "In dark times the eye begins to see." is relevant to this one. I've stayed with Avicus for a long time now and I've become to notice a lot of things that I do not like: Killfarming allowed, players banned on avicus being banned on TS, robotic-like and cold mods, and the faults on the players' side is simply too much to list.
  • #3: Loss of motivation, I've lost my will to make any more maps. I have "mapmaker's block" right now. Also, I've been a mapmaker since around the ending of MCZone and all the way to here and still not mapdev, been trying my hardest but meh. (Especially since my recent maps have great gameplay and YML seems to pan out quite well.) Still don't have it and it really rubs me the wrong way to see that people who are newer to mapmaking have gotten mapdev with practically a snap of a finger :T I feel like I've been slapped in the face and my work spit on. Oh well. 

(Do not take this the wrong way as I do not want to get into an unreasonable flame war.)
