Quality and things by Alekpower March 26, 2015 at 4:03 PM UTC

I am sure most of the older Minecrafter's have noticed that Avicus' player count dropped, due to their time on the server. This is half-sad to me because this was one of my favorite servers back when I used to play. Problem? I think so. There are so many problems that I see straight off the bat. So for this topic, I am just going to rant some issues and hope that the staff tries to fix them.

  • THE LOBBY: I was a mod at the time when the current lobby was being done by the staff. Secret rooms were being made, and unmade, signs were getting fine-touched, and people would just come on to admire it. It is, doubtless, very impressive. Huge, detailed, and... huge. My screen couldn't even render the signs from the middle or the top of the lobby. No one wants that. I think that a smaller lobby, perhaps the size of the original Avicus one, would be more welcomed.

  • TRY-HARD MODS: This is just my opinion and all, but I think you guys are really not caring who becomes a mod. Like look at the Staff Apps. Skype is not a requirement? Really? That is honestly just ridiculous. How can y'all contact each other effectively and constantly? The staff team needs to be able to be in contact for issues and for staff meetings and shizzle. And, from what I can tell, some of these mods are incredibly strict... Like they have a trigger finger to just get someone kicked at the slightest offence. That ain't cool... Like they say one bad word and instantly get warned or kicked. People have their moments. (Pls bringk bak Mr Nice Guy)


  • NOT ENOUGH ADVERTISING: I don't see Avicus on any of the major server websites... They may be there but no one votes for them. Simply, just bring back the McZone system... Person with most votes on all websites for that month gets diamond rank, second highest gets emerald, third highest gets gold. I enjoyed that and it definitely made me vote.


  • MAP QUALITY: Yeah... The map quality is not too great. All the pretty maps get accepted. Sadly, the gameplay ain't too great on them. We should raise the standards and criteria for the maps. I have seen a few good ones get denied. The people who made them were not well known by anyone. I have also seen many crap ones get accepted. The people who made those are considered really great builders.
