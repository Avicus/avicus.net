Welcome to the Secret Fly!


Who are we?
We are Avicus members who want to get better coverage around the server by sending in reports of hacking and misbehavior to moderators.

History
Around almost 2 years ago StromzInvader founded the "Secret Fly - Forensics and Investigations" Agency. (From him is also the thread format and the other information). So around 6 months ago Agency split up. Almost no one was online on skype and some of the mods that were in the team got demoted or retired. Now After these 6 months I'd like to revive the whole Agency again!
(Old Agency Thread: https://avicus.net/forums/69a337c8c)

What do you do in the Secret Fly?
We basically just find people and report them to the staff in the reports tab of the website. We know very well that moderators can't always be on the server every single hour of the day. Therefore, we make it a habit and objective to be able to help them.

How do you apply?
I just need to see your dedication to help the members of Avicus and you should post on this thread if you are interested to join. I will be looking at your infractions for behavioral concerns. This time around, I will be more strict regarding inactivity within the group. This is what caused the Secret Fly to fail the first time. Put your skype name down below, and we will contact you.

Requirements
Our means of communication is Skype. We will require you to have this. 
You will also need to have a recording software in order to capture evidence of hacking within the server. 

Contact Us
If you have any problems that we can address, we are more than happy to help you! Contact us through replying on this thread or contact me on Skype. My Skype ID is flo.schmenni.
