What are you guys thankful for? (Please Read) by SirCyaniide April 27, 2015 at 8:04 PM UTC

Today at 11AM I get a knock at my door. I open the door to see only my neighbor on the floor asking me to call 911. He was showing symptoms of a mild heart attack, this man helped me through tough times and was there every minute of my life. He helped us settle in, he came to every birthday party we had just so he wasn't making us feel alone in this world. The ambulance drove away, he looked healthy, smiling and telling my mom and I that he was going to be fine....


Around 2pm we get a call from his daughter saying he passed away due to heart complications. 

This post isn't so I can get views or replies, nor is it meant to bring sadness. This post is for people who are struggling in their life. Whether it's from loosing a loved one, being bullied, or anything else. Know that there's always that one person who will sacrifice their time just so they can see if you're ok. You may or may not have met this person yet. But if you have, grab them give them a big hug, and tell them how thankful you are. 

Comment down what you are thankful for. I need a distraction from my life right now. 

RIP Rob Bayer 4/27/15
