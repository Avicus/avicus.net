I miss my childhood by TortouseMincer June 2, 2014 at 6:06 PM UTC

Hey guys,


So I'm not sure how many of you people out there have began to think about their childhood memories when getting older.

Well here's my childhood in a nutshell:

I used to love Lego; Lego was my life and I would play with it all day, which brings me to TV

At the age of 7 Disney channel released in South Africa. I was staying in a hotel away from home and I woke up early to see the release. It was more than I had ever imagined. Disney was my childhood, Lilo and Stitch, Loyd in Space, Recess and a lot of others were the magic in my life. The friends I used to play with loved it; memories of my crush also remain in my childhood. I loved Disney for 6 years straight, but suddenly everything changed. (When the fire nation attacked xD)

I suddenly saw the new TV shows on Disney as boring.  Suddenly Lego became less amusing. Suddenly my childhood was gone. All the old TV shows vanished. The magic was gone.

Are memories sweeter in your head? Is your childhood that great? Or is your childhood never ending?

I don't know if you guys are reaching this point, but I thought I'd just like to share my thoughts because I know a lot of people also think like this!


Anyways thanks for reading :D


~Tortouse :D
