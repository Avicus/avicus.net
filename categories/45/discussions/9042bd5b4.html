Ikr, heres my opinion on the DeathAdder. Personally, the rivalry between the Logitech G502 and the DeathAdder is easy to sum up. The DeathAdder has nothing wrong, yet the Logitech is a lot better in a lot of ways, but also has flaws. For instance, they made a system where you can add weights, but the mouse is already 30 g heavier. The scroll wheel is so stiff, that when you wanna click it you scroll. the extra buttons feel a but mushy, but that isnt that much of a big deal. The DeathAdder is perfect in being simple, which makes it perfect for FPS games. Its light, has 2 extra buttons, has an ok sensor, pretty high DPI, and a perfect scroll wheel. The thing is, you always want a little but more with the DeathAdder, which makes you have doubts. But I cant live without a light mouse, I need it to have a good scroll wheel too.
