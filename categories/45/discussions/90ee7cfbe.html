RCMP_Mountie October 27, 2014 at 7:10 AM UTC

RegClean Pro presents itself as a free system-optimization utility and registry cleaner that rids Windows of system errors and stray files. Registry cleaners are extremely powerful applications that can damage the registry by using aggressive cleaning routines and cause your computer to become unstable, i.e. crash.

Go to Start>Control Panel>Programs>Uninstall a program>Right click RegClean Pro and click "Uninstall". Next, scan your computer with a reputable anti-virus software, and check to see if there are any bits and pieces left from any unwanted programs.

This may or may not be 100% correct, so don't judge me as I found this myself from the internet. Hope this helped :)
