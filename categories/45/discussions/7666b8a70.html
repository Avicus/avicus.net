How to get higher fps in 1.8 by Upsoar September 19, 2014 at 3:09 AM UTC

I've seen quite a few people complaining that they get a low amount of fps in the new 1.8 update. I was rather surprised by that because 1.8 was meant  to IMPROVE preformance. Even so, these three little things may make a big difference in your fps. Try them out and see if they help you (They do for me)
1. Options -> Video Settings -> Use VBOS "on"
2. Options -> Video Settings -> Alternate Blocks "off"
3. Options -> Video Settings -> Use VSYNC "off"
(Additionally, render distances are now selectable by chunk instead of short, normal, and far. 8 Chunks is the same as the old normal render distance.)
