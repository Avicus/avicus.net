A Community Montage? by Ciaan September 4, 2016 at 11:09 PM UTC

Hey everyone. Lately I've been thinking about starting a 'long term' editing project and wanted it to be Avicus related. I figured that the best choice would be to do a community montage. 
For those that don't know, a community montage is a montage/compilation of clips submitted by the community and highlights those that submit the clips. Probably a really poor explanation but I tried.
Anyway, before I went off and made a thread saying that I was for sure going to do it I decided to get opinions from the community (which would make sense), share my ideas and hear yours.
The basic requirements (that I thought of):
  • Try to keep clips 10-15 seconds long maximum.
  • Try to keep clips Avicus related (whether on public avicus, avicus scrim servers or private scrim servers with avicus teams)
  • Try to have your video quality at least 30fps 720p, (the better the quality the better I guess?)
  • Upload your clips to YouTube and leave the link below.
Again, this is not the official thread and I haven't 100% decided if I will do this yet. Please just leave your opinions and ideas (specifically on the requirements) below. If I missed out on anything, feel free to ask and/or let me know.

o/
Ciaan.
