The Gym thread by lordteddey June 20, 2016 at 7:06 PM UTC

Sup, I thought it would be cool to create a gym thread for the community. Here you can share your story, tips and your split.

I'll start with my story. In August 2015, I decide to sign up to the Gym, I'm an ectomorph who weighed 45kgs. I was literally a stick. I stayed consisted and had good nutrition, I was eating almost 5000 calories per day, that's quite impressive, that's like bodybuilding material. (For those who don't know, I was bulking, also know as eating more then your calorie maintenance). Anyways, a month passed and I was gaining, during the 2 and a half month period, I weighed 57, that's 12 kilos in 2 months and a half, I was over the moon! I kept on going until today where I weigh 74 kilos with 10% body fat. I actually have arms now, a chest, a back, abs, shoulders, legs, you name it.

Hers my current split: 
Sunday: Biceps and Chest
Monday: Triceps and Back
Tuesday: Shoulders and Legs 
Wednesday: Calfs, forearms.
Thursday: Chest (I mainly try to isolate my upper chest, like focus my upper chest) Usually a 30-40 minute workout.

I do chest twice a week because I feel it's probably my weakest muscle; therefore, I'm prioritising it.  I also do Abs every other day.

My workouts last around an hour to an hour and a half sometimes two. Depends whether I'm in my cutting phase or bulking phase. 

Now it's your turn :)
