[GAME] A poem to a friend. by IviFetita December 18, 2014 at 1:12 AM UTC

Hello there!
Here is a new Forum Minigame called A poem to a friend.
It is pretty easy;
You have to write a poem to a friend, to your best friend.
It's steps are first think about your best friend, someone who you love with your heart and would give anything for his/her safety, or a really good friend. Think in what he/she makes you happy, why is your friend, why he/she makes you feel special, etc.

I'll give mine as example.
Mine: 
"I know I say it and repeat it everytime, but I never can stop feeling very glad for have you as friend: Thank you for be my friend. Thank you for understand me everytime. Basically, you are the reason of why I'm alive, otherwise, I surely won't be alive. You are what makes me happy, what makes me wake up each morning, and go to sleep each night. You are the reason of why now I'm being happy, and why I feel I'm special only with you. You occupied a very special place in my heart, and if you'd stop being my friend, my life would stop having sense. the best part of all is that everything I wrote is truth, and I wrote it with my heart. I love you, friend. Promise me you will never stop being my friend, because  you are who helps me and supports me in the downs and the ups." - I don't feel very comfortable publicly saying to who I wrote this poem, but if you want to know, feel free to ask.

P.S.: Please note that you can if you want tell to who was directed the poem tell us, otherwise it isn't necessary. Thank you. :)
