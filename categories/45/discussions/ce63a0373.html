Hey everyone! Galrandir here and this will be the place to ask for an outro! To get an outro, though, you will have to fill out this question thingy:

  • A uncopyrighted song of your choice
  • A moving cinematic background (yes or no)
  • The video file (mp4, wmv, ect)
  • Your mc skin (yes or no)
  • A box for last video (yes or no)
  • How long (please specify)
  • How you want me to send it to you (email, Skype, ect)
  • If you want your name written at the top of it ( yes or no)
  • The date you want it to be done by (please specify)
  • Your youtube name
