Should be short, but real words. No "oijaosiejtoi"
Very important: NO NUMBERS!!! If you want your name to look cool or spiffy, it should be 1-2 real words with NO Numbers!!!
Example: SpiffyBoi
Example of a bad name: junko11

In case you didn't know, junko11 was my old ign that I first joined Avicus with 3 years ago.

If you want your pro looking name to have 2 words, have either a _ or a Lowercase Uppercase to signify a space.
Example: HelloDiscord
the o and the D are lowercase uppercase making it look like two separate words when it's really one.



Okay! Now you have your cool ign! Time to get a skin to go with it! There's nothing more newbieish than playing with a steve skin!!!!

The biggest thing with choosing a skin is making it fit your ign. If you have a name SpiffyBoi, you don't want a skin of a crocodile. Notice my name and skin: MonkeySlap, and my skin is a picture of a wild ass monkey.

Okay! You've got the skin and the ign. Now you want to get a swanky cape! This is the only step that requires money. The easiest way to get a cape is to donate $10.00 USD to OptiFine, and they will assign you a cape. Make sure that you pick out a cool banner for your swanky cape! For instance, my cape is a lightning bolt!
