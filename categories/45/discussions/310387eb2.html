This defies lots of religious matters and I know how well that goes on the Internet, so if this seems like it's turning into a flame-war, I'll try to resolve the issue or delete it ASAP.
Anyways, let's begin..
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The world is something we of through our consciousness. 
When we die, we have no consciousness. 
When we die, we will forget all the memories-- all the events-- the world has given us to make yourself you.
When we die, all those memories and events will become nothing, for they are part of our consciousness. 
Since all of us will die and loose our consciousness, we will have no idea the world existed or ever did.. which means that it never existed.
When we think about something, we are aware of it. If you are not aware of something, it does not exist.
Think about it this way: if you can not see, taste, smell, feel, or hear (I hope you can't hear this.. xD), or think about a cookie lying infront of you, you are not aware of it. 
Since you are not aware of it, you will not think about it and from what you understand, that cookie never existed and never will until you gain the senses to understand it. 
Now think of the Universe as a cookie. You've lost all memories of interacting with it and you never will be able to interact with it.
That means you can not think about the Universe or understand anything that ever has or ever will happen.
The Universe doesn't even exist.
You make actions based on what you feel and understand. 
Even though your human brain wants you to think life matters in order for motivation and for your species to thrive, it does not. 
People make beliefs in order to have motivation to get through your day and to comfort the fear of death. A lot of them want to think that they will never die, so they feel confident about the future, not terrified, even though they know that they will be consumed by the nothingness.
Once you die, everything will be and was always nothing.
Time is relative. From what we understand, it may seem different, but I assure you, once you're dying, you'll notice life went by like a flash.  
Right now, every second, the Universe is nothing.
No matter how much you disagree, nothing matters.
