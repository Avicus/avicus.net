Getting demoted - A good thing? by Tempus October 5, 2014 at 6:10 AM UTC

Hello everyone! I'm not sure why I got demoted, and I haven't been told - I don't expect to either!

Anyway, I realized that this thing - gaming, as a whole, and minecraft too, has gotten to big for me... I know, I just got a new computer, etc, etc.. And I'll still play, occasionally, or whenever my friends want to go on, but for me, gaming is too big of a problem - I play too much, and, really, I'm not even that amazing. So, I really feel that I need to take a break from gaming

As I said earlier, I'll be on occasionally, but not really much... I'm going to try to earn some more pocket money, and start moving on with my life.

As SWEET_Johny said, "None of this will matter in 2 years!", and I realized that. I'm going to try to be more active, with my family, friends, and really, try to do something with the remaining years of my childhood / teenagehood (is that a word?).

I think the reason I got into gaming, like, on this level was because I had seen all of these youtubers gaming, earning lots of money. I started to get into it, and, bam - I was addicted. I've tried to stay away, to stop, but I just keep getting sucked back in.. :/

I guess what I'm trying to say here is, well, goodbye, on a much smaller scale, and I'm trying to warn you of the dangers of becoming addicted. Don't get caught in the same trap I did.

See you all later, people of Avicus...

I'd really like to thank

Avumbaca, SWEET_Johny, AwarriorVenom, and the people who made my stay at Avic.us worth a million bucks. I am glad that I at least got a chance, to moderate and keep people in check, temporarily.

<3 -Hero
