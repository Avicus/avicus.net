Knockback Strategies. by BoldAndBrash March 22, 2015 at 10:03 PM UTC

Hello! I realize I'm a bad pvper, but I decided to make this anyways. Recently, I've been seeing lot's of people asking how to get such knockback. This is a topic that will list numerous strategies that will tell you how :D (Please comment some strategies you use!)

The AD Strategy
This strategy can give you just enough KB if used correctly. This is also very simple. You click as fast as you can while quickly tapping A and D. If the timing is correct, you will be able to get your opponent into a good combo.

The Sprint Reset Strategy 
This strategy gives you a great amount of KB. It's literally what it's called. You sprint and hit someone, then stop sprinting then sprint again, and so on. After you get your opponent into a 1-2 hit combo, if you start crouching fairly quickly, you'll reset your sprint multiple times, allowing you to get some awesome knockback.

The Hybrid Blockhit Strategy
This is a really powerful strategy. In this one, you hit someone normally 2x then do a quick blockhit. This strategy can give a great amount of KB and make a great combo, too.

The Head Twitch Strategy
In this strategy, you must only do it if you're aim is good. If your aim isn't good enough, you'll miss wildly. What you have to do do is swiftly twitch your head to send you opponent flying through the air.

Mathematical March 22, 2015 at 11:03 PM UTC

I guess I'll just paste a tutorial thingy I made a while ago :P-

Knockback-
Knockback is a really wacky thing in Minecraft. People have found ways to sort of control knockback. Basically, in Minecraft there is a thing called sprint knockback. Sprint knockback is where you deal more knockback than just standing by sprinting toward your opponent. There is a glitch however. When the sprint key was released in 1.7 you are now able to hold it and you can keep sprinting even after you hit your enemy, but only the first hit is sprint knockbacked, the hits after your first hit will deal the same knockback as if you were just standing. People have found this glitch and found out that after you hit someone and reset your sprint you can enhance your knockback. There are a couple ways to increase knockback: W repressing, hybrid blocking, and hybrid sneaking, sprint key spam, and hybrid "s"ing. To do a W repress you have to quickly release and repress w after you hit your enemy. To do a hybrid block/sneak/s you quickly right click/shift/press s after you hit your enemy. To sprint key spam you, well, spam control (or you can time it by pressing it quickly once after each hit). I prefer using the w repress because you can make up a bunch of cool strafes with it.

Also, there seems to be another glitch. If you change your motion right when you are hit the knockback you receive will be decreased to almost no knockback given. I also found a couple ways to do this: You can press w and d to circle strafe and then press a right when you are hit (You can also press w and a and press d when you are hit). You can do a special zig-zag strafe where you start by pressing w and d and then when you are hit you let go of d and press a instead, then all you have to do is to repeat the pattern. You can switch from pressing w to pressing s when you are hit. And there is another cool trick that Stimulating made, but he wanted to keep it a secret :P.

There is also a trick where you can enhance the knockback YOU take, its called S-Stepping. Its where you start critting your enemy (By holding the space bar) and then you press and hold the s key right when you get hit.
