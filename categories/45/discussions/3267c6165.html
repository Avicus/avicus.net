Introducing MapStack! by Crazy_ January 13, 2017 at 10:01 PM UTC

This is not associated with any official Avicus services.

Hi there. I'd like to introduce MapStack.

MapStack is a general hub (or repository) with the purpose of allowing the community to download maps to play with friends or for scrimmages - all in one central area. Here you're able to easily search for maps you want by name, or by game mode.

This is where you guys come in! If you've created a map and would like to have it added to MapStack, please post your map, along with a link to the original forum thread, and a download (if the thread didn't have one). Please do not post a map unless it is your own.

You can also create a pull-request and add your map yourself on our repository (please read CONTRIBUTING.md). This makes it much easier for us.

By default all maps will have a CC BY-SA licence. Alternatively, your map can have a CC BY-NC-SA license. Please specify if you'd rather this one when submitting maps.

If you have any friends who are map makers, share this forum topic with them. The more maps we get, the more maps you'll have available to download.

I'd like to thank  Matic0B and  mitchiii_ for making this all possible, and helping out so much with everything.
