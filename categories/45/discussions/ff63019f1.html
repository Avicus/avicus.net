How do I start a server

vladthegreat100 April 20, 2016 at 12:04 AM UTC

You could either run it off your own computer, which I highly do not recommend since it could be a security issue with your computer and internet if someone decides to hack the server. It could also run really slowly if your computer isn't powerful enough nor will it be 24/7 unless you leave your computer open 24/7 with no restarts.

Usually using a host is best. I've used in the past hostehorde and ggservers.

Hostehorde is good if you are looking for one server, no bungee.
If you want a bungee ggservers is pretty good.
