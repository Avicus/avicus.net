Princezz_Dubstep June 10, 2014 at 11:06 PM UTC

  I went through something like this... A guy lead me on (irl) and told me flattering words etc. Then next thing you know, he plays you and goes to date your "Best Friend." I was so surprised that he did this to me. I couldn't get over him. I felt so in love with him. Time and time went by, but still the same feelings. 
  The hardest part is to let go, because it was supposed to be that way. If it was ment to be the relationship should fall together not apart. 
   Think to yourself I can't continue my life this way. Find your happiness, your enjoyment, your pleasure. I'm sure she feels the same way about you too.
  Keep in contact with her, so you both can talk and enjoy eachothers company. Tell her how you won your sports game! Keep in contact with her, but as you do that make yourself happy. "Life's too short to be upset and sad, savor the little things." 
  Find things to keep you happy is what i'm trying to say, you'll get over her trust me. A lot of my friends date like people in other schools, but you know what they do? They hang out on the weekends, see movies, swim, and most importantly... Keep in contact. 
 You can do the same with her (as friends if you want). Don't leave the relationship right away and move on. It's impossible to do that unless it was a bad predicament. I wouldn't recommend to start dating with a new girl right away, because your ex still may have feelings for you, possibly you too.
    Remember this, keep your self busy, and stay in contact! Hope this helps!:D Talk to me on Skype if you need support.I would be more than happy to help!
