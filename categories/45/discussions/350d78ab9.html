Inventory Glitch Fix ¬ by XtraMajestical March 8, 2015 at 8:03 AM UTC

The is a glitch where sometimes you may spawn with half a inventory or no inventory. This is a quick post on how to fix it!
----------------------------------------------------{HOW TO FIX}----------------------------------------------------

Step 1:
Press 'E' on you keyboard and open inventory-


Step 2:
Put you mouse in one of the empty slots-
(My mouse isn't showing but you can see it is where the white slot is!)



Step 3:
Click the slot where your mouse is and then you inventory will 'update' and all your items will appear!



That is it a quick easy way of getting you inventory updated! This also works with a furnace i think not sure i haven't tested but come up to a furnace and click it i does the same job!
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hope it was helpful took about 15 mins to do and i believe the glitch doesn't appear much now but just in case :D
