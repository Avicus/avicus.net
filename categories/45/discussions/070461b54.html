R.I.P Terry Wogan by Myworld6 January 31, 2016 at 1:01 PM UTC

As you may have heard, Terry Wogan passed away today at 77 years old after a short battle with cancer. For those who don't know, Terry Wogan was one of the greatest broadcasters in British history. His career spanned 50 years, and in this time he appeared on shows such as Children in Need and Wake up To Wogan, to name but a few.

R.I.P Terry Wogan. You were one of the greatest broadcasters Britain has ever produced, and you will be sorely missed.
