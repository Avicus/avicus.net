Plug.DJ Event Friday! by MidnightBeats November 5, 2014 at 11:11 PM UTC

Hi everyone!
Recently we've had requests from players to bring back Plug.DJ...
So I am holding a PLUG.DJ session!

When is it?
The event will be held this Friday, at 6:30 PM EST or 3:30 PM PST.
There is no specific end time- the main point of this event is to get people to start using plug.dj again!

What do you do?
If this is your first time ever using plug.dj, you will need to sign up. Go to their page here:

https://plug.dj/dashboard

Follow the instructions to create a profile.
Keep in mind that once you create a username, you cannot change it in the future, so do not impersonate others.

I already have a profile, where do I go?
We have an official plug.dj hangout. If you're already logged into your profile, go to this link right here!

https://plug.dj/avicus


Once you "plug" into the hangout (haha), create a playlist of songs by searching up your favourites on YouTube, and then join the wait list! Once it's your turn to DJ, everyone in the hangout can dance, listen, and have a good time to your song!

What Types of Music is allowed?
We allow all types of music except for these kinds:
Inappropriate, violent, gory, or racist songs.
Get creative with what you share! Don't share the same type of music all the time! 

Hope to see you all there!

~Midnight

Special Thanks:
Thanks to Howseh for originally starting this!

Leave a comment in case you are participating, along with your plug.dj username; I just want to roughly estimate how many people ARE coming. Merci beaucoup <3
