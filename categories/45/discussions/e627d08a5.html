Welcome back to another PLUG.DJ session! 
This week's theme is DUBSTEP NIGHT! 
You are restricted to playing Dubstep and EDM music only!

I already have an account. What do I do?
If you didn't already know, we have an official plug.dj hangout! To go to it, press the link below:

https://plug.dj/avicus


From there on, you can start DJing with the rest of the community!

I've never used PLUG.DJ :(
If this is your first time ever using Plug.DJ, follow the tutorial below to create a profile.
Go to their page here:

https://plug.dj/dashboard

Follow the on-screen instructions to create a profile. The user interface should be friendly enough for you to easily make an account.

To begin DJ'ing, press the button on the bottom left. You must have a playlist of songs to begin DJ'ing, which you can compose using YouTube videos.

REMINDER: You will be asked to make a username. You CANNOT change your username in the future, so if you are caught impersonating someone, you will be banned. 

Which types of music is allowed?
For this week only Dubstep and EDM music is allowed.
The basic rules also come into play as follows;
No inappropriate, violent, gory, or sexual songs.

Hope to see you all there!
