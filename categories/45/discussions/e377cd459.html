Monstercat - Free Compilation Album by gobernment July 1, 2016 at 10:07 PM UTC



For those interested in electronic music, Monstercat is hosting their 5 year anniversary and is going to release a new track for free every day for two weeks. The artists behind it will be Pegboard Nerds, Rogue, Tristam, Ephixa, Aero Chord, and Slips & Slurs plus a lot of the other roster members involved releasing, however some of the tracklist is secret. To sign up for the tracks, you can sign up here.

For backgrounds of these albums, check out this album by 2ndKeeper (reddit user).
For the promotion video of this, check out this youtube video by Monstercat's channel.
