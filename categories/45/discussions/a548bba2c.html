We All Get The Same by Myv August 10, 2014 at 6:08 PM UTC

Just a quick thought, probably won't do much. Now, when I play any Avicus map, no matter what, there is always a vast array of different things you can get during the match - whether it be special bows and swords, or higher spec armour.Now this might make me sound like a noob, but as my computer has generally poor FPS ( I'm lucky to hit 40 ) then when the match starts I get terrible lag, and everyone has got all the special stuff before I even leave spawn, which annoys me, and then you get the guys on the opposite team with all the good stuff rushing us and killing loads of us because they have better stuff, which can be frustrating. What I'm suggesting is that we all start with the same stuff on every map ( Iron Armor, Iron Swords and materials etc. ) ( Except for maps like Zao ) and you cannot get anything else. If this happened, I think that it would be a fairer playing field and we would all have to try harder to reach monuments and kill people because we all have the same materials. Opinions on this?
