Egg Legends 2015 - Best CTH In History by Myv November 27, 2015 at 8:11 PM UTC

Well, I don't really know how to explain it, but I asked for people to join CTH and we had from what I can recall one of the best games in Avicus history.

It lasted for about 45 minutes, and it had a peak of 23 players.

It was so hard to get into the hill as everyone was using Demo!

It was too much fun, I earned 500 credits from it.

Green team emerged victorious by 60 points, after a very hard fought and long battle of egg grenades.

Thanks to everyone who was part of it, it was hilarious, I hope it happens again sometime!

I also think that this match helped build the CTH community as a whole - hopefully it has now encouraged players to join more often, me being a big fan of CTH for a long time.

Here's to more CTH!
Myv
