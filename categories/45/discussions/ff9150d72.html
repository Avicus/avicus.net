Wildcard Weekend is done, Ravens vs Patriots, Colts vs Broncos, Panthers vs Seahawks, Cowboys vs Packers are the matchups for the divisional rounds.

I'm rooting for Panthers, but they are pretty much screwed.
Here are my predictions:

Divisional Round
Patriots win against Ravens 24-20
Patriots don't blowout, since Ravens pull up a good match, but they still come up on top.
Colts win against Broncos 28-26
The Colts' offense is too strong for Broncos to hang on, Colts come up on top with a close match.
Seahawks win against Panthers 17-10
Although Panthers killed Cardinals, Cardinals' offense did nothing itself, not including Panthers' defense. Panthers have a strong defense, sadly Seahawks have a stronger one, this defensive match goes to the Seahawks.
Packers win against Cowboys 31-10
Cowboys no doubt played pretty sloppy today against Lions, their offense and defense were overall lacking. Cowboys are also not used to playing in the freezing tundra which is Green Bay. This is blowout.
Conference Championships
Colts win against Patriots 30-28
This is very indeed a close match. After seeing Luck's performance vs Broncs, Colts can easily win against Patriots
Packers win against Seahawks 27-24
Packers' offense is more than Seahawks can handle, Seahawks' defense is useless. Their offense can't pull up enough sadly.
Super Bowl
Colts win against Packers 28-21
Rodger's leg is really going to hurt the team. Colts are a very good team, good enough to hold Packers down. Colts win.
