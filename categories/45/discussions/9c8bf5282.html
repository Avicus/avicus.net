Retiring... :c by SirCyanide August 24, 2015 at 3:08 AM UTC

So basically like other people, I have school/homework/other stuff to worry about. This game was my favorite and no this is NOT a leaving post. I hate to leave this position, but a lot of stuff made me realize that this game should be the least of my concerns. I don't know if ill come back in the future, and yes I'm still deciding on whether this is just temporary (hopefully if it is ill get accepted back). I'm also done with staff getting shat on just because they had to do their job. Yes some of us make mistakes because we're f****** humans, and we don't deserve to have it pointed out. I'm also sick and tired of moderators getting no voice in some staff decisions, we try our best everyday to request for new donation perks, or new rules, and new tools that may help us do our jobs easier, but as always we never get an answer OR it gets shot down. I will not point any fingers but you know who you are. These are some people i would like to thank during my position as a moderator.
