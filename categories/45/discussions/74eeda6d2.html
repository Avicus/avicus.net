You are free to apply for staff when apps re-open regardless of how many infractions you have.

However, if the Senior Staff think that you would cause problems, you won't get accepted.

Also, there is no set page limit of when you are punished for excessive infractions, it depends on the severity of the punishments, the time interval between them, and how many of a certain type there is (multiple tempbans could be a perma, multiple kicks could be a tempban)
