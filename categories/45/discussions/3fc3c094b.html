Alright, before I start, please do not state that Leaving Posts are not Allowed, I believe that I stayed a long time for this, and I may never come back, please don’t lock nor argue.

 I have to leave, why? Due to exams this year. These are very important exams that could and will determine my entire life. It is really important. Avicus has been a wonderful journey, even if it has some ups and downs. My journey started off when me and Billy (April) were looking for a server to play the Walls, that is when we found a suitable server with less lag called McZone. Our Journey occurred then. But then I heard that Billy got Jr Mod and I wanted to be a Mod as well. That is when I met all of you brilliant people who have brilliant independent qualities of your own. I met so many people who I made friends with, you lot who are brilliant people. I did my Staff Application and I got accepted and I got into my interview. That was one of my terrifying moments in Avicus, Detty, Howsie and Jozzy seemed like Gods compared to me (These guys interviewed me), it seemed like they were a dark shadow overlooking over my shoulder. But after the interview, I realised that these guys are like me, they weren’t terrifying people judging your every move. I thank you, the community for an amazing journey across the amazing land of Avicus, once known as McZone.
