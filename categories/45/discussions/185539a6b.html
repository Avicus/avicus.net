AtditC January 8, 2017 at 2:01 PM UTC

You can have a credit system AND prestige system AND even the nebula shop at the same time!

Credits: You get credits for killing, mining monuments, picking up wool etc etc. With a rank, the amount of credits you get can be multiplied. With credits, you only can buy cosmetic stuff.

XP, Levels & Prestige: You get XY XP for killing, mining monuments, picking up wool etc etc. but it doesn't say +XY XP. With a rank, the amount of XP you get can't be multiplied. At level 50, you can do /prestige and get the little * after your name. Then, you will have to start from 0 XP again.
Also, each * badge from the shop in the lobby would have to be removed if prestiges come back.

Nebula Shop: For each kill, you get 1 point. For each monument you mine, you get, let's say, 8 points. Values can be different, of course. In the shop, each item is accessible for everyone, but some stuff can only be bought if you have 10 Levels, 25 Levels, Prestige Level 1, Prestige Level 2 etc.. Just don't make the items too overpowered.

Many players miss the Nebula Shop. And we don't really know why it was removed.
#BringBackPrestigesAndNebulaShop
