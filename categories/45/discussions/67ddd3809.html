BoldAndBrash March 16, 2015 at 11:03 AM UTC

This happened just yesterday...One of the best rushes ever!

I was on Senex 1. I was hiding under one iron pile and i killed a total of 5 people down there. I decided to get read to rush the monuments, so i went inside the iron. But then i thought, "they'll attack me!" Then, I crouch and began to mine iron blocks out of the supply to make it look like i was a teammate. Around 9 people walked past me or looked at me and did nothing. Suddenly, someone else started mining the iron, i yelled "NOO" at my PC. Then he suddenly smacked me in the face with his pickaxe. He was so confused he just started walking backwards and i hit him with my sword and he fell out of the world. Then, I had some time so i looked at the monument and saw a ked defending. I ran through the team's spawn and ran along the bridge the defender had made. The guy didn't see me enter and he was making walls, so I shot him out of the world in 1 shot. Then, I broke 1 monument. He came back 2 times and i killed him both times. After that, he just gave up. I bridged over to the second monument and mined it.

I'll give a cookie to whoever read all that and understood it.
