So as you may now there is a new service out in the US and other places called YouTube Red.

What YouTube Red is, it's a service which allows subscribers to watch YouTube videos without Advertissments but it will cost you $9.99 a month..

So you must be thinking what does this have to do with you, well unless your a youtuber with 500+ subscribers and have decent amount of views on your video and get paid a bit for it, it will mean that you won't get paid if people are watching your content through YouTube Red instead of regular old Youtube.

Now I'm not Saying this is a problem at all it will just be pretty much the death of popular youtubers who do this as a job because if a lot of people by the subscription of 9.99$ a month for YT Red they can't get paid so they won't make money so that means they would have to stop YouTube.
