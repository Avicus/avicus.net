All good things come to an end... by Myworld6 January 3, 2017 at 2:01 PM UTC

Hey everyone. Not trying to make this into a big deal, but my time here is over. As of now, I'm leaving Avicus after 3 years.
Before I go however, I want to say a personal "thank you" to some of the people on this server who made my 
time here truly enjoyable. I consider you to be my friends, and I can never thank you enough. 
To those who didn't particularly enjoy my tenure as a moderator; I'm sorry. I failed to live up to the expectations
of a moderator, and I'm truly sorry.

I know some (probably not that many) are wondering why I'm choosing to leave. Well, it's complicated. Over the year that I've been staff member, I've noticed how much the staff team has changed, and I've disagreed with a number of these decisions. That's not their fault. Gods know, they're doing what they believe to be right and they should be supported in that, but I no longer feel like I can continue to support something that I don't believe in.

It's not just the staff team, however. I'm not blind to the way that the community is shrinking and so many of the players I've considered to be friends have left, from Bang to Mels, I don't find Avicus as fun or as inviting anymore. Each day, when I log on to moderate, I sigh and log on reluctantly, and I don't want to moderate when I don't enjoy it. I won't be working at the best of my ability, and the community deserves someone better than I in both ability and attitude.

Because I know what people are going to say, this thread is not to garner attention. It's simply to thank
everyone who befriended me in my time here, considering that I won't see most of them due to different time-zones.

Lokigh: Ah, Lokigh. You were the one who first introduced me to Avicus, and I will always be grateful to you for that. You're one of my oldest friends, and I still remember coming home from school all those years ago, jumping on Skype with you and heading straight for Halycon. Good times. I know that we haven't really played together for a while, and that's a shame. Best of luck, mate. I know that if you ever apply for mod, you'll make a great one.

CatCutie_ and zero9178: My two favourite people on the server (sorry, Sayah!). We've only really started talking in the last few months, but in that time I've really gotten to know you both. I hate that people like Mufasa and Shahmi were mocking you both when neither of them knew you. You are without a shadow of a doubt two of the nicest, funniest and all-around most stand-up people I've ever met. It's a shame that we're from different countries. I'd love to meet you someday. xD

voidloop: I only really started talking to you in the last year or so, but in that time I've really gotten to know you.
You made my last few months really enjoyable and you cheered me up whenever I felt down, and I'm grateful for that. 
You're honestly one of the friendliest people I know, and good luck with whatever you decide to do. Like Lokigh, it's a shame that you never got a trial as junior mod, as I personally believe that you're one of the best people on this server.

Buster_U: I've known you for years, but we've only really started talking on Snapchat in the last few months. You're a really nice person and you're hilarious. You love Harry Potter too which is so cool, and you're one of my favourite non-staff members now, even if you don't really play Avicus anymore or care about it. Please reveal your face sometime. xDDD

resepignev: Despite your disturbing messages, you were always able to make me laugh, and since I became a staff member you really helped me to improve. You were always willing to listen whenever something bothered me, and I'm so grateful. You were the one who gave me tips and advice on moderating, as well as being the one who showed me the ropes when I started. Even up until my retirement, you were giving me advice on how to improve and you took everyone's thoughts into consideration. You are a perfect candidate for senior moderator, both due to your laid-back attitude but also your considerable contributions towards the staff team.

BoldAndBrash: My fellow Star Wars nerd. You're such a cool person, and like Rese you're a perfect candidate for senior moderator. You don't withold your opinion and you always tell someone the truth if you don't agree with what they're saying, a trait which I respect and admire.

EncryptedShoesHD, Camemes and Junr: Like Rese, you were always able to make me laugh even when I didn't feel like it. Thank you.

VenomousViper: My fellow Doctor Who fan. You, Rese, Cammy and Junr are all great mods. Good luck in the future.

Posighdun: My favourite admin. You scared me a bit at first, but now I really like you as an admin. You're active, friendly and mature, and you do your job well.

Spartemex: My favourite mod. Good luck in your trial! I know you'll be one of the best mods. =)

FrostedCupcakez and Midnightstorm: You both have great taste in music and are really nice. I'm sorry for how rude I was to you. You're amazing people.

Lasuz: My new favourite community member. You're hilarious, really friendly, and an all-around brilliant person. I wish you all the luck in the world, and I hope you get a trial as a junior. You're a great guy and you'd be an amazing mod.

_iMan and Myv: I still remember all of the games of Nexus and CTH that we used to play, and you always managed to cheer me up. Thanks for all of those hours of fun. 

Iron, PieZ, ViceTechnicolour, Acceqted, Legoche, JDKL and all of the other people I've managed to offend, embarrass or piss off in any other way: I'm sorry. I tried to rise to the maturity that the position of moderator required, and I failed. I've made you uncomfortable more times than I can count, and I should never have put you in that position. 

Seniors/Admins/Moderators/Other staff members: I know that I've become more of a liability than an asset in the last few months, and I'm truly sorry that I couldn't be the mod that the server deserved, but I wanted to thank you for the opportunity you gave me as a moderator. It's opened my eyes to what you have to put up with, and I'm grateful for the chance. It's been an honour. 

And that's really it. Sorry if I forgot anyone. There's such a long list of people that I don't think that I could name them all given a week.

I have no idea if I'll ever come back, but I doubt I'll ever forget my time here. I genuinely feel as if Avicus has helped
me in more ways than I can count, including my self-confidence. I'm not going to say goodbye, though.
As one of my favourite characters once said, "Never say goodbye. If you don't say goodbye then, you aren't really gone. You just aren't here right now".

I'll probably still be on the forums and the Community Slack, but I'll probably end up leaving them as well and just use Snapchat instead.

For now, this is Pingu, signing off for the last time.
