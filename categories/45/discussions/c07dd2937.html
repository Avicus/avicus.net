_Phyre September 6, 2014 at 4:09 AM UTC

How to use Plug.dj™

    1.
    Pick songs that, you enjoy listening to, make a playlist and place the song into the playlist if you created one. If not do so by clicking the Purple arrow at the bottom of the DJ table. Select "Create new playlist". Name it then hit create. One you have completed that. Go into the top search menu. To the right of it Select "SoundCloud, Or Youtube" (Youtube recommended). Once you have done that, Click the search bar to the right of it. Type in the song(s) name. When you hover over the song, Hit the "+" sign that comes up next to the trashcan on the right of the song title. Then hit "Add to (Playlist name)." Once you have done that Follow step 2:

    2.
    When you have completed step 1 please read the following: Click the purple arrow facing Downwards, to exit out of the Box. Make sure the song you want to play, is on the tab or "line" bellow the DJ table. Next to the purple arrow. Then once you have completed that, Follow with the following: Hit "Add to wait list", Above the Purple arrow; Next to the DJ table (To the left). Once you have hit that, Go to the top right corner of the website! Click the "wail list" section; Confirm you have joined the wait list. Follow step 3 for the next procedure:
 
    3.
    Please read this after reading "Step 2". Once you have confirmed you are in the Wait list. Enjoy the following music; Songs, that come along, And be patient to wait your turn. If the music is to loud On the top of the website, Next to the song title that the website is currently playing, To the right of it, Hover over "Volume" Then select the percentage of the volume you want to set. Once you have adjusted to your likings, enjoy the following music, then when your song comes up, Enjoy. Follow Step 4 for "Liking, Adding, And disliking":
 
    4. 
    Please read this Paragraph after reading "Step 3". To like a song, Simple hit the "Woot" To the Right of the DJ table; To add the current song to your playlist, Simply hit "Grab" Next to "Woot"; To dislike a song, Hit "Meh" next to "Grab". 

I hope this helped! To set up an account, Just use your email (Most recommended) :D
