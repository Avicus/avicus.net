Goodbye by _DipperPines April 3, 2016 at 10:04 PM UTC

Hey Guys!

So, I don't know how to start this off...

I first joined Avicus in 2013 when it was still McZone. I had just started Minecraft and I asked my cousin if he knew any good servers. He brought me here. (This was the first server I joined). Avicus was easily the best server I ever played on. I went through many good times and bad times on this server. However, recently they have been bad. I was recently promoted to Jr. Mod, but then demoted and that kind of started the decline. It was then that Avicus started fading away. I started coming on less and less because each time I got annoyed by the players on it and sometimes (although rarely) got frustrated at the staff (for things such as never being on TS [That is just my observation]). Basically I liked it better when it was a quite server with players I was used to. But enough with the badness, I DID say I had some good times. Now I would just like to point out some people:

resepignev: You are awesome, stay that way. But seriously, you are probably my best friend on Avicus.

Ryilo: I always had fun chatting with you on TS and skype. Not to mention you are amazing at  PVP.

Random_Guy: Thanks for always testing out my maps. You are really funny and make me laugh a lot.

_TinyBear: You are just awesome.

Spartamex: Even though it was short lived, thanks so much for the build server.

So I know I did not include everyone (you know who you are), these are just the people I knew the longest, or who have done the most for me. So anyhoo, I guess it is time for me to say goodbye! (That just got really bittersweet as I typed that thinking back on all the good times I have had). Wish you guys the best and good luck in life!

                                                                                                                                         Sincerely,
                                                                               Joseph (Running_Boy_12), (x_Redstone_), x_KyloRen_), and (DipperPines_12)
