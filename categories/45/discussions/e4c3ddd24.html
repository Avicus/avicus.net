"Don't be sad because you're saying Goodbye, You should be happy, Because you'll have memories with them that you'll cherish forever." Hey everybody. I had a lot of good things go on the game and such but I think its time for me to move on to other things. I have school and big tests and projects coming up. I also haven't getting a proper sleep time. Instead of 8 hours I would now usually get 6-7 which isn't good. Plus everyone I know stopped playing it. I'm not sure when I'm going to leave but, its soon, probably in 2-3 days. I want to thank everyone on Avicus for letting me play and have fun for the past 3 years. Cya
