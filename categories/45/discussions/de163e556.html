BmB August 30, 2015 at 6:08 PM UTC

Btw, it's a laptop, not a PC :p
Laptop, like me, make sure you are playing with the charger in. Click on the battery icon in the bottom right and check that you are set to "High Performance".

Turn VBO's on.
On 1.8 scroll fps lock all the way to 0 and it will alter settings.
Use OF
Check Java is updated.
Open task manager and close any unwanted tasks. 


The overnight thing is most likely your power plan or not playing with it charging.

What;s 1.8 scroll fps lock?
