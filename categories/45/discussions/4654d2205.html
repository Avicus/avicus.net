The relationship between Avicus and OCN by ayy July 4, 2014 at 8:07 PM UTC

I am really not sure wether it is appropriate to make this thread, so I am sorry if people view this as spam, or advertising. I feel if anyone is going to make this thread, it has to be me, as I am a regular on both Avicus and OCN.

Very recently, a small group of OCN mumble users got together and used hacked clients on Avicus, although in the past OCN hasn't dealt with it's players in Avicus related incidents, I can assure you all that they have all been punished. Although what these individuals did was stupid, reckless and thoughtless, I don't think anyone can deny that as a whole OCN has improved it's attitude towards Avicus. I know that many of you are very open minded about the OCN-Avicus situation, but those who aren't, be aware that things between servers are getting better.

I hope one day both servers can get along as they should be. Obviously I will not give out the names of the individuals who took part, it would be completely unnecessary. 

Thanks for reading this- Gibbo
