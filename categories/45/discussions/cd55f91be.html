awesome_apricot October 10, 2015 at 10:10 PM UTC

With world edit, make your region by:
- Using the axe
- Or typing //pos1 and //pos2 
both in the respective corners to make a cube.

Then, where the front-center of your completed side is, type //copy.
Then type //rotate 180
Move to the front center of the non existent side, and type //paste

Expect lag, but it will paste.
