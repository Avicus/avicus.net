When I jitter click, I keep a light grip on my mouse and keep my thumb off of it, but I put my palm on the heel of the mouse. The rubber grip on my mouse helps me keep it under control. It generally just helps me aim and jitter at the same time. However, practice is key to perfecting jitter clicking.

Gaming mice definitely helps for clicking imo, since their buttons have a better feel and are easier to click.
