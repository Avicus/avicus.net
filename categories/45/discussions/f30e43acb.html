Where did it all go so, so wrong?

AtditC October 14, 2017 at 8:10 AM UTC

There are a few big updates where things just started to get "crappy" & where Avicus went into the wrong direction.

The biggest one probably was the change from McZone > Avicus and with Avicus just not being advertised enough. Then a lot of smaller mistakes were made (still no advertising, block lag for a long time, no anti cheat, nexus & walls gone, nebula shop gone, prestige gone etc.) which all are reasons why people left the server. Then, Throwback was removed which was another stupid decision to make with a low player count resulting in another portion of people leaving the server.

I'm sure that the "remastering" of a lot of good old maps also made the players who aren't as good in Minecraft as others a bit more angry. They liked to just play on simple maps with simple layouts, but also better players who just enjoyed playing old maps more, compared to newer maps (where aesthetics & """"gameplay"""" play the biggest role) too (including me).

Basically if you want to fix Avicus, just reverse all the updates which came out until prestiges were added back and add Throwback back (and not just a Sunday Throwback version. At this point it's important to keep the players who play on the server, and not to try and get more players before you fix the problem of players who leave.)

tl;dr add Throwback as a whole server & get rid of most "remastered" maps (especially very old maps who were remastered)

thx
