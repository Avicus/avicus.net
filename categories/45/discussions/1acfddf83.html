Howsie September 19, 2015 at 3:09 PM UTC

Wouldn't cheap out on the power supply if I were you. Worst mistake in PC building apart from not slotting in the I/O shield. 

Use this http://outervision.com/power-supply-calculator
Calculate how much you need and one-up it by 100w. AND MOST IMPORTANTLY, NEVER BUY ANYTHING WITHOUT AT LEAST AN 80+ rating. Would recommend 80+ gold if you can, for the sake of the longevity of your PC.
