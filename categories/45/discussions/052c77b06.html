Bit of Storm wisdom here.  I know what I have to say doesnt really matter now that I no longer staff, but maybe somebody will listen :3                                                                                              
 You hear a lot that school is a waste of time, that you don't really need it.  You see all the people on the internet who dropped out or didn't go at all and are doing fine.  The problem is you don't see the people who don't succeed.  The ones who drop out and end up homeless and think "Man I should have stayed in school," because those people aren't deemed as "interesting."  Just know that school is something most people should go through.  The grand majority of people need school to get an income and own a house, and only the select few that don't seem to be the only ones who people look up to.  Just keep that in mind.
