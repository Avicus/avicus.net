Fanfics. Staff hate them so much that they forced us to compress them. Nonetheless, this is the fanfic of the new Avicus. Episode 1 has not been written yet. After the thread, I will start on that.

Preview: What happens when someone gets banned? We know. The guardians of the land, dubbed the moderators, hit them with the hammer. The banhammer. The banned have 2 options. Then can either give up and fall into the banspace, like most do, or they can fight into a cyber-purgatory. From there they are given a trial. If they are decided to be brought back, then they can re-unite with their bodies and roam the land once more. Under secret eyes.

But if they fail? They fall into the banspace, a world of unknown terrors. We have nightmares about it. Falling, in agony, eternal regret, it is the most scary place for us.

And what about bringing them back? Only the higher up can do that. But only if that wasn't true? What would happen?
