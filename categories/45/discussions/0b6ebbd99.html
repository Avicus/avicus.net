Crazy_ March 8, 2017 at 4:03 PM UTC

Okay, how do i learn XML?
The way I've kinda learned XML, or at least what each part does, is by looking at a map you know fairly well and looking at each part and what they do.

So take a look at Clay War's XML - https://maps.avicus.net/atlas/DTM/ClayWars/map.xml
Right at the top, you can see there's the map name, and map author. A little below that, the teams are defined, then the spawn points & observer spawn. And so on with the other components of the map.

https://docs.avicus.net/en/ of course is another great tool, you get to look at each module, and look at what extent you can use your XML to do things with. Some XMLs can be basic, and others can be 3000 lines worth of a beast.
