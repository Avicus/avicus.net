An Avicus Tale (Military style) by Zintenka September 4, 2014 at 4:09 AM UTC

"Zintenka, provide suppressive fire. I'm flanking them," A rough voice ordered.

There was a grunt of confirmation followed by a heaving grunt as someone vaulted over their cover and strafed the positon ahead with gunfire.

"Quesa, that doesn't seem like a wise move," A baritone voice explained, assuming it belongs to Zintenka. "Ah hell." 

Quesadillas sprinted around the distracted hostiles behind their cover as Zintenka was providing covering fire.

"Eat this you P2W bastards," Quesa shouted out.

The hostiles screamed as they were torn and ripped apart by bullets.

"Hostile units neutralized." 

Quesadillas kicked over their corpses and examined their weapons and gear.

"Hm, these guns are pretty high spec. And by the way their armor looks and their name tags, these guys were donors..." 

"As expected from P2W servers. Too bad they don't know how to use their weapons well." Zintenka responded. 

Suddenly, there was a rumbling noise that was shaking the ground. 

"What, is that?" 

"I don't know, but stay alert. Could be something bad..." Quesadillas said wearily.

"Gee, thanks for stating the obvious," Zintenka mocked. 

Quesadillas playfully punched his shoulder. 

"We better keep moving to the rally point, night fall is coming soon and I don't wanna be here when the Zombies come. Damn things grew smarter and faster..." 

They hiked for several miles. Occasionally encountering enemy forces.

                         xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

"Hey Quesa, I've been hearing that rumbling noise for a while now. Any guesses what it could be?" Zintenka asked.

"Hmm. I don't know. Anything can happen now that update 2.0 came out. Guns, giant withers, a gunship, a-"

BOOM

Both were knocked several meters away by the explosion that occured. 

They looked up and stared in abstract horror.

"A-a T-t-tank! RUN!"

They got up and started running away from the menacing monstrosity.

"Since when did the update mention tanks!?" Zintenka yelled angrily all the while spraying bullets at the tank. 

To his chagrin, the bullets only pinged off the armor.

"Since Mojang decided to turn Minecraft into something else! An open world, military, faction FPS and TPS!" Quesadillas yelled back.

The tank continued to pursue. 

Zombies joined the chase and were sprinting and shooting at both parties all the while frothing from their rotten mouths. 

The tank took its attention away from them and was tearing apart the Zombies. 

"Quickly! Run while its distracted!" Zintenka yelled. 

"Way ahead of ya!"

They ran and hid. Hearing the tank's treads a very close distance away.

They waited and stared at the tank with bated breaths.

The tank slowly turned its main cannon at their cover. "X-ray mode activated." A monotonous mechanical voice rang out.

"Oh you son of a bi-"

Quesadillas' voice was cut off as an explosion rocked the whole tank. 

When the smoke cleared, the tank was still standing but it was motionless.

Slowly, they crawled out of their cover.

A silhouette walked out from the tree line.

It revealed to be Evocal carrying a weird looking gun.

"Airburst. Air pressure kills the crew inside." He explained, looking at our gawking expressions.

After a moment of them exchanging pleasantries, they crawled into the tank.

"Now come on, the rally point awaits us."
