Razer Taipan Review! Not in full detail by Da_Baus October 10, 2014 at 5:10 AM UTC

So dismorning i went out to the shops and bought the Razer Taipan for $94 on special :D It was a bit pricey so if you after a budget one this wont be your choice (Maybe a logitech G series mouse)

But after playing with it for a few hours i found these things 

The clicks are very east and smooth
L E Ds You dont need them but they look cool. XD
The side buttons are very responsive I set mine to a optifine zoom
You can go from 400 to 8400 DPI and the side grips are good
Its light weight if thats what your looking for but it has no adjustable weight.

Overall its a very good mouse
Sorry for a lack of detail ive only had it for a few hours
