Hello, all. I'm just trying to get to know you all better, so I thought I might ask: what are your biggest interests? They can be anything, as long as they aren't inappropriate and/or irrelevant.

My interests include:

  • Electricity. Using a kit that I've had since I was 5 or 6 years old, I created (from scratch) an OR gate, an AND gate, a NOR gate, a NAND gate, an XOR gate, and an XNOR gate using only batteries, wires, two switches (of course), a resistor, and a diode.

  • Super Smash Bros, especially the Wii U version. I don't play it competitively, but I consider myself to be OK at it. (Gamer ftw!)
  • Crew. I row, and I'm especially happy to be rowing because I have a race tomorrow and I just did a 2k in 7 minutes 30.4 seconds (And I just got promoted to 1st boat! :D).
  • The Avicus network. I got to admit, I wasn't sure about this when I first came to this server, but once I got settled in, I knew there was no turning back. Keep up the good work, guys!
Even though I know I have more to talk about than this, do we happen to have any interests in common based on the above information?
