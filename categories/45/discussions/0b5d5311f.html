Suicide; people just generally want the pain to stop. There is a way out but attempting to harm yourself and lead your way out of the world by killing yourself is not the proper way out of the problem. You go with what can make you happy, you can try to block out the world, you can get treatment. Anything to save yourself and you're not only hurting yourself, you're hurting others. There is help out there and there is no sense in killing yourself as it's a permanent solution to a potentially temporary problem. It is unfortunate how life can be sometimes, how the world can seem like a cruel place. But you have to hold on and do your best to get by.

I'm sure nobody wants you maimed or you yourself; it's a mask that shades yourself out from people who bully or treat you wrong. You need to take off that mask and try to grow stronger. I am not one with wise words as I have gone through these problems myself, but please don't leave by hurting yourself. Whatever intention this thread is generally or for you directly, you have a role in something. You're appreciated and loved. While your family may not be a family, you can stay in our home. Avicus is the largest roof and from this perspective, we can do our best to shield you away from the dangers. Video games may not be a logical way to escape safely, but whatever truly makes you smile, go with it. We are a family, and I learned that myself. You just need to avoid the hazards.

Family, if it's that big of an issue that persists like that, I recommend if you attend school talk to a guidance counselor or a staff member there and I'm sure they'll do their best to support you. Keep your chin up, your shoulders high, and stay strong. You'll find a hand to pull you up.
