Tips From Kizzy *New tips added* by Itsameekizzii November 25, 2015 at 4:11 PM UTC

Suspicious Chests:
When you are defending the monument and you see a chest in an odd place (in the wall, under the obsidian) there could be someone behind/under it, remove it quickly, this person is mining the monument from behind/under it.


Crafting tables:
Use crafting tables as a defense mechanism, they will stop the player from block climbing up to the obsidian in a hurry and will slow them down, possibly giving your teammates a chance to catch him/her or use them to stop block skipping/running (what ever you call it). Use them around your monument.

Cob webs:
If there's no water but there is a cobweb(s) around use them as an advantage to break your fall and not take damage.

Golden Apples(Gapples):
Eat a golden apple before combat it gives you an upper hand at surviving a fight, think of it as a blanket that just came out of the dryer, you feel safe and warm, eat it.

Count down CTH(Control The Hill):  When the count down shows up get to the "hill" ASAP, your team will get points for staying on the hill/getting there in time, fighting really isn't the top priority in this game, it's getting to the hill and being there in time for the count down to reach 0.

Bridging for beginners(ignore if you are good at bridging):  Bridge diagonally to go faster  Do not bridge straight on, it will slow you down and your teammates are counting on you, tappin their toes looking at their watches, GO GO! Bridge diagonally, please!

Axes:  Axes come in great handy, a lot of people are  covering themselves in wood and it is hard to get them away from mon when you do not have an axe to clean up so first thing to do in game is make armor and make axes!!
 
Thank you for reading, if you want more tips just let me know!
