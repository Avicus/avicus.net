Hello everyone.
As most of you know, I have a YouTube channel with just over 325 subscribers.
However, I'm not sure if I should continue it, or if I should just leave it alone, and not spend so much time on it.
I've been thinking about this for a while, and I'm really not sure what to do anymore.
It just seems like I'm not really having as much fun recording videos as I used to a couple of months ago.

Really, what I need is just some type of series that I can do, and more people to record with who don't just sit there and play the game while I record and do 90% of the commentary. I've probably recorded over 20 videos where that has been the case, and I haven't uploaded them because they were probably the most boring videos I've ever watched in my life.

Also, YouTube is becoming more of a problem for some things in Minecraft, such as staff applications on several servers. When I submit an application, the person who is reading it always checks my YouTube, to see what type of content I provide, and I always "seem" to be acting "immature".
I haven't been a staff member on any"big" servers (biggest server had 100 players on it max), but I want to become a staff member on a bigger server. YouTube is usually the reason for why I get denied, and I don't want that to affect my chances anymore. If I could somehow fix this, I might still consider making "mature" videos.

So I ask of you, please give me your opinion on this, because it means a lot to me.

Also, if you do want to make a video with me, you can send me a request on YouTube or on this thread, but please don't be boring during the video, as I will not upload it.

Thanks for understanding guys, and I really look forward to your feedback.

-Porky
