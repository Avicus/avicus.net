Thanks for everything. by AlvaroMorata September 8, 2016 at 7:09 PM UTC

Alright, I think it's time to officially say goodbye. It's been a wonderful run, but I've been trying to enjoy the game for the past 3 months and the only thing that was stopping me from leaving is losing all my skill. I don't want to seem cocky, but I feel like I'm pretty good with a sword, and leaving would make me feel like I wasted all my efforts into getting better with a sword. It's embarrassing that I still play this shitty block game, however, I'll never forget the memories I had on Avicus, especially Moderating this server for 6 months. I really hope Avicus and Atlas reaches its full potential and all the luck going forward. Lastly, I just want to mention that all my accounts have been sold and this is the only account that I still have and will keep. 

Thank you for everything.
