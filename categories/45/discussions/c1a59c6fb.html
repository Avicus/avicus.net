Moving on... :) by faht December 10, 2016 at 9:12 PM UTC

If you can't be bothered reading all of this, I'm basically quitting so ya. 

I found this server about 3 years ago after getting permanently banned on Project Ares (ocn) for combat logging. I searched for a Destroy The Monument server since that was basically the only gamemode I enjoyed playing at that time. Luckily I found this server called Avicus. I think I got into the community about 2 years ago, when I was using the account "adrianosborne". Back then, the server had hundreds of people online, and it was actually enjoyable. After quite a while, I got into the team "Storm Chasers II" (ily marius), and later on Bad Company. Bad Company was problably the best thing for me on this server. But later, BC disbanded, and some of my best friends left the server, and it became boring.

I was promoted to junior moderator after tryharding for like 5 months. Soon after that, I got promoted to Mod, Official, and also Map Developer. Having 3 ranks at the same time was stressing for me. I spent basically all my spare time on this server. I am actually happy that I joined the staff team, especially when Jozz and Mango were admins. 

After about 6 months as a moderator, Sessp promoted me to Senior and then, everything got worse and worse. Avicus started to get boring, since I did not have alot of time actually playing. 

After the Atlas Update, I barely even played public anymore. For me, the server was basically dead. The only thing that made me stay was the tournaments. But as I said, after Atlas, there was no point in playing anymore. It just felt like a waste of time.

I could write alot more, but I just want to thank a few people:

I got to know you better when I joined the staff team and it actually was a pleasure talking with you. You later joined Grim on OCN and we started to talk less. Thank you for being a good friend Budah :)
