PSA: "lag" by scolton March 28, 2015 at 12:03 AM UTC

I know that this is going to do nothing but annoy some people but here we go:

Saying "lag" into the chat, or anything else for that matter, actually increases the amount of lag that you'll experience.  Don't believe me?

When you send a message to the server, what happens is it has to a) receive your message, b) get a list of everyone on the server, and c) send it to each and every one of them.  And then you want it to also process every step that you and every other player makes, while sending a message to everyone else on the server about how much you are annoyed with it?  Why are you wasting your time complaining?  You're making it worse!
