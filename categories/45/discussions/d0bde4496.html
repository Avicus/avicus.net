Mathematical June 1, 2014 at 3:06 AM UTC

From my profile, I have a lot more but these seem to be most useful:
-Strafe (make it hard for them to see you and they can't hit you) 
-Combo (click as fast as you can, faster than your opponet so they can't hit you, somtimes they say you have reach hacks so I don't recommend it , jk xD I don't care) 
-Critical hits (when you fall from something like a jump you do more damage) 
-Sprint (spam space under a 2 block high ceiling so you can go faster and do more critical damage, also sprint to knock players back more) 
-Lower advantage (go under your opponet so they can't reach you but you can reach them) 
-Ladder (When 7 or more blocks away from your opponet, climb a ladder to regenerate health, eat, drink potions and other stuff if low on health, once you reach the top and someone followed you, kill him xD) 
-Bow first (if you have a bow or a different projectile, shoot him/her first to hurt them more and easier to combo since it stops their sprint) 
-Water (reccommended if no ladder, low on health, regenerate or eat in the water, make sure you have a bow)
