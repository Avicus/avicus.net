Bye bye by Nutbustulon June 11, 2014 at 10:06 PM UTC

I was never an extremely popular member I the community, but I have not been on the forums or ingame much recently. I've come to realize something. We're wasting precious time, all of us. Watching a screen and doing the same things over and over. Over and over. All for some goals on that same screen that never truly fills us. It's us avoiding ourselves. Avoiding our futures. Never gaining anything, just repeating until we're known for repeating well. Some may argue that you gain skill in the game. On the screen. That screen means nothing.

I've come to see there is more. There is much more to experience, much more to be had from life. Instead of focusing on being better at being a mindless zombie, you could better yourself. Be a better friend, a better person. Make a craft your own. My goals are to focus on music and thearter while learning handiwork from my father. These will help me through life, as a more helpful, while deeper and more mistifying, as I gather lessons. I'm not being forced to do this by anyone, my grades are fine, above average even. I, on my own accord, are seeking out more. Everyone has a talent to hone, wether they know it or not. Find yours, become great, and never get sucked back into the ever repeating abyss that is a video game.

In addition, I do not wish to be interviewed for Jr. Mod any longer. I don't want to be floating through life anymore, and Jr. Mod would be counterproductive. While you will still see me around to take a load off sometimes, I'll just be another face. I thank you for your time..
