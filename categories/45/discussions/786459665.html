A thank you to the community <3 by AstroPizza October 25, 2015 at 8:10 PM UTC

Well where to start , I've never had the chance to tell you guys how much i actually appreciate this community. To think i've nearly been here 3 years , on some block game. I would have left about a year ago if it wasn't for you guys.  All i every try to be is positive/happy because it's all i want others to be. The community   helps with so many personal problems without realizing , and i just want to tell you guys , thank you. We may not have 1000 players anymore but as long as this community is here everyday to cheer me and others up , i don't care. When you stop and think , players from all around the world all sitting down together playing a game , it really does feel special. Sure i might not be who you want me to be , but you guys have always been what i wanted you to be. I can't not imagine  being part of this community now. One day i'll look back at this blocky game called 'Minecraft' and remember that small server  'Avicus' and of course , you guys. I would make a massive paragraph about each and every one of you but you've all been part of this community to me in one way or another. I hope you guys remember  , every single one of you are amazing , never forget that.

Ok cringe over.
Love you guys , I'll be here until the end (Even if that means just posting on the forums). I'm sure this community won't die anytime soon though , would take a lot for all of  this just to vanish. :)

Before anyone even asks , no i'm not leaving xD 
I just simply feel like I've never managed to try put it in to words how much this community means to me.
