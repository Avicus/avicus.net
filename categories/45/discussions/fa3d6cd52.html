Badlion community is better and the Anti-cheat is 95% accurate, plus all the separate ladders/gamemodes I like, but I disllike the fact that if you play unranked that it's either just not a challenge, or people who play wayyyyyy over-aggro. 

If you play ranked, everyone ultra-tryhards and you tend to get matched with people WAYYYY out of your elo, and even if you feel like tryharding the day away, only 10 matches per-day. A good example of ridiculous matching is me going into ranked Kohi ladder on BL and getting matched with StanMPL 3 times in a row. (1400 vs 2000 elo?) not to mention getting matched with people at 1700-1800 constantly, even though I never play ranked and am sitting at just under 1400 because I never play it anymore, obviously due to the reasons previously stated (not to mention some of the maps are trash on BL). 

Kohi's community is worse, but I just ignore the chat. Ranked Isn't as stupid in matchmaking as BL and if I choose unranked I have more of a chance of getting a somewhat challenging fight on P2. 

P1 I like, as I can use it for warm-ups and if I just want stomp matches/practicing new techniques and jitterclicking. 

P2 Is also a great option for people with PCs that don't get great fps, as the maps aren't laggy, with the exception of one or two. P1 has more laggy maps than P2. 

Kohi's maps are also better, but the anti-cheat is awful. 

For me, I enjoy playing on Kohi more.

Edit: I also like the teamfight system on Kohi more than BLs.  

Frustration-fueled Side note: I could be sitting around 1600, maybe even 1700 elo if the matchmaking on BL wasn't so stupid and If I actually went on BL. This is why I hate the matchmaking.
