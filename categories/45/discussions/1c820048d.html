Explanation (copied from Reddit)

---------------------------------------------------

Fnatic are now: Olof, dennis, wenton, Lekr0, twist  
Godsent are now: Pronax, znajder, JW, flusha, krimz

This Swedish shuffle one of the biggest roster shuffles ever in CS:GO. It is significant in many ways. Fnatic have been a dominating team for quite a while now and they were unstoppable in the past.

This is the end of an incredible era as 3 of their players, JW, Flusha and krimz have left fnatic and joined Godsent, most likely making Godsent a legend team (which means they get an automatic entry to the next CS:GO Major tournament). Pronax of Godsent was also a part of a dominating Fnatic team in the past and now is rejoined by JW, flusha and krimz.

Another important thing to note is that Godsent are playing Virtus Pro right now to decide which team joins the ESL Pro League Season 4.
