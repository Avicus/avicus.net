joelous January 26, 2017 at 3:01 AM UTC

Hi, 
We appreciate your feedback and suggestions, and I will try my best to give you an honest response to each. 

Blood effect. This was removed silently, and no one knows why, not even us. It will be awesome to see it return as it did actually help us identify auto clickers with accuracy. I will see what a developer has to say about this, because majority of the time, there is reason with everything they do.

Vanish. This has actually been in discussion for a while with the staff members, and there is a fairly positive response internally. I'll see what the developers say about this... I don't really see any issue with it. If it were to be abused we can easily monitored usage and take action on those who misuse it.

Stats. Is /match not working anymore? I could have sworn that showed statistics per game. 

Donor privileges. This is actually a feature that was implemented to deter and prevent team stacking. We do not want donators allnstackingnon one team, to an extent where it is unfair. For example, one team may be filled with 20 players, most of them donators while the other team only has 10 or less. It has been an issue, and I do not believe we need to revert this change. However it is very possible to change the limit of players allowed per map by changing a configuration in the XML. 

More moderators. We are reluctant to recruit moderators OFTEN, because of the possible surplus of staff compared to the players we get per day. People need to understand that moderating is not a job, it is volunteer work, so that means we cannot force people to do their job at specific times because they may be busy in real life. We understand your frustrations, and we are discussion it internally: what can we do to accomodate timezones more effectively? I believe the moderation team is doing a fantastic job staying dedicated and loyal to the server, trying to come online whenever possible. Wherever you go, players who cause trouble to others are always present, and it is very difficult to catch every single one of them. That is why we require your assistance in reporting them online.

Dev_revs. I do not want to comment on this as I do not have control over his status, however it would be good to see him return as he is one of the most dedicated players on Avicus, and as far as I am concerned he did a very good job covering the tournaments as a streamer.

Prestiges. They are not returning. I got confirmation by Keenan months ago about this, and he said that badges and Prestiges would conflict. Don't know if he has changed his mind about this, but over the few years we have switched between Prestiges and badges.
