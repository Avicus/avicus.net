AbusiveUser August 14, 2016 at 7:08 PM UTC

Did you buy your account in the official Minecraft website or you bought it in pages like "Minecraft Full Access for 1 dollar"

If you bought your account from the official website, you should have the "Transaction ID" in your email, if you have that code you must send a message to support with that code so you will get your account back.

If you bought your account via the second way so you lost your account.
