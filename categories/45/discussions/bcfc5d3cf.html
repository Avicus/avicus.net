The Food Game - Forum Game by KeewiDood March 6, 2017 at 12:03 PM UTC

Recently Crazy made a forum game with the purpose of naming a place, and then the next person to reply would say another place that starts with the last letter of the previous place. I took some inspiration from Crazy and made another game, but without talking about places this one is gonna be with different food of sorts. If I say a food, the last letter in that food, and then that happens. 

On the last forum game that crazy made he said that you couldn't have one page starting with only A's in this game you can't have a page with food sorts that starts with the same letter. 

This is how it's kind of gonna work:

Me: Potato 

Someone else: Onion

And that's how it goes, like a repetitive game.
