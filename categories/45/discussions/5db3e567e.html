Goodbye (No Longer Rick Roll) by Stimulating April 2, 2015 at 2:04 AM UTC

So after making a temp leaving thread, I was planning to come back and play here more after the update, which was wonderfully done, apart from the server being full 1.8. 

Ok, 1.8 is garb for PvP and IMO it makes zero sense for a server based around PvP to be 100% 1.8, the update many called "The Death of PvP". As a PvPer, I find this especially frustrating, as usually when you don't have speed, blockhitting is usually a core component of PvP, with that being removed, it takes away a big aspect of PvP. Combos are awful in 1.8, I find it frustrating not being able to combo-lock people that may not be at a certain level of PvP, because I feel when there's a certain skill differential, you should get some benefit out if it. You get next to nothing out of having a skill advantage, basically. Also how PvP in 1.8 is 100% ping/latency and cps pisses me off, and it especially screws up players that are not located in North America. 
Also, the lag is real.

Now that I'm done with my mini-rant... 

Farewell all my friends, and I hope you stay well and have fun, but I really can't play on 1.8...I just despise it, regardless of the quality of the update and I can't play on a server on such a version.  

Good luck everyone.... 


I just hope there's realization that a lot of regulars are leaving because of this and there's a decent amount of opposition against this...and maybe hold off on the 1.8 change. It's not a matter of the players not wanting change (for the most part), It's about the quality and gameplay that comes with 1.8 that people dislike. I'll try the update a bit before 100% leaving though.  

I will be on TS and on the Forums, so you know.

I will still play scrims and most likely the tourney though.

I wish you all well, and I apologise for any behaviour that may have been...a bit much.

I guess this is me really saying goodbye. Signing out,

-Stimulating/Jackson
