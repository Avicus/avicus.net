[Rant] Apple. by MidnightBeats September 17, 2014 at 5:09 PM UTC

So this is just a little rant i'm doing. Now before I begin this rant, let me just clarify that I DO NOT HATE APPLE'S PRODUCTS! Nothing is perfect (If you have't realized that, go home), and I'm simply stating the bad things I've seen over the time that I've been using their products. I use Apple Product's myself and I enjoy using them. These are just the problems I've seen.

If you don't like my rant, that's fine. I won't get butthurt and I take constructive criticism well. EDIT: This has been a long time since I've done one of my rants so I'm going all out here.

So first off...
THEIR iPHONE CHARGERS.

Jesus. Don't even get me started. Their chargers suck, and they are such a pain in the ass. I've had four of them break over the course of 3 months. People always tell me "treat your chargers better and they won't break." Um no. Their chargers just naturally break. I plugged one into my brother's iPad and I pulled it out. It snapped. And I wasn't even pulling hard. Of course, I've had to go replace them, which is such a pain. And because it's APPLE, their chargers are like $30. I've had to spend over $100 on CHARGERS alone. Just recently, another of my chargers broke and I'm not about to go pull $30 out of my wallet.

The Price.
Apple, we all know you use some quality material to make your phones and tablets but let's be honest. The price is too much. Who the hell wants to pay $400 for an iPad 2? And $600 for the iPad Air? Hell no. And we know that your products are good, but I've seen Android make some better shit than you have for a cheaper price.  


Seeing little kids with it.
Yes, I've seen one too many kids with an iPhone 5s. And then I see their parents with like a flip phone. I take a little look at the kid, and the kid glares at me or gives me a smart-ass look, knowing that he has a better phone than me. My only thought. Go shit yourself.Why the hell does a little kid have the NEWEST phone (this was before the iPhone 6)? This kid already looks like a spoiled brat and you are giving him a device that he SURE as HELL doesn't NEED! People tell me, "There's a reason he might need a phone." Duh, Probably. But why the hell would you give him an iPhone 5s? Just get him a) a Prepaid phone b) Home Phone and c) (If you're really going to give him an iPhone...) iPhone 4s and below.


Finally.
iPhone 6.

My God, just build the iPod touch 6. So currently I use an iPod 5 (because I feel no need to have a phone atm. No I'm not crazy), and after looking at the iPhone 6 design, I just shook my head and said "what's the difference?" Actually, the iPod 5 and iPhone 6 are so similar. The only major difference? Larger screen and the ability to use data and call. What I don't like about the iPhone 6? CAMERA. 
So yes, I like to take pictures, whether it be of scenery or just a still picture, I really like to just capture a photo. But the new camera on the iPhone 6? No. The thing I hated about the iPod touch 5 is the camera that comes out a little (because the iPod touch 5 is so thin), and now they had to pass that idea on to iPhone 6. Forget the new iPhone- If i get a chance to get one, I'll just stick with the 5S.

So I know this was a short rant, I'll add on to it later if you have any suggestions. 

If you agree, just say so. If you don't, then OK! 
Just DON'T START A FLAME WAR!

Is there really a reason to start a fight over a product?
No.

So thanks and in off topic,
Good luck everyone in their Mod Applications (To those who are applying).

Ok, I'm not trying to be rude, but I wanna counter your arguments. I'm not trying to start a flame war at all.

Chargers
"Their chargers just naturally break." They really don't, if you take good care of them. I bought an iPhone 4S when it came out, in November 2011. The charger is still good as new. And also, if you do actually break one, you can just buy a more affordable one on Amazon or something; I've gotten like a 5-pack of chargers for $10, and they work fine.

Price
"Who the hell wants to pay $400 for an iPad 2? And $600 for the iPad Air?" iPad 2 is no longer sold by Apple. The iPad Air is $500, only $50 more than the Surface RT, and $50 less than the Galaxy Tab 10.5. You have to admit, Apple products are higher quality. Plastic vs Aluminum. Gorilla Glass 3 vs ion-strengthened glass.

Seeing little kids with it.
I completely agree with you on this one. It really pisses me off when I have to work like 16 hours each weekend to earn money, and little kids get a free phone from their parents.

iPhone 6
Honestly, the iPhone 6 is a lot different from the iPod Touch 5th generation. The iPhone 6 has a much larger screen, as already mentioned, but also has a handful of new features, including a NFC chip, a 401 ppi display, Touch ID, and a motion coprocessor, which makes performance a lot better. I do have to agree, though, that most new features it has, Android has already been using for years. However, Android has an NFC chip, but Google never made agreements with credit card companies to implement a feature like ApplePay.
