Like zero said, I recommend block hitting only when someone approaches you, and then begin strafing and/or using any other technique. Also, use this in tunnels and enclosed spaces where you cannot strafe. Block hitting excessively only slows you down and is less beneficial.

If used correctly, block hitting is actually extremely OP and I highly recommend you try out new things with it.

As to its "nerfing" in 1.8, its animation was changed slightly and I do think that not all hits are registered simply due to the blocking revamp, but overall it is not terribly changed and you can still pretty much block hit.
But 1.8 pales in comparison to 1.7 in terms of PvP experience and stability, so I suggest just sticking to 1.7 anyhow unless you really want 1.8.
