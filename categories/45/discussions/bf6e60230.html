Mathematical July 22, 2015 at 12:07 AM UTC

I think the equations to check scores are:

Nebula:
75(Monuments)+1.5(Kills)+(-Deaths)=DTM Score
75(Wools)+1.5(Kills)+(-Deaths)=CTW Score
(Kills)+(-Deaths)=TDM Score

The Walls:
100(Wins)+1.5(Kills)+(-Deaths)=Walls Score

Nexus:
50(Amount of 25 Killstreaks)+15(Amount of 10 Killstreaks)+1.5(Kills)+(-Deaths)=Nexus Score

Control the Hill:
50(Controls)+1.5(Kills)+(-Deaths)=CTH Score

Hardcore Games:
100(Wins)+1.5(Kills)+(-Deaths)=HG Score


Note: Scores are rounded to the nearest integer.
