Goodbye the whole of Avicus! by Riilo August 5, 2016 at 10:08 PM UTC

Well, I want to say that I know I have made a lot of leaving posts but this one has a somewhat decent reason for it. |

I'm joined Avicus Febuary 2014 because my irl friend (cadeknuth21) told me about it. I played with a lot of my in real like friends and we loved to play walls. I always remember that I thought nebula was terrible. Basically, I started to love the server and I got more walls kits. That's when I wanted to buy Gold rank. I bought gold rank and I was pretty happy. Then all of my irl friends stopped playing Minecraft in general, and it was just me playing it. I had no one to talk to on skype, because I didn't want to talk to "strangers". I finally overcome that fear and added StormzInvader and avumbaca (Avy) on skype, and I talked to them. Avumbuca told me about TeamSpeak, so I joined it and met some new people. After getting involved on that side of the community, I joined the forums, and I was pretty much a forum spammer, but I didn't do it intentionally. I donated to emerald later that year and got banned on the forums. I waited it out and then got appealed, and realised I had been invited to a team. I joined the team (CrookArmy), and it was a walls team. I started enjoying nebula in 2015 aswell. Crookarmy was my first ever team and I loved to muck around in their fun scrims. That later disbanded so I made a new team named Xenarch. We never actually won a match but we were really close friends. Glesmon and I got into a fight and everyone started to leave breaking our friendship up. For like 2 months being disbanded I created a new one and named it Xenarch, and Cake later joined. This means cake and I are the only Xenarch players left. I also donated to diamond. Then 2016 came and there wasn't anything special about that.

Thank-Yous:

Xenarch - Thank you everyone for being amazing teammates

Nathy - Thank you for making me get into CS:GO and actually being a good moderator

The actual decent staff - Thanks for being decent staff

Ryzinq - One of my most closest friends on the server thanks

Dawson - Thanks dawson for being a really good friend

I have probably missed a lot of people and I'm sorry for that.

For the main reason on why I am leaving, it is just that I don't have ANY interest in minecraft anymore. It is getting so boring, and I just prefer other games. I have really enjpyed avicus and Im sorry for being toxic on the forums lately.

Goodbye avicus.
