Why ask for stuff we already have?! by Thomas_Shoes January 26, 2015 at 11:01 PM UTC

Lately I have noticed a lot of game modes suggestions especially with HuNger/Survival games.

This bothers me. 

Avicus has three great gamemodes; Avicade, Control the Hill, and Elimination*), almost always left empty.

Avicade: Full of fun mini-games, like red alert and minotaur.  Both very popular fun minigames, with very well built and thought out maps.

Control The Hill:  Classic, different kits, different items, perks, a very fun game

Elimination*:  Get a certain amount of time to mine for material, and then fight, originial and classic.
Elimination is pretty much a unique, modified version of Survival/Hunger games. 

Note:  I am not saying to stop suggesting mini-games for the server, but please explore all of Avicus wonders before suggesting different things on forums!
